Abstract. In visual relationship detection (VRD), the diversity of relationships often results in many unseen (i.e. zero-shot) relationships in the test set. Predicting zero-shot relationships poses a significant challenge. Traditional methods often incorporate semantic knowledge and spatial features from images, which still rely on priors in language and annotations in the dataset. Therefore, we propose encoding spatial structure information in the knowledge graph and incorporating spatial relationships from commonsense to guide predictions. The model comprises two modules: logic tensor networks that encoded the negative domain of semantic and spatial knowledge, and a commonsense knowledge graph module updated by local spatial structure as positive domain semantic knowledge. Predictions are further constrained by region connection calculus (RCC). Experimental results demonstrate competitive performance on the Visual Relationship Datasets under the zero-shot setting and the entire subset of Visual Genome. In predicate detection, it achieves comparable results to benchmarks, while significantly outperforming benchmarks in relationship and phrase detection.
摘要。在视觉关系检测（VRD）中，关系的多样性往往导致测试集中存在许多未见（即零样本）关系。预测零样本关系是一个重大挑战。传统方法通常结合图像中的语义知识和空间特征，这仍然依赖于语言中的先验知识和数据集中的注释。因此，我们提出在知识图中编码空间结构信息，并结合常识中的空间关系来指导预测。模型包括两个模块：编码语义和空间知识负域的逻辑张量网络，以及通过局部空间结构更新的常识知识图模块作为正域语义知识。预测进一步受到区域连接演算（RCC）的约束。实验结果表明，在零样本设置下，模型在视觉关系数据集和整个视觉基因组子集上具有竞争力，在谓词检测中，模型达到了基准的水平，而在关系和短语检测中显著优于基准。

1 Introduction
1 引言

Visual Relationship Detection (VRD) is a fundamental task in computer vision, expected to extract more detailed information from images than object detection [1]. Given an image, VRD identifies the relation types between object pairs in the form of R(O1,O2) . It is obvious that the diversity of objects and relations can blow up the number of predicted triples [2]. Accordingly, it is expensive to gather a dataset with each triple annotated in a normal scenario [2,3] . Zero-shot VRD refers to predicting the triples that were not observed during training. The approaches of zero-shot VRD have already found applications in many advanced computer vision tasks, such as image segmentation, image captioning, and human-object interactions [4].
视觉关系检测（VRD）是计算机视觉中的基本任务，旨在从图像中提取比目标检测更详细的信息 [1]。给定一个图像，VRD 以 R(O1,O2) 的形式识别对象对之间的关系类型。显然，对象和关系的多样性可能会使预测的三元组数量激增 [2]。因此，在正常场景中收集每个三元组都标注的数据集代价高昂 [2,3]。零样本 VRD 指的是预测训练期间未观察到的三元组。零样本 VRD 的方法已经在许多先进的计算机视觉任务中找到应用，例如图像分割、图像标注和人与对象的交互 [4]。
An important approach to enhancing zero-shot VRD is to incorporate semantic knowledge, which can leverage the similarity with triples in the training set, or utilize high-level descriptions of relationships to supervise the predictions of neural networks. Lu et al. [1] utilized word embeddings to fine-tune the likelihood of a predicted relationship, while Yu et al. [5] encoded first-order logic knowledge into Markov logic networks as symbolic knowledge to correct the erroneous reasoning of neural networks. Donadello et al. [2] improved predictions by manually incorporating semantic knowledge into logic tensor networks. Wan et al. [6] and Zareian et al. [7] utilized commonsense knowledge graphs to supervise neural networks through iterative training and interaction with knowledge graphs. While manually modeling knowledge yields high accuracy and complements incomplete annotations [2], it is costly and limited. Introducing knowledge graphs can reduce costs, but existing methods typically focus only on the triples within a knowledge graph. The semantic correlation between adjacent relationships is also part of the semantic knowledge in the knowledge graph, and it is correlated with the topology between relationships [8]. For example, in predicting the relationship between person and bike, although there is no person - hold - bike in the knowledge graph, it can be inferred through a multi-hop path: person - hand - hold - handle - bike. Furthermore, it can be observed that for hold(person, bike), person - hand - hold is more important than hand - hold - handle. Our proposed model reduces manual workload by focusing on negative domain constraints and includes a commonsense knowledge graph module. We use multi-hop paths and different topological relationships in neighboring subgraphs to update the knowledge graph during training.
提升零样本虚拟关系检测（VRD）的一个重要方法是融入语义知识，这可以利用训练集中三元组的相似性，或者使用关系的的高级描述来监督神经网络的预测。Lu等人[1]利用词嵌入微调预测关系的可能性，而Yu等人[5]将一阶逻辑知识编码为马尔可夫逻辑网络中的符号知识，以纠正神经网络的错误推理。Donadello等人[2]通过手动将语义知识融入逻辑张量网络来改进预测。Wan等人[6]和Zareian等人[7]利用常识知识图谱通过迭代训练和与知识图谱的交互来监督神经网络。虽然手动建模知识可以获得高精度并补充不完整的注释[2]，但它成本高昂且有限。引入知识图谱可以降低成本，但现有方法通常只关注知识图内的三元组。相邻关系之间的语义相关性也是知识图中语义知识的一部分，它与关系之间的拓扑结构相关[8]。例如，在预测人与自行车之间的关系时，尽管知识图中没有“人-持有-自行车”这一关系，但可以通过多跳路径推断：人-手-持有-把手-自行车。此外，可以观察到对于“持有（人，自行车）”，“人-手-持有”比“手-持有-把手”更重要。我们提出的模型通过关注负领域约束减少了手动工作负担，并包含了一个常识知识图模块。我们在训练过程中使用多跳路径和邻近子图中的不同拓扑关系来更新知识图。

Relying solely on semantic and visual features may cause the model to heavily rely on priors in language and datasets [9]. For example, in Fig. 1, the relationship between the blue box and red box is likely to be predicted as ride (person, bike) according to common triples. However, considering the spatial positions of the boxes, we can exclude ride. Therefore, adding spatial features has also been an approach for zero-shot learning. Liu et al. [10] utilized information from different views of a picture for prediction. Chiou et al. [11] and Gkanatsios et al. [12] proposed attention modules to capture spatial features. Liang et al. [3] and Jung et al. [13] fused visual, semantic, and spatial position features as inputs to neural networks. However, these methods still rely on spatial relationships between annotated boxes in the dataset. Unlike these approaches that only consider spatial relationships within pictures, we encode common spatial relationships as external knowledge as well, and utilize RCC [14] to constrain predictions. Qualitative spatial calculus plays an important role in computer vision tasks [15], and RCC is one commonly used method in this domain. RCC is a formal model which can represent spatial entities as arbitrary plane regions based on a set of jointly exhaustive and pairwise disjoint relations [16]. Research on RCC has yielded theoretical and applied results on qualitative spatial representation and reasoning [17,18] . We can use RCC to label different predicates and use the calculus between RCC(O1,O2) and RCC(O2,O3) to reason about the relationship between O1 and O3 .
单独依赖语义和视觉特征可能导致模型在语言和数据集上过度依赖先验知识 [9]。例如，在图1中，蓝色框和红色框之间的关系很可能根据常见的三元组预测为 ride (人, 自行车)。然而，考虑到框的空间位置，我们可以排除 ride。因此，添加空间特征也成为零样本学习的一种方法。Liu等人[10]利用图片不同视角的信息进行预测。Chiou等人[11]和Gkanatsios等人[12]提出了注意模块来捕捉空间特征。Liang等人[3]和Jung等人[13]将视觉、语义和空间位置特征融合作为神经网络的输入。然而，这些方法仍然依赖数据集中标注框之间的空间关系。与这些仅考虑图片内空间关系的方法不同，我们将常见空间关系编码为外部知识，并利用RCC [14] 来约束预测。定性空间微积分在计算机视觉任务中发挥着重要作用[15]，而RCC是此领域中常用的方法之一。RCC是一种形式模型，可以根据一组共同穷尽且两两互斥的关系将空间实体表示为任意的平面区域[16]。对RCC的研究在定性空间表示和推理的理论与应用方面取得了成果 [17,18]。我们可以使用RCC来标记不同的谓词，并使用 RCC(O1,O2) 与 RCC(O2,O3) 之间的微积分来推理 O1 与 O3 之间的关系。
Combining the issues addressed above,this paper introduces a model 1 consisting of two main modules: LTNs incorporating negative domains of semantic and spatial knowledge as background knowledge, and a knowledge graph constructed using positive domains of semantic knowledge. The knowledge graph is updated utilizing neighboring subgraphs and weights based on relationship topology. The final prediction is constrained by RCC. Experimental results demonstrate the competitiveness of the model on datasets.
结合上述问题，本文介绍了一个由两个主要模块组成的模型 1：一个是融入语义和空间知识负域作为背景知识的LTN，另一个是使用语义知识正域构建的知识图谱。知识图谱通过利用相邻子图和基于关系拓扑的权重进行更新。最终预测受到RCC的约束。实验结果表明，该模型在数据集上的竞争力。

2 Related Work
2 相关工作

VRD is an important task that recognizes complex relationships and interactions among various objects in an image. As it serves as the basis for many important tasks, research on VRD has attracted increasing attention in recent years.
VRD是一个识别图像中不同物体之间复杂关系和交互的重要任务。由于它是许多重要任务的基础，近年来VRD研究受到了越来越多的关注。

2.1 Visual Relationship Detection
2.1 视觉关系检测

Fundamentally, VRD is a task of categorizing predicates. Jung et al. [19] used a softmax model to classify predicates instead of the previous binary classifier, which improved the accuracy and efficiency of the model. In addition to enhancing classification accuracy, researchers have focused more on improving visual features. Russakovsky et al. [20] proposed that input from humans can be used to intervene in object detection. Xu et al. [21] introduced a method based on multi-scale context modeling, which enhances the accuracy of scene graph generation by fusing features at different scales. Peng et al. [22] used a method that combines dataset characteristics and inputs them into a fully-connected layer to obtain fused representation.
从根本上说，VRD是分类谓词的任务。Jung等人[19]使用softmax模型来分类谓词，而不是之前的二分类器，这提高了模型的准确性和效率。除了提高分类准确性，研究者们更多地关注改善视觉特征。Russakovsky等人[20]提出可以使用人类的输入来干预对象检测。Xu等人[21]引入了一种基于多尺度上下文建模的方法，通过融合不同尺度的特征来增强场景图生成的准确性。Peng等人[22]使用了一种结合数据集特征并将它们输入到全连接层以获得融合表示的方法。

2.2 Zero-Shot Learning with Knowledge
2.2 基于知识的零样本学习

The goal of VRD is to better understand the scene in a picture by recognizing the relationships between objects. Prediction effectiveness heavily relies on training. However, due to the large workload, VRD datasets annotations are incomplete and unbalanced. Thus, evaluating predictions on zero-shot triples is crucial. The performance of the model on zero-shot learning can be improved not only by processing the visual features but also by utilizing fusion knowledge wisely.
VRD的目标是通过识别物体之间的关系来更好地理解图片中的场景。预测的有效性严重依赖于训练。然而，由于工作量大，VRD数据集的注释是不完整和不平衡的。因此，对零样本三元组的预测进行评估至关重要。模型的零样本学习能力不仅可以通过处理视觉特征来提高，还可以通过明智地利用融合知识来提高。
Encoding semantic knowledge into logical constraints is a common approach to embedding semantic information into neural networks, which creates neural-symbolic systems with enhanced perception and cognition [23]. Lu et al. [1] proposed a VRD model combined with a linguistic model. Through a semantic module using pre-trained word embeddings, objects are mapped to a vector space where objects with similar semantic features are closer. Yu et al. [24] applied knowledge distillation to transfer linguistic knowledge to a visual model by pretraining a language model on a large corpus. Donadello et al. [2] extended LTNs and applied them to the classification of bounding boxes and object detection. They encoded common knowledge as logical constraints, which were trained as the background knowledge of the LTNs. The introduction of logical knowledge solves the zero-shot problem more effectively. Manigrasso et al. [25] combined LTNs with a convolution module, achieving higher accuracy in object detection compared to traditional convolutional architectures. Buffelli et al. [26] drew inspiration from LTNs to build a logic constraint module that injects semantic knowledge into various neural networks for relationship detection.
将语义知识编码为逻辑约束是将语义信息嵌入神经网络的常见方法，这创建了具有增强感知和认知能力的神经符号系统[23]。Lu等人[1]提出了一种结合语言模型的VRD模型。通过使用预训练词嵌入的语义模块，将对象映射到向量空间，其中具有相似语义特征的对象更接近。Yu等人[24]将知识蒸馏应用于将语言知识迁移到视觉模型，通过在大型语料库上预训练语言模型。Donadello等人[2]扩展了LTN，并将其应用于边界框分类和对象检测。他们将常见知识编码为逻辑约束，这些约束作为LTN的背景知识进行训练。逻辑知识的引入更有效地解决了零样本问题。Manigrasso等人[25]将LTN与卷积模块结合，相比传统卷积架构在对象检测中实现了更高的准确度。Buffelli等人[26]从LTN中获得灵感，构建了一个逻辑约束模块，将语义知识注入各种神经网络进行关系检测。
Excessive semantic knowledge can lead to predictions that heavily rely on the frequency of relationships in the knowledge. Therefore, research has been conducted on methods for fusing visual, semantic, and spatial features. Gkanatsios et al. [12] proposed ATRNet, a scene graph generation model based on attention, which combines semantic features and spatial translations between regions to build a multi-scale attention mechanism. Gkanatsios et al. [27] introduced a method combining semantic and local contextual information, improving the model's ability to represent relationships in a visual scene. Hu et al. [28] proposed a message-passing algorithm integrating linguistic prior and spatial features to propagate contextual information. Chiou et al. [11] explored the spatial module and mask attention module to capture spatial features.
过多的语义知识可能导致预测过度依赖知识中关系的频率。因此，已经进行了融合视觉、语义和空间特征的方法研究。Gkanatsios等人[12]提出了ATRNet，一个基于注意力的场景图生成模型，它结合了语义特征和区域之间的空间转换来构建多尺度注意力机制。Gkanatsios等人[27]引入了一种结合语义和局部上下文信息的方法，提高了模型表示视觉场景中关系的能力。Hu等人[28]提出了一种结合语言先验和空间特征的消息传递算法来传播上下文信息。Chiou等人[11]探讨了空间模块和掩码注意力模块以捕捉空间特征。
Combining these methods to improve the model, we jointly train our model using visual information, semantic knowledge, and spatial knowledge. We encode spatial structure information in a knowledge graph and common spatial relationships to minimize the effect of priors on the datasets and semantic knowledge.
结合这些方法来改进模型，我们通过视觉信息、语义知识和空间知识联合训练我们的模型。我们在知识图中编码空间结构信息以及常见空间关系，以最小化先验知识对数据集和语义知识的影响。

3.1 逻辑张量网络

LTNs结合了张量网络 [29] 和通过一阶逻辑 L 进行推理。我们使用 grounding（表示为 G ）来解释现实世界中的 L 以及

3.2 Knowledge Graph
3.2 知识图谱

A commonsense knowledge graph (CKG) is a knowledge graph constructed with a set of nodes N and edges E ,where the nodes represent different semantic labels (entities or predicate classes) and the edges represent relationships between nodes. Each edge is labeled with a confidence value indicating the probability of the relationship between the connected nodes. If two concepts are not related, there is no edge between them.
常见知识图谱（CKG）是一种由节点集合 N 和边集合 E 构成的知识图谱，其中节点代表不同的语义标签（实体或谓词类别），边代表节点之间的关系。每条边都标记有一个置信值，表示连接节点之间关系的概率。如果两个概念不相关，它们之间就没有边。

3.3 RCC8

RCC is a model used for spatial qualitative representation and inference [14]. RCC8 [17] is the most prevalent subset of RCC, specifying eight basic relationships between two spatial regions. Figure 2 shows examples of these relationships. These relations are disjoint and cover all possible relationships between regions. Since the relationship between any two regions is for only one of RCC8 relationships, RCC8 can be used to represent definite knowledge between two spatial regions, while indefinite knowledge can be represented by the disjunction of possible relationships. The composition table for RCC8 relations can be seen in Ref. [17].
RCC 是用于空间定性表示和推理的模型 [14]。RCC8 [17] 是 RCC 中最普遍的子集，它定义了两个空间区域之间的八种基本关系。图 2 展示了这些关系的示例。这些关系是互斥的，并涵盖了区域之间所有可能的关系。由于任意两个区域之间的关系仅限于 RCC8 中的某一种关系，因此 RCC8 可以用来表示两个空间区域之间的确定性知识，而不确定性知识可以通过可能关系的析取来表示。RCC8 关系的组合表可以在参考文献 [17] 中看到。

4.1 Neural Network Prediction
4.1 神经网络预测

As in Ref. [2],we use LTNs to encode VRD tasks. Let TRD=⟨KRD,GRD⟩ be the grounded theory,where KRD is the knowledge base of LTNs. The knowledge base KRD includes two parts: positive and negative examples obtained by analyzing annotations of the dataset, and background knowledge. For background knowledge, we not only use semantic knowledge but also take spatial knowledge into account, which is one of our innovations. For ease of representation, the background knowledge is encoded in the form of negation of logical constraints. For semantic knowledge, we consider manually writing common sense as semantic knowledge. For spatial knowledge, we refer to RCC8 in Fig. 2 for encoding. By analyzing all the images in the training set, we can obtain the impossible RCC8 relations of predicates. For instance, for talk, the set of negative RCC8 relations is {EQ,EC,TPP,NTPP} . One corresponding representation is ∀xy(EQ(x,y))→¬talk(x,y) .
与参考文献 [2] 中一样，我们使用 LTN 编码 VRD 任务。令 TRD=⟨KRD,GRD⟩ 为 grounded theory，其中 KRD 是 LTN 的知识库。知识库 KRD 包括两部分：通过分析数据集注释获得的正例和反例，以及背景知识。对于背景知识，我们不仅使用语义知识，还考虑了空间知识，这是我们的一项创新。为了方便表示，背景知识以逻辑约束的否定形式编码。对于语义知识，我们考虑手动编写常见常识作为语义知识。对于空间知识，我们参考图 2 中的 RCC8 进行编码。通过分析训练集中的所有图像，我们可以获得谓词的不可能 RCC8 关系集合。例如，对于 talk，负 RCC8 关系的集合是 {EQ,EC,TPP,NTPP}。一个相应的表示是 ∀xy(EQ(x,y))→¬talk(x,y)。

After calculating the grounding value of the atomic formula, the logical constraints in the KRD are combined based on logical operators. However,the calculation of quantifiers is susceptible to special relations with a small number of samples, which can seriously affect the subsequent steps. Therefore, Ref [2] proposed using the harmonic mean to improve the calculation of quantifiers. Building upon this, we introduce the frequency of predicates to further minimize the impact.
计算原子公式的接地值后，基于逻辑运算符将 KRD 中的逻辑约束进行组合。然而，量词的计算容易受到少量样本的特殊关系的影响，这可能会严重影响后续步骤。因此，文献 [2] 提出使用调和平均数来改进量词的计算。在此基础上，我们引入谓词的频率，以进一步最小化影响。

4.2 Knowledge Graph Prediction
4.2 知识图谱预测

Our CKG is primarily derived from ConceptNet, WordNet, and datasets. We query the CKG for word embedding n ,and according to the positional relationship of bounding boxes, we use a one-hot vector to represent RCC8 relationships. Then, we concatenate these vectors to get the relationship embedding.
我们的CKG主要来源于ConceptNet、WordNet和数据集。我们查询CKG以获取词嵌入 n，并根据边界框的位置关系，我们使用独热向量表示RCC8关系。然后，我们将这些向量连接起来以获得关系嵌入。
The relationship embedding is updated iteratively during training. The relationship between two related classes may involve a multi-hop path in CKG. The nodes in the path can influence the prediction of the relationship. Such multi-hop paths contain more information than one-hop paths. Therefore, during training, we obtain multi-hop paths between subjects and objects and segment them into closed subgraphs for iterative updating. In the process of constructing the subgraph,for each node in the subgraph,we label it with ⟨dsub,dobj⟩ ,where dsub denotes the shortest path distance of the node from the subject (likewise for dobj ). 5 Evaluation
5 评估

5.1 Evaluation Protocol
5.1 评估协议

The experimental dataset used are Visual Relationship Datasets (VRDs) [1] and a subset of Visual Genome proposed in Ref. [21], called VG150. VRDs includes 5000 images containing 70 predicates and 100 entities, which comprise 37,993 visual relation instances and 6,672 triples types. There are 1,877 relationships that appear only in the test set, which are used to evaluate zero-shot learning. VG150 includes 108,703 images containing 50 predicates and 150 entities. It has 1,174,692 visual relation instances and 19,237 triples types in total.
实验中使用的数据集是视觉关系数据集（VRDs）[1] 和文献 [21] 中提出的 Visual Genome 的一个子集，称为 VG150。VRDs 包括包含 70 个谓词和 100 个实体的 5000 张图像，包括 37,993 个视觉关系实例和 6,672 个三元组类型。在测试集中仅出现 1,877 个关系，用于评估零样本学习。VG150 包括包含 50 个谓词和 150 个实体的 108,703 张图像。它总共有 1,174,692 个视觉关系实例和 19,237 个三元组类型。
Following Ref. [1] we use Recall as our performance metric, denoted as Recall@x (R@x) ,which refers to the proportion of relationships that actually exist among the first x relationships predicted. We evaluate our method on the three classical visual relationship detection tasks proposed in Ref. [1]: predicate detection, phrase detection, and relationship detection.
根据文献 [1]，我们使用召回率作为性能指标，表示为 Recall@x (R@x)，指的是在预测的前 x 个关系中实际存在的关系比例。我们在文献 [1] 提出的三个经典视觉关系检测任务上评估我们的方法：谓词检测、短语检测和关系检测。

5.2 Results
5.2 结果

To validate the effectiveness of our method, we selected six methods for zero-shot prediction using either semantic or spatial information for comparison: LP [1] integrates visual and language features before detecting visual relationships. JVSE [30] proposes a model using the similarity between visual vectors and semantic vectors. Multi-view [10] proposes a multi-view image generation approach that utilizes spatial information to transfer the 2D visual space to the 3D multi-view space. IVDRC [6] utilizes a knowledge graph constructed from the dataset and iteratively updated by a neural network for prediction. DSR [3] introduces a framework, which facilitates the co-occurrence of relationships. LTN [2] is the first to propose the LTNs module for VRD. In predicate detection, the entity pairs are fixed. Therefore, spatial constraints do not work. Instead, we use word frequency to constrain the neural network predictions.
为了验证我们方法的有效性，我们选择了六种用于零样本预测的方法，它们分别使用语义或空间信息进行比较：LP [1] 在检测视觉关系之前整合视觉和语言特征。JVSE [30] 提出了一个使用视觉向量和语义向量之间相似性的模型。Multi-view [10] 提出了一种多视角图像生成方法，该方法利用空间信息将 2D 视觉空间转换到 3D 多视角空间。IVDRC [6] 利用从数据集构建的知识图，并通过神经网络迭代更新来进行预测。DSR [3] 引入了一个框架，该框架促进了关系的共现。LTN [2] 是第一个提出用于视觉关系检测的 LTNs 模块。在谓词检测中，实体对是固定的。因此，空间约束不起作用。相反，我们使用词频来约束神经网络的预测。

We use the object detection results provided in Ref. [1], detected by RCNN [31], the same object detection data used for JVSE and LTN. Faster-RCNN for object detection is used by others. In the entire VG150, for predicate detection, our method outperforms LTN and SOTA. After weighting the knowledge in the knowledge base by word frequency, the more frequently occurring knowledge is more fully utilized, which correspondingly reduces the influence of uncommon knowledge, resulting in a more significant improvement. 6 Conclusion
6 结论

Due to the large scale of the relation detection dataset and the complexity of the images, annotating all images in the training set with complete detail is unfeasible. Consequently, zero-shot learning emerges as a critical metric in VRD. 26. Buffelli, D., Tsamoura, E.: Scalable theory-driven regularization of scene graph spectives. Semantic knowledge primarily stems from multi-hop information and the topology of paths in CKG, while spatial knowledge is derived from RCC8. We encode common spatial relations as spatial knowledge and propose a model that integrates external Semantic and spatial knowledge for VRD. The final prediction fuses results from the neural network and knowledge graph modules, further constrained by RCC8. Through experimental comparisons, we observe that employing the Semantic and spatial knowledge fusion method improves the model's recall. Our approach achieves comparable or even superior performance to the state-of-the-art under the zero-shot setting. Future research will focus on integrating visual features with external knowledge to further enhance predictions.
