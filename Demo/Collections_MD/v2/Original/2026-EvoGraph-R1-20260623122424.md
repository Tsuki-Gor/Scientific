## Abstract
## 摘要


Retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) has emerged as a critical paradigm for grounding Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs) in external knowledge. Recent GraphRAG methods introduce structured entity-relation graphs to improve retrieval and reasoning. However, they remain limited by treating knowledge graphs as static data structures built offline and queried in a single pass. This static paradigm misaligns with the interactive, iterative nature of knowledge-intensive reasoning, creating three bottlenecks: (i) text-centric fragmentation that impedes cross-modal reasoning, (ii) frozen structures unable to incorporate new evidence or correct errors, and (iii) rigid single-pass retrieval without adaptive refinement. To overcome these limitations, we introduce EvoGraph-R1, a self-evolving GraphRAG framework that reconceptualizes knowledge graphs as dynamic environments shaped through agent interactions. We formulate retrieval as a Markov Decision Process (MDP) where the agent observes the graph state and executes actions to query (GRAPHRETRIEVE), expand (WEBSEARCH), refine (GRAPHEDIT), or terminate (ANSWER) the reasoning. These actions reshape the hypergraph structure and generate feedback signals that guide subsequent evolution. Through this closed loop, the hypergraph evolves by integrating new evidence, correcting errors, and refining structure to support multi-hop reasoning. Experiments on multimodal VQA and text QA benchmarks demonstrate substantial improvements over existing RAG baselines in accuracy, coverage, and traceability, establishing self-evolving knowledge graphs as a fundamental paradigm across modalities.
检索增强生成（RAG）已成为一种关键范式，用于将多模态大语言模型（MLLM）扎根于外部知识。近期的 GraphRAG 方法引入结构化的实体—关系图，以提升检索与推理。然而，它们仍受限于将知识图当作离线构建的静态数据结构，并以单次遍历进行查询。这种静态范式与知识密集型推理的交互、迭代特性不匹配，形成三大瓶颈：（i）以文本为中心的碎片化阻碍跨模态推理；（ii）结构被冻结，无法吸纳新证据或纠正错误；（iii）缺乏自适应精修的僵化单次检索。为克服这些限制，我们提出 EvoGraph-R1，这是一种自进化的 GraphRAG 框架，将知识图重新设想为由智能体交互塑造的动态环境。我们将检索建模为马尔可夫决策过程（MDP）：智能体观察图状态，并执行动作来查询（GRAPHRETRIEVE）、扩展（WEBSEARCH）、精修（GRAPHEDIT）或终止（ANSWER）推理。这些动作会重塑超图结构，并生成反馈信号以指导后续演化。通过这种闭环，超图能够通过整合新证据、纠正错误并精炼结构来进化，从而支持多跳推理。在多模态 VQA 和文本问答基准上的实验表明，相较现有 RAG 基线，在准确率、覆盖率和可追溯性方面均有显著提升，确立了自进化知识图作为跨模态的基础范式。


## 1. Introduction
## 1. 引言

Multimodal large language models (MLLMs) excel at visual reasoning tasks [1, 19], yet their parametric memory remains limited [23]. No model can internalize the vast, ever-evolving knowledge of the open world. Retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) addresses this by enabling dynamic access to external knowledge at inference time, improving factual grounding in knowledge-intensive visual question answering (VQA) [11]. However, existing multimodal RAG frameworks struggle with complex compositional reasoning [16], as retrieved evidence is often reduced to disjoint text snippets or coarse page-level screen-shots [6, 22, 33], hindering fine-grained structural modeling and cross-modal alignment.
多模态大语言模型（MLLMs）在视觉推理任务上表现出色[1, 19]，但其参数化记忆仍然有限[23]。没有任何模型能够内化开放世界中海量且不断演化的知识。检索增强生成（RAG）通过在推理时启用对外部知识的动态访问，从而提升知识密集型视觉问答（VQA）中的事实落地能力[11]。然而，现有的多模态RAG框架在复杂的组合式推理[16]上仍然吃力，因为检索到的证据往往被简化为彼此割裂的文本片段或粗粒度的逐页截图[6, 22, 33]，从而难以进行细粒度的结构建模以及跨模态对齐。

Recent GraphRAG methods $\left\lbrack  {5,8,9,{21}}\right\rbrack$ mitigate these limitations by leveraging knowledge graphs for retrieval and reasoning, thereby facilitating structured reasoning. The typical pipeline comprises three stages: (i) constructing graphs via LLM-based entity-relation extraction [41]; (ii) retrieving relevant subgraphs; and (iii) generating answers conditioned on graph knowledge. However, we identify a key limitation: existing systems treat the knowledge graph as a static structure, built offline in a single pass. This one-shot process makes graph quality difficult to guarantee, especially on long or multimodal inputs. As illustrated in Figure 1, such static pipelines often yield incomplete or noisy graphs that propagate errors throughout retrieval and reasoning. Consequently, these limitations manifest as three bottlenecks: (1) Text-centric fragmentation. LLM-based extraction collapses rich multimodal evidence into isolated textual tuples, leading to missing relations, fragmented structures, and loss of long-range or cross-modal dependencies [35]. (2) Static structure. Graphs are constructed offline and remain fixed during inference, preventing the incorporation of new evidence, error correction, or coverage of unseen topics [26]. (3) Rigid retrieval. Retrieval is executed as a single-pass operation without iterative refinement. When initial evidence is insufficient, the system cannot revise its strategy, explore alternatives, or invoke external search [34].
近期的GraphRAG方法$\left\lbrack  {5,8,9,{21}}\right\rbrack$通过利用知识图谱进行检索与推理来缓解这些限制，从而促进结构化推理。典型流程包含三个阶段：（i）通过基于LLM的实体-关系抽取构建图[41]；（ii）检索相关子图；（iii）基于图谱知识生成答案。然而，我们发现一个关键限制：现有系统将知识图谱视为静态结构，并在离线单次构建完成。这个“一次性”过程使得图的质量难以保证，尤其是面对长文本或多模态输入时。如图1所示，这类静态管线往往会产生不完整或含噪的图，并将错误在检索与推理过程中不断放大。因此，这些限制主要体现在三个瓶颈：（1）以文本为中心的碎片化。基于LLM的抽取会把丰富的多模态证据压缩成孤立的文本元组，导致缺失关系、结构被切碎，以及长距离或跨模态依赖的丢失[35]。（2）静态结构。图谱在离线构建后在推理阶段保持不变，无法纳入新证据、进行错误修正或覆盖未见主题[26]。（3）僵化的检索。检索以单次操作完成，没有迭代式的精炼。当初始证据不足时，系统无法调整策略、探索替代方案或调用外部搜索[34]。


These limitations stem from treating the knowledge graph as a passive data structure rather than an active reasoning substrate. Human reasoning over knowledge is inherently interactive and iterative [2]: we collect initial evidence, identify gaps, seek additional information, reconcile contradictions, and progressively refine our understanding. This mirrors the agent-environment interaction paradigm in reinforcement learning [20], where agents perceive states, take actions that modify those states, and receive feedback to guide future decisions. Inspired by this paradigm, we reconceptualize multimodal GraphRAG as a Markov Decision Process (MDP) [17], where the knowledge graph becomes a dynamic environment that co-evolves with the reasoning process:
这些限制源于将知识图谱当作被动数据结构，而非主动推理载体。人类在知识上的推理本质上是交互且迭代的[2]：我们收集初始证据，识别空缺，寻求更多信息，调和矛盾，并逐步精炼理解。这与强化学习中的智能体-环境交互范式类似[20]：智能体感知状态，采取能改变状态的行动，并获得反馈以指导未来决策。受此启发，我们将多模态GraphRAG重新表述为马尔可夫决策过程（MDP）[17]：知识图谱成为一个动态环境，与推理过程共同演化：


- Environment State: the multimodal knowledge hyper-graph, encoding retrieved evidence (entities, relations, visual objects) and their structural organization (connectivity, alignment, relevance scores).
- 环境状态：多模态知识超图，编码检索到的证据（实体、关系、视觉对象）及其结构组织（连通性、对齐、相关性分数）。


- Actions: operations that query the graph (GRAPHRETRIEVE), expand it (WEBSEARCH) for external knowledge), refine it (GRAPHEDIT to insert/update/delete elements), or terminate (ANSWER).
- 行动：对图谱的查询操作（GRAPHRETRIEVE），为外部知识扩展图谱（WEBSEARCH），通过GRAPHEDIT对其进行修订（插入/更新/删除元素），或终止（ANSWER）。


- Rewards: trajectory-level signals that assess reasoning quality (structural correctness and answer accuracy) and efficiency (computational cost).
- 奖励：对推理质量的轨迹级信号（结构正确性与答案准确性）以及对效率的评估（计算成本）。


- Policy: the agent's action selection strategy, learned through reinforcement learning to maximize answer accuracy while maintaining efficiency.
- 策略：智能体的行动选择策略，通过强化学习获得，以在保证效率的同时最大化答案准确性。


This formulation enables a self-evolving paradigm [3]: the agent does not merely retrieve from a fixed graph but actively shapes it by adding verified evidence, correcting inconsistencies, pruning noise, and reorganizing structures to support multi-hop inference.
这种表述带来了一种自我演化范式[3]：智能体并不只是从固定图谱中检索，而是通过加入已验证证据、纠正不一致、剪除噪声并重组结构来主动塑造它，以支持多跳推理。


To instantiate this paradigm, we present EvoGraph-R1, an agentic multimodal GraphRAG framework that unifies retrieval and knowledge evolution into a closed-loop interaction process. Initialized from a minimal hypergraph, EvoGraph-R1 progressively refines its structure through agent-environment interactions. At each step, the agent observes the current graph state to identify knowledge gaps, then selects an action that reshapes the hypergraph and generates feedback signals to guide subsequent decisions. Through this iterative cycle, EvoGraph-R1 transforms an initially sparse, noisy hypergraph into a coherent, query-specific knowledge state. Our self-evolving MDP paradigm generalizes beyond multimodal settings. By removing visual components while retaining the agent-driven evolution mechanism, the framework naturally adapts to text-only scenarios. We demonstrate state-of-the-art performance on both multimodal [12, 30] and text QA benchmarks [40], validating that treating knowledge graphs as interactive environments provides value independent of modality. Our main contributions are as follows:
为落地这一范式，我们提出EvoGraph-R1：一种面向智能体的多模态GraphRAG框架，将检索与知识演化统一为闭环的交互过程。EvoGraph-R1从一个最小的超图初始化，并通过智能体-环境交互逐步细化其结构。每一步，智能体先观察当前图谱状态以定位知识缺口，然后选择一种重塑超图的行动，并生成反馈信号以指导后续决策。通过这种迭代循环，EvoGraph-R1将最初稀疏且含噪的超图转化为连贯的、面向查询的知识状态。我们的自我演化MDP范式可推广到多模态之外：在移除视觉组件的同时保留由智能体驱动的演化机制，该框架能够自然适配文本-only场景。我们在多模态[12, 30]与文本问答基准[40]上展示了最先进的性能，验证了将知识图谱视为交互式环境能够带来与模态无关的价值。我们的主要贡献如下：


- Self-evolving retrieval paradigm. We introduce the first framework that models multimodal knowledge graphs as MDP environments, unifying retrieval, reasoning, and knowledge evolution through agent-environment interaction.
- 自我演化的检索范式。我们提出首个框架，将多模态知识图谱建模为MDP环境，通过智能体—环境交互，将检索、推理与知识演化统一起来。


- EvoGraph-R1 framework. We design an autonomous agent operating over multimodal knowledge hypergraphs via four action types (GRAPHRETRIEVE, WEBSEARCH, GRAPHEDIT, ANSWER), implementing closed-loop coevolution of graph structure and reasoning.
- EvoGraph-R1 框架。我们设计了一个自主智能体，借助四种动作类型（GRAPHRETRIEVE、WEBSEARCH、GRAPHEDIT、ANSWER）在多模态知识超图上运行，实现图结构与推理的闭环共进化。


- State-of-the-art performance. We conduct multiple experiments on knowledge-intensive multimodal VQA and text QA benchmarks. Experimental results show our framework substantially outperforms existing RAG, GraphRAG, and search-augmented baselines in accuracy, efficiency, and evidential traceability.
- 最先进的性能。我们在知识密集型多模态VQA与文本问答基准上开展了多项实验。实验结果表明，我们的框架在准确性、效率和证据可追溯性方面显著优于现有的RAG、GraphRAG以及搜索增强基线。


## 2. Related Work
## 2. 相关工作


RAG and GraphRAG. Retrieval-augmented generation enhances LLM factuality by retrieving external evidence during inference [18]. While standard RAG retrieves flat text chunks without structured reasoning [27], GraphRAG [5] represents knowledge as entity-relation graphs, retrieving subgraphs and reasoning paths [5, 13]. Graph-R1 [20] frames retrieval as interactive agent-environment interaction over hypergraphs, optimizing multi-turn exploration with reinforcement learning [7, 20]. Despite these advances, most GraphRAG systems assume offline, fixed graphs with one-shot retrieval. Graphs cannot be updated with new evidence or repaired at inference time, limiting robustness in open-world settings. EvoGraph-R1 departs from this static paradigm by enabling online graph expansion, updating, and pruning through agent-driven evolution.
RAG 与 GraphRAG。检索增强生成通过在推理过程中检索外部证据来提升 LLM 的事实性[18]。而标准 RAG 通常只检索扁平的文本片段，不具备结构化推理能力[27]；GraphRAG [5] 将知识表示为实体-关系图，检索子图并规划推理路径[5, 13]。Graph-R1 [20] 将检索建模为在超图上的交互式智能体-环境交互，通过强化学习优化多轮探索[7, 20]。尽管如此，大多数 GraphRAG 系统仍假设离线、固定图，并采用一次性检索。图无法在推理时依据新证据进行更新或修复，因而在开放世界场景下鲁棒性受限。EvoGraph-R1 摆脱这种静态范式，通过智能体驱动的演化实现在线图扩展、更新与剪枝。


Multimodal RAG. Multimodal RAG extends text-only retrieval across modalities through modality-specific pipelines: document-oriented systems preserve layouts as images [6, 36, 39] but lack relational grounding; scene-graph approaches link visual regions to text but flatten structured elements [35]. VLM-based methods [22, 32, 33] retrieve document images to avoid OCR errors but introduce redundancy. RAG-Anything [9] constructs graphs encoding cross-modal relationships, yet still assumes static, offline structures. EvoGraph-R1 incrementally builds, updates, and prunes multimodal hypergraphs during inference, shifting retrieval from a static one-shot answering paradigm to maintaining a persistent, evolving knowledge state.
多模态 RAG。多模态 RAG 将文本为主的检索扩展到多种模态，通常通过面向模态的流水线来实现：文档导向系统保留版式并以图像形式表示[6, 36, 39]，但缺乏关系层面的落地；场景图方法将视觉区域与文本关联起来，却会把结构化元素摊平[35]。基于 VLM 的方法[22, 32, 33]通过检索文档图像来避免 OCR 错误，但会引入冗余。RAG-Anything [9] 构建用于编码跨模态关系的图，但仍沿用静态、离线结构的假设。EvoGraph-R1 在推理过程中对多模态超图进行增量构建、更新与剪枝，将检索从“静态的一次性回答”范式转变为维护一个持久且可演化的知识状态。


Web-search Equipped MLLMs. Recent work equips MLLMs with active web access for autonomous search and result integration. Search-R1 [14] uses RL to interleave reasoning with web search; MMSearch-R1 [38] extends this to multimodal settings with end-to-end RL. While demonstrating the value of interactive retrieval, these systems treat retrieved content as transient prompt context. EvoGraph-R1 instead regards each retrieval step, including web evidence, as a state update to a persistent hypergraph that is continuously expanded, revised, and filtered, thereby enabling systematic multi-hop reasoning with explicit traceability.
配备网页搜索的 MLLM。近期工作让 MLLM 具备主动的网络访问能力，用于自主搜索与结果整合。Search-R1 [14] 使用强化学习将推理与网页搜索交织；MMSearch-R1 [38] 将其扩展到多模态场景，并实现端到端强化学习。尽管这些系统展示了交互式检索的价值，但它们把检索到的内容视为短暂的提示上下文。EvoGraph-R1 则相反：将每一步检索（包括网页证据）都视为对一个持久超图的状态更新，并持续扩展、修订与过滤，从而实现带有明确可追溯性的系统化多跳推理。

## 3. 方法


### 3.1. Overview
### 3.1. 概述


EvoGraph-R1 comprises three components: (1) Multimodal Hypergraph Construction creates a unified cross-modal knowledge representation; (2) Agent-Based Graph Evolution implements iterative graph refinement through four actions; (3) Reinforcement Learning optimizes the agent policy using trajectory-level rewards. An overview is presented in Figure 2.
EvoGraph-R1由三个组件组成：(1)多模态超图构建创建统一的跨模态知识表示；(2)基于智能体的图演化通过四种动作实现迭代式图精炼；(3)强化学习利用轨迹级奖励优化智能体策略。图2给出了总体概览。


### 3.2. Problem Setup
### 3.2. 问题设置


In knowledge-driven VQA, the model receives an image-question pair $\left( {{I}_{q},q}\right)$ and generates an answer $a$ by retrieving relevant knowledge from $\mathcal{B} = {\left\{  \left( {T}_{i},{I}_{i}\right) \right\}  }_{i = 1}^{N}$ ,where each document contains text ${T}_{i}$ and image ${I}_{i}$ . This requires visual and textual reasoning, demanding that the model align and synthesize cross-modal content effectively. Unlike traditional RAG systems that retrieve static documents, our framework maintains a persistent knowledge hypergraph that is incrementally expanded, updated, and pruned in response to queries, enabling systematic reasoning over interconnected multimodal evidence.
在知识驱动的 VQA 中，模型接收一对图像-问题 $\left( {{I}_{q},q}\right)$，并通过从 $\mathcal{B} = {\left\{  \left( {T}_{i},{I}_{i}\right) \right\}  }_{i = 1}^{N}$ 检索相关知识生成答案 $a$，其中每个文档都包含文本 ${T}_{i}$ 和图像 ${I}_{i}$。这需要视觉与文本推理，要求模型有效对齐并综合跨模态内容。不同于检索静态文档的传统 RAG 系统，我们的框架维护一个持久的知识超图，并根据查询逐步扩展、更新和剪枝，从而实现对相互关联的多模态证据进行系统推理。

### 3.3. Multimodal Hypergraph Construction
### 3.3. 多模态超图构建


We construct a unified cross-modal knowledge hypergraph ${\mathcal{G}}_{H} = \left( {\mathcal{V},\mathcal{E}}\right)$ from a multimodal corpus through three stages: textual extraction, visual construction, and cross-modal fusion. This hypergraph serves as the initial environment state for agent-based retrieval and reasoning.
我们通过三个阶段——文本抽取、视觉构建和跨模态融合——从多模态语料中构建统一的跨模态知识超图 ${\mathcal{G}}_{H} = \left( {\mathcal{V},\mathcal{E}}\right)$。该超图作为基于智能体的检索与推理的初始环境状态。


Textual Subgraph Extraction. Given the input text $d$ ,we segment it into knowledge fragments represented as hyperedges ${\mathcal{E}}_{H}^{\left( d\right) } = \left\{  {{e}_{1},\ldots ,{e}_{k}}\right\}$ ,where each ${e}_{i} = \; \left( {{e}_{i}^{\text{ text }},{\mathcal{V}}_{{e}_{i}},{r}_{i},{\sigma }_{i}}\right)$ contains a natural-language description, entity set,relation type,and confidence score ${\sigma }_{i} \in  (0,{10}\rbrack$ . Following prior work on hypergraph-structured knowledge $\left\lbrack  {8,{21}}\right\rbrack$ ,we employ an $n$ -ary relation extraction prompt with an MLLM-based extractor ${\pi }_{\text{ ext }}$ :
文本子图抽取。给定输入文本 $d$ ，我们将其分割为表示为超边的知识片段 ${\mathcal{E}}_{H}^{\left( d\right) } = \left\{  {{e}_{1},\ldots ,{e}_{k}}\right\}$ ，其中每个 ${e}_{i} = \; \left( {{e}_{i}^{\text{ text }},{\mathcal{V}}_{{e}_{i}},{r}_{i},{\sigma }_{i}}\right)$ 包含自然语言描述、实体集合、关系类型以及置信度分数 ${\sigma }_{i} \in  (0,{10}\rbrack$。遵循关于超图结构化知识 $\left\lbrack  {8,{21}}\right\rbrack$ 的既有工作，我们使用一个带 $n$ -元关系抽取的提示，并采用基于 MLLM 的抽取器 ${\pi }_{\text{ ext }}$：


$$
{\mathcal{F}}_{d}^{\left( n\right) } = \left\{  {{f}_{1},\ldots ,{f}_{k}}\right\}   \sim  {\pi }_{\text{ ext }}\left( d\right) , \tag{1}
$$



where each extracted ${f}_{\ell } = \left( {{e}_{\ell },{\mathcal{V}}_{{e}_{\ell }}}\right)$ associates a hyperedge ${e}_{\ell }$ with its participating entity set ${\mathcal{V}}_{{e}_{\ell }}$ . This formulation allows each text segment to contribute multiple, potentially overlapping $n$ -ary relational facts to the unified textual hy-pergraph ${\mathcal{G}}_{H}^{\text{ text }} = \left( {{\mathcal{V}}^{\text{ text }},{\mathcal{E}}^{\text{ text }}}\right)$ ,facilitating high-order relational reasoning beyond binary relations.
其中，每个抽取到的 ${f}_{\ell } = \left( {{e}_{\ell },{\mathcal{V}}_{{e}_{\ell }}}\right)$ 将一个超边 ${e}_{\ell }$ 关联到其参与的实体集合 ${\mathcal{V}}_{{e}_{\ell }}$。这种表述使得每个文本片段都能为统一文本超图 ${\mathcal{G}}_{H}^{\text{ text }} = \left( {{\mathcal{V}}^{\text{ text }},{\mathcal{E}}^{\text{ text }}}\right)$ 提供多个、可能重叠的 $n$ -元关系事实，从而在超越二元关系的层面上支持高阶关系推理。


Visual Subgraph Construction. While text naturally lends itself to entity-relation extraction, visual content requires special handling to preserve scene-level semantics. For each image $x \in  {I}_{i}$ ,we employ extractor ${\pi }_{\text{ ext }}$ to generate a detailed natural language scene description together with the primary object names detected within the image. This scene description is then encoded as a high-order hy-peredge, serving as an "anchor" for all entities and relations grounded in the image:
视觉子图构建。尽管文本天然适合进行实体-关系抽取，但视觉内容需要特殊处理以保留场景级语义。对于每张图像 $x \in  {I}_{i}$ ，我们使用抽取器 ${\pi }_{\text{ ext }}$ 生成详细的自然语言场景描述，并同时给出图像中检测到的主要物体名称。随后，将该场景描述编码为高阶超边，用作所有以图像为基础的实体与关系的“锚点”：


$$
{u}_{x} = \left( {\mathrm{{id}} = x,\text{ type } = \text{ image,mod } = \text{ vis }}\right) , \tag{2}
$$



where ${u}_{x}$ is the anchor node representing the image. Entities and finer-grained visual relational facts are subsequently extracted from the scene description and associated with ${u}_{x}$ ,yielding
其中 ${u}_{x}$ 是表示该图像的锚点节点。接下来从场景描述中抽取实体以及更细粒度的视觉关系事实，并与 ${u}_{x}$ 关联，从而形成


$$
\left( {{\mathcal{V}}_{x}^{\text{ vis }},{\mathcal{E}}_{x}^{\text{ vis }}}\right)  = {\pi }_{\text{ ext }}\left( x\right) . \tag{3}
$$



Each visual hyperedge $e = \left( {{\mathcal{V}}_{e},{r}_{e},{\sigma }_{e},}\right.$ mod $=$ vis $)$ includes the anchor node $\left( {{u}_{x} \in  {\mathcal{V}}_{e}}\right)$ ensuring all extracted information remains grounded in its image context, preventing visual facts from floating without source attribution. All image-specific subgraphs ${\mathcal{G}}_{x}^{\text{ vis }} = \left( {\left\{  {u}_{x}\right\}   \cup  {\mathcal{V}}_{x}^{\text{ vis }},{\mathcal{E}}_{x}^{\text{ vis }}}\right)$ are integrated across the corpus, forming the complete visual subgraph ${\mathcal{G}}_{H}^{\text{ vis }} = \left( {{\mathcal{V}}^{\text{ vis }},{\mathcal{E}}^{\text{ vis }}}\right)$ .
每个视觉超边 $e = \left( {{\mathcal{V}}_{e},{r}_{e},{\sigma }_{e},}\right.$ mod $=$ vis $)$ 都包含锚点节点 $\left( {{u}_{x} \in  {\mathcal{V}}_{e}}\right)$，以确保所有抽取信息都扎根于其图像上下文，避免视觉事实脱离来源而漂浮。所有与图像相关的子图 ${\mathcal{G}}_{x}^{\text{ vis }} = \left( {\left\{  {u}_{x}\right\}   \cup  {\mathcal{V}}_{x}^{\text{ vis }},{\mathcal{E}}_{x}^{\text{ vis }}}\right)$ 会在整个语料中进行整合，形成完整的视觉子图 ${\mathcal{G}}_{H}^{\text{ vis }} = \left( {{\mathcal{V}}^{\text{ vis }},{\mathcal{E}}^{\text{ vis }}}\right)$。


Cross-Modal Graph Fusion. To unify textual and visual subgraphs, we normalize entity labels and apply the entity-resolution function $\phi$ ,matching visual and textual mentions using string similarity and embedding proximity. Matched entities merge into canonical nodes, inheriting attributes and contextual edges from both modalities. All hyperedges from original subgraphs reconnect to merged nodes,yielding the fused graph ${\mathcal{G}}_{H} = \left( {\mathcal{V},\mathcal{E}}\right)$ :
跨模态图融合。为了统一文本与视觉子图，我们对实体标签进行归一化，并应用实体解析函数 $\phi$，通过字符串相似度和嵌入接近度匹配视觉与文本中的提及。匹配到的实体会合并为规范节点，并继承来自两种模态的属性与上下文边。原始子图中的所有超边都会重新连接到这些合并节点，从而得到融合图 ${\mathcal{G}}_{H} = \left( {\mathcal{V},\mathcal{E}}\right)$：


$$
\mathcal{V} = \operatorname{Resolve}\left( {{\mathcal{V}}^{\text{ text }} \cup  {\mathcal{V}}^{\text{ vis }},\phi }\right) ,\;\mathcal{E} = {\mathcal{E}}^{\text{ text }} \cup  {\mathcal{E}}^{\text{ vis }}. \tag{4}
$$



Offline Graph Indexing. For efficient retrieval during agent interaction, we embed all graph elements (textual entities, visual entities, hyperedges) using multimodal encoder $f\left( \cdot \right)$ (e.g.,GME [42]) into a shared semantic space:
离线图索引。为提高智能体交互期间的检索效率，我们使用多模态编码器$f\left( \cdot \right)$（如 GME [42]）将所有图元素（文本实体、视觉实体、超边）嵌入到共享语义空间中：


$$
{\phi }_{\text{ entity }} = f\left( {{v}^{\text{ text }} \cup  {v}^{\text{ vis }}}\right) ,\;{\phi }_{\text{ edge }} = f\left( e\right) ,\forall e \in  \mathcal{E}. \tag{5}
$$



This unified hypergraph ${\mathcal{G}}_{H} = \left( {\mathcal{V},\mathcal{E}}\right)$ retains fine-grained cross-modal semantics and forms the initial environment state for agent-driven evolution.
该统一超图${\mathcal{G}}_{H} = \left( {\mathcal{V},\mathcal{E}}\right)$保留了细粒度的跨模态语义，并构成智能体驱动演化的初始环境状态。


### 3.4. Agent-Based Graph Evolution
### 3.4. 基于智能体的图演化


While the initial hypergraph ${\mathcal{G}}_{H}$ provides structured knowledge, automatically constructed graphs are often incomplete and noisy. Moreover, static graphs fundamentally cannot adapt to query-specific information needs or incorporate newly discovered evidence. To address these limitations, we formulate retrieval as an agent-environment interaction process where the knowledge graph becomes a dynamic environment that co-evolves with reasoning.
尽管初始超图${\mathcal{G}}_{H}$提供了结构化知识，但自动构建的图往往不完整且噪声较多。此外，静态图从根本上无法适应特定查询的信息需求，也无法纳入新发现的证据。为解决这些限制，我们将检索表述为一种智能体-环境交互过程，使知识图谱成为一个动态环境，并与推理共同演化。


##### 3.4.1.MDP Formulation
##### 3.4.1. MDP 形式化


We model the agent-graph interaction as a discrete-time Markov decision process over steps $t = 0,1,\ldots ,T$ . At initialization,the agent receives the multimodal hypergraph ${\mathcal{G}}_{0}$ and query $q$ . The process terminates when the agent selects ANSWER action or reaches step limit $T$ ,then it generates a final answer conditioned on the evolved hypergraph ${\mathcal{G}}_{T}$ . We formalize the key components of this MDP as:
我们将智能体-图交互建模为跨步数 $t = 0,1,\ldots ,T$ 的离散时间马尔可夫决策过程。初始化时，智能体接收多模态超图 ${\mathcal{G}}_{0}$ 和查询 $q$。当智能体选择 ANSWER 动作或达到步数上限 $T$ 时，过程终止，然后基于演化后的超图 ${\mathcal{G}}_{T}$ 生成最终答案。我们将该 MDP 的关键组成形式化如下：


Environment State. At step $t$ ,the environment state captures the evolving knowledge context:
环境状态。在步骤 $t$ ，环境状态刻画不断演化的知识上下文：


$$
{s}_{t} = \left( {{\mathcal{G}}_{t},{\mathcal{H}}_{t},q}\right) , \tag{6}
$$



where ${\mathcal{G}}_{t} = \left( {{\mathcal{V}}_{t},{\mathcal{E}}_{t}}\right)$ is the current hypergraph encoding retrieved evidence (entities, relations, visual regions) and their structural organization (connectivity, cross-modal alignments,relevance scores), ${\mathcal{H}}_{t}$ is the action history (a sequence of tuples $\left( {{a}_{k},{r}_{k},{\mathcal{G}}_{k + 1}}\right)$ for $k < t$ ),and $q$ is the input query. This representation ensures the agent conditions its behavior on both current graph structure and reasoning trajectory, enabling coherent multi-turn reasoning and credit assignment.
其中，${\mathcal{G}}_{t} = \left( {{\mathcal{V}}_{t},{\mathcal{E}}_{t}}\right)$ 为当前超图，编码了检索到的证据（实体、关系、视觉区域）及其结构组织（连通性、跨模态对齐、相关性得分），${\mathcal{H}}_{t}$ 为动作历史（由若干元组 $\left( {{a}_{k},{r}_{k},{\mathcal{G}}_{k + 1}}\right)$ 构成的序列，适用于 $k < t$ ），$q$ 为输入查询。该表示确保智能体在当前图结构与推理轨迹两者的条件下进行行为决策，从而实现连贯的多轮推理与信用分配。


Actions. The agent operates over a discrete action space $\mathcal{A}$ with four action types:
动作。智能体在离散动作空间 $\mathcal{A}$ 上运行，包含四种动作类型：


(1) GRAPHRETRIEVE queries the current environment via elements in hypergraph ${\mathcal{G}}_{t}$ . It supports cross-modal entity-level lookups over surrounding subgraphs and hyperedge-level vector retrieval, enabling the agent to exploit structured, semantically linked higher-order facts.
(1) GRAPHRETRIEVE 通过超图 ${\mathcal{G}}_{t}$ 中的元素查询当前环境。它支持对周边子图的跨模态实体级检索，以及超边级向量检索，使智能体能够利用结构化、语义关联的高阶事实。


(2) WEBSEARCH is invoked when in-graph evidence proves insufficient. The agent formulates a query ${q}_{\text{ web }}$ and retrieves external information from web search engines. The returned content is aligned with the current state and appended to the textual context for subsequent generation, allowing the agent to incorporate external evidence beyond the static knowledge base.
(2) WEBSEARCH 在图内证据不足时调用。智能体构造查询 ${q}_{\text{ web }}$ 并从网页搜索引擎中检索外部信息。返回内容会与当前状态对齐，并附加到文本上下文中供后续生成使用，使智能体能够纳入静态知识库之外的外部证据。


(3) GRAPHEDIT refines the environment through direct graph-structure modification via three complementary sub-operations: INSERT, which introduces new entities or hy-peredges derived from retrieved content and expands the graph with verified evidence; UPDATE, which revises existing hyperedges to correct errors, resolve conflicts, or strengthen factual grounding based on newly discovered information; and DELETE, which performs soft removal by lowering the confidence scores of low-quality or contradicted elements, thereby pruning noise. Together, these operations enable self-correction and adaptive refinement of the knowledge graph as reasoning progresses, effectively addressing the inherent incompleteness and noise of automatically constructed graphs.
(3) GRAPHEDIT 通过三种互补的子操作直接修改图结构，从而细化环境：INSERT，根据检索内容引入新的实体或超边，并以已验证证据扩展图；UPDATE，依据新发现的信息修正现有超边，以纠正错误、消解冲突或强化事实依据；DELETE，通过降低低质量或被反驳元素的置信度进行软删除，从而清除噪声。这些操作共同支持在推理过程中对知识图谱进行自我纠正与自适应细化，有效应对自动构建图固有的不完整性与噪声。


(4) ANSWER terminates the reasoning process. Once the agent determines that the environment has evolved sufficiently and accumulated adequate evidence, it invokes this action to produce the final response based on the current graph state ${\mathcal{G}}_{t}$ .
(4) ANSWER 终止推理过程。一旦智能体判断环境已充分演化且积累了足够证据，便调用该动作，基于当前图状态 ${\mathcal{G}}_{t}$ 生成最终响应。


Rewards. After each action, the agent receives reward signals based on three factors: (1) whether reasoning steps follow the intended interaction protocol (structural correctness), (2) semantic alignment between predicted and ground-truth answers (answer quality), and (3) the computational cost of executed actions (efficiency). These rewards guide policy learning to maximize answer accuracy while maintaining efficient graph evolution (Section 3.5).
奖励。在每个动作之后，智能体会根据三个因素接收奖励信号：（1）推理步骤是否遵循预期交互协议（结构正确性），（2）预测答案与真实答案之间的语义一致性（答案质量），以及（3）已执行动作的计算成本（效率）。这些奖励指导策略学习，在保持高效图演化的同时最大化答案准确性（第 3.5 节）。


Policy. The agent’s policy ${\pi }_{\theta }\left( {{a}_{t} \mid  {s}_{t}}\right)$ determines action selection based on current state, learned through reinforcement learning (Section 3.5) to maximize answer accuracy while maintaining efficiency.
策略。智能体的策略 ${\pi }_{\theta }\left( {{a}_{t} \mid  {s}_{t}}\right)$ 根据当前状态决定动作选择，并通过强化学习（第 3.5 节）进行学习，以在保持效率的同时最大化答案准确性。


##### 3.4.2.MDP State Transition Dynamics
##### 3.4.2.MDP 状态转移动力学


The hypergraph is dynamic and responsive to agent actions, enabling self-evolution. At each step $t$ ,action ${a}_{t}$ induces a transition via evolution policy ${\pi }_{\text{ evolve }}$ :
该超图是动态且会响应智能体的动作，从而实现自我演化。每一步 $t$ ，动作 ${a}_{t}$ 通过演化策略 ${\pi }_{\text{ evolve }}$ 触发一次转移：


$$
{\mathcal{G}}_{t + 1} = {\pi }_{\text{ evolve }}\left( {{a}_{t},{\mathcal{G}}_{t}}\right) , \tag{7}
$$



where different action types induce different state changes:
其中，不同类型的动作会引发不同的状态变化：


- GRAPHRETRIEVE marks which entities and relations have been accessed without altering structure.
- GRAPHRETRIEVE 用于标记已访问的实体与关系，而不改变结构。


- WEBSEARCH acts as an external interface, retrieving evidence to validate and augment the graph with new facts and cross-modal alignments.
- WEBSEARCH 作为外部接口，检索证据以验证并增强图谱，引入新的事实以及跨模态对齐。


- GRAPHEDIT operations directly refine graph quality by consolidating fragmented knowledge, correcting inconsistencies, and removing noise.
- GRAPHEDIT 通过整合碎片化知识、纠正不一致并移除噪声，直接提升图谱质量。


Each transition is appended to the action history:
每次转移都会追加到动作历史中：


$$
{\mathcal{H}}_{t + 1} = {\mathcal{H}}_{t} \cup  \left( {{a}_{t},{r}_{t},{\mathcal{G}}_{t + 1}}\right) , \tag{8}
$$



forming a cumulative record that guides online decision-making and provides supervision signals for reinforcement learning. The final output is computed as:
形成累积记录，用于指导在线决策，并为强化学习提供监督信号。最终输出计算为：


$$
{a}^{ * } \leftarrow  {\pi }_{\text{ resp }}^{\text{ final }}\left( {q,{\mathcal{H}}_{T}}\right) . \tag{9}
$$



Through this recurrent update mechanism, the agent gradually transforms an initially sparse, noisy hypergraph ${\mathcal{G}}_{0}$ into a coherent,query-specific knowledge state ${\mathcal{G}}_{T}$ optimized for reasoning. This evolutionary process yields three benefits that address the limitations of static GraphRAG: (1) fine-grained multimodal entities and cross-modal alignments are dynamically constructed rather than pre-defined, overcoming text-centric fragmentation; (2) the graph continuously evolves to integrate new evidence and correct errors rather than remaining frozen, eliminating static structure constraints; (3) retrieval adapts through multi-turn exploration guided by intermediate reasoning rather than single-pass lookup, removing rigid pipeline limitations.
通过这种循环更新机制，智能体会逐步将最初稀疏且充满噪声的超图 ${\mathcal{G}}_{0}$ 转化为连贯的、面向查询的知识状态 ${\mathcal{G}}_{T}$ ，并对推理进行优化。该演化过程带来三点收益，解决静态 GraphRAG 的不足：（1）细粒度的多模态实体及跨模态对齐是动态构建的，而非预先定义，从而克服文本中心导致的碎片化；（2）图谱会持续演化以整合新证据并纠正错误，而不是冻结不变，消除静态结构约束；（3）检索会通过多轮探索自适应地进行，由中间推理引导，而非单次查找，从而移除僵硬的流水线限制。


### 3.5. Reinforcement Learning Optimization
### 3.5. 强化学习优化


We train the agent's policy to efficiently evolve the knowledge hypergraph using Group Relative Policy Optimization (GRPO) [31] with a trajectory-level reward function that balances answer correctness, reasoning coherence, and retrieval efficiency.
我们使用组相对策略优化（GRPO）[31]训练智能体的策略，以高效演化知识超图，并采用轨迹级奖励函数，在答案正确性、推理连贯性和检索效率之间取得平衡。


#### 3.5.1. Training Setup
####3.5.1.训练设置


Given a dataset of questions ${\mathcal{D}}_{Q}$ ,we train the agent through experience collected from its interactions with the hyper-graph environment. For each question $q \in  {\mathcal{D}}_{Q}$ ,an initial state ${s}_{1} \sim  P\left( q\right)$ is sampled (consisting of the query $q$ ,a relevant initial subgraph,and an empty history). At step $t$ ,the agent observes $\left( {{s}_{t - 1},{\mathcal{G}}_{H}}\right)$ and selects an action ${a}_{t} \sim  {\pi }_{\theta }\left( {\cdot  \mid  {s}_{t - 1};{\mathcal{G}}_{H}}\right)$ according to the current policy parameterized by $\theta$ . If ${a}_{t}$ is an GRAPHEDIT action,the agent also proposes specific modifications ${m}_{t}^{\prime }$ to commit to ${\mathcal{G}}_{H}$ . A complete trajectory is defined as:
给定一个问题数据集${\mathcal{D}}_{Q}$，我们通过与超图环境交互所收集的经验来训练智能体。对于每个问题$q \in  {\mathcal{D}}_{Q}$，采样一个初始状态${s}_{1} \sim  P\left( q\right)$（由查询$q$、相关的初始子图以及空历史组成）。在第$t$步，智能体观察$\left( {{s}_{t - 1},{\mathcal{G}}_{H}}\right)$并根据由$\theta$参数化的当前策略选择一个动作${a}_{t} \sim  {\pi }_{\theta }\left( {\cdot  \mid  {s}_{t - 1};{\mathcal{G}}_{H}}\right)$。如果${a}_{t}$是GRAPHEDIT动作，智能体还会提出具体修改${m}_{t}^{\prime }$以提交到${\mathcal{G}}_{H}$。完整轨迹定义如下：


$$
\tau  = \left( {{s}_{1},{a}_{1},{s}_{2},{a}_{2},\ldots ,{s}_{\left| \tau \right| }}\right)  \in  {\mathcal{T}}_{q}, \tag{10}
$$



where only GRAPHEDIT actions modify the graph state ${\mathcal{G}}_{H}$ ,while other actions (e.g.,GRAPHRETRIEVE,WEB-SEARCH) gather information without altering structure.
其中只有GRAPHEDIT动作会修改图状态${\mathcal{G}}_{H}$，而其他动作（如GRAPHRETRIEVE、WEB-SEARCH）仅收集信息，不改变结构。


#### 3.5.2. Reward Design and Policy Optimization
#### 3.5.2. 奖励设计与策略优化


To optimize both reasoning quality and graph evolution efficiency,we define a trajectory-level reward function $R\left( \tau \right)$ composed of three complementary components:
为同时优化推理质量与图演化效率，我们定义一个轨迹级奖励函数 $R\left( \tau \right)$ ，由三个相互补充的部分构成：


(i) Structural Reward evaluates whether each reasoning step follows the intended interaction protocol. At each step $\left( {{s}_{t},{a}_{t}}\right)$ ,the agent produces an intermediate block $\left( {{a}_{t}^{\text{ think }},{\alpha }_{t},{a}_{t}^{\text{ out }}}\right)$ . Well-formed steps are rewarded,up to a capped total:
(i) 结构奖励用于评估每个推理步骤是否遵循预定的交互协议。在每一步 $\left( {{s}_{t},{a}_{t}}\right)$ ，智能体生成一个中间块 $\left( {{a}_{t}^{\text{ think }},{\alpha }_{t},{a}_{t}^{\text{ out }}}\right)$ 。对格式良好的步骤给予奖励，且总奖励设有上限：


$$
{R}_{\text{ struct }}\left( \tau \right)  = \min \left( {{1.0},\eta \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{t = 1}}^{\left| \tau \right| }{\mathbb{I}}_{\text{ valid }}\left( t\right) }\right) , \tag{11}
$$



where $\mathbb{I}\left\lbrack  \cdot \right\rbrack$ denotes the indicator function,and $\eta$ is a stepwise scaling factor set to 0.5. This component encourages disciplined reasoning and syntactic consistency across multi-turn trajectories.
其中 $\mathbb{I}\left\lbrack  \cdot \right\rbrack$ 表示指示函数，$\eta$ 为逐步的缩放因子，取值为 0.5。该部分鼓励在多轮轨迹中进行有纪律的推理，并保持句法一致性。


(ii) Answer Reward measures semantic fidelity using a token-level overlap between the predicted answer and the ground-truth label ${y}_{q}^{ * }$ :
(ii) 答案奖励通过预测答案与真实标签 ${y}_{q}^{ * }$ 之间的词元级重叠来衡量语义忠实度：


$$
{R}_{\text{ ans }}\left( {a}_{T}^{\text{ ans }}\right)  = \frac{2\left| {\operatorname{tok}\left( {a}_{T}^{\text{ ans }}\right)  \cap  \operatorname{tok}\left( {y}_{q}^{ * }\right) }\right| }{\left| {\operatorname{tok}\left( {a}_{T}^{\text{ ans }}\right) }\right|  + \left| {\operatorname{tok}\left( {y}_{q}^{ * }\right) }\right| }, \tag{12}
$$



where tok(·) applies normalization such as lowercasing and whitespace removal. This F1-style score rewards semantic alignment without requiring exact string matching.
其中 tok(·) 采用诸如小写化与去除空白等归一化处理。该类 F1 分数奖励语义对齐，而不要求精确的字符串匹配。


(iii) Overall Outcome Reward integrates correctness, structure, and efficiency:
(iii) 整体结果奖励综合考虑正确性、结构与效率：


$$
R\left( \tau \right)  =  - \lambda \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{t = 1}}^{\left| \tau \right| }c\left( {a}_{t}\right)  + {R}_{\text{ struct }}\left( \tau \right) \tag{13}
$$



$$

This formulation ensures correctness is rewarded only when the reasoning process remains structurally coherent, thereby promoting high-quality, cost-efficient graph evolution.

(iv) Policy Optimization. We optimize the policy using GRPO, which computes group-level advantages by comparing each trajectory's reward to the group mean. This enables stable updates and efficient learning from multiple rollouts per question. Through this optimization, the agent learns to navigate the action space, selectively retrieving and editing graph elements to construct query-specific knowledge states that enable accurate question answering.
(iv) 策略优化。我们使用 GRPO 来优化策略：通过将每条轨迹的奖励与该组的平均奖励进行比较，计算组级优势。这使得更新更稳定，并能从每个问题的多次回合中高效学习。通过这种优化，智能体学会在动作空间中进行导航：有选择地检索并编辑图元素，构建面向查询的知识状态，从而实现准确的问答。

### 4.1. Experiment Setup
### 4.1. 实验设置


Datasets. We evaluate on both multimodal and text-only settings to demonstrate the generalizability of our self-evolving paradigm. For multimodal tasks, we use E-VQA [12], InfoSeek [4], and OK-VQA [24], with Wikipedia as the knowledge source filtered via EchoSight [11]. For text-only tasks, we use 2WikiMul-tiHopQA [10], HotpotQA [40], and Natural Questions (NQ) [18, 40] with official Wikipedia dumps.
数据集。我们在多模态和纯文本两种设置下进行评估，以展示我们自演化范式的通用性。对于多模态任务，我们使用 E-VQA [12]、InfoSeek [4] 和 OK-VQA [24]，并以经 EchoSight [11] 过滤的 Wikipedia 作为知识源。对于纯文本任务，我们使用 2WikiMul-tiHopQA [10]、HotpotQA [40] 和 Natural Questions（NQ）[18, 40]，并使用官方 Wikipedia 转储。


Evaluation Metrics. We use the LLM-as-Judge framework for multimodal accuracy, F1 [15] for text-only performance, and G-E [28] for answer quality.
评估指标。我们使用 LLM-as-Judge 框架衡量多模态准确率，使用 F1 [15] 衡量纯文本性能，使用 G-E [28] 衡量答案质量。


Baselines. We compare against three categories: (i) Standard RAG methods: NaiveRAG [18], EchoSight [11], ReflectiVA [32], and MMKB-RAG; (ii) GraphRAG methods: GraphRAG [5], LightRAG [8], HippoRAG2, and Hy-perGraphRAG [21]; (iii) RL-augmented methods: Search-R1 [14], MMSearch-R1 [38] and Graph-R1 [20].
基线。我们与三类方法进行比较：(i) 标准 RAG 方法：NaiveRAG [18]、EchoSight [11]、ReflectiVA [32] 和 MMKB-RAG；(ii) GraphRAG 方法：GraphRAG [5]、LightRAG [8]、HippoRAG2 和 Hy-perGraphRAG [21]；(iii) RL 增强方法：Search-R1 [14]、MMSearch-R1 [38] 和 Graph-R1 [20]。


Implementation Details. We employ GPT-40-mini for knowledge construction in both the EvoGraph-R1 and GraphRAG baselines. For retrieval, we use GME [42]. EvoGraph-R1 is implemented with Qwen2.5-VL-7B as the base MLLM for multimodal tasks and Qwen2.5-7B-Instruct for text-only tasks. We conduct experiments using three random seeds and run all experiments on four 80GB NVIDIA A100 GPUs. Additional implementation details are provided in the supplementary document.
实现细节。我们在 EvoGraph-R1 和 GraphRAG 基线中均采用 GPT-40-mini 进行知识构建。对于检索，我们使用 GME [42]。EvoGraph-R1 以 Qwen2.5-VL-7B 作为多模态任务的基础 MLLM，并以 Qwen2.5-7B-Instruct 作为纯文本任务的基础模型。我们使用三个随机种子进行实验，并在四块 80GB NVIDIA A100 GPU 上运行所有实验。更多实现细节见补充文档。

## 5. Conclusion
## 5. 结论


We introduced EvoGraph-R1, a self-evolving GraphRAG framework that reconceptualizes knowledge graphs as dynamic MDP environments rather than static structures. An autonomous agent iteratively refines a multimodal knowledge hypergraph, transforming sparse initial graphs into coherent, query-specific knowledge states. Comprehensive experiments demonstrate state-of-the-art performance on both text and multimodal benchmarks, validating that self-evolving graphs generalize across modalities. This work establishes agent-driven graph evolution as a fundamental paradigm for adaptive retrieval, enabling robust reasoning over incomplete knowledge and dynamic environments.
我们提出了 EvoGraph-R1，一种自进化的 GraphRAG 框架，它将知识图谱重新构想为动态的 MDP 环境，而非静态结构。一个自主智能体迭代地优化多模态知识超图，将稀疏的初始图转化为连贯、面向查询的知识状态。全面实验表明，该方法在文本和多模态基准上均达到最先进性能，验证了自进化图可跨模态泛化。这项工作确立了由智能体驱动的图演化作为自适应检索的基础范式，使其能够在不完整知识和动态环境上进行稳健推理。
