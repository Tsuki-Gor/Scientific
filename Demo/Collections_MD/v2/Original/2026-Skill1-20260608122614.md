## Abstract
## 摘要


A persistent skill library allows language model agents to reuse successful strategies across tasks. Maintaining such a library requires three coupled capabilities. The agent selects a relevant skill, utilizes it during execution, and distills new skills from experience. Existing methods optimize these capabilities in isolation or with separate reward sources, resulting in partial and conflicting evolution. We propose Skill1, a framework that trains a single policy to co-evolve skill selection, utilization, and distillation toward a shared task-outcome objective. The policy generates a query to search the skill library, re-ranks candidates to select one, solves the task conditioned on it, and distills a new skill from the trajectory. All learning derives from a single task-outcome signal. Its low-frequency trend credits selection and its high-frequency variation credits distillation. Experiments on ALFWorld and WebShop show that Skill 1 outperforms prior skill-based and reinforcement learning baselines. Training dynamics confirm the co-evolution of the three capabilities, and ablations show that removing any credit signal degrades the evolution. Our code is available at https://github.com/AlphaLab-USTC/Skill1.
持久化技能库使语言模型智能体能够在不同任务间复用成功策略。维护这样的技能库需要三种相互耦合的能力。智能体选择相关技能，在执行中加以利用，并从经验中提炼新技能。现有方法通常将这些能力分开优化，或使用彼此独立的奖励来源，导致演化不完整且相互冲突。我们提出 Skill1，一个训练单一策略以围绕共享的任务结果目标协同演化技能选择、利用和提炼的框架。该策略生成查询以检索技能库，重排序候选项并选取其一，在其条件下完成任务，并从轨迹中提炼新技能。所有学习均来自单一的任务结果信号。其低频趋势对技能选择进行归因，而高频变化则对技能提炼进行归因。ALFWorld 和 WebShop 上的实验表明，Skill1 优于先前基于技能和强化学习的基线方法。训练动态证实了三种能力的协同演化，而消融实验表明，移除任何一种归因信号都会削弱这种演化。我们的代码已发布于 https://github.com/AlphaLab-USTC/Skill1。


## 1 Introduction
## 1 引言


Reinforcement learning (RL) (Sutton and Barto, 2018; Schulman et al., 2017; Shao et al., 2024) has become an important paradigm for training large language model (LLM) agents that interact with complex environments (Guo et al., 2025; Yang et al., 2024; Team et al., 2026; Touvron et al., 2023; Shridhar et al., 2021; Yao et al., 2022a; Xi et al., 2025). Standard RL training treats each task as an isolated episode, where the strategies that lead to success are absorbed only implicitly into the policy parameters and cannot be explicitly reused on future tasks. A natural solution is to augment agents with a persistent skill library that accumulates reusable strategies from past experience, so that the agent can draw on previously successful approaches instead of solving every task from scratch (Wang et al., 2023; Zhao et al., 2024; Xia et al., 2026; Zhang et al., 2026a; Muhtar et al., 2026; Lu et al., 2026). The workflow of these skill-augmented agents can be organized around a three-stage lifecycle (Jiang et al., 2026a): skill selection, where the agent selects a relevant skill from the library for the current task; skill utilization, where the agent executes guided by the selected skill; and skill distillation, where the agent derives new reusable skills from the trajectories.
强化学习（RL）（Sutton and Barto, 2018; Schulman et al., 2017; Shao et al., 2024）已成为训练与复杂环境交互的大语言模型（LLM）智能体的重要范式（Guo et al., 2025; Yang et al., 2024; Team et al., 2026; Touvron et al., 2023; Shridhar et al., 2021; Yao et al., 2022a; Xi et al., 2025）。标准RL训练将每个任务视为一个独立回合，成功所依赖的策略只会隐式吸收到策略参数中，无法在未来任务中显式复用。一个自然的解决方案是为智能体配备一个持久的技能库，将过往经验中可复用的策略不断积累起来，使智能体能够调用先前成功的方法，而非从零解决每个任务（Wang et al., 2023; Zhao et al., 2024; Xia et al., 2026; Zhang et al., 2026a; Muhtar et al., 2026; Lu et al., 2026）。这类技能增强型智能体的工作流可围绕三阶段生命周期组织（Jiang et al., 2026a）：技能选择，即智能体从库中为当前任务选取相关技能；技能利用，即智能体在所选技能的引导下执行；以及技能蒸馏，即智能体从轨迹中提炼新的可复用技能。


Existing methods have advanced each stage through RL, improving how agents select skills (Zhang et al., 2026b; Wang et al., 2026; Li et al., 2026a; Wu et al., 2025), utilize them (Xia et al., 2026; Muhtar et al., 2026; Zhang et al., 2026a; Li et al., 2026a; Wang et al., 2025a), and distill reusable knowledge (Zhang et al., 2026a; Wang et al., 2025a; Muhtar et al., 2026; Wu et al., 2025). Existing methods apply policy updates to only a subset of the lifecycle, leaving at least one capability unoptimized, leading to optimization bottlenecks (Xia et al., 2026; Muhtar et al., 2026; Zhang et al., 2026a; Wang et al., 2025a). We present Skill1, a framework that achieves unified evolution of skill-augmented agents by training a single policy to co-evolve skill selection, utilization, and distillation. As illustrated in Figure 1, given a new task, the policy first generates a natural-language query to retrieve candidate skills from the library, and then re-ranks the retrieved candidates to select the best match. The policy then performs multi-turn interaction with the environment conditioned on the top-ranked skill. After execution, the policy distills reusable skills from the experience based on its rollouts.
我们提出Skill1，一个通过训练单一策略来协同进化技能选择、利用与蒸馏、从而实现技能增强型智能体统一演化的框架。如图1所示，面对新任务时，策略首先生成自然语言查询，从库中检索候选技能，然后对检索到的候选项重新排序，选出最匹配的技能。随后，策略在所选最高排名技能的条件下，与环境进行多轮交互。执行结束后，策略根据其rollout经验蒸馏出可复用技能。


We achieve co-evolution of all three capabilities through credit assignment on a single task-outcome signal $r\left( \tau \right)$ . The outcome directly measures how well the policy solves the current task and serves as the utilization reward. To credit selection and distillation, we decompose this signal into its low-frequency trend and high-frequency variation. The low-frequency trend is defined as the moving average of outcomes associated with each skill. This term reflects skill utility and guides the policy toward consistently effective skills. The high-frequency variation is approximated with the deviation of the current outcome from the trend. This term captures whether a newly distilled skill improves upon the library's current boundary, and rewards the policy for producing useful skills.
我们通过对单一任务结果信号$r\left( \tau \right)$进行信用分配，实现了三种能力的协同进化。该结果直接衡量策略完成当前任务的程度，并作为利用奖励。为了对选择和蒸馏进行归因，我们将这一信号分解为低频趋势和高频变化。低频趋势定义为与每个技能对应结果的移动平均。该项反映技能效用，并引导策略转向持续有效的技能。高频变化则用当前结果相对趋势的偏差来近似。该项捕捉新蒸馏出的技能是否优于库的当前边界，并奖励策略生成有用技能。


We empirically evaluate Skill1 on ALFWorld (Shridhar et al., 2021) and WebShop (Yao et al., 2022a). Skill1 achieves 97.5% success rate on ALFWorld, surpassing all other baseline skill-augmented agents. Training dynamics confirm that selection precision, utilization success rate, and library quality improve simultaneously under the shared signal. Ablations show that removing any single stage's credit-assignment signal degrades all three capabilities, providing evidence for their mutual dependence.
我们在 ALFWorld（Shridhar 等，2021）和 WebShop（Yao 等，2022a）上对 Skill1 进行了实证评估。Skill1 在 ALFWorld 上的成功率达到 97.5%，超越所有其他基线的技能增强型智能体。训练动态表明，在共享信号的驱动下，选择精度、使用成功率与知识库质量同时提升。消融实验显示，移除任意单一阶段的归因信号都会削弱这三项能力，证明它们相互依赖。

## 3 Method
## 3 方法


We introduce Skill1,a framework that trains a single policy ${\pi }_{\theta }$ to co-evolve skill selection,utilization, and distillation toward a shared task-outcome objective (Figure 2). We first describe the workflow (§3.1),then derive all learning signals from the task outcome $r\left( \tau \right)$ (§3.2),and finally formulate the joint optimization objective (§3.3).
我们提出 Skill1，这是一种框架，训练单一策略 ${\pi }_{\theta }$，使其在共享的任务结果目标下共同演化技能选择、技能运用与蒸馏能力（图2）。我们首先描述工作流程（§3.1），随后从任务结果 $r\left( \tau \right)$ 推导所有学习信号（§3.2），最后构建联合优化目标（§3.3）。


### 3.1 Agent Workflow
### 3.1 智能体工作流


For each task $x \sim  \mathcal{D}$ ,the policy ${\pi }_{\theta }$ performs three stages in sequence. A complete trajectory takes the form $\tau  = \left( {q,z,{a}_{1},{o}_{1},\ldots ,{a}_{T},{o}_{T},{s}_{\text{ new }}}\right)$ ,where $q$ is the selection query, $z$ is the selected skill (or $\varnothing$ ),the action-observation pairs constitute the multi-turn interaction,and ${s}_{\text{ new }}$ is the distilled skill. The environment returns a terminal reward $r\left( \tau \right)  \in  \{ 0,1\}$ . Prompt templates are in Appendix G.
对于每个任务 $x \sim  \mathcal{D}$ ，策略 ${\pi }_{\theta }$ 依次执行三个阶段。完整轨迹可表示为 $\tau  = \left( {q,z,{a}_{1},{o}_{1},\ldots ,{a}_{T},{o}_{T},{s}_{\text{ new }}}\right)$ ，其中 $q$ 是选择查询，$z$ 是所选技能（或 $\varnothing$ ），动作-观察对构成多轮交互，${s}_{\text{ new }}$ 是蒸馏后的技能。环境返回终止奖励 $r\left( \tau \right)  \in  \{ 0,1\}$ 。提示模板见附录 G。


Skill selection. Given a task $x$ ,the policy generates a natural-language query $q \sim  {\pi }_{\theta }\left( {\cdot  \mid  x}\right)$ to search the skill library $\mathcal{B}$ . A frozen encoder $\mathcal{E}$ retrieves the top- $K$ candidates by semantic similarity:
技能选择。给定一个任务 $x$ ，策略生成一个自然语言查询 $q \sim  {\pi }_{\theta }\left( {\cdot  \mid  x}\right)$ 以检索技能库 $\mathcal{B}$ 。一个冻结的编码器 $\mathcal{E}$ 根据语义相似度检索前 $K$ 个候选：


$$
{\mathcal{B}}_{K} = \operatorname{top} - {\mathrm{K}}_{s \in  \mathcal{B}}\operatorname{sim}\left( {\mathcal{E}\left( q\right) ,\mathcal{E}\left( {s\text{ .desc }}\right) }\right) . \tag{3}
$$



The policy then re-ranks these candidates by generating a permutation $\sigma  \sim  {\pi }_{\theta }\left( {\cdot  \mid  x,{\mathcal{B}}_{K}}\right)$ ,and the top-ranked skill $z$ is provided for utilization. Both query generation and re-ranking are produced by ${\pi }_{\theta }$ ,so selection is directly optimizable through the policy gradient.
随后，策略通过生成一个排列 $\sigma  \sim  {\pi }_{\theta }\left( {\cdot  \mid  x,{\mathcal{B}}_{K}}\right)$ 对这些候选重新排序，排名最高的技能 $z$ 被提供用于使用。查询生成和重新排序都由 ${\pi }_{\theta }$ 产生，因此选择可通过策略梯度直接优化。


Skill utilization. The policy interacts with the environment for up to $T$ turns conditioned on the selected skill: $\tau  \sim  {\pi }_{\theta }\left( {\cdot  \mid  x,z\text{ .strat, }{o}_{ \leq  t}}\right)$ . For each task, $G$ rollouts are sampled independently,each performing its own selection, utilization, and distillation.
技能使用。策略在所选技能的条件下与环境交互，最多进行 $T$ 轮：$\tau  \sim  {\pi }_{\theta }\left( {\cdot  \mid  x,z\text{ .strat, }{o}_{ \leq  t}}\right)$ 。对于每个任务，采样 $G$ 次 rollout，且各自独立执行选择、使用和蒸馏。


Skill distillation. After each rollout, ${\pi }_{\theta }$ reflects on the trajectory to produce: (i) a reusable strategy ${s}_{\text{ new }}$ .strat $\sim  {\pi }_{\theta }\left( {\cdot  \mid  x,\tau }\right)$ summarizing the approach,and (ii) a scenario description ${s}_{\text{ new }}$ .desc $\sim  {\pi }_{\theta }( \cdot   \mid \; x,\tau )$ characterizing when the skill applies. A new skill is admitted to $\mathcal{B}$ only when $r\left( \tau \right)  = 1$ . When the library reaches its capacity $\left| \mathcal{B}\right|  = {N}_{\max }$ ,the skill with the lowest retirement score $U\left( s\right)  \cdot  \log \left( {n\left( s\right) }\right)$ is removed,where $n\left( s\right)$ is the number of times $s$ has been selected. This heuristic retires skills that are both low-utility and infrequently used while preserving well-tested high-utility skills.
技能蒸馏。每次 rollout 之后，${\pi }_{\theta }$ 会反思轨迹以生成：(i) 一个可复用的策略 ${s}_{\text{ new }}$ .strat $\sim  {\pi }_{\theta }\left( {\cdot  \mid  x,\tau }\right)$ ，概括该方法；以及 (ii) 一个场景描述 ${s}_{\text{ new }}$ .desc $\sim  {\pi }_{\theta }( \cdot   \mid \; x,\tau )$ ，刻画该技能适用的时机。只有当 $r\left( \tau \right)  = 1$ 时，才会将新技能纳入 $\mathcal{B}$ 。当技能库达到容量 $\left| \mathcal{B}\right|  = {N}_{\max }$ 时，得分最低的退休技能 $U\left( s\right)  \cdot  \log \left( {n\left( s\right) }\right)$ 会被移除，其中 $n\left( s\right)$ 是 $s$ 被选中的次数。该启发式方法会淘汰既低效又少用的技能，同时保留经过充分验证且高效的技能。


### 3.2 Reward Assignment
### 3.2 奖励分配


Co-evolution requires that each capability receives targeted learning signals from the shared task outcome $r\left( \tau \right)$ . The challenge is that the three capabilities operate at different temporal scopes: utilization concerns the current episode, selection concerns which skills are consistently effective across episodes, and distillation concerns whether new experience improves upon what the library already covers. We address this by decomposing $r\left( \tau \right)$ into its low-frequency trend and high-frequency variation, assigning credit to each capability without auxiliary models or additional rollouts.
协同进化要求每种能力都能从共享的任务结果 $r\left( \tau \right)$ 获得有针对性的学习信号。难点在于三种能力的时间尺度不同：利用关注当前回合，选择关注跨回合哪些技能始终有效，蒸馏则关注新经验是否优于库中已有的覆盖。我们通过将 $r\left( \tau \right)$ 分解为低频趋势和高频波动，在不使用辅助模型或额外采样的情况下，对每种能力进行归因。


Crediting utilization. The task outcome directly measures how well the policy executes with the given skill and serves as the utilization reward:
归功于利用。任务结果直接衡量策略在给定技能下执行得多好，因此利用奖励定义为：


$$
{R}_{i}^{\text{ util }} = r\left( {\tau }_{i}\right) . \tag{4}
$$



Crediting selection. Selection improves through two mechanisms. First,the query $q$ is part of the rollout prefix and receives policy gradients through the utilization objective (Eq. 8). Better queries retrieve better candidates and lead to higher $r\left( \tau \right)$ ,so query quality co-improves with task performance without a dedicated reward.
归功于选择。选择会通过两种机制得到改进。首先，查询 $q$ 是回合前缀的一部分，并通过利用目标（式 8）获得策略梯度。更好的查询会检索到更优候选，从而带来更高的 $r\left( \tau \right)$ ，因此查询质量会随任务表现共同提升，而无需专门的奖励。


Second, re-ranking requires an explicit signal that reflects long-term skill quality rather than single-episode outcomes. We maintain the trend of each skill as a per-skill utility score, updated after each rollout via exponential moving average:
其次，重排序需要一个显式信号，用来反映长期的技能质量，而非单回合结果。我们为每个技能维护其趋势，并将其作为按技能计算的效用分数，在每次回合后通过指数滑动平均更新：


$$
U\left( s\right)  \leftarrow  \left( {1 - \alpha }\right)  \cdot  U\left( s\right)  + \alpha  \cdot  r\left( {\tau }_{i}\right) ,\;\forall s \in  {\mathcal{B}}_{K}. \tag{5}
$$



We update all retrieved candidates rather than only the selected one, treating co-retrieval as evidence of relevance to the same task distribution. The trend smooths out per-episode variance and accumulates each skill’s long-term contribution. We denote the best available utility as ${\widehat{U}}_{i} = \mathop{\max }\limits_{{s \in  {\mathcal{B}}_{K}^{i}}}U\left( s\right)$ , which serves as the library baseline for subsequent reward derivations. The trend supervises re-ranking by rewarding the policy for producing a permutation ${\sigma }_{i}$ that agrees with the utility ordering. Here we use normalized discounted cumulative gain (NDCG) as the rubric:
我们更新所有检索到的候选，而不仅是被选择的那个，将协同检索视为与同一任务分布相关性的证据。该趋势能平滑每回合的方差，并累积每个技能的长期贡献。我们将可获得的最佳效用记为 ${\widehat{U}}_{i} = \mathop{\max }\limits_{{s \in  {\mathcal{B}}_{K}^{i}}}U\left( s\right)$ ，它作为后续奖励推导的库基线。该趋势通过以下方式监督重排序：当策略产生的一个排列 ${\sigma }_{i}$ 与效用排序一致时给予奖励。这里我们使用标准化折扣累计增益（NDCG）作为度量：


$$
{R}_{i}^{\text{ rerank }} = \operatorname{NDCG}\left( {{\sigma }_{i},\operatorname{argsort}\left( {-U\left( {\mathcal{B}}_{K}^{i}\right) }\right) }\right) . \tag{6}
$$



Crediting distillation. The ideal distillation signal would measure whether a newly distilled skill improves future task performance, but that future outcome is unavailable at training time. We approximate it with the variation of the current outcome relative to the library's trend:
归功于蒸馏。理想的蒸馏信号应衡量新蒸馏出的技能是否提升未来任务表现，但未来结果在训练时不可得。我们用当前结果相对库趋势的波动来近似：


$$
{R}_{i}^{\text{ distill }} = r\left( {\tau }_{i}\right)  - {\widehat{U}}_{i}, \tag{7}
$$



where ${\widehat{U}}_{i} = \mathop{\max }\limits_{{s \in  {\mathcal{B}}_{K}^{i}}}U\left( s\right)$ is the highest trend among the retrieved candidates. A positive variation indicates that the current experience surpasses what the library already covers, so the distilled skill is worth admitting. A negative variation discourages redundant distillation.
其中 ${\widehat{U}}_{i} = \mathop{\max }\limits_{{s \in  {\mathcal{B}}_{K}^{i}}}U\left( s\right)$ 是所有被检索候选中的最高趋势。若波动为正，说明当前经验超出了库已有覆盖，因此该蒸馏技能值得接纳。若波动为负，则会抑制多余的蒸馏。

### 3.3 Joint Optimization
### 3.3 联合优化


Each rollout ${\tau }_{i}$ is a concatenation of four generation segments produced by ${\pi }_{\theta }$ : the selection query ${q}_{i}$ ,the re-ranking permutation ${\sigma }_{i}$ ,the action sequence ${a}_{1 : T}$ ,and the distilled skill ${s}_{\text{ new },i}$ . We assign each segment its own reward signal (§3.2) and optimize them jointly in a single gradient step using GRPO (Shao et al.,2024) (cf. Appendix B),which normalizes rewards within the $G$ rollouts of each task into group-relative advantages.
每次 rollout ${\tau }_{i}$ 都由 ${\pi }_{\theta }$ 生成的四段连续生成组成：选择查询 ${q}_{i}$ 、重排序置换 ${\sigma }_{i}$ 、动作序列 ${a}_{1 : T}$ 以及蒸馏后的技能 ${s}_{\text{ new },i}$ 。我们为每一段分配其独立的奖励信号（§3.2），并使用 GRPO（Shao et al.,2024）（参见附录 B）在一次梯度更新中对它们进行联合优化；该方法会在每个任务的 $G$ rollouts 内对奖励进行归一化，转化为组相对优势。


Utilization and query. The action tokens ${a}_{1 : T}$ are conditioned on $\left( {{x}_{i},{z}_{i}}\right)$ and optimized by the task outcome ${R}_{i}^{\text{ util }} = r\left( {\tau }_{i}\right)$ . The query ${q}_{i}$ precedes the actions in the same sequence and receives gradients through the same objective:
利用与查询。动作 token ${a}_{1 : T}$ 由 $\left( {{x}_{i},{z}_{i}}\right)$ 条件化，并根据任务结果 ${R}_{i}^{\text{ util }} = r\left( {\tau }_{i}\right)$ 进行优化。查询 ${q}_{i}$ 在同一序列中位于动作之前，并通过相同的目标接收梯度：


$$
{\mathcal{J}}^{\text{ util }}\left( \theta \right)  = {\mathcal{J}}_{\text{ GRPO }}\left( {\theta ;\left\{  {{\tau }_{1},\ldots ,{\tau }_{G}}\right\}  ,\left\{  {{\widehat{A}}_{1},\ldots ,{\widehat{A}}_{G}}\right\}  }\right) . \tag{8}
$$



Re-ranking. The permutation ${\sigma }_{i}$ is generated conditioned on the task ${x}_{i}$ and retrieved candidates ${\mathcal{B}}_{K}^{i}$ ,and reinforced by the ranking reward ${R}_{i}^{\text{ rerank }}$ . Since different rollouts generate different queries, their retrieved candidate sets ${\mathcal{B}}_{K}^{i}$ differ,thus inner group comparison becomes meaningless. We thus optimize each permutation independently with a REINFORCE-style (Williams, 1992) objective:
重排序。置换 ${\sigma }_{i}$ 在任务 ${x}_{i}$ 与检索候选 ${\mathcal{B}}_{K}^{i}$ 的条件下生成，并由排序奖励 ${R}_{i}^{\text{ rerank }}$ 得到强化。由于不同 rollouts 生成的是不同查询，它们检索到的候选集合 ${\mathcal{B}}_{K}^{i}$ 也不同，因此组内比较没有意义。我们因此为每个置换分别采用类似 REINFORCE（Williams, 1992）的目标进行优化：


$$
{\mathcal{J}}^{\text{ rerank }}\left( \theta \right)  = \frac{1}{N \cdot  G}\mathop{\sum }\limits_{i}{R}_{i}^{\text{ rerank }} \cdot  \log {\pi }_{\theta }\left( {{\sigma }_{i} \mid  {x}_{i},{\mathcal{B}}_{K}^{i}}\right) . \tag{9}
$$



Distillation. The distilled skill tokens $\left( {{s}_{\text{ new },i}\text{ .strat, }{s}_{\text{ new },i}}\right.$ .desc) are generated conditioned on the task ${x}_{i}$ and trajectory ${\tau }_{i}$ ,and reinforced by the variation ${R}_{i}^{\text{ distill }}$ . Advantages ${\widehat{A}}_{i}^{\text{ distill }}$ are normalized separately from those of utilization since the two rewards measure different aspects of same outcomes:
蒸馏。蒸馏后的技能 token $\left( {{s}_{\text{ new },i}\text{ .strat, }{s}_{\text{ new },i}}\right.$ .desc) 在任务 ${x}_{i}$ 与轨迹 ${\tau }_{i}$ 的条件下生成，并由变化 ${R}_{i}^{\text{ distill }}$ 得到强化。优势 ${\widehat{A}}_{i}^{\text{ distill }}$ 会分别进行归一化，因为这两个奖励衡量的是同一结果的不同方面：


$$
{\mathcal{J}}^{\text{ distill }}\left( \theta \right)  = {\mathcal{J}}_{\text{ GRPO }}\left( {\theta ;\left\{  {{s}_{\text{ new, }1},\ldots ,{s}_{\text{ new, }G}}\right\}  ,\left\{  {{\widehat{A}}_{1}^{\text{ distill }},\ldots ,{\widehat{A}}_{G}^{\text{ distill }}}\right\}  }\right) . \tag{10}
$$



Total objective. All terms are combined in a single update:
总目标。所有项在一次更新中合并：


$$
\mathcal{J}\left( \theta \right)  = {\mathcal{J}}^{\text{ util }}\left( \theta \right)  + {\lambda }_{1}{\mathcal{J}}^{\text{ rerank }}\left( \theta \right)  + {\lambda }_{2}{\mathcal{J}}^{\text{ distill }}\left( \theta \right) . \tag{11}
$$



The utility score $U\left( s\right)$ is updated non-parametrically via Eq. (5). The full procedure is summarized in Algorithm 1. Training hyperparameter settings are in Appendix C.
效用分数 $U\left( s\right)$ 会通过式（5）以非参数方式进行更新。完整流程见算法 1。训练超参数设置见附录 C。

### 4.3 Analysis
### 4.3 分析


#### 4.3.1 Ablation Study
#### 4.3.1 消融研究


We remove workflow components and zero out auxiliary objective weights to isolate each design choice. All variants share the same base model and training budget. Results are reported in Table 2.
我们移除工作流组件并将辅助目标权重置零，以隔离各项设计选择。所有变体共享相同的基础模型和训练预算。结果见表2。


The skill library is the foundation, and distillation makes it effective. Removing the library entirely causes the largest drop, from 97.5% to 80.9%, with Heat and Pick2 losing over 28 points each. These task types require composing multi-step sub-procedures that benefit most from reusable skills. Removing distillation while keeping the library still reduces performance by 5.1 points. Without distillation the library stores raw trajectories rather than condensed strategies, making selection noisier and reuse less effective.
技能库是基础，而蒸馏使其发挥作用。完全移除技能库会造成最大幅度的下降，从97.5%降至80.9%，其中Heat和Pick2各自下降超过28个百分点。这些任务类型需要组合多步子程序，最能受益于可复用技能。保留技能库但移除蒸馏仍会使性能下降5.1个百分点。没有蒸馏时，技能库存储的是原始轨迹而非压缩后的策略，使选择更嘈杂，复用也更不有效。


Selection loss propagates to downstream stages. Without selection the average drops by 5.7 points, concentrated on Heat and Pick2 where routing to the correct multi-step skill matters most. Notably, this degradation occurs even though the utilization reward remains intact, showing that poor skill routing bottlenecks the entire pipeline regardless of the policy's solving ability.
选择损失会向下游阶段传播。去掉选择后，平均性能下降5.7个百分点，主要集中在Heat和Pick2上，因为在这些任务中路由到正确的多步技能最为关键。值得注意的是，即使利用奖励仍然保留，这种退化依然发生，说明不良的技能路由会成为整个流水线的瓶颈，而不受策略求解能力影响。


The two auxiliary objectives are complementary. Setting ${\lambda }_{1} = 0$ or ${\lambda }_{2} = 0$ individually reduces performance by 3.5 and 2.6 points respectively. Removing both yields a sharper decline to ${90.2}\%$ , worse than removing each stage individually. This gap shows that the signals benefit utilization beyond their direct targets, confirming that both signals are necessary to sustain full co-evolution.
这两个辅助目标是互补的。单独设置${\lambda }_{1} = 0$或${\lambda }_{2} = 0$会使性能分别下降3.5和2.6个百分点。两者同时移除则会导致更明显的下降至${90.2}\%$，比单独移除任一阶段都更差。这一差距表明，这些信号对利用的促进超出了其直接目标，印证了这两个信号对于维持完整共演化都是必要的。

#### 4.3.3 Selection and Distillation Quality
#### 4.3.3 技能选择与蒸馏质量


The previous section shows that capability metrics rise together. Here we examine the qualitative nature of that improvement: does the policy actually learn to select more relevant skills and distill higher-quality ones?
上一节表明能力指标会同步提升。这里我们从定性角度检验这种进步的性质：策略是否确实学会选择更相关的技能，并蒸馏出更高质量的技能？


The policy learns to generate increasingly precise selection queries. Figure 4 measures task-skill similarity at three checkpoints. Full Skill1 improves from 0.51 to 0.60 across training because the trend signal rewards queries that retrieve historically high-utility skills, gradually sharpening the policy's ability to describe what it needs. Removing the selection signal slows this learning, and without learned selection entirely, similarity stays almost flat at the lowest level.
策略学会生成越来越精确的选择查询。图4在三个检查点上衡量任务-技能相似度。完整的 Skill1 在训练过程中由 0.51 提升到 0.60，因为趋势信号会奖励能检索到历史高效技能的查询，从而逐步增强策略对自身需求的表述能力。移除选择信号会减慢这一学习；如果完全没有学习到选择机制，相似度也会几乎保持不变在最低水平。


The library ceiling rises as the policy learns to distill better skills. Figure 5 tracks $\widehat{U}$ ,the utility of the top-ranked skill per task. A rising $\widehat{U}$ means increasingly effective skills are entering the library, not merely more skills. Full Skill1 reaches 0.91 by step 85 while both ablations lag by approximately 0.10. The variation signal creates this pressure: producing a skill similar to existing ones yields little reward, so the policy must discover genuinely better strategies to obtain positive gradient.
随着策略学会蒸馏更好的技能，库容量上限也随之提高。图5追踪每项任务中排名第一的技能的 $\widehat{U}$（效用）。$\widehat{U}$ 的上升意味着越来越有效的技能进入了技能库，而不仅仅是技能数量更多。完整的 Skill1 在第 85 步达到 0.91，而两个消融实验大约落后 0.10。变化信号制造了这种压力：生成与现有技能相似的技能几乎得不到奖励，因此策略必须发现真正更好的策略，才能获得正梯度。

#### 4.3.5 Computational Overhead
#### 4.3.5 计算开销


We compare wall-clock time and library size for Skill1, SkillRL, and two ablations under identical hardware of 8 H800 80GB GPUs.
我们在相同的8张H800 80GB GPU硬件条件下，对Skill1、SkillRL以及两个消融版本的墙钟时间和库大小进行比较。


Skill1 adds moderate overhead over baseline methods. GRPO without a library runs at approximately 290s per step. SkillRL maintains near-constant cost because its library grows minimally from 60 to 83 skills, but this static library limits final performance to 89.9% compared to 97.5% for Skill1. Skill1 operates at 387 to 494s, roughly 1.3 to 1.7 times as slow as GRPO, with the increase stemming from the growing library context. The selection step itself adds negligible overhead as query generation and re-ranking operate on short sequences compared to multi-turn interactions against the environment.
与基线方法相比，Skill1带来适度的开销。无库的GRPO每步运行时间约为290秒。SkillRL的成本几乎保持恒定，因为其库规模仅从60个技能小幅增长到83个技能，但这种静态库将最终性能限制在89.9%，而Skill1可达97.5%。Skill1的运行时间为387到494秒，约为GRPO的1.3到1.7倍，增加主要来自不断扩大的库上下文。选择步骤本身的开销可忽略不计，因为相较于与环境的多轮交互，查询生成和重排序仅处理较短序列。


Distillation controls both library quality and computational cost. Without distillation, raw trajectories enter the library directly, growing it at 2.4 times the rate of Skill1. The larger library lengthens the selection context, making the variant without distillation 69% slower by step 60 and saturating the 5,000-skill cap far earlier. Distillation compresses experience into concise skills, improving library quality while keeping computational cost in check.
蒸馏同时控制库质量与计算成本。若不进行蒸馏，原始轨迹会直接进入库中，使其增长速度达到Skill1的2.4倍。更大的库会拉长选择上下文，使不蒸馏版本在第60步时慢69%，并更早达到5,000技能上限。蒸馏将经验压缩为简洁的技能，在控制计算成本的同时提升库质量。


## 5 Conclusion and Limitations
## 5 结论与局限性


Conclusion. We present Skill1, a framework that trains a single policy to co-evolve skill selection, utilization, and distillation toward a shared task-outcome objective. By decomposing this signal into its low-frequency trend and high-frequency variation, Skill1 derives per-capability credit assignment without auxiliary rewards. Experiments on ALFWorld and WebShop show consistent gains over prior skill-based and RL baselines, and ablations confirm that the three capabilities evolve in a coupled manner. A natural next step is to extend this lifecycle to hierarchical or multi-agent settings, where skill sharing and conflict resolution introduce new challenges for unified credit assignment.
结论。我们提出了Skill1，一个训练单一策略以围绕共享的任务结果目标共同演化技能选择、使用与蒸馏的框架。通过将该信号分解为低频趋势和高频波动，Skill1在无需辅助奖励的情况下，为各项能力推导出逐项信用分配。在ALFWorld和WebShop上的实验表明，相较于此前基于技能和基于RL的基线方法，Skill1始终取得更好的效果；消融实验也证实这三种能力是以耦合方式演化的。一个自然的下一步是将这一生命周期扩展到分层或多智能体场景，在这些场景中，技能共享与冲突解决会为统一的信用分配带来新的挑战。


Limitations. While Skill1 achieves strong performance, several limitations remain.
局限性。尽管Skill1取得了很强的性能，但仍存在若干局限。


- Environment coverage. Our evaluation is limited to two representative text-based agent environments. Whether the co-evolution framework generalizes to more environments (e.g., deep search environments) or those with visual observations remains unexplored.
- 环境覆盖范围。我们的评估仅限于两个具有代表性的文本型智能体环境。共演化框架能否推广到更多环境（例如深度搜索环境）或带有视觉观测的环境，仍有待探索。


- Scalability of the skill library. The library capacity in this work is capped at 5,000 entries. As the diversity of tasks grows, the fixed-size library may become a bottleneck, and more sophisticated eviction or hierarchical organization strategies may be required.
- 技能库的可扩展性。本文中的库容量上限为5,000条。随着任务多样性的增加，固定大小的库可能成为瓶颈，可能需要更复杂的淘汰或分层组织策略。

## A Related Work
## 相关工作


Reinforcement Learning for LLM Agents. Core algorithmic advances include GRPO (Shao et al., 2024), anchor-state grouping (Feng et al., 2025), and dynamic sampling with asymmetric clipping (Yu et al., 2025). Multi-turn RL methods address long-horizon challenges through hierarchical value functions (Zhou et al., 2024), leave-one-out advantage estimation (Chen et al., 2025), MCTS-guided search (Putta et al., 2024), exploration-based trajectory optimization (Song et al., 2024), multi-turn self-evolution (Wang et al., 2025b; Zhang et al., 2025a), and cross-episode meta-RL (Jiang et al., 2025b). Recent work further refines credit assignment via stepwise progress attribution (Wang et al., 2025c; Wei et al., 2025a) or intrinsic exploration signals (Gao et al., 2025; Wang et al., 2025d). Prompt-based methods such as ReAct (Yao et al., 2022b) and Reflexion (Shinn et al., 2023) enable reasoning without parameter updates but are upper-bounded by the frozen policy (Abel et al., 2023). Skill 1 extends GRPO by decomposing a single task-outcome signal into stage-specific gradients for selection, utilization, and distillation within a unified RL objective.
面向 LLM 代理的强化学习。核心算法进展包括 GRPO（Shao et al., 2024）、锚状态分组（Feng et al., 2025）以及带非对称裁剪的动态采样（Yu et al., 2025）。多轮 RL 方法通过层次化价值函数（Zhou et al., 2024）、留一优势估计（Chen et al., 2025）、MCTS 引导搜索（Putta et al., 2024）、基于探索的轨迹优化（Song et al., 2024）、多轮自进化（Wang et al., 2025b; Zhang et al., 2025a）以及跨回合元 RL（Jiang et al., 2025b）来应对长时程挑战。近期工作进一步通过逐步进展归因（Wang et al., 2025c; Wei et al., 2025a）或内在探索信号（Gao et al., 2025; Wang et al., 2025d）细化信用分配。ReAct（Yao et al., 2022b）和 Reflexion（Shinn et al., 2023）等基于提示的方法无需参数更新即可实现推理，但其上限受冻结策略所限（Abel et al., 2023）。Skill1 在统一 RL 目标下，将单一任务结果信号分解为用于选择、利用和蒸馏的阶段特定梯度，从而扩展了 GRPO。


Experience Reusing. Structuring past experience for reuse improves RL sample efficiency (Zhan et al., 2025; Ye et al., 2026; Muhtar et al., 2026), and explicit memory systems that store interaction histories (Wei et al., 2025b; Liu et al., 2026a,b) or distilled lessons (Fang et al., 2025; Zhou et al., 2025; Wang et al., 2025e) support continuous adaptation. RetroAgent (Zhang et al., 2026a) combines intrinsic progress rewards with language-based lesson extraction and a utility-aware selection strategy (Auer et al., 2002). Critique-GRPO (Zhang et al., 2025b) integrates natural-language critiques with numerical rewards, and RL-based self-distillation (Hubotter et al., 2026) refines failed trajectories into policy updates. Retrospective self-correction through natural-language critiques (Madaan et al., 2023; Yao et al., 2023) further enables agents to learn from failures (Liu and Van Der Schaar, 2025). Skill1 builds on these insights but derives all learning signals from a single task-outcome signal, eliminating the need for separate intrinsic reward design.
经验复用。将过往经验结构化以供复用，可提升 RL 样本效率（Zhan et al., 2025; Ye et al., 2026; Muhtar et al., 2026）；而存储交互历史（Wei et al., 2025b; Liu et al., 2026a,b）或蒸馏经验（Fang et al., 2025; Zhou et al., 2025; Wang et al., 2025e）的显式记忆系统，则支持持续适应。RetroAgent（Zhang et al., 2026a）将内在进展奖励、基于语言的经验提取以及效用感知选择策略（Auer et al., 2002）结合起来。Critique-GRPO（Zhang et al., 2025b）将自然语言批评与数值奖励整合，而基于 RL 的自蒸馏（Hubotter et al., 2026）则将失败轨迹细化为策略更新。通过自然语言批评进行回顾性自我纠正（Madaan et al., 2023; Yao et al., 2023）进一步使代理能够从失败中学习（Liu and Van Der Schaar, 2025）。Skill1 借鉴了这些思路，但其所有学习信号均由单一任务结果信号导出，无需单独设计内在奖励。


Skill Libraries for LLM Agents. A growing body of work equips LLM agents with persistent skill libraries (Jiang et al., 2026a; Xu and Yan, 2026; Li et al., 2026b; Jiang et al., 2026b; Anthropic, 2025). For selection, approaches include frozen embedding selectors (Xia et al., 2026; Muhtar et al., 2026), heuristic scoring (Zhang et al., 2026a), learned routing (Zhang et al., 2026b; Wang et al., 2026), and policy log-probability ranking (Li et al., 2026a; Wu et al., 2025). For utilization, RL-based methods condition the policy on selected skills (Xia et al., 2026; Muhtar et al., 2026; Zhang et al., 2026a; Li et al., 2026a; Wang et al., 2025a), sometimes with hierarchical rewards to incentivize skill use (Li et al., 2026a; Muhtar et al., 2026). For distillation, methods range from prompt-based extraction (Zhao et al., 2024) and training-free skill versioning (Yang et al., 2026) to teacher-driven generation (Xia et al., 2026), co-evolving extractors (Muhtar et al., 2026), and self-reflection (Zhang et al., 2026a; Wang et al., 2025a; Wu et al., 2025; Qiao et al., 2026). Existing methods have not yet achieved RL-optimized status on all three stages simultaneously, and those that optimize multiple stages use heterogeneous learning signals without a unified objective. Skill0 (Lu et al., 2026) internalizes skills into model parameters with zero external skills; Skill1 co-evolves all three stages through one policy model and a unified task outcome signal.
面向 LLM 代理的技能库。越来越多的工作为 LLM 代理配备持久化技能库（Jiang et al., 2026a; Xu and Yan, 2026; Li et al., 2026b; Jiang et al., 2026b; Anthropic, 2025）。在选择方面，方法包括冻结的嵌入选择器（Xia et al., 2026; Muhtar et al., 2026）、启发式评分（Zhang et al., 2026a）、学习式路由（Zhang et al., 2026b; Wang et al., 2026）以及策略对数概率排序（Li et al., 2026a; Wu et al., 2025）。在利用方面，基于 RL 的方法以所选技能为条件来约束策略（Xia et al., 2026; Muhtar et al., 2026; Zhang et al., 2026a; Li et al., 2026a; Wang et al., 2025a），有时还结合层次化奖励来激励技能使用（Li et al., 2026a; Muhtar et al., 2026）。在蒸馏方面，方法从基于提示的提取（Zhao et al., 2024）和无训练技能版本化（Yang et al., 2026），到教师驱动生成（Xia et al., 2026）、共演化提取器（Muhtar et al., 2026）以及自我反思（Zhang et al., 2026a; Wang et al., 2025a; Wu et al., 2025; Qiao et al., 2026）不等。现有方法尚未在三个阶段上同时达到 RL 优化状态，而那些优化多个阶段的方法使用的是异构学习信号，缺乏统一目标。Skill0（Lu et al., 2026）将技能内化到模型参数中，外部技能为零；Skill1 则通过一个策略模型和统一的任务结果信号协同演化全部三个阶段。


## B Algorithm Details
## B 算法细节


We use Group Relative Policy Optimization (GRPO) (Shao et al., 2024) as the optimization method, which eliminates the need for a separate value network by computing advantages relative to a group of rollouts sampled from the same task. For each task $d$ ,a group of $G$ rollouts ${\left\{  {\tau }_{i}\right\}  }_{i = 1}^{G}$ is sampled from ${\pi }_{{\theta }_{\text{ old }}}$ . The group-relative advantage for rollout $i$ is:
我们使用组相对策略优化（GRPO）（Shao 等，2024）作为优化方法，它无需额外的价值网络：通过在同一任务上采样得到一组轨迹来计算相对于该组的优势。对每个任务 $d$ ，从 ${\pi }_{{\theta }_{\text{ old }}}$ 采样得到一组 $G$ 个轨迹 ${\left\{  {\tau }_{i}\right\}  }_{i = 1}^{G}$。轨迹 $i$ 的组相对优势为：


$$
{\widehat{A}}_{i} = \frac{r\left( {\tau }_{i}\right)  - \operatorname{mean}\left( \left\{  {r\left( {\tau }_{1}\right) ,\ldots ,r\left( {\tau }_{G}\right) }\right\}  \right) }{\operatorname{std}\left( \left\{  {r\left( {\tau }_{1}\right) ,\ldots ,r\left( {\tau }_{G}\right) }\right\}  \right) }. \tag{12}
$$



Let ${\rho }_{t}^{\left( i\right) }\left( \theta \right)  = {\pi }_{\theta }\left( {{a}_{t}^{\left( i\right) } \mid  {s}_{t}^{\left( i\right) }}\right) /{\pi }_{{\theta }_{\text{ old }}}\left( {{a}_{t}^{\left( i\right) } \mid  {s}_{t}^{\left( i\right) }}\right)$ denote the per-token importance ratio. The GRPO objective maximizes the clipped surrogate:
令 ${\rho }_{t}^{\left( i\right) }\left( \theta \right)  = {\pi }_{\theta }\left( {{a}_{t}^{\left( i\right) } \mid  {s}_{t}^{\left( i\right) }}\right) /{\pi }_{{\theta }_{\text{ old }}}\left( {{a}_{t}^{\left( i\right) } \mid  {s}_{t}^{\left( i\right) }}\right)$ 表示逐 token 的重要性比率。GRPO 目标最大化截断的替代目标：


$$
{\mathcal{J}}_{\mathrm{{GRPO}}}\left( \theta \right)  = \frac{1}{G}\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{G}\frac{1}{\left| {\tau }_{i}\right| }\mathop{\sum }\limits_{{t = 1}}^{\left| {\tau }_{i}\right| }\min \left( {{\rho }_{t}^{\left( i\right) }{\widehat{A}}_{i},\operatorname{clip}\left( {{\rho }_{t}^{\left( i\right) },1 - \epsilon ,1 + \epsilon }\right) {\widehat{A}}_{i}}\right)  - \beta {D}_{\mathrm{{KL}}}\left\lbrack  {{\pi }_{\theta }\parallel {\pi }_{\mathrm{{ref}}}}\right\rbrack  , \tag{13}
$$



where $\epsilon$ is the clipping ratio, $\beta$ controls KL regularization toward a reference policy ${\pi }_{\text{ ref }}$ ,and $\left| {\tau }_{i}\right|$ is the number of tokens in rollout $i$ .
其中 $\epsilon$ 为截断比率，$\beta$ 控制对参考策略 ${\pi }_{\text{ ref }}$ 的 KL 正则化，且 $\left| {\tau }_{i}\right|$ 为轨迹 $i$ 中的 token 数。

## E Broader Impacts
## E 更广泛影响


This work develops a framework for LLM agents to autonomously acquire and reuse behavioral skills through reinforcement learning. On the positive side, the approach can reduce the manual engineering effort required to build capable agents and enable more sample-efficient learning in interactive environments.
这项工作为 LLM 智能体开发了一套框架，使其能够通过强化学习自主获取并复用行为技能。就积极方面而言，该方法可以降低构建高能力智能体所需的手动工程工作，并在交互式环境中实现更高样本效率的学习。


On the negative side, agents that autonomously accumulate skills may exhibit emergent behaviors that are difficult to predict or audit. In high-stakes deployment scenarios, an unconstrained skill library could encode harmful action sequences, and brings new injection risks. We recommend deploying such systems with human-in-the-loop oversight and constraining the action space in safe domains.
就消极方面而言，自主积累技能的智能体可能会出现新生的行为模式，这些模式难以预测或审计。在高风险部署场景中，缺乏约束的技能库可能会编码有害的行动序列，并带来新的注入风险。我们建议在引入人类在场监督的前提下部署此类系统，并在安全领域内约束行动空间。
