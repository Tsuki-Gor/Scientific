## Abstract
## 摘要


Embodied AI research is increasingly moving beyond single-task, single-environment policy learning toward multi-task, multi-scene, and multi-model settings. This shift substantially increases the engineering overhead and development time required for stages such as evaluation environment construction, trajectory collection, model training, and evaluation. To address this challenge, we propose a new paradigm for embodied AI development in which users express goals and constraints through conversation, and the system automatically plans and executes the development workflow. We instantiate this paradigm with EmbodiedClaw, a conversational agent that turns high-frequency, high-cost embodied research activities, including environment creation and revision, benchmark transformation, trajectory synthesis, model evaluation, and asset expansion, into executable skills. Experiments on end-to-end workflow tasks, capability-specific evaluations, human researcher studies, and ablations show that EmbodiedClaw reduces manual engineering effort while improving executability, consistency, and reproducibility. These results suggest a shift from manual toolchains to conversationally executable workflows for embodied AI development.
具身智能研究正日益从单任务、单环境的策略学习，转向多任务、多场景和多模型的设置。这种转变极大地增加了评估环境构建、轨迹收集、模型训练和评估等阶段的工程开销与开发时间。为应对这一挑战，我们提出了一种具身智能开发的新范式：用户通过对话表达目标与约束，系统自动规划并执行开发工作流。我们通过 EmbodiedClaw 实现了这一范式，这是一个对话式智能体，它将环境创建与修订、基准转换、轨迹合成、模型评估及资产扩展等高频、高成本的具身研究活动转化为可执行的技能。在端到端工作流任务、特定能力评估、人类研究员实验及消融实验中的结果表明，EmbodiedClaw 在减少手动工程工作量的同时，提升了可执行性、一致性和可复现性。这些结果表明，具身智能开发正从手动工具链向对话式可执行工作流转变。


## 1 Introduction
## 1 引言


As embodied AI moves from single-task, single-environment research prototypes toward multitask, multi-scene, and multi-model development, the bottleneck of embodied research is shifting from solving individual tasks to managing increasingly complex development workflows [146]. Researchers now spend substantial time on environment construction, trajectory collection, model training, and evaluation, while repeatedly adapting tasks, assets, and model backends across platforms. This engineering overhead slows the iteration of ideas and algorithms, increases development cost, and makes embodied research harder to execute, reproduce, and extend [7-11].
随着具身智能从单任务、单环境的研究原型向多任务、多场景和多模型开发演进，具身研究的瓶颈正从解决单个任务转向管理日益复杂的工作流 [146]。研究人员现在花费大量时间在环境构建、轨迹收集、模型训练和评估上，同时需在不同平台间反复适配任务、资产和模型后端。这种工程开销减缓了想法和算法的迭代，增加了开发成本，并使得具身研究更难执行、复现和扩展 [7-11]。


Despite rapid progress in simulators, benchmarks, and automated data generation methods [11-20], embodied AI development remains highly platform-bound and difficult to scale. Much of this burden comes from learning platform-specific task definitions and asset organizations, deploying environments, and adapting code across simulators, benchmarks, and model stacks [21-26]. In practice, these efforts repeatedly concentrate in some high-frequency activities: simulation task creation, task revision, trajectory collection, model training and evaluation [18, 27, 28]. Inspired by "vibe coding", we argue that embodied AI development should move from manually assembling platform-specific experimental loops to conversationally driven execution of embodied development skills.
尽管模拟器、基准测试和自动化数据生成方法取得了快速进展 [11-20]，但具身智能开发仍然高度依赖特定平台且难以扩展。这种负担很大程度上源于学习特定平台的任务定义和资产组织、部署环境，以及在模拟器、基准测试和模型栈之间适配代码 [21-26]。在实践中，这些工作反复集中在一些高频活动中：仿真任务创建、任务修订、轨迹收集、模型训练和评估 [18, 27, 28]。受“氛围编程”（vibe coding）的启发，我们认为具身智能开发应从手动组装特定平台的实验循环，转向对话驱动的具身开发技能执行。


Therefore, we present EmbodiedClaw, which turns high-frequency, time-consuming embodied engineering tasks into an executable workflow driven by user intent through dialogue. It organizes embodied development around three classes of objects: simulation environments, data, and models. On top of these objects, EmbodiedClaw supports three core capabilities: simulation environment synthesis, trajectory data collection, and model deployment. This formulation is motivated by recent progress in language-driven embodied agents and general-purpose workflow automation systems [29-32], while targeting the more structured and tool-dependent setting of embodied AI development [33-37].
因此，我们提出了 EmbodiedClaw，它将高频、耗时的具身工程任务转化为由用户意图通过对话驱动的可执行工作流。它围绕仿真环境、数据和模型这三类对象组织具身开发。在此基础上，EmbodiedClaw 支持三项核心能力：仿真环境合成、轨迹数据收集和模型部署。这一构想受到语言驱动具身智能体和通用工作流自动化系统最新进展的启发 [29-32]，同时针对具身智能开发中更结构化且依赖工具的场景 [33-37]。


We evaluate this paradigm through end-to-end workflow tasks, capability-specific evaluations, human researcher studies, and ablations. Our results show that EmbodiedClaw reduces manual engineering burden while improving executability, consistency, and reproducibility in embodied AI development.
我们通过端到端工作流任务、特定能力评估、人类研究员实验及消融实验对该范式进行了评估。结果表明，EmbodiedClaw 在减少手动工程负担的同时，提升了具身智能开发的可执行性、一致性和可复现性。


Our main contributions are as follows:
我们的主要贡献如下：


- We propose a new view of embodied AI research in which high-frequency and time-intensive engineering activities are unified as a class of conversationally driven executable development skills. This abstraction turns previously fragmented engineering routines into automatic workflows and compresses workflows that previously required days of manual effort into only a few hours.
- 我们提出了一种具身智能研究的新视角，将高频且耗时的工程活动统一为一类对话驱动的可执行开发技能。这种抽象将以往碎片化的工程流程转化为自动化工作流，并将以往需要数天手动完成的工作流压缩至仅需几小时。


- We introduce EmbodiedClaw, a dialogue-driven agent system that translates user input into executable workflows and supports arbitrary operations over three core embodied research objects: simulation environments, data, and models.
- 我们引入了 EmbodiedClaw，这是一个对话驱动的智能体系统，它将用户输入转化为可执行工作流，并支持对仿真环境、数据和模型这三个核心具身研究对象进行任意操作。


- Extensive experiments show that EmbodiedClaw delivers substantial gains on a range of high-cost embodied development tasks, including batch simulation environment creation and editing, trajectory data collection, and model deployment and evaluation. In these settings, EmbodiedClaw achieves up to an order-of-magnitude improvement in efficiency while reaching accuracy close to that of human experts.
- 大量实验表明，EmbodiedClaw 在一系列高成本具身智能开发任务中表现卓越，包括批量仿真环境的创建与编辑、轨迹数据采集以及模型部署与评估。在这些场景下，EmbodiedClaw 将效率提升了近一个数量级，同时达到了接近人类专家的准确度。

## 3 Method
## 3 方法


Building on the formulation in Sec. 2, we instantiate EmbodiedClaw as a conversational execution system for embodied AI development. At each turn $t$ ,the system takes user input ${x}_{t}$ together with the current operational context ${\mathbf{o}}_{t} = \left( {{o}_{t}^{S},{o}_{t}^{D},{o}_{t}^{M}}\right)$ ,infers an intent representation,and executes a workflow that updates simulation, data, and model states in a closed loop. The central design principle is to decouple intent inference, workflow planning, and platform execution, so that the same high-level user request can be reliably realized across heterogeneous simulators, assets, datasets, and model stacks.
基于第 2 节的公式，我们将 EmbodiedClaw 实例化为一个用于具身智能开发的对话式执行系统。在每一轮 $t$ 中，系统接收用户输入 ${x}_{t}$ 以及当前的运行上下文 ${\mathbf{o}}_{t} = \left( {{o}_{t}^{S},{o}_{t}^{D},{o}_{t}^{M}}\right)$，推断出意图表示，并执行一个在闭环中更新模拟、数据和模型状态的工作流。其核心设计原则是将意图推断、工作流规划和平台执行解耦，从而使相同的高层用户请求能够在异构的模拟器、资产、数据集和模型栈中可靠地实现。

### 3.2 Intent Understanding
### 3.2 意图理解


The intent understanding module predicts what operation should be performed and which operational objects should be updated. Given $\left( {{x}_{t},{\mathbf{o}}_{t}}\right)$ ,it outputs $\left( {{z}_{t}^{I},{\widehat{\mathcal{O}}}_{t}}\right)$ ,where ${z}_{t}^{I} \in  {\mathcal{Z}}^{I}$ and ${\mathcal{O}}_{t} \subseteq  \left\{  {{o}_{t}^{S},{o}_{t}^{D},{o}_{t}^{M}}\right\}$ . The input ${x}_{t}$ may include conversational context,explicit task instructions, and multimodal signals such as images or other structured observations. In contrast to treating raw user input as directly executable, this module converts under-specified conversational requests into a structured representation that can support downstream planning and verification.
意图理解模块负责预测应执行的操作以及应更新的操作对象。给定 $\left( {{x}_{t},{\mathbf{o}}_{t}}\right)$，它输出 $\left( {{z}_{t}^{I},{\widehat{\mathcal{O}}}_{t}}\right)$，其中 ${z}_{t}^{I} \in  {\mathcal{Z}}^{I}$ 和 ${\mathcal{O}}_{t} \subseteq  \left\{  {{o}_{t}^{S},{o}_{t}^{D},{o}_{t}^{M}}\right\}$。输入 ${x}_{t}$ 可能包含对话上下文、明确的任务指令以及图像或其他结构化观测等模态信号。与将原始用户输入直接视为可执行指令不同，该模块将描述不足的对话请求转换为结构化表示，以支持后续的规划和验证。


In practice,the intent space ${\mathcal{Z}}^{I}$ covers the high-frequency operations introduced in Sec. 2,including simulation environment synthesis, trajectory synthesis, and model engineering. The module also performs object grounding, which resolves user mentions into executable references such as scene identifiers, robot configurations, dataset versions, checkpoints, or code assets. This grounding step is critical because downstream execution must operate on concrete entities rather than ambiguous spans in natural language.
在实践中，意图空间 ${\mathcal{Z}}^{I}$ 涵盖了第 2 节中介绍的高频操作，包括模拟环境合成、轨迹合成和模型工程。该模块还执行对象接地（object grounding），将用户提及的内容解析为可执行的引用，如场景标识符、机器人配置、数据集版本、检查点或代码资产。这一接地步骤至关重要，因为下游执行必须基于具体实体进行操作，而非自然语言中的模糊片段。


### 3.3 Workflow Orchestration and Skill-Grounded Execution
### 3.3 工作流编排与基于技能的执行


Given $\left( {{z}_{t}^{I},{\mathcal{O}}_{t},{\mathbf{o}}_{t}}\right)$ ,the orchestrator generates a skill sequence ${\pi }_{t} = \left( {{g}_{1},\ldots ,{g}_{K}}\right)$ from a reusable skill library $\mathcal{G}$ . Each skill exposes a normalized interface over the operational objects,
给定 $\left( {{z}_{t}^{I},{\mathcal{O}}_{t},{\mathbf{o}}_{t}}\right)$，编排器从可重用技能库 $\mathcal{G}$ 中生成技能序列 ${\pi }_{t} = \left( {{g}_{1},\ldots ,{g}_{K}}\right)$。每项技能都针对操作对象提供标准化的接口，


$$
{g}_{k} : \left( {{o}^{S},{o}^{D},{o}^{M}}\right)  \mapsto  \left( {{o}^{\prime S},{o}^{\prime D},{o}^{\prime M}}\right) , \tag{9}
$$



with explicit input-output contracts that support composition across heterogeneous workflows. At this level, skills remain abstract: they encode what transformation should occur, but not yet how it is realized on a particular backend.
并带有明确的输入输出契约，以支持跨异构工作流的组合。在此层级上，技能仍是抽象的：它们编码了应执行何种转换，但尚未定义如何在特定后端上实现。


The skill library spans both perception-oriented and action-oriented embodied development primitives. Representative skills include object recognition, spatial localization, asset matching and retrieval, simulator-state editing, trajectory generation, dataset transformation, code editing, training launch, and evaluation dispatch. This design allows the orchestrator to compose heterogeneous capabilities-for example, using object recognition and spatial localization to ground a user request to scene entities, followed by asset matching to retrieve compatible resources, and then invoking execution skills to modify the simulator, generate data, or update model artifacts.
技能库涵盖了面向感知和面向动作的具身开发原语。代表性技能包括物体识别、空间定位、资产匹配与检索、模拟器状态编辑、轨迹生成、数据集转换、代码编辑、训练启动以及评估调度。这种设计使编排器能够组合异构能力——例如，利用物体识别和空间定位将用户请求映射到场景实体，随后通过资产匹配检索兼容资源，最后调用执行技能来修改模拟器、生成数据或更新模型工件。


Execution then grounds each abstract skill into concrete operations such as API calls, script invocations, simulator controls, dataset transformations, or model code updates. This separation between workflow semantics and platform realization is central to EmbodiedClaw. It allows the same workflow pattern to be reused across distinct simulators, asset libraries, and model stacks, while confining platform-specific complexity to the grounding layer.
执行过程随后将每个抽象技能落地为具体操作，如 API 调用、脚本执行、模拟器控制、数据集转换或模型代码更新。这种工作流语义与平台实现的分离是 EmbodiedClaw 的核心。它允许同一工作流模式在不同的模拟器、资产库和模型栈之间复用，同时将平台特定的复杂性限制在落地层。


### 3.4 Closed-Loop Verification and Recovery
### 3.4 闭环验证与恢复


EmbodiedClaw performs step-wise verification after every skill call to improve reliability over long-horizon, multi-stage workflows. The verifier outputs
EmbodiedClaw 在每次技能调用后执行分步验证，以提高长程、多阶段工作流的可靠性。验证器输出


$$
{v}_{t}^{k} = \text{ (status,message,rollback), } \tag{10}
$$



where status indicates whether the current step succeeds, message records structured diagnostics, and rollback indicates whether the latest valid state should be restored.
其中 status 指示当前步骤是否成功，message 记录结构化诊断信息，rollback 指示是否应恢复至最近的有效状态。


When verification fails, the system halts unsafe downstream execution, rolls back if necessary, and feeds the diagnostic signal back to the orchestrator for recovery, such as argument repair, skill substitution, or workflow replanning. This closed-loop design reduces error accumulation, improves state consistency across long execution chains, and enables EmbodiedClaw to recover from execution-time failures rather than treating them as terminal outcomes.
当验证失败时，系统会停止不安全的下游执行，必要时进行回滚，并将诊断信号反馈给编排器以进行恢复，例如参数修复、技能替换或工作流重规划。这种闭环设计减少了误差累积，提高了长执行链中的状态一致性，并使 EmbodiedClaw 能够从执行时故障中恢复，而非将其视为最终失败。


### 3.5 Asset and Platform Adaptation
### 3.5 资产与平台适配


EmbodiedClaw treats assets and simulator backends as pluggable execution contexts rather than as part of the workflow definition itself. Given an abstract workflow ${\pi }_{t}$ ,the adaptation module binds object references to concrete assets ${\mathcal{A}}_{t}$ and backend ${\mathcal{P}}_{t}$ ,
EmbodiedClaw 将资产和模拟器后端视为可插拔的执行上下文，而非工作流定义本身的一部分。给定抽象工作流 ${\pi }_{t}$，适配模块将对象引用绑定到具体资产 ${\mathcal{A}}_{t}$ 和后端 ${\mathcal{P}}_{t}$，


$$
{\phi }_{\mathrm{{AP}}}\left( {{\pi }_{t},{\mathcal{A}}_{t},{\mathcal{P}}_{t}}\right) \text{ . } \tag{11}
$$



Beyond binding existing assets, EmbodiedClaw maintains a predefined asset library for commonly used embodied objects and environments, while also supporting optional third-party 3D asset sources for long-tail asset acquisition. To bridge these sources with executable simulators, we implement dedicated skills for asset retrieval, downloading, normalization, and automatic registration into the target simulation environment. As a result, a user request can be resolved either to an existing in-library asset or to a newly downloaded external asset that is automatically imported and registered for subsequent workflow execution.
除了绑定现有资产外，EmbodiedClaw 还维护了一个用于常用具身对象和环境的预定义资产库，同时也支持可选的第三方 3D 资产源以获取长尾资产。为了将这些资源与可执行模拟器连接，我们实现了专门的资产检索、下载、标准化以及自动注册到目标模拟环境的技能。因此，用户请求既可以解析为库内现有资产，也可以解析为新下载的外部资产，并自动导入和注册以供后续工作流执行。


This design enables a workflow to be transferred across platforms without rewriting the user-facing interaction logic or the high-level plan structure, while also expanding the reachable asset space beyond the predefined library. In our implementation, EmbodiedClaw is instantiated on RoboTwin, where the adaptation layer supports asset registration and expansion, third-party asset ingestion, and cross-platform execution under a unified conversational interface.
这种设计使得工作流无需重写面向用户的交互逻辑或高层计划结构即可跨平台迁移，同时也扩展了预定义库之外的可达资产空间。在我们的实现中，EmbodiedClaw 实例化于 RoboTwin 之上，其中适配层支持资产注册与扩展、第三方资产摄取，以及在统一对话接口下的跨平台执行。

## 5 Related Work
## 5 相关工作


Repetitive and Time-Consuming Operations in Embodied AI Research. Embodied AI has witnessed explosive growth in simulation-based research, with a wide range of platforms—including AI2-THOR [12], Habitat [13, 14], ManiSkill3 [18], Isaac Gym [16], SAPIEN [15], and Virtual-Home [40]—each providing distinct interfaces, asset formats, and physics engines. Despite this diversity, constructing even a single research pipeline spanning data generation, simulation configuration, policy training, and evaluation demands substantial manual effort across all of these platforms. As highlighted by several recent works [1, 8, 41, 42], a typical embodied AI workflow stitches together these stages through proprietary interfaces and task-specific integration logic, imposing heavy recurring overhead on every new research iteration. Furthermore, the absence of unified interfaces means that transitions between perception, planning, and control modules still rely on manually configured logic, limiting reusability and increasing integration overhead across deployment scenarios [2, 8]. These repetitive, time-consuming operations remain a persistent bottleneck that fundamentally limits research throughput in the field.
具身智能研究中重复且耗时的操作。具身智能在基于模拟的研究中经历了爆炸式增长，拥有广泛的平台——包括 AI2-THOR [12]、Habitat [13, 14]、ManiSkill3 [18]、Isaac Gym [16]、SAPIEN [15] 和 Virtual-Home [40]——每个平台都提供不同的接口、资产格式和物理引擎。尽管存在这种多样性，但构建一个涵盖数据生成、模拟配置、策略训练和评估的单一研究流水线，在所有这些平台上都需要大量的人工投入。正如近期多项研究 [1, 8, 41, 42] 所强调的，典型的具身智能工作流通过专有接口和特定任务的集成逻辑将这些阶段拼接在一起，给每一次新的研究迭代带来了沉重的重复性开销。此外，缺乏统一的接口意味着感知、规划和控制模块之间的转换仍依赖于手动配置的逻辑，限制了可重用性并增加了跨部署场景的集成开销 [2, 8]。这些重复且耗时的操作仍然是一个持续存在的瓶颈，从根本上限制了该领域的研究吞吐量。

Automation in Embodied AI Research. Several efforts have begun to automate isolated stages of the embodied AI research pipeline. For environment construction, ProcTHOR [19] procedurally generates large-scale interactive indoor environments, and Holodeck [20] uses LLMs to automatically build diverse 3D scenes from language prompts. More recently, SAGE [43] proposes an agentic framework that generates simulation-ready 3D environments through iterative reasoning and adaptive tool selection, while EmbodiedGen [44] provides a generative platform for producing physics-grounded 3D assets directly importable into mainstream simulators such as MuJoCo [45] and Isaac Sim. For skill and data acquisition, RoboGen [11] decomposes high-level tasks into sub-tasks, selects an appropriate learning paradigm-reinforcement learning, motion planning, or trajectory optimization-and generates training supervision automatically. ${\mathrm{M}}^{2}$ Diffuser [46] similarly constructs automated data collection pipelines for mobile manipulation trajectories. Scenethesis [47] further integrates LLM-based scene planning with vision-guided layout refinement to generate physically plausible interactive environments. Despite these advances, existing work remains fragmented and mutually incompatible: each system targets a single pipeline stage, exposes distinct interfaces, and operates on incompatible data formats. Even within individual frameworks, stage transitions still rely on manually configured logic, limiting reusability and increasing integration overhead [8]. No unified system addresses the full embodied AI research workflow end-to-end.
具身智能研究中的自动化。一些研究工作已开始尝试自动化具身智能研究流水线的孤立阶段。在环境构建方面，ProcTHOR [19] 程序化生成大规模交互式室内环境，Holodeck [20] 利用大语言模型（LLM）根据语言提示自动构建多样化的 3D 场景。最近，SAGE [43] 提出了一个代理框架，通过迭代推理和自适应工具选择生成可用于模拟的 3D 环境；而 EmbodiedGen [44] 提供了一个生成式平台，用于生产可直接导入 MuJoCo [45] 和 Isaac Sim 等主流模拟器的物理基础 3D 资产。在技能和数据获取方面，RoboGen [11] 将高层任务分解为子任务，选择合适的学习范式（强化学习、运动规划或轨迹优化），并自动生成训练监督。${\mathrm{M}}^{2}$ Diffuser [46] 同样为移动操作轨迹构建了自动化数据收集流水线。Scenethesis [47] 进一步将基于 LLM 的场景规划与视觉引导的布局优化相结合，以生成物理上合理的交互式环境。尽管取得了这些进展，但现有工作仍然碎片化且互不兼容：每个系统仅针对流水线的一个阶段，暴露不同的接口，并运行在不兼容的数据格式上。即使在单个框架内，阶段转换仍依赖于手动配置的逻辑，限制了可重用性并增加了集成开销 [8]。目前尚无统一的系统能够端到端地解决完整的具身智能研究工作流。

AI-Powered Workflow Automation Systems. General-purpose AI agent frameworks have demonstrated strong automation capability across broad domains. Systems such as OpenClaw [32, 48] accept natural-language commands and autonomously execute complex multi-step workflows-from web scraping and code submission to file management-serving as versatile personal automation agents. Their key strength lies in flexible, LLM-driven orchestration: the agent evaluates available tools and chains them into execution pipelines through a unified workflow shell [32]. Related agentic frameworks, including those built on LangChain [2], AutoGen [49], and CrewAI, further support multi-agent collaboration and dynamic task planning for general-purpose workflows. However, such systems exhibit clear limitations when applied to tasks requiring deep domain expertise [50, 51]. Applied to embodied AI research—which demands specialized knowledge in simulator configuration, robot kinematics, cross-platform asset handling, and training pipeline orchestration—general-purpose agents lack the requisite domain knowledge to construct valid and reproducible workflows, and thus fail to deliver meaningful productivity gains. To bridge this gap, we present EmbodiedClaw, a workflow automation system purpose-built for embodied AI research. EmbodiedClaw encapsulates domain knowledge across scene construction, trajectory collection, training configuration, and cross-platform evaluation into natural-language-driven automated pipelines, enabling researchers to accomplish complex tasks simply through chat.
AI 驱动的工作流自动化系统。通用 AI 智能体框架已在广泛领域展现出强大的自动化能力。诸如 OpenClaw [32, 48] 等系统能够接收自然语言指令，并自主执行复杂的多步工作流——从网页抓取、代码提交到文件管理——充当多功能的个人自动化智能体。其核心优势在于灵活的、由大语言模型（LLM）驱动的编排：智能体评估可用工具，并通过统一的工作流 Shell 将其串联成执行流水线 [32]。其他相关智能体框架，包括基于 LangChain [2]、AutoGen [49] 和 CrewAI 构建的系统，进一步支持通用工作流的多智能体协作与动态任务规划。然而，此类系统在处理需要深厚领域专业知识的任务时表现出明显的局限性 [50, 51]。应用于具身智能研究时——该领域需要仿真器配置、机器人运动学、跨平台资产处理及训练流水线编排等专业知识——通用智能体缺乏构建有效且可复现工作流所需的领域知识，因此无法带来显著的生产力提升。为弥补这一差距，我们提出了 EmbodiedClaw，这是一个专为具身智能研究打造的工作流自动化系统。EmbodiedClaw 将场景构建、轨迹采集、训练配置和跨平台评估等领域的知识封装进自然语言驱动的自动化流水线中，使研究人员仅通过对话即可完成复杂任务。


## 6 Conclusion
## 6 结论


We presented EmbodiedClaw, a conversational workflow execution system for embodied AI development. By grounding user intent in executable operations over environments, data, and models, EmbodiedClaw transforms high-frequency embodied research activities, including environment synthesis and editing, trajectory collection, model training, and evaluation, into unified and executable workflows. Experiments show that EmbodiedClaw improves development efficiency while also increasing executability and reliability across diverse embodied AI tasks.
我们提出了 EmbodiedClaw，这是一个用于具身智能开发的对话式工作流执行系统。通过将用户意图与环境、数据和模型上的可执行操作相结合，EmbodiedClaw 将高频的具身研究活动（包括环境合成与编辑、轨迹采集、模型训练和评估）转化为统一且可执行的工作流。实验表明，EmbodiedClaw 在提升开发效率的同时，也增强了各类具身智能任务的执行力与可靠性。
