Abstract: In Embodied Question Answering (EQA), agents must explore and develop a semantic understanding of an unseen environment to answer a situated question with confidence. This problem remains challenging in robotics, due to the difficulties in obtaining useful semantic representations, updating these representations online, and leveraging prior world knowledge for efficient planning and exploration. To address these limitations, we propose GraphEQA, a novel approach that utilizes real-time 3D metric-semantic scene graphs (3DSGs) and task relevant images as multi-modal memory for grounding Vision-Language Models (VLMs) to perform EQA tasks in unseen environments. We employ a hierarchical planning approach that exploits the hierarchical nature of 3DSGs for structured planning and semantics-guided exploration. We evaluate GraphEQA in simulation on two benchmark datasets, HM-EQA and OpenEQA, and demonstrate that it outperforms key baselines by completing EQA tasks with higher success rates and fewer planning steps. We further demonstrate GraphEQA in multiple real-world home and office environments. Videos and code: website.
摘要：在具身问答（EQA）中，智能体必须探索并建立对未知环境的语义理解，才能自信地回答情境问题。由于难以获得有用的语义表征、在线更新这些表征，以及为高效规划与探索利用先验世界知识，该问题在机器人领域仍然具有挑战性。为了解决这些限制，我们提出 GraphEQA，一种新的方法：它利用实时三维度量语义场景图（3DSGs）以及任务相关图像，作为多模态记忆，用于为视觉-语言模型（VLM）提供锚定，从而在未知环境中完成 EQA 任务。我们采用分层规划方法，借助 3DSGs 的分层特性实现结构化规划，并引导语义驱动的探索。我们在仿真中于两个基准数据集 HM-EQA 和 OpenEQA 上评估 GraphEQA，并表明它能以更高的成功率和更少的规划步骤完成 EQA 任务，从而优于关键基线。我们还在多个真实世界的家庭与办公环境中展示了 GraphEQA。视频与代码：网站。

## 1 Introduction
## 1 引言


Embodied Question Answering (EQA) [1] is a challenging task in robotics, wherein an agent is required to explore and understand a previously unseen environment sufficiently well, to answer an embodied question in natural language. Accomplishing this task efficiently requires agents to rely on both commonsense knowledge of human environments as well as ground its exploration strategy in the current environment context. For example, to answer the question "How many chairs are there at the dining table?", the agent might rely on commonsense knowledge to understand that dining tables are often associated with dining rooms and dining rooms are usually near the kitchen towards the back of a home. A reasonable navigation strategy would involve navigating to the back of the house to locate a kitchen. To ground this search in the current environment, however, requires the agent to continually maintain an understanding of where it is, memory of where it has been, and what further exploratory actions will lead it to relevant regions. Finally, the agent needs to observe the target object(s) and perform visual grounding to reason about the number of chairs around the dining table, and confidently answer the question correctly.
具身问答（EQA）[1] 是机器人领域的一项具有挑战性的任务：智能体需要充分探索并理解一个先前未见的环境，才能用自然语言回答具身问题。高效完成该任务要求智能体既依赖关于人类环境的常识知识，又将其探索策略落到当前环境的上下文中。例如，对于问题“餐桌上有多少把椅子？”，智能体可能会利用常识来理解：餐桌通常对应餐厅，而餐厅通常靠近住宅后方的厨房。合理的导航策略是前往房屋后部以找到厨房。然而，要将这种搜索落到当前环境中，就要求智能体持续维持对自身位置的理解、对已走过区域的记忆，以及进一步探索行动将带它到达哪些相关区域。最后，智能体还需要观察目标物体，并进行视觉落地推理，以判断餐桌周围椅子的数量，并自信地正确回答问题。


Maintaining a concise and effective memory and using it to take actions in the environment is critical for solving EQA tasks. Prior works have demonstrated the impressive commonsense reasoning capabilities of Vision Language Models (VLMs) as planning agents, while leveraging a semantic map for retrieval [2] or semantic exploration [3]. In such approaches, the VLMs are not grounded in the current environment, and commonsense reasoning and context-based decision-making are disconnected. Recent works $\left\lbrack  {4,5,6,7,8}\right\rbrack$ focus on maintaining memory modules that can be queried by VLM agents for grounded planning. To construct a semantically rich memory, prior works either maintain a large, extensive set of images [8, 9] or have to perform an expensive offline processing step to obtain a compact representation $\left\lbrack  {6,7,{10}}\right\rbrack$ . Thus,such semantic memory modules are either semantically rich $\left\lbrack  {7,2,{11}}\right\rbrack$ ,compact $\left\lbrack  {4,5}\right\rbrack$ ,or online $\left\lbrack  5\right\rbrack$ ,but not all at the same time.
保持简洁且有效的记忆，并用它来采取环境中的行动，对于解决 EQA 任务至关重要。已有工作表明，视觉语言模型（VLM）作为规划智能体具有令人印象深刻的常识推理能力，同时借助语义地图进行检索[2]或进行语义探索[3]。在这类方法中，VLM 并未扎根于当前环境，常识推理与基于上下文的决策是脱节的。近期工作 $\left\lbrack  {4,5,6,7,8}\right\rbrack$ 关注维护可被 VLM 智能体查询的记忆模块，用于扎根式规划。为构建语义丰富的记忆，既有工作要么维护一个庞大且冗长的图像集合[8, 9]，要么必须进行代价高昂的离线处理步骤以获得紧凑表示 $\left\lbrack  {6,7,{10}}\right\rbrack$ 。因此，这些语义记忆模块要么语义上丰富 $\left\lbrack  {7,2,{11}}\right\rbrack$ 、要么紧凑 $\left\lbrack  {4,5}\right\rbrack$ ，要么是在线 $\left\lbrack  5\right\rbrack$ ，但无法同时全部具备。

To address these limitations, we propose GraphEQA, an approach for embodied question answering that builds an online, compact, multimodal semantic memory combining global, semantically-sparse, and task-agnostic information from real-time 3D scene graphs [12] with local and semantically-rich information from task-relevant images [10]. GraphEQA uses this multimodal representation for grounding vision-language planners to solve EQA tasks in unseen environments. Specifically, we utilize a recent spatial perception system [12] that incrementally creates a real-time 3D metric-semantic hierarchical scene graph (3DSG), given sequential egocentric image frames. We further augment this scene graph with semantic room labels and semantically-enriched frontiers, while maintaining a task-relevant visual memory that keeps track of task-relevant images as the robot explores the environment. Finally, we employ a hierarchical planning approach that utilizes the hierarchical nature of scene graphs and semantically relevant frontiers for structured planning and exploration in an unseen environment before using the multimodal memory to answer the embodied question with high confidence.
为了解决这些限制，我们提出 GraphEQA：一种具身问答方法，它构建在线、紧凑的多模态语义记忆，将来自实时三维场景图[12] 的全局、语义稀疏、且与任务无关的信息，与来自任务相关图像[10] 的局部、语义丰富信息相结合。GraphEQA 使用这种多模态表征来为视觉语言规划器提供视觉落地，从而在未见环境中解决 EQA 任务。具体而言，我们利用一种近期的空间感知系统[12]：在给定连续的以智能体视角（egocentric）图像帧的情况下，逐步生成实时三维度量-语义分层场景图（3DSG）。我们进一步为该场景图补充语义房间标签以及语义增强的前沿（frontiers），同时保持与任务相关的视觉记忆，用于在机器人探索环境时追踪与任务相关的图像。最后，我们采用一种分层规划方法：在使用多模态记忆回答具身问题并提高置信度之前，利用场景图的分层特性以及语义相关的前沿，在未见环境中进行结构化规划与探索。


We demonstrate that given our real-time multimodal memory and hierarchical planning approach, the agent is able to accomplish long-horizon tasks with significantly fewer VLM planning steps, explores explainable task-relevant frontiers, and succeeds in EQA tasks with a higher rate than previous approaches. We demonstrate our results on the HM-EQA [3] and OpenEQA [13] datasets in the Habitat simulation environment [14] and also in the real world using the Hello Robot Stretch mobile manipulator in two scenes. While the focus of this work is EQA tasks, our contributions advance the broader goal of grounding vision-language foundation models in unseen 3D environments, making GraphEQA applicable to a wider range of robotics tasks. ## 2 Related Work
## 2 相关工作


3D Semantic Scene Graph Representations for Planning: 3D semantic scene graphs (3DSGs) [15, 16, 17, 18, 19] have emerged as compact, semantically-rich representations of indoor environments, spurring advances in both offline [11, 2, 20, 21] and online [12, 22, 23] prediction. Offline approaches [11, 2] focus on enriching 3DSG nodes and edges with open-vocabulary semantics via Vision-Language Models (VLMs) [24, 25], supporting retrieval-based, language-guided tasks. Online methods [12, 22] enable real-time deployment of embodied agents but often rely on closed-set semantics [12] or fixed task sets [22], limiting open-world generalization. Our approach bridges these paradigms by building a multimodal memory: an online-constructed 3DSG with closed-set semantics guides a VLM agent toward task-relevant areas to capture semantically rich images [10], enabling open-world embodied question answering (EQA). Recent work has leveraged textual 3DSG representations for VLM-based planning [4, 26, 27, 28], primarily targeting object search or rearrangement tasks, but without addressing the deeper semantic understanding required for EQA. Other spatio-temporal representations of environments have also been effective for planning, e.g., [29], which constructs a custom 3D data structure to maintain dynamic memory.
用于规划的 3D 语义场景图表征：3D 语义场景图（3DSGs）[15, 16, 17, 18, 19] 已成为紧凑且语义丰富的室内环境表征，推动了离线 [11, 2, 20, 21] 与在线 [12, 22, 23] 预测的进展。离线方法 [11, 2] 通过视觉-语言模型（VLMs）[24, 25]，用开放词汇语义来丰富 3DSG 的节点与边，从而支持基于检索、语言引导的任务。在线方法 [12, 22] 使具身智能体能够实时部署，但往往依赖封闭集合语义 [12] 或固定任务集 [22]，从而限制开放世界泛化。我们的方案通过构建多模态记忆来衔接上述范式：先在线构建一个带封闭集合语义的 3DSG，引导 VLM 智能体定位任务相关区域，以捕获语义丰富的图像 [10]，从而实现开放世界具身问答（EQA）。近期工作还利用文本形式的 3DSG 表征进行 VLM 驱动的规划 [4, 26, 27, 28]，主要面向物体搜索或重排任务，但尚未解决 EQA 所需的更深层语义理解。其他时空环境表征也对规划有效，例如 [29] 通过构建自定义 3D 数据结构来维护动态记忆。


VLMs for 3D Scene Understanding and Planning: Several previous works leverage the commonsense reasoning capabilities of foundation models for long-horizon planning [30, 31, 32]. However, these methods are not grounded in the context of the current environment and additional tools are required to translate the LLM plan to executable actions [33, 34, 31, 35]. Previous works have explored the use of VLMs for building dense queryable open-vocabulary 3D semantic representations using explicit pixel-level [36, 37, 38, 39, 40] or implicit neural [41, 42, 43] representations. However, these maps are built offline, before they are used for downstream retrieval-based tasks. Moreover, such dense representations are not scalable to large environments and cannot be used to ground VLM-based planners. Recent advancements in grounding VLM-based planners using videos [44, 8] are promising, but struggle with scalability for long-horizon tasks in large environments. VLMs have been used as planners while leveraging semantic maps for retrieval $\left\lbrack  {{45},2,{11}}\right\rbrack$ or semantic exploration $\left\lbrack  {3,{46},{47},{48},{49}}\right\rbrack$ ,however such approaches disconnect context-based decision-making and commonsense reasoning. Offline methods that build topological maps [50], keyframe selections [10], 3D semantic graphs [2, 11, 51, 20, 21] and experience summaries [6, 7, 52, 53] are unsuitable for real-time deployment in novel settings. Online semantic scene graphs, while real-time, are limited by closed-set semantics. Our approach introduces an online, compact, and semantically rich multimodal memory to effectively ground VLM planners for EQA tasks.
用于 3D 场景理解与规划的 VLM：已有多项工作利用基础模型的常识推理能力来进行长时域规划 [30, 31, 32]。然而，这些方法并未基于当前环境语境，并且需要额外工具将 LLM 计划转换为可执行动作 [33, 34, 31, 35]。先前工作还探讨了使用 VLM 构建密集可查询的开放词汇 3D 语义表征，方式包括显式像素级 [36, 37, 38, 39, 40] 或隐式神经 [41, 42, 43] 表征。但这些图在用于下游基于检索任务之前，是离线构建的。此外，这类密集表征难以扩展到大规模环境，且无法用于为 VLM 驱动的规划器提供落地点。利用视频对 VLM 驱动规划器进行落地（grounding）的最新进展 [44, 8] 很有前景，但在大规模环境中的长时域任务上仍面临可扩展性挑战。VLM 被用作规划器，同时利用语义地图进行检索 $\left\lbrack  {{45},2,{11}}\right\rbrack$ 或语义探索 $\left\lbrack  {3,{46},{47},{48},{49}}\right\rbrack$ ，然而这类方法会割裂基于语境的决策与常识推理。构建拓扑图 [50]、关键帧选择 [10]、3D 语义图 [2, 11, 51, 20, 21] 以及经验摘要 [6, 7, 52, 53] 的离线方法，不适用于新环境中的实时部署。尽管在线语义场景图具有实时性，但仍受限于封闭集合语义。我们的方案提出一种在线、紧凑且语义丰富的多模态记忆，用于有效落地 VLM 规划器以完成 EQA 任务。


Embodied Question Answering: Embodied Question Answering [1, 54, 55, 56] has emerged as a challenging paradigm for testing robotic task planning systems on their ability to incrementally build a semantic understanding of an environment in order to correctly answer an embodied question with confidence. Ren et al. [3] build an explicit task-specific 2D semantic map of the environment to guide exploration, Anwar et al. [6], Xie et al. [7] build offline experience modules that the LLM can query, and Majumdar et al. [9] uses video memory to answer embodied questions using long-context VLMs. We focus on building agents that do not disconnect the semantic memory from the planner by grounding the planner in a compact scene representation for solving EQA tasks online.
具身问答：具身问答 [1, 54, 55, 56] 已成为一种具有挑战性的范式，用于检验机器人任务规划系统：它们能否在环境中逐步构建语义理解，并以信心正确回答具身问题。Ren 等人 [3] 构建了环境的显式、任务特定 2D 语义地图来引导探索；Anwar 等人 [6]、Xie 等人 [7] 构建离线经验模块，供 LLM 查询；Majumdar 等人 [9] 使用视频记忆，并借助长上下文 VLM 来回答具身问题。我们关注的是构建智能体：通过以紧凑的场景表征在线解决 EQA 任务，将规划器落地在该表征上，从而避免将语义记忆与规划器割裂。


## 3 Method
## 3 方法


### 3.1 Preliminaries
### 3.1 预备知识


Hierarchical 3D Metric-semantic Scene Graphs. 3D metric-semantic scene graphs (3DSGs) provide a structured, layered representation of environments and encode spatial, semantic, and relational information [15, 16, 17]. Recent works like Clio [22], Hydra [12], and Open Scene Graphs [57] introduce efficient real-time frameworks for incremental construction of hierarchical SG layers consisting of objects, regions, rooms, buildings, etc. See Appendix A. 3 for details.
层次化3D度量-语义场景图。3D度量-语义场景图（3DSG）为环境提供一种结构化、分层的表示，并编码空间、语义和关系信息[15, 16, 17]。Clio[22]、Hydra[12]和Open Scene Graphs[57]等近期工作提出了高效的实时框架，可增量构建由物体、区域、房间、建筑等组成的层次化SG层。详见附录A.3。


2D Occupancy Mapping and Frontier Detection. 3D voxel-based occupancy maps are an effective way for storing explored, occupied, and unexplored regions of an environment for planning and navigation. As the robot explores, using depth data and camera intrinsics, occupancy of the voxels is updated using Volumetric Truncated Signed Distance Function (TSDF) fusion. TSDF integrates depth observations to update voxels as occupied or free, while areas beyond a certain threshold are marked unexplored. Typically, the 3D occupancy map is projected into 2D, where frontiers-boundaries between explored and unexplored regions—are identified to guide further exploration. We employ this approach in our method for identifying frontiers, clustering them and adding them to the scene graph.
二维占据映射与边界检测。基于3D体素的占据地图是存储环境中已探索、已占据和未探索区域的有效方式，可用于规划和导航。机器人在探索时，利用深度数据和相机内参，通过体积截断签名距离函数（TSDF）融合更新体素占据情况。TSDF将深度观测融合起来，把体素更新为已占据或空闲，而超过某一阈值的区域则标记为未探索。通常，3D占据地图会投影到二维，其中边界——已探索与未探索区域之间的边界——会被识别出来，以引导进一步探索。我们在方法中采用这一思路来识别边界、对其聚类，并将其加入场景图。

As the agent explores the environment, it used its sensor data (RGBD images, semantic map, camera poses and intrinsics) to construct a 3D metric-semantic hierarchical scene graph (3DSG) as well as a 2D occupancy map for frontier selection in real time. The constructed 3DSG is enriched as discussed in Section 3.3. From the set of images collected during each trajectory execution, a task-relevant subset is selected, called the task-relevant visual memory ( Section 3.4). A VLM-based planner ( Section 3.5) takes as input the enriched scene graph, task-relevant visual memory, a history of past states and actions, and the embodied question and outputs the answer, its confidence in the selected answer, and the next step it needs to take in the environment. If the VLM agent is confident in its answer, the episode is terminated, else the proposed action is executed in the environment and the process repeats.
图2：GraphEQA整体架构。随着智能体探索环境，它利用传感器数据（RGBD图像、语义地图、相机位姿和内参）实时构建3D度量-语义层次场景图（3DSG）以及用于边界选择的二维占据地图。构建好的3DSG会按照3.3节所述进行丰富。针对每次轨迹执行过程中收集的图像集合，会选取一个与任务相关的子集，称为任务相关视觉记忆（3.4节）。基于VLM的规划器（3.5节）以增强后的场景图、任务相关视觉记忆、过去状态和动作历史以及具身问题作为输入，输出答案、对所选答案的置信度，以及它接下来需要在环境中采取的步骤。如果VLM智能体对答案有把握，回合就结束；否则就在环境中执行所提议的动作并重复该过程。


### 3.2 Problem Formulation
### 3.2 问题表述


An overview of our proposed method is shown in Figure 2. Given a multiple choice question $q$ ,we wish to find the correct answer ${a}^{ * } \in  \mathcal{A}$ where $\mathcal{A}$ is the set of multiple-choice answers to question $q$ available to the agent. To find ${a}^{ * }$ ,the agent is equipped with a VLM-based planner $\mathcal{V}\left( {q,{\mathcal{S}}_{t}^{e},{\left\{  {\mathcal{I}}_{k}\right\}  }_{k = 1}^{K},{\mathcal{H}}_{t},{X}_{t}}\right)  = \left( {{a}_{t},{c}_{t},{u}_{t}}\right)$ ,where ${\mathcal{S}}_{t}^{e}$ is the enriched scene graph constructed online at planning time $t$ ,and includes frontier nodes from 2D the occupancy map (Section 3.1). ${\left\{  {\mathcal{I}}_{k}\right\}  }_{k = 1}^{K}$ is a small set of task-relevant images maintained in memory (Section 3.4), ${a}_{t}$ is the current best answer to the multiple choice question $q$ and ${u}_{t}$ is the next action the agent should take in the environment. ${\mathcal{H}}_{t}$ represents the accrued history up to time $t$ and ${X}_{t}$ represents the current state of the agent. We query the VLM planner $\mathcal{V}$ (Section 3.5) at time $t$ with the inputs described above, with the scene graph ${\mathcal{S}}_{t}^{e}$ continually being constructed and a set of images ${\left\{  {\mathcal{I}}_{k}\right\}  }_{k = 1}^{K}$ chosen based on task and semantic relevance. The planner then outputs a high-level action ${u}_{t}$ which is executed in the environment while the scene graph, visual memory, and frontiers are all updated in real time. In the following sections we provide details for each of these components.
我们提出的方法概览如图2所示。给定一个多项选择题 $q$ ，我们希望找到正确答案 ${a}^{ * } \in  \mathcal{A}$，其中 $\mathcal{A}$ 是多项选择答案的集合，$\mathcal{A}$ 包含了代理可在问题 $q$ 下使用的所有选项。为找到 ${a}^{ * }$ ，代理配备了基于VLM的规划器 $\mathcal{V}\left( {q,{\mathcal{S}}_{t}^{e},{\left\{  {\mathcal{I}}_{k}\right\}  }_{k = 1}^{K},{\mathcal{H}}_{t},{X}_{t}}\right)  = \left( {{a}_{t},{c}_{t},{u}_{t}}\right)$，其中 ${\mathcal{S}}_{t}^{e}$ 是在规划时刻 $t$ 在线构建的增强场景图，且包含来自2D占据地图的前沿节点（第3.1节）。${\left\{  {\mathcal{I}}_{k}\right\}  }_{k = 1}^{K}$ 是一小组与任务相关的图像，存放在记忆中（第3.4节）；${a}_{t}$ 是多项选择题 $q$ 的当前最佳答案；${u}_{t}$ 是代理接下来应在环境中采取的动作。${\mathcal{H}}_{t}$ 表示截至时间 $t$ 的累计历史，${X}_{t}$ 表示代理当前状态。我们在时间 $t$ 将上述输入查询VLM规划器 $\mathcal{V}$（第3.5节），场景图 ${\mathcal{S}}_{t}^{e}$ 会持续构建，并根据任务与语义相关性从图像集合 ${\left\{  {\mathcal{I}}_{k}\right\}  }_{k = 1}^{K}$ 中选择图像。随后，规划器输出高层动作 ${u}_{t}$ 并在环境中执行，同时场景图、视觉记忆和前沿都会实时更新。下面各节将对这些组成部分分别提供细节。


### 3.3 Scene Graph Construction and Enrichment
### 3.3 场景图构建与增强


We use Hydra [12] to construct a layered metric-semantic scene graph (see Appendix A.3), while also maintaining a 2D occupancy map of the environment depicting the explored, occupied, and unexplored navigable regions of the environment as mentioned in Section 3.1. We perform room and frontier enrichment steps to enable semantic-guided exploration and hierarchical planning.
我们使用 Hydra [12] 构建分层的度量-语义场景图（见附录 A.3），同时还维护环境的二维占据图，描绘在第 3.1 节中提到的已探索、已占据和未探索的可通行区域。我们进行房间与前沿的增强步骤，以实现语义引导的探索与层级规划。


Room enrichment: Room nodes in Hydra's 3DSG are assigned generic labels such as R0, R1, etc. To enrich them with semantic information, we prompt an LLM to assign semantic labels to each of the room nodes. We use a simple prompt "Which room are these objects <object list> most likely located in?" where <object list> is the list of all objects located in a certain room in the scene graph. The output of the LLM is then used to update the room labels.
房间增强：Hydra 的 3DSG 中的房间节点会被赋予诸如 R0、R1 等通用标签。为使其获得语义信息，我们提示一个 LLM 为每个房间节点分配语义标签。我们使用一个简单提示：“这些物体 <object list> 最可能位于哪个房间？”其中 <object list> 是场景图中特定房间中出现的所有物体列表。随后使用 LLM 的输出来更新房间标签。


Frontier enrichment: To enrich our 3DSG with semantic information that can enable task-relevant exploration, we extract frontier nodes from the 2D occupancy map, cluster them, and add them as independent nodes to the scene graph. Next, we find top-j object nodes nearest to each clustered frontier node,within a maximum distance $d$ . We add edges to the scene graph connecting each frontier node to its top-j object neighbors. This semantic information can now be utilized by a VLM-based planner to select the most semantically-relevant frontier to explore next. For general exploration,for example,it could be useful to choose frontier nodes near doors. We use $j = 3$ and $d = 2$ meters in our experiments,but can be varied based on the environment.
前沿增强：为了让我们的 3DSG 具备可支持与任务相关探索的语义信息，我们从二维占据图中提取前沿节点，将它们聚类，并将其作为独立节点加入场景图。接着，我们为每个聚类后的前沿节点找到距离最近的前 top-j 个物体节点，且最大距离为 $d$ 。我们在场景图中添加边，将每个前沿节点连接到其 top-j 个物体邻居。现在，VLM 规划器就可以利用这份语义信息来选择下一步最具语义相关性的前沿节点。对于一般探索，例如，选择靠近门的前沿节点可能会有用。我们在实验中使用 $j = 3$ 和 $d = 2$ 米，但可以根据环境进行调整。


### 3.4 Task-relevant Visual Memory
### 3.4 与任务相关的视觉记忆


During action-execution, images are stored in a buffer at a specified sampling frequency to avoid multiple similar repeated images. Images from this buffer, along with keywords from the question/- task, are then processed using SigLIP [58] to obtain the text-image relevancy score for each image. Using this score, only the top-K most relevant images are maintained in the buffer and the rest are discarded. We use $K = 2$ ; we append these K images together,along with the agent’s current view, and use it as the visual input to the VLM planner at the next planning step, as shown in Figure 2.
执行动作期间，图像会以指定的采样频率存入缓冲区，以避免多次重复的相似图像。随后，使用该缓冲区中的图像以及问题/任务中的关键词，通过 SigLIP [58] 计算每张图像的文本-图像相关性分数。根据该分数，只保留最相关的前-K 张图像在缓冲区中，其余全部丢弃。我们使用 $K = 2$ ；我们将这 K 张图像与智能体当前视角拼接，并在下一次规划步骤中将其作为 VLM 规划器的视觉输入，如图 2 所示。

Figure 3: VLM Planner Architecture. The Hierarchical Vision-Language planner takes as input the question, enriched scene graph, task-relevant visual memory, current state of the robot (position and room) and a history of past states, actions, answers and confidence values. The planner chooses the next <Goto_Object_node> action hierarchically by first selecting the room node and then the object node. The <Goto_Frontier_node> action is chosen based on the object nodes connected to the frontier via edges in the scene graph. The planner is asked to output a brief reasoning behind choosing each action, an answer, confidence in its answer, reasoning behind the answer and confidence, the next action, a brief description of the scene graph, and the visual memory.
图 3：VLM 规划器架构。分层的视觉-语言规划器以问题、增强的场景图、与任务相关的视觉记忆、机器人当前状态（位置和房间）以及过去状态、动作、答案和置信度的历史为输入。规划器通过先选择房间节点再选择目标节点，以层级方式决定下一步 <Goto_Object_node> 动作。<Goto_Frontier_node> 动作则基于场景图中通过边与边界相连的目标节点来选择。规划器需要输出：选择每个动作的简要推理、答案、对答案的置信度、答案背后的推理与置信度、下一步动作、场景图的简要描述以及视觉记忆。


### 3.5 Hierarchical Vision-Language Planner
### 3.5 分层视觉-语言规划器


Inputs: At every planning step $t$ ,the VLM planner takes as input a multiple-choice question $q$ ,the set of multiple-choice answers $\mathcal{A}$ ,the enriched scene graph ${\mathcal{S}}_{t}^{e}$ ,and the task-relevant visual memory ${\left\{  {\mathcal{I}}_{k}\right\}  }_{k = 1}^{K};K$ is the number of images in memory. Additionally, we provide the planner with a structured history ${\mathcal{H}}_{t}$ and the agent’s current state ${X}_{t};{X}_{t}$ is defined in the following format: "The agent is currently at node <agent_node_id> at position <agent_position> in room <room_id> <room_name>", where information in ’ $<  \cdot   >$ ’ is populated from ${\mathcal{S}}_{t}^{e}$ (see Fig. 3).
输入：在每一步规划 $t$ ，VLM 规划器以多项选择题 $q$ 、多项选择答案集合 $\mathcal{A}$ 、增强后的场景图 ${\mathcal{S}}_{t}^{e}$ 以及任务相关的视觉记忆 ${\left\{  {\mathcal{I}}_{k}\right\}  }_{k = 1}^{K};K$（内存中的图像数量）作为输入。此外，我们为规划器提供结构化历史记录 ${\mathcal{H}}_{t}$ ，以及代理的当前状态 ${X}_{t};{X}_{t}$ ，其定义如下：“代理当前在节点 <agent_node_id> ，位置为 <agent_position> ，房间为 <room_id> <room_name>”，其中 ’ $<  \cdot   >$ ’ 中的信息由 ${\mathcal{S}}_{t}^{e}$ 填充（见图 3）。


Outputs: Given the above inputs, the planner must output an answer ${a}_{t} \in  \mathcal{A}$ for $q$ ,a boolean value ${c}_{t} \in  \{$ True,False $\}$ representing whether it is confident in answering the question, its current numeric confidence level ${p}_{t}^{c} \in  \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack$ , and the next action ${u}_{t}$ that the agent should take. We require the planner to also output the reasoning behind the choice of action and its confidence in the rationale. Finally, the planner is required to plan the next few steps, selecting from two high-level action types: <Goto_Object_node>(object_id) and <Goto_Frontier_node>(frontier_id), where object_id and frontier_id are selected from ${\mathcal{S}}_{t}^{e}$ . Selecting an object node enables further visual examination of relevant visited areas. Selecting a frontier node enables visitation of unexplored areas. Finally, the planner is required to output a brief description of the scene graph as well as a brief description of the input images. We update the history ${\mathcal{H}}_{t}$ such that ${\mathcal{H}}_{t + 1} = {X}_{t} + {a}_{t} + {c}_{t} + {p}_{t}^{c} + {u}_{t} + {\mathcal{H}}_{t}$ .
输出：基于上述输入，规划器必须为 $q$ 输出答案 ${a}_{t} \in  \mathcal{A}$ ，并输出一个布尔值 ${c}_{t} \in  \{$ True,False $\}$ ，表示它是否有把握回答问题；其当前的数值置信度级别 ${p}_{t}^{c} \in  \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack$ ，以及代理下一步应采取的行动 ${u}_{t}$ 。我们要求规划器也要给出选择该行动及其置信度的理由。最后，规划器需要计划接下来的若干步，从两种高层行动类型中进行选择：<Goto_Object_node>(object_id) 和 <Goto_Frontier_node>(frontier_id) ，其中 object_id 和 frontier_id 从 ${\mathcal{S}}_{t}^{e}$ 中选择。选择对象节点可以进一步对相关已访问区域进行视觉检查。选择前沿节点可以访问尚未探索的区域。最后，规划器还需要输出对场景图的简要描述，以及对输入图像的简要描述。我们更新历史 ${\mathcal{H}}_{t}$ ，使得 ${\mathcal{H}}_{t + 1} = {X}_{t} + {a}_{t} + {c}_{t} + {p}_{t}^{c} + {u}_{t} + {\mathcal{H}}_{t}$ 。


Hierarchical planner and frontier exploration: For <Goto_Object_node>(object_id) action types, we enforce a hierarchical planning behavior by requiring the planner to first reason about which room to go to by selecting a room node, then a region node (within the selected room), and finally the object node to go to. This planning behavior reflects the hierarchical structure of the 3DSG and forces the planner to reason about the hierarchical semantics of the scene to explore and answer the questions. For <Goto_Frontier_node>(frontier_id) action types, we require the planner to provide rationale for its choice of frontier node by referring to the object nodes connected to the selected frontier by edges in the scene graph, enforcing semantic reasoning in the frontier-selection process so that chosen frontiers are task-relevant and for information-gathering.
分层规划与前沿探索：对于 <Goto_Object_node>(object_id) 行动类型，我们强制规划器采用分层规划行为：要求规划器先通过选择一个房间节点来推理要去哪个房间，然后在所选房间内选择一个区域节点，最后选择要去的目标对象节点。该规划行为体现了 3DSG 的分层结构，并迫使规划器对场景的分层语义进行推理，以用于探索与回答问题。对于 <Goto_Frontier_node>(frontier_id) 行动类型，我们要求规划器通过查看场景图中从所选前沿通过边连接到的对象节点来说明其选择该前沿节点的理由，从而在前沿选择过程中实施语义推理，使得所选前沿与任务相关且有助于信息收集。


Termination condition: A planning episode is terminated when the planner outputs ${c}_{t} =$ True or ${p}_{t}^{c} > {0.9}$ ,i.e.,when it is confident in answering the question. The episode is also terminated if $t > {T}_{\max }$ ,when the maximum allowed planning steps have been reached.
终止条件：当规划器输出 ${c}_{t} =$ True 或 ${p}_{t}^{c} > {0.9}$ 时，一个规划回合终止，即当它对回答问题有把握时。若 $t > {T}_{\max }$（即达到允许的最大规划步数），回合也会终止。


Prompt description: We provide the planner with a system prompt detailing how to understand the scene graph structure, and explain the criteria behind choosing the actions-hierarchically for object nodes and task-relevant or information-gathering for frontier nodes. We explain that the 3DSG can be imperfect/incomplete and that the planner should always seek visual confirmation before answering the question with confidence while employing the scene graph as a semantic map for examining and exploring the scene. Finally, we prompt the VLM to provide a brief description of the scene graph and the input images, focusing on elements in the scene that are relevant to the current task. The complete prompt is available in Appendix B.
提示描述：我们为规划器提供系统提示，说明如何理解场景图结构，并解释在对象节点上如何分层选择行动、以及在前沿节点上如何选择任务相关或用于信息收集的行动标准。我们说明 3DSG 可能不完美/不完整，因此规划器在使用场景图作为语义地图来检查与探索场景以回答问题时，始终应在有把握回答之前寻求视觉确认。最后，我们提示 VLM 对场景图和输入图像进行简要描述，重点关注与当前任务相关的场景元素。完整提示见附录 B。


## 4 Experimental Setup
## 4 实验设置


We identify the key research questions that this work aims to evaluate: Q1) Do hierarchical 3D scene graphs provide an effective metric-semantic memory for solving embodied question answering tasks? Q2) How does the real-time nature of GraphEQA compare to offline approaches that provide the planner with full-state scene graphs? Specifically, we aim to evaluate if GraphEQA can utilize incrementally constructed state information to solve EQA tasks and terminate based on confidence, without needing to acquire full state information. Q3) Can GraphEQA effectively combine and reason about the high-level, semantically-sparse and task-agnostic information offered by scene graphs and the local, semantically-rich and task-relevant information from visual memory to actively take information gathering actions until it can confidently answer an embodied question?
我们确定本研究旨在评估的关键研究问题：Q1）分层3D场景图能否为解决具身问答任务提供有效的度量语义记忆？Q2）GraphEQA的实时特性与为规划器提供完整状态场景图的离线方法相比如何？具体而言，我们旨在评估GraphEQA能否利用增量构建的状态信息来完成EQA任务，并基于置信度终止，而无需获取完整状态信息。Q3）GraphEQA能否有效结合并推理场景图所提供的高层、语义稀疏且与任务无关的信息，以及来自视觉记忆的局部、语义丰富且与任务相关的信息，从而在能够自信回答具身问题之前主动采取信息获取行动？


### 4.1 Baselines and Ablations
### 4.1 基线与消融


To evaluate our method and answer the above research questions, we compare against several baselines and focus on methods that employ VLM-based planners for solving EQA or object goal navigation tasks. We compare against a strong baseline, Explore-EQA [3], which calibrates Prismatic-VLM [59] to answer embodied questions confidently while maintaining a 2D semantic memory and using prompted images to guide exploration. Note that ExploreEQA is always executed for a prespecified maximum number of steps, with the highest confidence step chosen to answer the question, while GraphEQA terminates based on a confidence threshold. We implement additional variants of ExploreEQA with newer foundation models—e.g., GPT40, Llama 4 Maverick, and Gemini 2.5 Pro-for fair comparison with respective VLM variants of GraphEQA.
为评估我们的方法并回答上述研究问题，我们与多种基线进行比较，并重点关注采用基于 VLM 的规划器来解决 EQA 或目标物导航任务的方法。我们对比一个强基线 Explore-EQA [3]：它将 Prismatic-VLM [59] 进行校准，使其在维持 2D 语义记忆的同时，能够自信回答具身问题，并使用提示图像来引导探索。需注意，ExploreEQA 总是以预设的最大步数执行，并选择置信度最高的那一步来回答问题；而 GraphEQA 会根据置信度阈值终止。为实现公平比较，我们还用更新的基础模型对 ExploreEQA 做了额外变体——例如 GPT40、Llama 4 Maverick 和 Gemini 2.5 Pro——对应 GraphEQA 也使用各自的 VLM 变体。


We also compare GraphEQA against a modified version of SayPlan [4] which we call SayPlanVi-sion. Similar to SayPlan, SayPlanVision first constructs a scene graph of the whole scene offline and then uses this scene graph for planning. For fair comparison, we further augment SayPlanVision with some abilities of GraphEQA in order to evaluate the effectiveness of our real-time approach and to answer Q2; we provide it with a task-relevant visual memory and confidence-based termination.
我们也将 GraphEQA 与我们改造后的 SayPlan [4] 版本进行比较，称为 SayPlanVi-sion。与 SayPlan 类似，SayPlanVision 会先在离线阶段构建整个场景的场景图，然后使用该场景图进行规划。为保证公平比较，我们进一步为 SayPlanVision 增加 GraphEQA 的部分能力，以评估我们这种实时方法的有效性并回答 Q2；我们为其提供与任务相关的视觉记忆，并使用基于置信度的终止机制。


We further evaluate our method for Q3, using two ablations: GraphEQA-SG, where the planner only has access to the real-time 3DSG and does not have access to images; and GraphEQA-Vis, where the planner only takes the visual memory as input and exploration is done via random frontier-based exploration. These ablations will help us evaluate the benefits of multimodality in GraphEQA.
我们还针对 Q3 进一步评估该方法，使用两个消融实验：GraphEQA-SG，其中规划器仅能访问实时 3DSG，无法访问图像；以及 GraphEQA-Vis，其中规划器只以视觉记忆作为输入，探索通过随机的基于前沿的探索来进行。这些消融将帮助我们评估 GraphEQA 中多模态性的收益。


Experimental Settings: Since we focus on multi-room environments, we evaluate GraphEQA and the baselines mentioned in Section 4.1 in simulation in Habitat-Sim [60] on scenes from HM3D-Semantics [14] on the HM-EQA and OpenEQA datasets and in the real-world in two unique home environments. GraphEQA supports open-vocabulary answers and evaluating them using an LLM, as in OpenEQA. However, to ensure consistent evaluation across both benchmarks, we augment the OpenEQA dataset with multiple choice answers using an LLM (see A.2). We perform numerous experiments with different foundation models as the VLM planner, including GPT40, Gemini 2.5 Pro, and Llama 4 Maverick. For the real-world setup, we deploy and evaluate our approach on the Hello Robot Stretch RE-2 mobile manipulation platform with the Stretch AI codebase [61]. All experiments are conducted on a desktop machine with two (2) NVIDIA TITAN RTX GPUs, 64GB of RAM, and an Intel i9-10900K CPU.

Metrics: We use the following three metrics to compare against the baselines and ablations in Section 4.1: 1) Success Rate (%): an episode is considered a success if the agent answers the embodied question correctly with high confidence; 2) Average # Planning Steps: For successful episodes, we calculate the average number of VLM planning steps. Note that while Explore-EQA runs for a preset maximum number of steps and post-calculates the maximum confidence step, we report the number of steps taken until the max confidence step; 3) Average Trajectory Length (meters): for successful episodes, we calculate the average length of the path traveled by the robot. For SayPlanVision, this includes the path traveled to generate the full scene graph.
指标：我们使用以下三个指标来与第 4.1 节中的基线和消融进行比较：1）成功率（%）：如果智能体以高置信度正确回答具身问题，则该回合视为成功；2）平均 # 规划步：对于成功的回合，我们计算 VLM 规划步的平均数量。需要注意的是，尽管 Explore-EQA 运行时会先按预设的最大步数执行，并在之后计算置信度最高的那一步，我们报告的是在达到最大置信度步之前所采取的步数；3）平均轨迹长度（米）：对于成功的回合，我们计算机器人所行路径的平均长度。对于 SayPlanVision，这包括生成完整场景图所行进的路径。

Baseline And Ablation Study. Regarding Q1, we observe from Table 1 that GraphEQA has higher success rate, compared to all Explore-EQA variants, across both HM-EQA and OpenEQA datasets, without the need to build an explicit 2D semantic task-specific memory. This demonstrates the capability of 3DSGs to provide an effective metric-semantic memory for EQA tasks. We also observe that GraphEQA requires a significantly lower number of planning steps as compared to Explore-EQA. This is because, unlike Explore-EQA, GraphEQA does not entirely rely on images as input to the VLM planner for building the semantic memory as well as planning, as this would constrain the planner to choose from only regions that are visible in the current image. GraphEQA, on the other hand, can use the hierarchical structure of the scene graph as well as semantically-enriched frontier nodes to plan across the entire explored space.
基线与消融研究。关于 Q1，从表 1 可以看出，在 HM-EQA 和 OpenEQA 两个数据集上，GraphEQA 的成功率高于所有 Explore-EQA 变体，且无需构建显式的 2D 任务特定记忆。这表明 3DSGs 能够为 EQA 任务提供有效的度量-语义记忆。我们还观察到，与 Explore-EQA 相比，GraphEQA 所需的规划步数显著更少。这是因为，与 Explore-EQA 不同，GraphEQA 不会完全依赖图像作为 VLM 规划器的输入来构建语义记忆并进行规划；这会将规划器限制为只能从当前图像中可见的区域中选择。相对而言，GraphEQA 能利用场景图的层次结构，以及语义增强的前沿节点，在整个已探索空间中进行规划。

As a result, the agent is overconfident about its choice of object node actions, leading to shorter trajectory lengths in successful cases, but also to increased failure cases. ## 5 Conclusion
## 5 结论


We present GraphEQA, an approach for solving embodied question answering tasks in unseen environments by grounding a vision-language based planner in the context of the current environment by providing as input textual representations of real-time 3D metric-semantic scene graphs and a task-relevant visual memory. We show that GraphEQA achieves improved performance over existing approaches on EQA tasks in both the HM-EQA and OpenEQA benchmarks, and validate performance across both closed and open multimodal foundation models. Furthermore, we validate its practical applicability through real-world deployments in two indoor environments.
我们提出了 GraphEQA，一种通过向当前环境提供实时 3D 度量-语义场景图的文本表示和任务相关的视觉记忆，将基于视觉-语言的规划器置于当前环境上下文中，从而解决未知环境中具身问答任务的方法。我们表明，GraphEQA 在 HM-EQA 和 OpenEQA 基准上的 EQA 任务中，相较现有方法取得了更优性能，并在封闭和开放多模态基础模型上验证了其表现。此外，我们还通过在两个室内环境中的真实部署验证了其实际适用性。


## 6 Limitations
## 6 局限性


A limitation of this approach is reliance on off-the-shelf segmentation and detection models for creating semantic maps required for 3DSG construction. The performance of our approach, hence, is directly impacted by the performance of the detection method used and the semantic categories in the scene graph are limited to the categories detected by the segmentation model. An exciting direction for future work includes enriching the scene graph online with task-relevant node and edge features using open-set models. Another limitation of our approach is that VLM-based planners can be overconfident or underconfident when answering embodied questions. Grounding calibrated models in real-time multi-modal memory is another important direction for future work. Additionally, GraphEQA is currently limited to single-floor scenes, and is unable to traverse multiple floors or buildings. Accommodating multi-floor scenes is within the scope of future work.
该方法的一个局限在于依赖现成的分割与检测模型来生成3DSG构建所需的语义地图。因此，我们方法的表现直接受到所使用检测方法的性能影响，且场景图中的语义类别仅限于分割模型检测到的类别。令人期待的未来方向之一是使用开放集模型，在线为场景图丰富与任务相关的节点与边特征。我们方法的另一个局限是，基于VLM的规划器在回答具身问题时可能过度自信或不够自信。将校准后的模型与实时多模态记忆进行对齐，是另一个重要的未来研究方向。此外，GraphEQA目前仅限于单层场景，无法在多层或多栋建筑之间穿行。支持多层场景属于未来工作的范畴。

## A 实验设置


### A.1 Habitat environment setup
### A.1 Habitat 环境设置


The Habitat-Sim setup for our experiments is identical to the setup used in [3]. The camera sensor settings are as follows: image width $= {640}$ ,image height $= {480}$ ,camera height $= {1.5}\mathrm{\;m}$ , camera tilt $=  - {30}$ degrees,field of view $= {120}$ degrees. For generating trajectories for the <Goto_Object_node>(object_id) and <Goto_Frontier_node>(frontier_id) actions, we find the shortest path between the current agent position and the desired object/frontier node location, on the obstacle-free voxel space of of the 2D occupancy map. We orient the agent such that camera always points towards the desired node location all along the trajectory. In case of the <Goto_Object_node>(object_id) action, this maximizes the number of views that capture the target object. In case of the <Goto_Frontier_node>(frontier_id) action, this makes the agent look outwards into the unexplored areas.
我们实验所用的 Habitat-Sim 设置与文献 [3] 中的设置相同。摄像头传感器参数如下：图像宽度 $= {640}$ ，图像高度 $= {480}$ ，相机高度 $= {1.5}\mathrm{\;m}$ ，相机俯仰角 $=  - {30}$ 度，视场角 $= {120}$ 度。为生成针对 <Goto_Object_node>(object_id) 与 <Goto_Frontier_node>(frontier_id) 动作的轨迹，我们在二维占据地图的无障碍体素空间中，计算当前智能体位置与目标物体/前沿节点位置之间的最短路径。我们在整个轨迹过程中将智能体朝向目标节点位置，使相机始终指向该位置。对于 <Goto_Object_node>(object_id) 动作，这将最大化能够捕捉到目标物体的视角数量。对于 <Goto_Frontier_node>(frontier_id) 动作，这会使智能体朝向尚未探索的区域向外观察。


### A.2 HM-EQA and OpenEQA Benchmarks
### A.2 HM-EQA 和 OpenEQA 基准


HM-EQA: The Habitat-Matterport Embodied Question Answering (HM-EQA) dataset introduced by Ren et al. [3] is based in the Habitat-Matterport 3D Research Dataset (HM3D) of photo-realistic, diverse indoor 3D scans [63]. The dataset is composed of 500 multiple choice questions from 267 different scenes which fall in the following categories: identification, counting, existence, state, and location. A sample from the identification category is as follows: Q: Which rug did I put next to the kitchen sink? A. There is no rug. B. White one. C. Gray one. D. Green one.
HM-EQA：Ren 等人 [3] 提出的 Habitat-Matterport Embodied Question Answering（HM-EQA）数据集基于 Habitat-Matterport 3D Research Dataset（HM3D），该数据集包含逼真且多样的室内 3D 扫描 [63]。该数据集由来自 267 个不同场景的 500 个选择题组成，分为以下类别：识别、计数、存在性、状态和位置。识别类别的一个示例如下：Q: 我把哪块地毯放在了厨房水槽旁边？A. 没有地毯。B. 白色那块。C. 灰色那块。D. 绿色那块。


OpenEQA: The OpenEQA benchmark [13] is composed of data for two different settings, EM- ${EQA}$ (episodic memory) and $A$ - ${EQA}$ (active exploration),with questions spanning seven categories, spatial understanding, object state recognition, functional reasoning, attribute recognition, world knowledge, object localization, and object recognition, in two different environments, HM3D and ScanNet. The active exploration dataset (A-EQA) consists of 557 questions and corresponding open vocabulary answers, for example, Q: Do I have Cayenne pepper left at home? A: I found a bottle of Cayenne pepper in the pantry. In this setting, the agent is supposed to explore the environment autonomously to answer the question. The episodic memory dataset (EM-EQA) consists of a sequence of historical sensory (RGB) observations in addition to each question-answer pair. In this setting, the agent is supposed to analyze the episodic memory to answer the question. To evaluate for success, an LLM is used to score the agent's answer based on similarity to the ground truth answer in the dataset. Evidently, the A-EQA dataset is relevant to our GraphEQA setting, where the agent needs to explore an unseen environment to solve an EQA task. We consider A-EQA questions only in the HM3D environment, resulting in a total of 113 questions. We exclude ScanNet questions, as ScanNet is predominantly composed of single-room environments and therefore does not present a substantial exploration challenge for GraphEQA. Furthermore, although GraphEQA supports querying natural language answers from the VLM planner (instead of multiple choice answers) and evaluating them using an LLM, as in OpenEQA; to ensure consistent evaluation to HM-EQA, we augmented the A-EQA dataset with multiple choice answers using an LLM. The correct answer is provided as one of the four choices.
OpenEQA：OpenEQA 基准 [13] 包含两种不同设置的数据，EM-${EQA}$（情景记忆）和 $A$-${EQA}$（主动探索），问题涵盖七个类别：空间理解、物体状态识别、功能推理、属性识别、世界知识、物体定位和物体识别，覆盖 HM3D 和 ScanNet 两种不同环境。主动探索数据集（A-EQA）由 557 个问题及其对应的开放词汇答案组成，例如，Q: 我家里还剩辣椒粉吗？A: 我在食品储藏室里找到了一瓶辣椒粉。在这种设置下，智能体应自主探索环境来回答问题。情景记忆数据集（EM-EQA）除每个问答对外，还包含一系列历史感知（RGB）观测。在这种设置下，智能体应分析情景记忆来回答问题。为评估是否成功，系统使用一个 LLM 根据智能体答案与数据集中真实答案的相似度进行评分。显然，A-EQA 数据集与我们的 GraphEQA 设置相关，其中智能体需要探索一个未见过的环境来完成 EQA 任务。我们仅考虑 HM3D 环境中的 A-EQA 问题，总计 113 个问题。我们排除了 ScanNet 问题，因为 ScanNet 主要由单房间环境组成，因此对 GraphEQA 而言并不构成实质性的探索挑战。此外，尽管 GraphEQA 支持从 VLM 规划器查询自然语言答案（而非选择题答案），并像 OpenEQA 中那样使用 LLM 进行评估；为了确保与 HM-EQA 的评估一致，我们使用 LLM 为 A-EQA 数据集补充了选择题答案。正确答案被作为四个选项之一提供。


### A.3 3D Scene Graph Construction
### A.3 三维场景图构建


We use Hydra [12] to construct a layered metric-semantic scene graph. Hydra 3DSGs are comprised of the following layers: Layer 1 (bottom): a metric-semantic 3D mesh, Layer 2: objects with corresponding semantic labels and the agent, Layer 3: regions or places, Layer 4: rooms, and Layer 5 (top): building. Intra-layer edges between nodes denote 'traversability', while inter-layer edges denote 'belonging'. For example, an edge between regions in Layer 3 denotes traversability between these regions and an edge between an object and a room denotes that the object is located in that room. 3DSGs are constructed using RGB and depth images, semantic segmentation masks, camera extrinsics, and intrinsics. The HM3D-Semantics dataset provides ground-truth semantic segmentation masks for simulation experiments. In real-world experiments, we use Detic [62] to obtain segmentation masks.
我们使用 Hydra [12] 构建分层的度量语义场景图。Hydra 3DSG 由以下层组成：第 1 层（底层）：度量语义三维网格，第 2 层：带有对应语义标签的物体以及智能体，第 3 层：区域或地点，第 4 层：房间，第 5 层（顶层）：建筑物。同一层内的节点间边表示“可通行性”，而跨层的节点间边表示“从属关系”。例如，第 3 层中两个区域之间的边表示这两个区域之间的可通行性；而连接物体与房间的边表示该物体位于该房间中。3DSG 使用 RGB 和深度图像、语义分割掩码、相机外参和内参进行构建。HM3D-Semantics 数据集为仿真实验提供真实的语义分割掩码。在真实环境实验中，我们使用 Detic [62] 获取分割掩码。

### B.2 Exploiting Hierarchical Nature of 3DSGs for Planning
### B.2 利用3DSG的层次结构进行规划


The portion of the prompt used to describe the scene graph in GraphEQA clarifies to the VLM how layers and nodes are organized in a 3DSG. We take advantage of this structure by requiring <Goto_object_node_step> to be organized such that the VLM first chooses a room (level 4) to navigate to, then choosing an object (level 2) in that room. This inherent structure and explanation of it in the prompt guides the VLM to choose actions that investigate objects that are semantically relevant to the question.
GraphEQA中用于描述场景图的提示词部分，向VLM说明了3DSG中各层与各节点的组织方式。我们利用这一结构，要求<Goto_object_node_step>按如下方式组织：VLM先选择一个房间（第4层）前往，再选择该房间中的一个对象（第2层）。这种内在结构及其在提示词中的说明，会引导VLM选择那些会调查与问题在语义上相关对象的动作。


Full VLM Prompt
完整VLM提示词


Agent prompt: You are an excellent hierarchical graph planning agent. Your goal is to navigate an unseen environment to confidently answer a multiple-choice question about the environment. As you explore the environment, your sensors are building a scene graph representation (in json format) and you have access to that scene graph. Scene graph description: A scene graph represents an indoor environment in a hierarchical tree structure consisting of nodes and edges/links. There are six types of nodes: building, rooms, visited areas, frontiers, objects, and agent in the environment. The tree structure is as follows: At the highest level 5 is a 'building' node. At level 4 are room nodes. There are links connecting the building node to each room node. At the lower level 3, are region and frontier nodes. 'region' node represent region of room that is already explored. Frontier nodes represent areas that are at the boundary of visited and unexplored areas. There are links from room nodes to corresponding region and frontier nodes depicted which room they are located in. At the lowest level 2 are object nodes and agent nodes. There is an edge from region node to each object node depicting which visited area of which room the object is located in. ### C.3 Additional Quantitative Analysis: Zero-shot reasoning for commonsense questions

We perform this evaluation to answer the following question: how many questions in the HM-EQA dataset can be answered correctly purely based on commonsense reasoning or guessing, without exploring the environment? We aim to perform this analysis to roughly estimate the percentage of false positives that can occur in the HM-EQA dataset using different VLMs. To answer this question we define the following three additional baselines: EQAZeroShotGPT4o, EQAZeroShotPris-matic and EQAZeroShotGPT4oQuestionOnly. EQAZeroShotGPT4o evaluates the zero-shot performance of GPT-40 when answering an EQA question using the initial image and the question. EQAZeroShotPrismatic evaluates the zero-shot performance of the calibrated Prismatic model from Explore-EQA [3] when answering an EQA question using the initial image and the question. EQAZeroShotQuestionOnly evaluates the zero-shot performance of GPT-4o when answering an EQA question using only the question. In all the above baselines, no exploration steps are taken. Prompts for the above baselines are identical to ones used by Explore-EQA [3]. An episode is considered a success if the question is answered correctly and with high confidence (> 0.5).

### C.7 Additional Discussion
### C.7 额外讨论


Novelty of GraphEQA without a learning component: Even without a learned component, several aspects of GraphEQA are novel - a real-time, compact, actionable, multimodal memory for grounding VLMs in unseen 3D environments and a semantically-informed efficient exploration strategy. Moreover, we argue that modular and easily customizable methodologies like GraphEAQ are effective for zero-shot deployment in novel real-world environments without requiring expensive end-to-end training, as reflected in our real-world experiments. The performance of certain modules can be improved with task-specific fine-tuning, however, that can come at the cost of open-world generalizability.
在不包含学习组件的情况下，GraphEQA 的新颖性：即使没有学习组件，GraphEQA 仍有多方面的创新——一种用于在未见 3D 环境中为 VLM 提供扎根依据的实时、紧凑、可执行的多模态记忆，以及一种在语义层面指导的高效探索策略。此外，我们认为像 GraphEAQ 这样的模块化且易于定制的方法，能够在无需昂贵端到端训练的情况下，实现对新颖真实世界环境的零样本部署，这也体现在我们的真实世界实验中。尽管对某些模块进行任务特定微调可以提升性能，但这可能以开放世界泛化能力为代价。


How can GraphEQA handle multiple questions in the same episode? To handle multiple questions, our method can be extended by maintaining a separate visual memory per question and discarding as each question is answered. However, handling temporally extended questions that require an unknown number of images remains an important direction for future work.
GraphEQA 如何在同一回合中处理多个问题？为处理多个问题，我们的方法可通过为每个问题维护独立的视觉记忆来扩展，并在每个问题被回答后丢弃对应记忆。然而，仍需在未来工作中探索如何处理那些需要未知数量图像的、时间跨度更长的问题。


Utility of using both binary and numeric confidence: Using both binary and numeric confidence for episode termination is empirically motivated. In practice, this redundancy improved the reliability of numerical scores and provided a more robust indicator of the VLM's confidence.
同时使用二元与数值置信度的作用：将二元与数值置信度用于回合终止具有经验动机。实践中，这种冗余提高了数值评分的可靠性，并为衡量 VLM 的置信度提供了更稳健的指标。


Broader applicability of GraphEQA beyond EQA tasks: Although we mainly focus on EQA tasks, GraphEQA is broadly applicable to other navigation tasks such as object-goal navigation, leveraging its ability to perform grounded exploration in unseen 3D environments. In particular, EQA presents a greater challenge than object-goal navigation, as it requires more complex semantic and spatial reasoning, including identification, counting, existence, state, and functional understanding (see OpenEQA [9], Fig. 5). Compared to GraphEQA, methods like ConceptGraphs [2], which rely on enriched scene graphs without visual context, face key limitations on EQA tasks: 1) Enriching scene graphs with relational information via LLMs may suffice for object-goal navigation but falls short for EQA, which often demands nuanced, fine-grained semantic understanding that may not be captured in the graph. While task-specific enrichment is possible, it is not scalable across diverse EQA tasks. GraphEQA addresses this challenge by maintaining semantically-rich task-relevant images in its multi-modal memory and using a VLM for in-context reasoning. 2) Open-vocabulary 3D instance segmentation, used in ConceptGraphs, is computationally expensive - even when applied incrementally - and limits the real-time applicability of these methods. GraphEQA maintains a light-weight yet semantically rich multi-modal memory which can be constructed in real-time.
GraphEQA 在超越 EQA 任务之外的更广泛适用性：尽管我们主要关注 EQA 任务，GraphEQA 仍可广泛应用于其他导航任务，例如物体-目标导航，利用其在未见 3D 环境中进行扎根式探索的能力。尤其是，EQA 的挑战程度高于物体-目标导航，因为它需要更复杂的语义与空间推理，包括识别、计数、存在性、状态以及功能理解（见 OpenEQA [9]，图 5）。相较于 GraphEQA，诸如 ConceptGraphs [2] 这类方法依赖的是增强了但缺少视觉上下文的场景图，在 EQA 任务上存在关键局限：1）通过 LLM 用关系信息丰富场景图，或许足以支持物体-目标导航，但对 EQA 往往不够，因为 EQA 常常需要细致、微粒度的语义理解，而图中未必能捕获。虽然可以进行任务特定的丰富，但难以扩展到多样的 EQA 任务。GraphEQA 通过在多模态记忆中保留语义丰富且与任务相关的图像，并使用 VLM 进行情境内推理来应对这一挑战。2）ConceptGraphs 使用的开放词汇 3D 实例分割计算代价很高——即便是增量使用也如此——从而限制了这些方法的实时性。GraphEQA 维护轻量但语义丰富的多模态记忆，并可在实时条件下构建。
