## Abstract
## 摘要


Long-horizon robotic manipulation remains challenging for reinforcement learning (RL) because sparse rewards provide limited guidance for credit assignment. Practical policy improvement thus relies on richer intermediate supervision, such as dense progress rewards, which are costly to obtain and ill-suited to non-monotonic behaviors such as backtracking and recovery. To address this, we propose Advantage Reward Modeling (ARM), a framework that shifts from hard-to-quantify absolute progress to estimating relative advantage. We introduce a cost-effective tri-state labeling strategy-Progressive, Regressive, and Stagnant-that reduces human cognitive overhead while ensuring high cross-annotator consistency. By training on these intuitive signals, ARM enables automated progress annotation for both complete demonstrations and fragmented DAgger-style data. Integrating ARM into an offline RL pipeline allows for adaptive action-reward reweighting, effectively filtering suboptimal samples. Our approach achieves a 99.4% success rate on a challenging long-horizon towel-folding task, demonstrating improved stability and data efficiency over current VLA baselines with near-zero human intervention during policy training.
长程机器人操作任务对强化学习（RL）而言依然充满挑战，因为稀疏奖励在信用分配方面提供的指导有限。因此，有效的策略改进依赖于更丰富的中间监督信号（如稠密进度奖励），但这类信号获取成本高昂，且不适用于回溯和恢复等非单调行为。为解决这一问题，我们提出了优势奖励建模（ARM），该框架将建模重点从难以量化的绝对进度转向估计相对优势。我们引入了一种经济高效的三态标注策略——进展（Progressive）、回归（Regressive）和停滞（Stagnant），在降低人工认知负担的同时，确保了高标注一致性。通过在这些直观信号上进行训练，ARM 能够为完整演示和碎片化的 DAgger 式数据实现自动进度标注。将 ARM 集成到离线 RL 流水线中，可实现自适应的动作-奖励重加权，从而有效过滤次优样本。我们的方法在极具挑战性的长程叠毛巾任务中达到了 99.4% 的成功率，相较于现有的 VLA 基线，在策略训练过程中几乎无需人工干预，展现出更强的稳定性和数据效率。

## 1. Introduction
## 1. 引言


The rapid evolution of Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models $\left\lbrack  {1,2,{13},{15}}\right\rbrack$ has advanced general-purpose robotic manipulation. However, most existing VLA approaches rely heavily on imitation learning (IL) [26], which demands massive datasets and incurs considerable human labor and physical resources costs during large-scale data collection $\left\lbrack  {3,{10},{14},{27},{35},{36}}\right\rbrack$ . Beyond data quantity,the inherent suboptimality and noise in human demonstrations, especially in complex, long-horizon tasks, often impede policy convergence. Reinforcement Learning (RL) [33] provides a promising alternative by enabling autonomous policy refinement beyond expert demonstrations [12, 18].
视觉-语言-动作（VLA）模型 $\left\lbrack  {1,2,{13},{15}}\right\rbrack$ 的快速演进推动了通用机器人操作的发展。然而，大多数现有的 VLA 方法严重依赖模仿学习（IL）[26]，这需要海量数据集，并在大规模数据收集过程中产生巨大的人力与物理资源成本 $\left\lbrack  {3,{10},{14},{27},{35},{36}}\right\rbrack$。除了数据量问题，人类演示中固有的次优性和噪声（尤其是在复杂长程任务中）往往会阻碍策略收敛。强化学习（RL）[33] 提供了一种有前景的替代方案，能够实现超越专家演示的自主策略优化 [12, 18]。


Nevertheless, effective RL in long-horizon manipulation hinges on informative reward signals. While sparse rewards (e.g., binary success indicators) are straightforward to specify, they struggle to yield effective learning signals, frequently leading to convergence difficulties in long-horizon manipulation tasks. Consequently, high-quality dense rewards or informative value functions are essential to provide continuous supervision and facilitate effective credit assignment.
然而，长程操作中有效的强化学习取决于信息丰富的奖励信号。虽然稀疏奖励（如二元成功指标）定义简单，但难以提供有效的学习信号，常导致长程操作任务收敛困难。因此，高质量的稠密奖励或信息丰富的价值函数对于提供持续监督和促进有效信用分配至关重要。


Current frameworks [6, 12] attempt to leverage dense signals through advantage estimation or sample reweight-ing. However, they depend on high-precision progress reward models to mitigate the notorious credit assignment problem. This dependency constitutes a pervasive "Reward Engineering Bottleneck", limiting both scalability and stability of VLA deployment in unstructured environments. Designing a cost-effective reward function that provides stable and high-frequency feedback remains a formidable challenge. In particular, existing evaluation paradigms predicated on absolute progress are limited by several critical bottlenecks [6, 12, 19, 23, 34, 37, 39]: First, Zero-shot VLMs suffer from considerable unreliability and prohibitive costs; they not only incur high inference overhead, but also yield low-precision annotations due to their lack of spatial-geometric grounding, which manifests as non-monotonic oscillations in reward signals [6, 23, 32]. Second, current schemes exhibit quantization ambiguity in failure states. By predicating progress modeling on a strict monotonicity assumption and relying on simplistic video rewinding $\left\lbrack  {6,{34},{39}}\right\rbrack$ to simulate regression,these methods fail to comprehensively characterize authentic, nonlinear operational errors. Moreover, the conventional reliance on coarse subtask partitions [6, 34] fails to capture the subtle intra-stage transitions essential for long-horizon tasks—such as critical recovery and corrective maneuvers [11]-ultimately yielding misaligned reward signals and erratic policy updates.
现有框架 [6, 12] 试图通过优势估计或样本重加权来利用稠密信号。然而，它们依赖高精度的进度奖励模型来缓解棘手的信用分配问题。这种依赖构成了普遍存在的“奖励工程瓶颈”，限制了 VLA 在非结构化环境中部署的可扩展性和稳定性。设计一种能提供稳定且高频反馈的经济高效奖励函数仍是一项艰巨挑战。特别是，现有基于绝对进度的评估范式受到几个关键瓶颈的限制 [6, 12, 19, 23, 34, 37, 39]：首先，零样本 VLM 可靠性差且成本高昂；它们不仅推理开销大，而且由于缺乏空间几何基础，导致标注精度低，表现为奖励信号中的非单调振荡 [6, 23, 32]。其次，现有方案在失败状态下存在量化歧义。通过将进度建模建立在严格的单调性假设上，并依赖简单的视频倒放 $\left\lbrack  {6,{34},{39}}\right\rbrack$ 来模拟回归，这些方法无法全面刻画真实的非线性操作错误。此外，传统上对粗粒度子任务划分的依赖 [6, 34] 无法捕捉长程任务中至关重要的细微阶段内转换（如关键的恢复和纠正动作 [11]），最终导致奖励信号失准和策略更新不稳定。

To address these challenges, we introduce Advantage Reward Modeling (ARM). Our core insight is that defining absolute progress necessitates ad-hoc, task-specific heuristics that are difficult to scale. In contrast, the relative advantage between states provides a more intuitive, concise, and task-agnostic primitive for annotation. While the recent work VLAC [38] also employs interval gain prediction, its methodology is predicated on the assumption of a positive correlation between task progress and time. By decoupling progress rewards from global temporal anchors, ARM naturally accommodates regressive behaviors and error recovery. Our core contributions are as follows:
为应对这些挑战，我们引入了优势奖励建模（ARM）。我们的核心见解是，定义绝对进度需要特定于任务的临时启发式方法，难以扩展。相比之下，状态间的相对优势为标注提供了一种更直观、简洁且与任务无关的基元。尽管近期工作 VLAC [38] 也采用了区间增益预测，但其方法论基于任务进度与时间呈正相关的假设。通过将进度奖励与全局时间锚点解耦，ARM 自然地适应了回归行为和错误恢复。我们的主要贡献如下：


- Tri-state Advantage Labeling Strategy: We introduce a labeling method based on three fundamental categories: Progressive, Regressive, and Stagnant. This scheme is task-agnostic, imposes low cognitive load, and is natively compatible with heterogeneous and fragmented datasets.
- 三态优势标注策略：我们引入了一种基于三个基本类别的标注方法：进展 (Progressive)、回归 (Regressive) 和停滞 (Stagnant)。该方案与任务无关，认知负荷低，且天然兼容异构和碎片化的数据集。


- Advantage Reward Model (ARM): We develop a multimodal reward model that integrates temporal video sequences with robotic proprioceptive states to estimate the relative progress gain of trajectory segments. By anchoring these predictions with a task-completion head, ARM can automatically reconstruct globally consistent dense progress trajectories from discrete tri-state labels.
- 优势奖励模型 (ARM)：我们开发了一种多模态奖励模型，通过整合时间视频序列与机器人本体感知状态，来估计轨迹片段的相对进度增益。通过利用任务完成度头对这些预测进行锚定，ARM 能够从离散的三态标签中自动重构出全局一致的密集进度轨迹。


- Advantage-Weighted Behavior Cloning (AW-BC): We extend the Reward-Aligned Behavior Cloning (RA-BC) paradigm [6] by incorporating adaptive scaling coefficients to ensure compatibility with fragmented DAgger data [31]. By leveraging predicted interval gains for advantage-aware reweighting, AW-BC effectively filters suboptimal samples and prioritizes high-value recovery trajectories. Empirically, our framework achieves a near-perfect success rate of 99.4% on the challenging, long-horizon towel-folding task, marking a notable advancement in VLA policy refinement.
- 优势加权行为克隆（AW - BC）：我们通过引入自适应缩放系数，扩展了奖励对齐行为克隆（RA - BC）范式[6]，以确保与碎片化的DAgger数据[31]兼容。通过利用预测的区间增益进行优势感知重加权，AW - BC有效地过滤掉次优样本，并优先处理高价值的恢复轨迹。根据经验，我们的框架在具有挑战性的长视野毛巾折叠任务中实现了近乎完美的99.4%成功率，标志着视觉 - 语言 - 动作（VLA）策略优化方面的显著进展。

## 2. 相关工作


### 2.1. Reward for Manipulation
### 2.1. 操作任务的奖励


Traditional reinforcement learning (RL) relies heavily on manual reward shaping, which is often labor-intensive and task-specific. To mitigate this, inverse reinforcement learning (IRL) [25] and learning from human feedback (RLHF) [8] infer reward functions but suffer from identifiability and scalability issues, respectively.
传统强化学习（RL）严重依赖手动奖励塑造，这通常既费力又针对特定任务。为缓解这一问题，逆强化学习（IRL）[25]和从人类反馈中学习（RLHF）[8]可推断奖励函数，但分别存在可识别性和可扩展性问题。


Vision-language models (VLMs) such as VIP [21] and LIV [22] provide self-supervised goal-distance signals but lack the precision required for fine-grained, contact-rich manipulation. As noted in SARM [6], single-objective distance metrics fail to capture intermediate progress in long-horizon tasks. A common limitation shared by methods such as GVL [23], ReWiND [39], SARM [6], and VIP [21] is their reliance on a strict monotonicity assumption, which equates task progress with chronological order. However, real-world offline demonstrations often contain mistakes, retries, and temporary regressions, leading to reward misspecification under temporal heuristics. Alternative approaches also present trade-offs: hop-based mechanisms such as Robo-Dopamine [34] sacrifice fine-grained action detail, while zero-shot VLM prompting [5, 7, 17] suffers from prediction noise, high latency, and inference cost. To address these issues, we introduce the Advantage Reward Model (ARM), which relaxes temporal monotonicity by evaluating relative progress against historical visual and proprioceptive states, enabling effective advantage estimation even under temporary trajectory deviations.
像 VIP [21] 和 LIV [22] 这类视觉语言模型（VLM）能提供自监督的目标距离信号，但缺乏细粒度、富接触操作所需的精度。正如 SARM [6] 中所指出的，单目标距离度量无法捕捉长周期任务中的中间进展。GVL [23]、ReWiND [39]、SARM [6] 和 VIP [21] 等方法存在一个共同的局限性，即它们依赖严格的单调性假设，将任务进展等同于时间顺序。然而，现实世界的离线演示往往包含错误、重试和暂时的倒退，这会导致在时间启发式方法下奖励指定错误。其他方法也存在权衡问题：像 Robo - Dopamine [34] 这类基于跳跃的机制会牺牲细粒度的动作细节，而零样本 VLM 提示 [5, 7, 17] 则存在预测噪声、高延迟和推理成本高等问题。为解决这些问题，我们引入了优势奖励模型（ARM），该模型通过对照历史视觉和本体感受状态评估相对进展来放宽时间单调性，即使在轨迹暂时偏离的情况下也能进行有效的优势估计。


### 2.2. Reward-Aligned Behavior Cloning (RA-BC)
### 2.2. 奖励对齐行为克隆 (RA-BC)


Learning from suboptimal demonstrations is a critical bottleneck for large-scale robot learning. To address this, the paradigm of Reweighted Behavior Cloning (BC) has been widely explored. Originating from classic baselines like Advantage-Weighted Regression (AWR) [28], Advantage-Weighted Actor-Critic (AWAC) [24], and Implicit Q-Learning (IQL) [16]—these approaches extract improved policies by applying advantage-based scalar weights to suppress suboptimal trajectories.
从次优演示中学习是大规模机器人学习的关键瓶颈。为解决这一问题，重新加权行为克隆（BC）范式已得到广泛探索。这些方法源自经典基线，如优势加权回归（AWR）[28]、优势加权演员 - 评论家（AWAC）[24]和隐式Q学习（IQL）[16] —— 它们通过应用基于优势的标量权重来抑制次优轨迹，从而提取更优策略。


However, traditional weighting paradigms are limited by a critical bottleneck: they inherently rely on explicit environment rewards to fit global value functions, which are notoriously inaccessible in vision-based, real-world settings. To bypass this, recent methods like SARM [6] introduced the Reward-Aligned Behavior Cloning (RA-BC) framework, leveraging a stage-aware reward model instead of environment rewards. While effective at mitigating data quality issues, SARM trades the reward bottleneck for a new constraint: it heavily relies on prohibitive manual language annotations. In contrast, our proposed ARM eliminates the need for explicit reward engineering by extracting advantage signals purely through relative progress comparisons.
然而，传统的加权范式受到一个关键瓶颈的限制：它们本质上依赖显式的环境奖励来拟合全局价值函数，而在基于视觉的现实世界环境中，这些奖励是出了名的难以获取。为了绕过这一问题，像 SARM [6] 这样的近期方法引入了奖励对齐行为克隆（RA - BC）框架，它利用阶段感知奖励模型来替代环境奖励。虽然 SARM 在缓解数据质量问题方面很有效，但它用一个新的限制取代了奖励瓶颈：它严重依赖大量的手动语言注释。相比之下，我们提出的 ARM 通过纯粹的相对进度比较提取优势信号，从而消除了对显式奖励工程的需求。

### 3.2. Advantage Reward Modeling
### 3.2. 优势奖励建模


The Advantage Reward Model (ARM) is designed to resolve the perceptual ambiguities inherent in isolated frames by shifting the reward estimation paradigm from absolute progress regression to relative advantage classification. Unlike traditional "Multi-Input Single-Output" (MISO) models [6, 23] that collapse temporal context into a single scalar, ARM formulates reward estimation as a Multi-Input Multi-Output (MIMO) sequence learning problem (Fig. 2). This design allows the model to contextualize local observations within a short-term history, analogous to how humans review recent temporal context to disambiguate intent and action quality.
优势奖励模型 (ARM) 旨在通过将奖励估计范式从绝对进度回归转向相对优势分类，解决孤立帧中固有的感知模糊性。与将时间上下文压缩为单个标量的传统“多输入单输出”(MISO) 模型 [6, 23] 不同，ARM 将奖励估计表述为多输入多输出 (MIMO) 序列学习问题（图 2）。这种设计允许模型在短期历史中对局部观测进行语境化，类似于人类通过回顾近期时间背景来消除意图和动作质量的歧义。


#### 3.2.1. MIMO Transformer Architecture
#### 3.2.1. MIMO Transformer 架构


We adopt the Transformer Sequential Aggregator from SARM [6] as our backbone, re-engineering it to support multi-frame causal reasoning and relative advantage estimation. ARM processes a sequence of historical observations within a causal window ${\mathcal{W}}_{t} = \left\{  {{o}_{t - {4k}},\ldots ,{o}_{t}}\right\}$ in parallel. By restricting the receptive field to past frames, this window-based approach ensures that predictions are informed by sufficient motion cues while maintaining real-time inference capabilities. Crucially, this causal formulation ensures seamless compatibility with both online and offline RL paradigms, as it facilitates instantaneous reward generation without any dependency on future trajectory segments.
我们采用 SARM [6] 中的 Transformer 序列聚合器作为骨干网络，并对其进行重构以支持多帧因果推理和相对优势估计。ARM 并行处理因果窗口 ${\mathcal{W}}_{t} = \left\{  {{o}_{t - {4k}},\ldots ,{o}_{t}}\right\}$ 内的历史观测序列。通过将感受野限制在过去帧，这种基于窗口的方法确保了预测能够获得充分的运动线索，同时保持实时推理能力。至关重要的是，这种因果表述确保了与在线和离线强化学习范式的无缝兼容，因为它无需依赖未来轨迹片段即可实现即时奖励生成。


Multimodal Fusion. For each timestep $i \in  {\mathcal{W}}_{t}$ ,ARM integrates three disparate signals: (i) CLIP-based [30] visual features ${v}_{i} \in  {\mathbb{R}}^{{d}_{\text{ vis }}}$ ,(ii) robot proprioceptive states ${s}_{i} \in \; {\mathbb{R}}^{{d}_{\text{ state }}}$ ,and (iii) task instructions $g \in  {\mathbb{R}}^{{d}_{\text{ lang }}}$ . These inputs are projected into a unified $d$ -dimensional latent space to form a fused multimodal embedding ${x}_{i}$ ,defined as:
多模态融合。对于每个时间步 $i \in  {\mathcal{W}}_{t}$，ARM 集成了三种不同的信号：(i) 基于 CLIP [30] 的视觉特征 ${v}_{i} \in  {\mathbb{R}}^{{d}_{\text{ vis }}}$，(ii) 机器人本体感知状态 ${s}_{i} \in \; {\mathbb{R}}^{{d}_{\text{ state }}}$，以及 (iii) 任务指令 $g \in  {\mathbb{R}}^{{d}_{\text{ lang }}}$。这些输入被投影到一个统一的 $d$ 维潜在空间中，形成融合的多模态嵌入 ${x}_{i}$，定义如下：


$$
{x}_{i} = \operatorname{MLP}\left( {v}_{i}\right)  + \operatorname{MLP}\left( {s}_{i}\right)  + \operatorname{MLP}\left( g\right) \tag{1}
$$



The resulting sequence ${\left\{  {x}_{i}\right\}  }_{i = t - {4k}}^{t}$ is then processed by an 8-layer Transformer Encoder to yield temporally enriched latent representations $\{ h\}$ :
所得序列 ${\left\{  {x}_{i}\right\}  }_{i = t - {4k}}^{t}$ 随后由 8 层 Transformer 编码器处理，以产生时间增强的潜在表示 $\{ h\}$：


$$
\left\{  {{h}_{t - {4k}},\ldots ,{h}_{t}}\right\}   = \operatorname{Transformer}\left( {\left\{  {x}_{i}\right\}  }_{i = t - {4k}}^{t}\right) \tag{2}
$$



where each ${h}_{i}$ encodes the historical evolution and kinematic state of the task at that specific moment.
其中每个 ${h}_{i}$ 编码了该特定时刻任务的历史演变和运动学状态。


Dual-Head Learning Objective. To balance sensitivity to local state transitions with the perception of global task goals, ARM is optimized via two synergistic output heads:
双头学习目标。为了平衡对局部状态转换的敏感性与对全局任务目标的感知，ARM 通过两个协同输出头进行优化：


1. Multi-frame Advantage Classification: The interval head infers the advantage transitions $\Delta \widehat{y}$ between consecutive hidden states $\left( {{h}_{i},{h}_{i + 1}}\right)$ . This branch is optimized via a standard cross-entropy loss,denoted as ${\mathcal{L}}_{\text{ int }}$ , which is supervised by the tri-state labels (detailed in Sec. 3.2.2). By reformulating reward estimation as a discrete classification task rather than continuous regression, the model exhibits significantly enhanced robustness against the non-linear noise and temporal stochas-ticity inherent in demonstrations.
1. 多帧优势分类：区间头推断连续隐藏状态 $\left( {{h}_{i},{h}_{i + 1}}\right)$ 之间的优势转换 $\Delta \widehat{y}$。该分支通过标准交叉熵损失进行优化，记为 ${\mathcal{L}}_{\text{ int }}$，并由三态标签（详见 3.2.2 节）监督。通过将奖励估计重新表述为离散分类任务而非连续回归，该模型在应对演示中固有的非线性噪声和时间随机性时表现出显著增强的鲁棒性。


2. Task Completion Prediction: To anchor relative advantage estimations to absolute task metrics, the completion head $C$ predicts the probability that the current observation ${s}_{t}$ constitutes a successful terminal state. This decoupled design not only facilitates the identification of successful task executions, but also extracts progress anchor points from the predictions. When jointly utilized with the Multi-frame Advantage Classification results, these anchor points enable highly consistent, dense progress reconstructions.
2. 任务完成预测：为了将相对优势估计锚定在绝对任务指标上，完成度头 $C$ 预测当前观测 ${s}_{t}$ 构成成功终止状态的概率。这种解耦设计不仅有助于识别成功的任务执行，还能从预测中提取进度锚点。当与多帧优势分类结果联合使用时，这些锚点能够实现高度一致的密集进度重构。


Moreover, since successful terminal frames are exceedingly rare within long-horizon continuous trajectories, this branch suffers from severe class imbalance. To effectively address this issue, we optimize the completion head using Focal Loss [20]:
此外，由于成功的终止帧在长程连续轨迹中极为罕见，该分支面临严重的类别不平衡问题。为有效解决此问题，我们使用 Focal Loss [20] 对完成度头进行优化：


$$
{\mathcal{L}}_{\text{ succ }} = \operatorname{FocalLoss}\left( {{C}_{t},\mathbb{k}\left\lbrack  {{P}_{t} \geq  1 - \epsilon }\right\rbrack  }\right) \tag{3}
$$



The total objective is defined as ${\mathcal{L}}_{ARM} = {\lambda }_{int}{\mathcal{L}}_{int} + \; {\lambda }_{\text{ succ }}{\mathcal{L}}_{\text{ succ }}$ . This joint training enables the model to not only recover continuous progress curves but also accurately identify regressive behaviors and critical task completion moments.
总目标定义为 ${\mathcal{L}}_{ARM} = {\lambda }_{int}{\mathcal{L}}_{int} + \; {\lambda }_{\text{ succ }}{\mathcal{L}}_{\text{ succ }}$。这种联合训练使模型不仅能恢复连续的进度曲线，还能准确识别回归行为和关键的任务完成时刻。


#### 3.2.2. Lightweight Tri-state Auto Labeling Strategy
#### 3.2.2. 轻量级三态自动标注策略


Traditional reward engineering for robotic manipulation typically requires annotators to assign a normalized scalar value $P \in  \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack$ to each video frame. This continuous labeling process imposes a high cognitive load and is prone to inter-annotator inconsistency, as the definition of "progress" is often subjective. Such noise in supervision signals frequently leads to suboptimal policy convergence and substantial engineering overhead.
传统的机器人操作奖励工程通常要求标注者为每一帧视频分配一个归一化的标量值 $P \in  \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack$。这种连续标注过程不仅认知负荷高，且由于“进度”定义的主观性，容易导致标注者间的不一致。监督信号中的此类噪声往往会导致策略收敛欠佳，并带来巨大的工程开销。


To address these issues, we redefine the annotation task as a tri-state categorical classification of relative advantage. As illustrated in Figure 3, for any observation pair $\left( {{s}_{t},{s}_{t + k}}\right)$ ,we define a progress-based advantage label $y \in \; \{  - 1,0, + 1\}$ according to the following rules:
为解决这些问题，我们将标注任务重新定义为相对优势的三态分类。如图 3 所示，对于任意观测对 $\left( {{s}_{t},{s}_{t + k}}\right)$，我们根据以下规则定义基于进度的优势标签 $y \in \; \{  - 1,0, + 1\}$：


- +1 (Progressing): The state effectively advances toward the task goal.
- +1（进展）：状态有效地向任务目标推进。


- -1 (Regressing): The state deviates from the goal, encounters an error, or results in failure.
- -1（倒退）：状态偏离目标、遇到错误或导致失败。


- 0 (Stagnant): No substantial progress is made, corresponding to waiting or idle behavior.
- 0（停滞）：未取得实质性进展，对应等待或空闲行为。


By acquiring initial human annotations through this simplified paradigm, we can efficiently cold-start our model. Subsequently, the trained model is utilized to perform inference on vast amounts of unannotated trajectories, automatically generating large-scale pseudo-labeled data for further training.
通过这种简化范式获取初始人工标注，我们可以高效地冷启动模型。随后，利用训练好的模型对海量未标注轨迹进行推理，自动生成大规模伪标签数据以供进一步训练。


### 3.3. Global Progress Reconstruction
### 3.3. 全局进度重构


As illustrated in Fig. 1B, leveraging the MIMO architecture enables ARM to decompose complete video demonstrations and systematically aggregate the resulting predictions to reconstruct a dense, full-sequence progress curve:
如图 1B 所示，利用 MIMO 架构，ARM 能够分解完整的视频演示，并系统地聚合所得预测结果，从而重构出密集的完整序列进度曲线：


1. Parallel Inference Efficiency: While traditional sliding-window methods suffer from redundant computations on overlapping frames, the MIMO architecture predicts sequences directly within its context window. By leveraging video clipping, lengthy episodic trajectories are partitioned into independent, non-overlapping segments. These segments can be processed concurrently as parallel batches in a single forward pass, significantly accelerating the overall inference process.
1. 并行推理效率：传统的滑动窗口方法在重叠帧上存在冗余计算，而 MIMO 架构直接在其上下文窗口内预测序列。通过视频切片，长程片段轨迹被划分为独立的非重叠段。这些片段可在单次前向传播中作为并行批次处理，显著加速了整体推理过程。


2. Sequence Alignment and Padding: For terminal video segments that are shorter than the model's specified window size, a tail-frame replication padding strategy is applied. During the final aggregation of the full episode, predictions corresponding to these synthetically padded regions are discarded to maintain temporal fidelity.
2. 序列对齐与填充：对于短于模型指定窗口大小的末端视频片段，采用尾帧复制填充策略。在最终聚合完整片段时，丢弃对应这些合成填充区域的预测，以保持时间保真度。

3. Coherent Progress Generation: To generate the global dense progress curve ${P}_{t}$ ,the system mathematically integrates the model-predicted relative state transitions $\Delta \widehat{y}$ with the absolute task completion signal ${C}_{t}$ . Specifically,treating ${C}_{t}$ as the definitive progress anchor (e.g., ${P}_{T} = {1.0}$ at task completion),the dense progress values for preceding frames are reconstructed via accumulation of $\Delta \widehat{y}$ .
3. 一致性进度生成：为生成全局密集进度曲线 ${P}_{t}$，系统在数学上将模型预测的相对状态转换 $\Delta \widehat{y}$ 与绝对任务完成信号 ${C}_{t}$ 进行整合。具体而言，将 ${C}_{t}$ 视为确定的进度锚点（例如任务完成时为 ${P}_{T} = {1.0}$），通过累加 $\Delta \widehat{y}$ 重构出前序帧的密集进度值。


This pipeline elegantly transforms discrete, local relative predictions into a coherent and dense global progress signal, thereby providing consistent, high-quality supervision for subsequent policy learning.
该流水线巧妙地将离散的局部相对预测转化为连贯且稠密的全局进度信号，从而为后续的策略学习提供了一致且高质量的监督。


### 3.4. Policy Optimization via AW-BC
### 3.4. 基于 AW-BC 的策略优化


Based on the dense progress signals reconstructed by ARM, we propose Advantage-Weighted Behavior Cloning (AW-BC). This framework prioritizes learning from high-advantage transitions while suppressing suboptimal behaviors through a statistically grounded reweighting mechanism, as illustrated in Fig. 1C.
基于 ARM 重构的稠密进度信号，我们提出了优势加权行为克隆（AW-BC）。如图 1C 所示，该框架通过一种基于统计学的重加权机制，优先学习高优势转换，同时抑制次优行为。


#### 3.4.1. Length-adaptive Gain Formulation
#### 3.4.1. 长度自适应增益公式


To mitigate the length bias inherent in heterogeneous demonstrations-where drastic variations in episode duration lead to inconsistent progress gradients (e.g., disproportionately steep slopes in shorter sequences)- we introduce an adaptive scaling mechanism. Such gradient volatility often induces instability and jitter in the learning dynamics, hindering smooth weight optimization. For an action chunk with horizon $H$ ,the length-adaptive gain $\Delta {G}_{t}$ is formulated as:
为了缓解异构演示中固有的长度偏差（即剧烈的片段时长差异会导致进度梯度不一致，例如较短序列中斜率过陡），我们引入了一种自适应缩放机制。这种梯度波动往往会导致学习动态的不稳定和抖动，阻碍权重的平滑优化。对于视界为 $H$ 的动作块，其长度自适应增益 $\Delta {G}_{t}$ 公式如下：


$$
\Delta {G}_{t} = \left( {{P}_{t + H} - {P}_{t}}\right)  \cdot  \frac{{L}_{\text{ seq }}}{\bar{L}} \tag{4}
$$



where ${P}_{t}$ denotes the progress value obtained via global progress reconstruction, ${L}_{\text{ seq }}$ represents the total length of the current episode,and $\bar{L}$ is the average episode length across the entire dataset. This normalization ensures that the derived advantage reflects the relative efficiency of a specific action sequence, effectively decoupling the reward signal from the absolute duration of the task.
其中 ${P}_{t}$ 表示通过全局进度重构获得的进度值，${L}_{\text{ seq }}$ 代表当前片段的总长度，$\bar{L}$ 为整个数据集的平均片段长度。这种归一化确保了导出的优势反映了特定动作序列的相对效率，有效地将奖励信号与任务的绝对时长解耦。


#### 3.4.2. Statistical Weighting and Objective
#### 3.4.2. 统计加权与目标函数


To convert raw gains into robust training weights, we employ a statistical normalization strategy based on the gain distribution of the current batch. Let $\mu$ and $\sigma$ be the mean and standard deviation of $\left\{  {\Delta {G}_{i}}\right\}$ . We define clipping bounds as ${b}_{\text{ lower }} = \mu  - {2\sigma }$ and ${b}_{\text{ upper }} = \mu  + {2\sigma }$ . The importance weight ${\widetilde{w}}_{i}$ for each sample is computed as:
为了将原始增益转化为稳健的训练权重，我们采用了一种基于当前批次增益分布的统计归一化策略。设 $\mu$ 和 $\sigma$ 分别为 $\left\{  {\Delta {G}_{i}}\right\}$ 的均值和标准差。我们将截断边界定义为 ${b}_{\text{ lower }} = \mu  - {2\sigma }$ 和 ${b}_{\text{ upper }} = \mu  + {2\sigma }$。每个样本的重要性权重 ${\widetilde{w}}_{i}$ 计算如下：


$$
{\widetilde{w}}_{i} = \operatorname{clamp}\left( {\frac{\Delta {G}_{i} - {b}_{\text{ lower }}}{{b}_{\text{ upper }} - {b}_{\text{ lower }} + \epsilon },0,1}\right) \tag{5}
$$



This clamping mechanism effectively filters out regressive data (weights $\rightarrow  0$ ) while capping the influence of outliers. The final AW-BC objective is to minimize the weighted negative log-likelihood:
这种截断机制有效地过滤了回归数据（权重 $\rightarrow  0$ ），同时限制了异常值的影响。最终的 AW-BC 目标是最小化加权负对数似然：


$$
{\mathcal{L}}_{{AW} - {BC}}\left( \theta \right)  = {\mathbb{E}}_{\left( {s,a}\right)  \sim  \mathcal{D}}\left\lbrack  {-\widetilde{w}\left( {s,a}\right) \log {\pi }_{\theta }\left( {a \mid  s}\right) }\right\rbrack \tag{6}
$$



#### 3.4.3. Theoretical Connection to Offline RL
#### 3.4.3. 与离线强化学习的理论联系


Our proposed formulation aligns with the principles of AWR [28]. Mathematically, this optimization problem can be viewed as maximizing the expected return of the policy under the constraint of remaining close to the behavior policy:
我们提出的公式与 AWR [28] 的原理相一致。从数学上讲，该优化问题可被视为在保持接近行为策略的约束下，最大化策略的期望回报：


$$
\mathop{\max }\limits_{\theta }{\mathbb{E}}_{\left( {s,a}\right)  \sim  \mathcal{D}}\left\lbrack  {\widetilde{w}\left( {s,a}\right) \log {\pi }_{\theta }\left( {a \mid  s}\right) }\right\rbrack \tag{7}
$$



Here, ARM functions as a learned Critic, providing the advantage estimate $\Delta {G}_{t}$ that guides the policy update. By prioritizing transitions with high relative advantage, our method effectively performs offline policy improvement, extracting an optimal policy from suboptimal demonstrations without explicit online interaction.
在此，ARM 充当了学习到的评价器（Critic），提供指导策略更新的优势估计 $\Delta {G}_{t}$。通过优先处理具有高相对优势的转换，我们的方法有效地执行了离线策略改进，在无需显式在线交互的情况下，从次优演示中提取出最优策略。

如表 4 所示，我们的 ARM 推理速度达到每秒 14.1 次迭代（计算方式为 5 输出 MIMO 配置下的 ${2.82} \times  5$），这比基于 VLM 的标注（1.03 it/s）快 13.7 倍，比 SARM（3.9 it/s）快 3.6 倍。这种显著的效率提升源于 MIMO 架构能够在单次前向传播中同时处理多个优势预测，消除了序列处理方法中固有的计算冗余。


The efficiency advantage becomes particularly crucial during large-scale deployment, where the ARM model must process extensive trajectory datasets for reward signal generation. ## 5. Conclusion
## 5. 结论


We propose Advantage Reward Model (ARM), a framework that addresses the reward engineering bottleneck in long-horizon robotic manipulation tasks. By modeling relative advantages, ARM overcomes inconsistency and high costs of traditional dense labeling. We introduce a tristate labeling strategy that reduces cognitive load for annotators while providing high-fidelity supervision signals and enabling automated labeling. In a challenging towel-folding task, ARM with Advantage-Weighted Behavior Cloning achieves a 99.4% success rate, outperforming existing Vision-Language-Action baselines. ARM provides a scalable and robust solution for training high-performance policies.
我们提出了优势奖励模型（ARM），这是一个旨在解决长程机器人操作任务中奖励工程瓶颈的框架。通过对相对优势进行建模，ARM 克服了传统密集标注的不一致性与高成本问题。我们引入了一种三态标注策略，在降低标注者认知负荷的同时，提供了高保真的监督信号并实现了自动化标注。在极具挑战性的叠毛巾任务中，结合优势加权行为克隆的 ARM 达到了 99.4% 的成功率，优于现有的视觉-语言-动作基线。ARM 为训练高性能策略提供了一种可扩展且稳健的解决方案。

## E. Folding Precision Evaluation Protocol
## E. 折叠精度评估协议


We define a quantitative folding precision score ranging from 0 to 5 to evaluate the quality of towel folding results:
我们定义了一个 0 到 5 分的定量折叠精度评分，用于评估毛巾折叠结果的质量：


- 5 points: The folding task is fully completed, with a folding precision within 1 cm.
- 5 分：折叠任务完全完成，折叠精度在 1 cm 以内。


- 4 points: The folding task is fully completed, with a folding precision between 1 cm and 2 cm.
- 4 分：折叠任务完全完成，折叠精度在 1 cm 到 2 cm 之间。


- 3 points: The folding task is fully completed, with a folding precision between $2\mathrm{\;{cm}}$ and $3\mathrm{\;{cm}}$ .
- 3 分：折叠任务完全完成，折叠精度在 $2\mathrm{\;{cm}}$ 和 $3\mathrm{\;{cm}}$ 之间。


- 2 points: The towel is successfully flattened, but the final folding is not completed, though partial folding steps are finished.
- 2 分：毛巾已成功展平，但最终折叠未完成，不过已完成部分折叠步骤。


- 1 point: The towel is successfully flattened, but no valid folding steps are performed.
- 1 分：毛巾已成功展平，但未执行任何有效的折叠步骤。


- 0 points: No task steps are successfully completed.
- 0 分：没有任何任务步骤成功完成。
