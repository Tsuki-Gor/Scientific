## Abstract
## 摘要


Learning from experience is critical for building capable large language model (LLM) agents, yet prevailing self-evolving paradigms remain inefficient: agents learn in isolation, repeatedly rediscover similar behaviors from limited experience, resulting in redundant exploration and poor generalization. To address this problem, we propose Skill $\mathbf{X}$ ,a fully automated framework for constructing a plug-and-play skill knowledge base that can be reused across agents and environments. SkillX operates through a fully automated pipeline built on three synergistic innovations: (i) Multi-Level Skills Design, which distills raw trajectories into three-tiered hierarchy of strategic plans, functional skills, and atomic skills; (ii) Iterative Skills Refinement, which automatically revises skills based on execution feedback to continuously improve library quality; and (iii) Exploratory Skills Expansion, which proactively generates and validates novel skills to expand coverage beyond seed training data. Using a strong backbone agent (GLM- 4.6), we automatically build a reusable skill library and evaluate its transferability on challenging long-horizon, user-interactive benchmarks, including AppWorld, BFCL-v3, and ${\tau }^{2}$ -Bench. Experiments show that SkillKB consistently improves task success and execution efficiency when plugged into weaker base agents, highlighting the importance of structured, hierarchical experience representations for generalizable agent learning. Our code will be publicly available soon at https://github.com/zjunlp/Skillx.
从经验中学习对于构建强大的大语言模型（LLM）智能体至关重要，但当前主流的自我进化范式效率低下：智能体在孤立状态下学习，反复从有限的经验中重新发现相似的行为，导致了冗余的探索和较差的泛化能力。为解决这一问题，我们提出了 Skill $\mathbf{X}$，这是一个全自动框架，用于构建可跨智能体和环境复用的即插即用型技能知识库。SkillX 通过一个基于三项协同创新的全自动流水线运行：(i) 多级技能设计，将原始轨迹提炼为由战略规划、功能技能和原子技能组成的三层架构；(ii) 迭代技能优化，根据执行反馈自动修订技能，持续提升库质量；以及 (iii) 探索性技能扩展，主动生成并验证新技能，以扩大覆盖范围，超越种子训练数据。利用强大的骨干智能体（GLM-4.6），我们自动构建了一个可复用的技能库，并在具有挑战性的长程、用户交互基准测试（包括 AppWorld、BFCL-v3 和 ${\tau }^{2}$ -Bench）上评估了其可迁移性。实验表明，当接入较弱的基础智能体时，SkillKB 能持续提升任务成功率和执行效率，凸显了结构化、分层经验表示对于通用智能体学习的重要性。我们的代码即将开源至 https://github.com/zjunlp/Skillx。


## 1. Introduction
## 1. 引言


Large language model (LLM) based agents (OpenAI, 2025; DeepSeek-AI, 2025; Team et al., 2025b; Yang et al., 2025) have recently demonstrated remarkable progress in long-horizon decision making with tools, enabling complex behaviors such as API calling (Trivedi et al., 2024; Patil et al., 2025; Li et al., 2025), web navigation (Yao et al., 2023; Zhou et al., 2024; Mialon et al., 2023), scientific discovery (Ou et al., 2025; Liu et al., 2025; Qiao et al., 2025; Novikov et al., 2025), and interactive assistants (Barres et al., 2025; Yao et al., 2024; He et al., 2025). Despite these advances, most agents still approach each new task largely from scratch, relying on direct reasoning or limited task-specific demonstrations. This paradigm is costly, brittle, and fundamentally at odds with how intelligent systems are expected to accumulate and reuse experience over time.
基于大语言模型（LLM）的智能体（OpenAI, 2025; DeepSeek-AI, 2025; Team et al., 2025b; Yang et al., 2025）近期在长程工具决策方面展现出显著进展，实现了诸如 API 调用（Trivedi et al., 2024; Patil et al., 2025; Li et al., 2025）、网页导航（Yao et al., 2023; Zhou et al., 2024; Mialon et al., 2023）、科学发现（Ou et al., 2025; Liu et al., 2025; Qiao et al., 2025; Novikov et al., 2025）以及交互式助手（Barres et al., 2025; Yao et al., 2024; He et al., 2025）等复杂行为。尽管取得了这些进步，大多数智能体在处理每项新任务时仍主要从零开始，依赖直接推理或有限的任务特定演示。这种范式成本高昂、脆弱，且从根本上违背了智能系统应随时间积累并复用经验的预期。

A natural resolution is to enable agents to learn from experience (Sutton, 2025). Recent work has explored self-evolving agents that iteratively reflect on past executions and improve their behavior over time (Wang et al., 2025c; Fang et al., 2025c; Zhao et al., 2024; Xu et al., 2025; Cao et al., 2025). While promising, these approaches often fail to deliver scalable and transferable gains. In practice, experience learning typically suffers from three structural limitations. (1) Isolated Learning: agents execute the same tasks repeatedly and re-extract similar experiences independently, leading to substantial redundancy. (2) Weak Generalization of Experience: in complex environments, high-quality training data are scarce, so the mined experiences often transfer poorly to new tasks. (3) Model Capability Bottleneck: when experience is harvested solely through an agent's own exploration and reflection, what can be extracted is ultimately capped by the agent's current capability frontier. These challenges point to a more fundamental question: What form of experience can be broadly reusable across agents of varying capabilities and across diverse environments?
一个自然的解决方案是使智能体能够从经验中学习（Sutton, 2025）。近期研究探索了能够迭代反思过去执行情况并随时间改进行为的自我进化智能体（Wang et al., 2025c; Fang et al., 2025c; Zhao et al., 2024; Xu et al., 2025; Cao et al., 2025）。尽管前景广阔，但这些方法往往难以带来可扩展且可迁移的收益。在实践中，经验学习通常存在三个结构性局限：(1) 孤立学习：智能体重复执行相同任务并独立重新提取相似经验，导致大量冗余。(2) 经验泛化能力弱：在复杂环境中，高质量训练数据稀缺，因此挖掘出的经验往往难以迁移到新任务。(3) 模型能力瓶颈：当经验仅通过智能体自身的探索和反思获取时，所能提取的内容最终受限于智能体当前的能力边界。这些挑战指向了一个更根本的问题：什么样的经验形式能够跨越不同能力的智能体和多样化的环境实现广泛复用？

Existing work has proposed multiple representations of experience, such as insights (Cao et al., 2025; Ouyang et al., 2025), workflows (Wang et al., 2025c;b; Han et al., 2025), or trajectories (Zhao et al., 2024; Fang et al., 2025c). However, none of these representations simultaneously offer strong transferability, efficient retrieval, and direct executability. Inspired by Claude Skills (Anthropic, 2025), we argue that skills provide a more suitable abstraction: they encapsulate reusable competencies that directly support task execution. Nonetheless, prior skill-based designs often rely on long-context, progressive disclosure, which place heavy demands on reasoning and environment instrumentation, limiting robustness and practical reuse, as illustrated in Figure 1.
现有研究提出了多种经验表示方法，如洞察（Cao et al., 2025; Ouyang et al., 2025）、工作流（Wang et al., 2025c;b; Han et al., 2025）或轨迹（Zhao et al., 2024; Fang et al., 2025c）。然而，这些表示方法均无法同时兼顾强大的可迁移性、高效的检索能力和直接的可执行性。受 Claude Skills（Anthropic, 2025）启发，我们认为技能提供了一种更合适的抽象：它们封装了直接支持任务执行的可重用能力。尽管如此，先前的技能设计往往依赖于长上下文和渐进式披露，这对推理和环境配置提出了严苛要求，限制了其鲁棒性和实际重用性，如图1所示。


In this work, we introduce SkillX, a fully automated framework for constructing a plug-and-play skill knowledge base from agent experience. Our core insight is that transferable experience should be organized hierarchically, rather than as monolithic behaviors. SkillX therefore represents experience at three complementary levels: $\left( i\right)$ Planning Skills, which capture high-level task organization; (ii) Functional Skills, which implement reusable, tool-based subroutines; and (iii) Atomic Skills, which encode execution-oriented usage patterns and constraints. This multi-level design yields skills that are concise, composable, and robust to distributional shifts. SkillX builds such a skill library through a fully automated pipeline. A strong backbone agent first performs rollouts on training tasks and distills multi-level skills from successful trajectories. The extracted skills are then iteratively refined through consolidation and validation, improving library quality over time. Finally, SkillX performs experience-guided exploration to proactively expand the skill space by targeting under-utilized tools and failure-prone behaviors, enabling generalization beyond the initial training distribution.
本文中，我们引入了 SkillX，这是一个从智能体经验中构建即插即用技能知识库的全自动化框架。我们的核心见解是，可迁移的经验应分层组织，而非作为单一行为存在。因此，SkillX 在三个互补层面表示经验：$\left( i\right)$ 规划技能，捕捉高层任务组织；(ii) 功能技能，实现可重用的、基于工具的子程序；以及 (iii) 原子技能，编码面向执行的使用模式和约束。这种多层设计使得技能简洁、可组合且对分布偏移具有鲁棒性。SkillX 通过全自动流水线构建此类技能库。强大的骨干智能体首先在训练任务上进行演练，并从成功轨迹中提炼多层技能。随后，提取的技能通过整合与验证进行迭代优化，从而持续提升库质量。最后，SkillX 执行经验引导的探索，通过针对利用率不足的工具和易错行为主动扩展技能空间，实现超越初始训练分布的泛化。


To build a reliable, plug-and-play skill library, we instantiate SkillX with a strong agent backbone, GLM-4.6 (Team et al., 2025a), and pre-build a skill library on challenging, user-interactive, long-horizon benchmarks, including: Ap-pWorld (Trivedi et al., 2024), BFCL-v3 (Patil et al., 2025), and ${\tau }^{2}$ -Bench (Barres et al.,2025). Our experiments show that this plug-and-play skill library can be directly plugged into base agents (e.g., Qwen3-32B (Yang et al., 2025)), yielding around a 10% performance improvement while also improving execution efficiency. We further demonstrate the advantages of our multi-level skill design for experience representation, and show that both iterative refinement and skill expansion provide additional gains. In a nutshell, we conclude our contributions as:
为了构建可靠的即插即用技能库，我们利用强大的智能体骨干 GLM-4.6 (Team et al., 2025a) 实例化 SkillX，并在具有挑战性的用户交互式长程基准测试上预构建技能库，包括：AppWorld (Trivedi et al., 2024)、BFCL-v3 (Patil et al., 2025) 和 ${\tau }^{2}$ -Bench (Barres et al., 2025)。实验表明，该即插即用技能库可直接接入基础智能体（如 Qwen3-32B (Yang et al., 2025)），在提升执行效率的同时带来约 10% 的性能提升。我们进一步证明了多层技能设计在经验表示上的优势，并展示了迭代优化和技能扩展均能带来额外增益。简而言之，我们的贡献总结如下：


- We propose a hierarchical skill representation that transforms raw trajectories into reusable planning, functional, and atomic skills.
- 我们提出了一种分层技能表示方法，将原始轨迹转化为可重用的规划、功能和原子技能。


- We present SkillX, a fully automated and extensible framework for pre-building plug-and-play skill libraries for LLM agents, featuring iterative refinement and skill expansion.
- 我们提出了 SkillX，一个用于为 LLM 智能体预构建即插即用技能库的全自动且可扩展框架，具备迭代优化和技能扩展功能。


- We release the resulting plug-and-play skill library and provide strong empirical evidence across multiple agent benchmarks that it can directly enhance the capabilities of weaker agents.
- 我们发布了所得的即插即用技能库，并提供了强有力的实证证据，证明其在多个智能体基准测试中能直接增强较弱智能体的能力。


## 2. Preliminaries
## 2. 预备知识


Agent Definition We consider a general interactive setting where an agent solves tasks by acting in an environment. An environment is defined as $\mathcal{E} = \left( {\mathcal{S},\mathcal{A},\mathcal{P}}\right)$ ,where $\mathcal{A}$ is the set of executable actions, $\mathcal{S}$ is the set of observable states, and $\mathcal{P}\left( {{s}^{\prime } \mid  s,a}\right)$ is the transition dynamics. At time step $t$ , the agent receives an observation ${o}_{t} \in  \mathcal{O}$ and produces an action ${a}_{t} \in  \mathcal{A}$ . Following the ReAct style formulation,the agent therefore selects an action ${\widehat{a}}_{t} \in  \widehat{\mathcal{A}}$ conditioned on its context ${c}_{t} = \left( {{o}_{1},{\widehat{a}}_{1},\ldots ,{o}_{t - 1},{\widehat{a}}_{t - 1},{o}_{t}}\right)$ :
智能体定义 我们考虑一个通用的交互设置，其中智能体通过在环境中采取行动来解决任务。环境定义为 $\mathcal{E} = \left( {\mathcal{S},\mathcal{A},\mathcal{P}}\right)$，其中 $\mathcal{A}$ 是可执行动作集，$\mathcal{S}$ 是可观测状态集，$\mathcal{P}\left( {{s}^{\prime } \mid  s,a}\right)$ 是转移动态。在时间步 $t$，智能体接收观测 ${o}_{t} \in  \mathcal{O}$ 并产生动作 ${a}_{t} \in  \mathcal{A}$。遵循 ReAct 风格的公式，智能体根据其上下文 ${c}_{t} = \left( {{o}_{1},{\widehat{a}}_{1},\ldots ,{o}_{t - 1},{\widehat{a}}_{t - 1},{o}_{t}}\right)$ 选择动作 ${\widehat{a}}_{t} \in  \widehat{\mathcal{A}}$：


$$
{\widehat{a}}_{t} \sim  \pi \left( {\cdot  \mid  {c}_{t}}\right) ,\;{\widehat{a}}_{t} \in  \widehat{\mathcal{A}}. \tag{1}
$$



Executing ${\widehat{a}}_{t} \in  \mathcal{A}$ yields a new observation via the environment. The final trajectory is $\tau  = \left( {{o}_{1},{\widehat{a}}_{1},\ldots ,{o}_{T},{\widehat{a}}_{T}}\right)$ .
执行 ${\widehat{a}}_{t} \in  \mathcal{A}$ 会通过环境产生新的观测。最终轨迹为 $\tau  = \left( {{o}_{1},{\widehat{a}}_{1},\ldots ,{o}_{T},{\widehat{a}}_{T}}\right)$ 。


LLM Agent and Skill-Conditioned Execution. Let $\mathcal{Q}$ be the tasks set. We write $q \in  \mathcal{Q}$ for sampling a task,and let $R\left( {\tau ,q}\right)  \in  \{ 0,1\}$ be a task-dependent success indicator. We model the LLM agent as a policy $\pi$ that induces a trajectory distribution. Without external skills, the agent generates trajectories by direct reasoning:
LLM 智能体与技能条件化执行。设 $\mathcal{Q}$ 为任务集。我们用 $q \in  \mathcal{Q}$ 表示任务采样，并设 $R\left( {\tau ,q}\right)  \in  \{ 0,1\}$ 为任务相关的成功指标。我们将 LLM 智能体建模为诱导轨迹分布的策略 $\pi$ 。在没有外部技能的情况下，智能体通过直接推理生成轨迹：


$$
\tau  \sim  \pi \left( {\cdot  \mid  q}\right) ,\;q \in  \mathcal{Q}. \tag{2}
$$



To reduce redundant exploration and improve task completion,we equip the agent with a skills library $\mathcal{D} = \; \left\{  {{s}_{1},\ldots ,{s}_{\left| \mathcal{D}\right| }}\right\}$ and a skill retriever that recalls a set of relevant skills for the current task. Concretely,given $q \in  \mathcal{Q}$ , a retrieval function (typically implemented via semantic-similarity retrieval) $\rho  : \mathcal{Q} \rightarrow  {2}^{\mathcal{D}}$ . returns a skill subset ${\mathcal{S}}_{q} = \rho \left( q\right) ,{\mathcal{S}}_{q} \subseteq  \mathcal{D}$ . The LLM agent then generates a trajectory by conditioning on the retrieved skill set:
为了减少冗余探索并提高任务完成率，我们为智能体配备了技能库 $\mathcal{D} = \; \left\{  {{s}_{1},\ldots ,{s}_{\left| \mathcal{D}\right| }}\right\}$ 和一个技能检索器，用于为当前任务召回一组相关技能。具体而言，给定 $q \in  \mathcal{Q}$ ，检索函数（通常通过语义相似度检索实现） $\rho  : \mathcal{Q} \rightarrow  {2}^{\mathcal{D}}$ 返回一个技能子集 ${\mathcal{S}}_{q} = \rho \left( q\right) ,{\mathcal{S}}_{q} \subseteq  \mathcal{D}$ 。随后，LLM 智能体通过以检索到的技能集为条件来生成轨迹：


$$
{\tau }^{\prime } \sim  \pi \left( {\cdot  \mid  {\mathcal{S}}_{q},q}\right) ,\;q \in  \mathcal{Q}. \tag{3}
$$



Our objective is to design the skills library $\mathcal{D}$ and the usage within $\pi$ such that the expected success rate is improved:
我们的目标是设计技能库 $\mathcal{D}$ 及其在 $\pi$ 中的使用方式，以提高预期成功率：


$$
{\mathbb{E}}_{q \in  \mathcal{Q},{\tau }^{\prime } \sim  \pi \left( {\cdot  \mid  {\mathcal{S}}_{q},q}\right) }R\left( {{\tau }^{\prime },q}\right)  > {\mathbb{E}}_{q \in  \mathcal{Q},\tau  \sim  \pi \left( {\cdot  \mid  q}\right) }R\left( {\tau ,q}\right) . \tag{4}
$$



## 3. Skillx Design and Implementation
## 3. Skillx 设计与实现


### 3.1. Multi-Level Skills Design
### 3.1. 多级技能设计


In tool-centric agent scenarios, we structure the skills required by the model into three levels (see Figure 2):
在以工具为中心的智能体场景中，我们将模型所需的技能分为三个层级（见图 2）：


$$
\mathcal{D} = {S}_{\text{ plan }} \oplus  {S}_{\text{ func }} \oplus  {S}_{\text{ atomic }}, \tag{5}
$$



corresponding to planning skills, functional skills, and atomic skills,respectively. In a given environment $\mathcal{E}$ ,let $\mathcal{T}$ denote the set of tool actions. (i) Atomic skill ${s}_{\text{ atomic }}$ is aligned with a single tool $t \in  \mathcal{T}$ and is modeled as an extended semantic specification of $t$ ,e.g.,as enriched descriptions, constraints, or usage patterns that refine the effective behavior of $t$ . (ii) Functional skill ${s}_{\text{ func }}$ abstracts a subtask and can be regarded as a macro-operation that accomplishes a sub-query. We assume each task $q$ admits a decomposition into $n$ subtasks, $\left\{  {{q}_{\text{ subtask },1},{q}_{\text{ subtask },2},\ldots ,{q}_{\text{ subtask },n}}\right\}$ and each ${s}_{\text{ func }}$ corresponds to skills to accomplish ${q}_{\text{ subtask },i}$ . Specifically, ${s}_{\text{ func }}$ is grounded in a set of tool actions,which can be instantiated as a composition of tools ${\mathcal{T}}_{\text{ func }} \subseteq  \mathcal{T}$ . (iii) planning skill ${s}_{\text{ plan }}$ aligns with the organizational structure of the subtasks (e.g., ordering, dependencies, and branching), specifying how functional skills should be composed to solve $q$ . Next,we describe the extraction methods for the three skill levels.
分别对应规划技能、功能技能和原子技能。在给定环境 $\mathcal{E}$ 中，设 $\mathcal{T}$ 为工具动作集。(i) 原子技能 ${s}_{\text{ atomic }}$ 与单个工具 $t \in  \mathcal{T}$ 对齐，并被建模为 $t$ 的扩展语义规范，例如作为细化 $t$ 有效行为的增强描述、约束或使用模式。(ii) 功能技能 ${s}_{\text{ func }}$ 抽象了一个子任务，可被视为完成子查询的宏操作。我们假设每个任务 $q$ 都可以分解为 $n$ 个子任务 $\left\{  {{q}_{\text{ subtask },1},{q}_{\text{ subtask },2},\ldots ,{q}_{\text{ subtask },n}}\right\}$ ，且每个 ${s}_{\text{ func }}$ 对应于完成 ${q}_{\text{ subtask },i}$ 的技能。具体而言， ${s}_{\text{ func }}$ 基于一组工具动作，可实例化为工具 ${\mathcal{T}}_{\text{ func }} \subseteq  \mathcal{T}$ 的组合。(iii) 规划技能 ${s}_{\text{ plan }}$ 与子任务的组织结构（如排序、依赖关系和分支）对齐，指定了如何组合功能技能以解决 $q$ 。接下来，我们将描述这三个技能层级的提取方法。


### 3.2. Rollout and Skills Extraction
### 3.2. 运行与技能提取


Given a task $q$ ,we first perform $m$ -sized rollouts,reusing the agent's inference procedure to collect trajectories. We then extract the multi-level skills from these trajectories, with skill extractor $f$ . Details of the inference procedure are provided in Section 4.
给定任务 $q$ ，我们首先执行 $m$ 次运行，复用智能体的推理过程以收集轨迹。然后，我们利用技能提取器 $f$ 从这些轨迹中提取多级技能。推理过程的详细信息见第 4 节。


Planning Skills Extraction. Given a successful trajectory, we extract the planning skill ${s}_{\text{ plan }}$ by compressing the trajectory into an ordered set of high-level steps. During this compression, we explicitly filter out non-essential transitions such as exploration, backtracking, and trial-and-error behaviors that are incidental to the final solution but detrimental to skill reuse. Moreover, for excessively long or verbose environment feedback, we apply summarization to obtain compact state descriptions, which improves the stability and fidelity of the extracted high-level skills.
规划技能提取。给定一条成功轨迹，我们通过将轨迹压缩为有序的高级步骤集来提取规划技能 ${s}_{\text{ plan }}$ 。在压缩过程中，我们显式过滤掉非必要的转换，例如对最终解决方案而言是偶然的、但对技能复用有害的探索、回溯和试错行为。此外，对于过长或冗余的环境反馈，我们应用摘要技术以获得紧凑的状态描述，从而提高所提取高级技能的稳定性和保真度。


Functional Skills Extraction. We leverage the previously extracted planning skill ${s}_{\text{ plan }}$ to guide the extraction of functional skills. Concretely, given a plan and its corresponding trajectory, we iteratively prompt the model to extract the functional skill ${s}_{\text{ func }}$ that aligns with the objective of each subtask ${q}_{\text{ subtask },i}$ . Formally,each ${s}_{\text{ func }}$ is represented with three key fields: name (the skill name), document (a description of inputs, outputs and usage notes), and content (the tool invocation pattern for completing subtask ${q}_{\text{ subtask },i}$ ).
功能技能提取。我们利用先前提取的规划技能 ${s}_{\text{ plan }}$ 来指导功能技能的提取。具体而言，给定一个计划及其对应的轨迹，我们迭代提示模型提取与每个子任务 ${q}_{\text{ subtask },i}$ 目标相一致的功能技能 ${s}_{\text{ func }}$。形式上，每个 ${s}_{\text{ func }}$ 由三个关键字段表示：名称（技能名称）、文档（关于输入、输出和使用说明的描述）以及内容（完成子任务 ${q}_{\text{ subtask },i}$ 的工具调用模式）。


Atomic Skills Extraction. Atomic skills are single tool specifications that extend the original tool schema with reusable, execution-oriented usage patterns. They serve as a low-level complement when higher-level functional skills ${s}_{\text{ func }}$ are missing or incomplete. We prompt the model to distill ${s}_{\text{ atomic }}$ from trajectories the invocation patterns, typical parameter configurations, and practical notes, especially constraints and common failure modes observed in real usage. The representation of ${s}_{\text{ atomic }}$ is unified with ${s}_{\text{ func }}$ .
原子技能提取。原子技能是单一的工具规范，通过可重用的、面向执行的使用模式扩展了原始工具架构。当更高级的功能技能 ${s}_{\text{ func }}$ 缺失或不完整时，它们可作为低级补充。我们提示模型从轨迹中提炼 ${s}_{\text{ atomic }}$ 的调用模式、典型参数配置以及实用说明，特别是实际使用中观察到的约束条件和常见故障模式。${s}_{\text{ atomic }}$ 的表示形式与 ${s}_{\text{ func }}$ 统一。


### 3.3. Iterative Skills Refinement
### 3.3. 迭代技能优化


With only a limited amount of seed training data, a key question is whether we can maximize the utility of the available supervision to extract additional skills and continuously improve existing ones. Inspired by prior works (Cai et al., 2025b;a; Yuksekgonul et al., 2024), we adopt a text-based iterative optimization paradigm for the skill library. Concretely,at $k$ -th iteration,we start from the current skill library ${\mathcal{D}}^{\left( k\right) }$ ,repeatedly rollouts from the training set,then extract multi-level skills. We subsequently apply a refinement operator $\phi$ ,including: Skills Merge and Skills Filter. Finally,we update the skill library ${\mathcal{D}}^{\left( k\right) }$ with the refined skills to obtain skill library ${\mathcal{D}}^{\left( k + 1\right) }$ ,including three update operations: add, modify or keep.
在仅有少量种子训练数据的情况下，一个关键问题是我们能否最大化现有监督信息的效用，以提取更多技能并持续改进现有技能。受前人工作（Cai et al., 2025b;a; Yuksekgonul et al., 2024）启发，我们为技能库采用了一种基于文本的迭代优化范式。具体而言，在第 $k$ 次迭代中，我们从当前技能库 ${\mathcal{D}}^{\left( k\right) }$ 出发，在训练集上重复进行推演，然后提取多级技能。随后，我们应用优化算子 $\phi$，包括：技能合并与技能过滤。最后，我们用优化后的技能更新技能库 ${\mathcal{D}}^{\left( k\right) }$ 以获得技能库 ${\mathcal{D}}^{\left( k + 1\right) }$，包括添加、修改或保留这三种更新操作。

Skills Merge. After extracting skills from each trajectory, we often obtain many functionally redundant skills that, despite surface differences, correspond to the same underlying skill pattern. How to update a single skill when multiple heterogeneous update directions are available? We merge skills from an optimization-based perspective. For a specific skill $s$ with current embedding,we first retrieve and cluster a set of semantically similar skills using cosine similarity. The resulting cluster can be interpreted as providing multiple complementary update directions for the same underlying skill,a multi-dimensional refinement of $s$ . Let $\mathcal{Z}\left( s\right)  = \{ 1,\ldots ,z\}$ index the semantically similar skills associated with skill $s$ . Each neighbor $i$ induces a candidate update direction ${\delta }_{i}$ ,yielding a candidate updated state
技能合并。在从每条轨迹中提取技能后，我们往往会获得许多功能冗余的技能，它们虽然表面不同，但对应着相同的底层技能模式。当存在多个异构更新方向时，如何更新单个技能？我们从基于优化的角度合并技能。对于具有当前嵌入的特定技能 $s$，我们首先使用余弦相似度检索并聚类一组语义相似的技能。所得聚类可被视为为同一底层技能提供了多个互补的更新方向，即 $s$ 的多维细化。令 $\mathcal{Z}\left( s\right)  = \{ 1,\ldots ,z\}$ 为与技能 $s$ 相关的语义相似技能的索引。每个邻居 $i$ 都会诱导出一个候选更新方向 ${\delta }_{i}$，从而产生一个候选更新状态


$$
{s}_{i}^{\prime } = s + {\delta }_{i},\;i \in  \mathcal{Z}\left( s\right) . \tag{9}
$$



We then aggregate these candidate directions into the final direction. The simplest form is to merge the directions: ${\delta }_{\text{ agg }} = \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i \in  \mathcal{Z}\left( s\right) }}{\delta }_{i}$ . The final update is applied as
然后，我们将这些候选方向聚合为最终方向。最简单的形式是合并这些方向：${\delta }_{\text{ agg }} = \mathop{\sum }\limits_{{i \in  \mathcal{Z}\left( s\right) }}{\delta }_{i}$。最终更新应用为


$$
{s}^{ + } = s + {\delta }_{\text{ agg }}. \tag{10}
$$



Specifically, we treat the semantically similar skills as multiple update views of the same skill, and we use the combined direction as the final update direction. Finally, we merge semantically similar skills into a single skill. If the merged skill becomes overly complex, we further decompose it into more modular, reusable skills.
具体而言，我们将语义相似的技能视为同一技能的多个更新视图，并使用组合方向作为最终更新方向。最后，我们将语义相似的技能合并为单个技能。如果合并后的技能变得过于复杂，我们会进一步将其分解为更模块化、可重用的技能。


Skills Filter. We enforce skill quality via a strict two-stage filtering procedure. (1) General Filter. This stage removes skills that are unlikely to be portable or compositional, including those that depend on extraneous Python packages, expose overly idiosyncratic function-style definitions, or overly-encapsulated skills. (2) Tool-specific Filter: This stage mitigates tool-use hallucinations by validating each skill against the environment-provided tool schema, rejecting skills that reference non-existent tools, invalid parameters, or schema-incompatible argument structures. Together, these filters maintain a high-precision skill library while preserving flexibility across heterogeneous agent benchmarks.
技能过滤。我们通过严格的两阶段过滤程序来确保技能质量。(1) 通用过滤：此阶段移除那些不太可能具有可移植性或组合性的技能，包括那些依赖无关 Python 包、暴露过于特殊的函数式定义或过度封装的技能。(2) 工具特定过滤：此阶段通过根据环境提供的工具模式验证每个技能来减轻工具使用幻觉，拒绝引用不存在的工具、无效参数或与模式不兼容的参数结构的技能。这些过滤器共同维护了一个高精度的技能库，同时保持了跨异构智能体基准测试的灵活性。


Skills Library Update. After completing Skill Merge and Skill Filter, we perform concrete updates to the skill library ${\mathcal{D}}^{k}$ for the $k$ -th iteration,including three types: add new skills, modify existing skills, and keep skills unchanged. Furthermore, the entire pipeline can be executed iteratively over multiple rounds. Through this continual update process, the skill library progressively improves in coverage, quality, and compositional richness, enabling increasingly effective skill reuse for downstream agent tasks.
技能库更新。在完成技能合并和技能过滤后，我们对第 $k$ 次迭代的技能库 ${\mathcal{D}}^{k}$ 进行具体更新，包括三种类型：添加新技能、修改现有技能以及保持技能不变。此外，整个流程可以在多轮中迭代执行。通过这种持续更新过程，技能库在覆盖范围、质量和组合丰富度上不断提升，从而为下游智能体任务实现更有效的技能重用。


### 3.4. Exploratory Skills Expansion
### 3.4. 探索性技能扩展


While skills distilled from a seed training set ${\mathcal{Q}}_{\text{ train }}$ can already improve an agent's performance, relying solely on scarce demonstrations is insufficient in complex environments with large tool spaces (e.g., (Trivedi et al., 2024) exposes hundreds of APIs). Inspired by Zhai et al. (2025), we adopt an Experience Guiding Exploration scheme to broaden coverage beyond what is observed in the seed data, encouraging the agent to interact with the environment and exercise a wider range of tools. We guide exploration using experience collected from rollouts on the seed set (e.g., tools the agent already uses reliably, tools with high failure rates, and tools that are never invoked), thereby prioritizing under-explored or failure-prone tools to improve sample efficiency. After collecting exploratory trajectories, we synthesize new tasks ${\mathcal{Q}}_{\text{ syn }}$ from these interactions,and then rerun our skill acquisition and refinement pipeline on the resulting data to iteratively expand the skill library. Compared to the random exploration strategy (Zhai et al., 2025), our approach discovers a more diverse set of skills.
虽然从种子训练集 ${\mathcal{Q}}_{\text{ train }}$ 中提炼出的技能已经可以提高智能体的性能，但在具有庞大工具空间的复杂环境中（例如，(Trivedi et al., 2024) 暴露了数百个 API），仅依赖稀缺的演示是不够的。受 Zhai et al. (2025) 的启发，我们采用“经验引导探索”方案来扩大覆盖范围，使其超越种子数据中的观察结果，鼓励智能体与环境交互并使用更广泛的工具。我们利用从种子集上的执行中收集的经验（例如，智能体已经可靠使用的工具、失败率高的工具以及从未被调用的工具）来引导探索，从而优先考虑未充分探索或易出错的工具，以提高样本效率。在收集探索性轨迹后，我们从这些交互中合成新任务 ${\mathcal{Q}}_{\text{ syn }}$，然后对所得数据重新运行我们的技能获取和细化流程，以迭代方式扩展技能库。与随机探索策略 (Zhai et al., 2025) 相比，我们的方法发现了更多样化的技能。


## 4. Skillx Usage
## 4. Skillx 使用


Planning Skills Retrieval and Pseudo-Plan Rewriting. For a novel and complex agent task $q$ ,directly retrieving past experiences based solely on task similarity may lead to a mismatch between retrieved experiences and the actual execution trajectory. This issue becomes particularly pronounced in environments where execution dynamics are strongly influenced by user profiles, contextual constraints, or other external factors. To improve retrieval relevance, inspired by (Gao et al., 2022), we first retrieve high-level planning skills associated with similar tasks $\mathcal{P}\left( q\right)  = \rho \left( q\right)$ , where $\rho$ is a similarity retrieval function and $\mathcal{P}\left( q\right)$ is the retrieved planning skills. Then we prompt the model to self-rewrite a task-specific pseudo-plan conditioned on the current task $\widetilde{p}\left( q\right)  = {\operatorname{LLM}}_{\text{ rewrite }}\left( {q,\mathcal{P}\left( q\right) }\right)$ . This rewritten pseudo-plan serves as an intermediate retrieval query to better align subsequent skill retrieval with the current execution setting. To mitigate hallucination risks and prevent speculative content from affecting agent behavior, the pseudo-plan is not injected into the final system prompt.
规划技能检索与伪计划重写。对于一项新颖且复杂的智能体任务 $q$，仅基于任务相似度检索过往经验可能会导致检索到的经验与实际执行轨迹不匹配。在执行动态受用户画像、上下文约束或其他外部因素强烈影响的环境中，这一问题尤为突出。为了提高检索相关性，受 (Gao et al., 2022) 启发，我们首先检索与相似任务 $\mathcal{P}\left( q\right)  = \rho \left( q\right)$ 相关的顶层规划技能，其中 $\rho$ 是相似度检索函数，$\mathcal{P}\left( q\right)$ 是检索到的规划技能。随后，我们提示模型根据当前任务 $\widetilde{p}\left( q\right)  = {\operatorname{LLM}}_{\text{ rewrite }}\left( {q,\mathcal{P}\left( q\right) }\right)$ 自行重写一个特定于任务的伪计划。该重写的伪计划作为中间检索查询，能更好地使后续的技能检索与当前执行环境对齐。为降低幻觉风险并防止推测性内容影响智能体行为，伪计划不会被注入到最终的系统提示词中。


Functional and Atomic Skills Retrieve. Given the rewritten pseudo-plan $\widetilde{p}\left( q\right)  = \left\{  {{\operatorname{step}}_{1},{\operatorname{step}}_{2},\ldots ,{\operatorname{step}}_{p}}\right\}$ ,we treat each step as a retrieval query to retrieve functional and atomic skills. For ${\operatorname{step}}_{i}$ ,we first retrieve relevant skills ${\mathcal{S}}_{i} = \rho \left( {\text{ step }}_{i}\right)$ and then remove duplicates across steps, ${\mathcal{S}}^{\prime } = \operatorname{dedup}\left( {\mathop{\bigcup }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{p}{\mathcal{S}}_{i}}\right)$ . To keep the context concise and task-relevant, we further ask the LLM to self-filter the retrieved candidates and retain only applicable skills ${\mathcal{S}}_{q} = \operatorname{LLM}\_ \operatorname{select}\left( {q,\widetilde{p}\left( q\right) ,{\mathcal{S}}^{\prime }}\right)$ ,where ${\mathcal{S}}_{q}$ is the final skill set used for solving the query $q$ .
功能与原子技能检索。给定重写后的伪计划 $\widetilde{p}\left( q\right)  = \left\{  {{\operatorname{step}}_{1},{\operatorname{step}}_{2},\ldots ,{\operatorname{step}}_{p}}\right\}$，我们将每一步视为一个检索查询，以检索功能技能和原子技能。对于 ${\operatorname{step}}_{i}$，我们首先检索相关技能 ${\mathcal{S}}_{i} = \rho \left( {\text{ step }}_{i}\right)$，然后去除各步骤间的重复项 ${\mathcal{S}}^{\prime } = \operatorname{dedup}\left( {\mathop{\bigcup }\limits_{{i = 1}}^{p}{\mathcal{S}}_{i}}\right)$。为了保持上下文简洁且与任务相关，我们进一步要求大模型对检索到的候选技能进行自筛选，仅保留适用的技能 ${\mathcal{S}}_{q} = \operatorname{LLM}\_ \operatorname{select}\left( {q,\widetilde{p}\left( q\right) ,{\mathcal{S}}^{\prime }}\right)$，其中 ${\mathcal{S}}_{q}$ 是用于解决查询 $q$ 的最终技能集。

### 5.1. Experimental Settings

Benchmarks and Metrics. We conduct the evaluation on complex, long-horizon, user-interactive agent benchmarks, including BFCL-v3 (Patil et al., 2025), AppWorld (Trivedi et al., 2024), and ${\tau }^{2}$ -bench (Barres et al., 2025). For BFCL-v3, we use the base multi-turn category and randomly split it into 50 training instances and 150 test instances. AppWorld provides 90 training instances and the Test Normal category as test set. ${\tau }^{2}$ -bench defines training and test splits for each sub-domain. Additional details are provided in the Appendix A.1. For AppWorld and BFCL-v3, we report Avg@4 and Pass@4, the average success rate over four independent runs and the probability of succeeding at least once across four runs, respectively. Following the (Barres et al., 2025) evaluation setup, we report Pass^1, the pass rate over running four times.
基准测试与指标。我们在复杂的长程用户交互智能体基准上进行评估，包括 BFCL-v3 (Patil et al., 2025)、AppWorld (Trivedi et al., 2024) 和 ${\tau }^{2}$ -bench (Barres et al., 2025)。对于 BFCL-v3，我们使用基础多轮对话类别，并将其随机划分为 50 个训练实例和 150 个测试实例。AppWorld 提供 90 个训练实例，并将 Test Normal 类别作为测试集。${\tau }^{2}$ -bench 为每个子领域定义了训练集和测试集划分。更多细节请参阅附录 A.1。对于 AppWorld 和 BFCL-v3，我们分别报告 Avg@4 和 Pass@4，即四次独立运行的平均成功率，以及四次运行中至少成功一次的概率。遵循 (Barres et al., 2025) 的评估设置，我们报告 Pass^1，即运行四次的通过率。


Models and Baselines. To assess the effectiveness of SkillX, we evaluate three Agentic base models that vary in model size and reasoning style (thinking and nonthinking), including Qwen3-32B (Yang et al., 2025), Kimi-K2-Instruct-0905 (Team et al., 2025b), and GLM-4.6 (Team et al., 2025a). Among them, GLM-4.6 has been reported to exhibit strong native agentic capabilities in agent mid-training, serving as a competitive backbone for our study.
模型与基线。为了评估 SkillX 的有效性，我们评估了三种在模型规模和推理风格（思考型与非思考型）上各异的智能体基础模型，包括 Qwen3-32B (Yang et al., 2025)、Kimi-K2-Instruct-0905 (Team et al., 2025b) 和 GLM-4.6 (Team et al., 2025a)。其中，据报道 GLM-4.6 在智能体中间训练阶段展现出强大的原生智能体能力，可作为我们研究的有力骨干模型。


We compare against four representative baselines: (1) No-memory, which performs inference without retrieving any prior experience; (2) A-Mem (Xu et al., 2025), a system that dynamically manages structured episodic memories; (3) AWM (Wang et al., 2025c), which reuses modular work-flows distilled from historical trajectories; and (4) ExpeL (Zhao et al., 2024), which retrieves relevant past trajectories as few-shot demonstrations and incorporates distilled insights to improve LLM performance. For a fair comparison, all methods retrieve experience only based on the user's initial query and insert the retrieved content into the system prompt following a unified protocol. Full baseline details are provided in the Appendix A.2.
我们与四个代表性基线进行了对比：(1) No-memory，即在不检索任何先验经验的情况下进行推理；(2) A-Mem (Xu et al., 2025)，一种动态管理结构化情景记忆的系统；(3) AWM (Wang et al., 2025c)，它重用从历史轨迹中提炼出的模块化工作流；以及 (4) ExpeL (Zhao et al., 2024)，它检索相关的过往轨迹作为少样本示例，并结合提炼出的见解来提升大语言模型的性能。为确保公平比较，所有方法均仅根据用户的初始查询检索经验，并遵循统一协议将检索到的内容插入到系统提示词中。完整的基线详情请参阅附录 A.2。


Implementation Details. To construct SkillX, we use GLM-4.6 (Team et al., 2025a) independently rollouts four times per training task, followed by skill extraction, skill refinement, and skill expansion. The maximum number of refinement iterations is set to 3 . For efficiency, we limit environment exploration to one rollout per training task; the sampling temperature is 1.0 during exploration. We use Qwen3-Embedding-8B (Zhang et al., 2025d) for both skill deduplication and skill retrieval, with a minimum cosine similarity threshold of 0.45 for retrieval. During solving new tasks, we use the same model for both Pseudo-Plan rewriting and action execution. For the other baselines, we evaluate two settings: (1) Distillation paradigm: a strong agent (GLM-4.6) is used to extract experiences to build an experience repository, and the execution model then performs inference; (2) Self-evolution paradigm: the experience extraction model is kept consistent to the execution model to enable self-extraction, following the original experimental protocol of each method. Additional implementation details are provided in the Appendix A.3.
实现细节。为构建 SkillX，我们使用 GLM-4.6 (Team et al., 2025a) 对每个训练任务独立进行四次执行，随后进行技能提取、技能精炼和技能扩展。精炼迭代的最大次数设为 3。为保证效率，我们将环境探索限制为每个训练任务一次执行；探索时的采样温度为 1.0。我们使用 Qwen3-Embedding-8B (Zhang et al., 2025d) 进行技能去重和技能检索，检索的最小余弦相似度阈值为 0.45。在解决新任务时，我们使用同一模型进行伪计划重写和动作执行。对于其他基线方法，我们评估了两种设置：(1) 蒸馏范式：使用强智能体 (GLM-4.6) 提取经验以构建经验库，随后由执行模型进行推理；(2) 自进化范式：保持经验提取模型与执行模型一致以实现自提取，遵循各方法原始的实验方案。更多实现细节请参阅附录 A.3。

### 5.2. 主要结果


SkillX Boost Agentic Performance of Base LLMs. As shown in Table 1, Skill X improves the base model's performance. In particular, Qwen3-32B gains roughly around 10 points across multiple benchmarks. For K2 (Kimi-K2- Instruct-0905), we observe a clear improvement on App-World, whereas the gains are modest on the other two tool call intensive benchmarks. We infer this is because K2 relies more heavily on the original tool schema and does not effectively leverage the additional contextual information.
SkillX 提升了基础大模型的智能体性能。如表 1 所示，SkillX 提升了基础模型的表现。特别是 Qwen3-32B 在多个基准测试中均获得了约 10 个点的提升。对于 K2 (Kimi-K2-Instruct-0905)，我们在 App-World 上观察到了明显的改进，但在其他两个工具调用密集的基准测试中增幅较小。我们推断这是因为 K2 更依赖原始工具模式，未能有效利用额外的上下文信息。


Multi-Level Skills Design Outperform Other Forms of Experience Representation. When the experience extraction model is aligned with the execution model, SkillX consistently outperforms all baseline methods, as indicated by the methods with * in Table 1. Among them, ExpeL retrieves past trajectories and uses them as few-shot demonstrations, which provides a more direct performance gain than the other baselines. However, the agent capability required for multi-level skill decoupling offers a more advantageous form of experience representation.
多级技能设计优于其他形式的经验表示。当经验提取模型与执行模型对齐时，SkillX 的表现始终优于所有基线方法，如表 1 中带 * 的方法所示。其中，ExpeL 通过检索历史轨迹并将其作为少样本示例，提供了比其他基线更直接的性能增益。然而，多级技能解耦所需的智能体能力提供了一种更具优势的经验表示形式。


Suboptimal Experience Representations Hinder Transfer Performance. We further evaluate the GLM-4.6 extracted experience with AWM and ExpeL on the weaker models,see the results of methods with $\ddagger$ in Table 1. However, the performance still lagged behind that of SkillX. This indicates that distilling experience from a strong model is effective, but the form of experience representation is even more critical. Consequently, suboptimal experience representation can hinder effective experience transfer. These results further demonstrate the advantage of SkillX in transferring experience across base models.
次优的经验表示会阻碍迁移性能。我们进一步评估了使用 AWM 和 ExpeL 在较弱模型上提取的 GLM-4.6 经验，结果见表 1 中带 $\ddagger$ 的方法。然而，其性能仍落后于 SkillX。这表明从强模型中提炼经验是有效的，但经验表示的形式更为关键。因此，次优的经验表示会阻碍有效的经验迁移。这些结果进一步证明了 SkillX 在跨基础模型迁移经验方面的优势。


## SkillX can Expand Base Model's Capability Boundary.
## SkillX 可以扩展基础模型的能力边界。


We observe that experience-based learning leads to substantial Pass@4 improvements for the weaker models, K2 and Qwen3-32B. This suggests that, in practice, the most direct way to extend the capability boundary of a base model is to distill knowledge from a stronger model (Yue et al., 2025). In contrast, for the stronger model GLM-4.6, neither the baseline nor SkillX yields a significant gain in Pass@4.This indicates that stronger models already possess robust capabilities in exploration, planning, and tool use, leaving limited headroom for further capability expansion via experience-based augmentation. Nevertheless, the modest improvements still support the effectiveness of SkillX.
我们观察到，基于经验的学习使较弱模型 K2 和 Qwen3-32B 的 Pass@4 得到了显著提升。这表明在实践中，扩展基础模型能力边界最直接的方法是从更强的模型中提炼知识 (Yue et al., 2025)。相比之下，对于较强模型 GLM-4.6，基线方法和 SkillX 均未在 Pass@4 上产生显著增益。这表明较强模型在探索、规划和工具使用方面已具备稳健能力，通过基于经验的增强进一步扩展能力的空间有限。尽管如此，小幅度的提升仍支持了 SkillX 的有效性。

### 5.3. Analysis
### 5.3. 分析


Which skill is more effective? We analyze the behaviors of our multi-level skill across models on AppWorld, and the results are shown in Figure 3 (a) and Figure 3 (b). (i) Planning skills consistently reduce the number of execution steps across all models, with particularly pronounced gains for weaker models such as Qwen3-32B and K2, especially when combined with Functional Skills. We attribute this to their limited exploration capability in complex environments. Notably, for Qwen3-32B, adding Functional and Atomic Skills can even hurt performance, as the model tends to over-imitate retrieved skills rather than adapt them to novel tasks. For stronger models, pseudo-planning may fail to faithfully capture underlying environment dynamics in complex scenarios, and can therefore become counterproductive. (ii) Functional skills contribute the most to overall performance improvements: equipping K2 and GLM-4.6 with Functional and Atomic Skills alone already yields observable gains, highlighting the advantage of skills as an effective representation of experience. (iii) Atomic skills provide crucial clarifications for key APIs. When they are absent, performance drops substantially, further validating the need to supplement tool schemas and to cover tools missing from Functional Skills. Finally, we find that GLM-4.6 benefits the most from using all skill types; K2 performs best with Functional + Atomic Skills; and Qwen3-32B achieves its best performance when only Planning Skills are enabled. This further demonstrates that multi-level skills can comprehensively cover the capabilities required for diverse models to execute agent tasks.
哪种技能更有效？我们分析了多级技能在 AppWorld 中各模型上的行为，结果如图 3 (a) 和图 3 (b) 所示。(i) 规划技能在所有模型中均能持续减少执行步骤，对于 Qwen3-32B 和 K2 等较弱模型，尤其是在与功能技能结合时，增益尤为显著。我们将此归因于它们在复杂环境中有限的探索能力。值得注意的是，对于 Qwen3-32B，添加功能技能和原子技能甚至可能损害性能，因为模型倾向于过度模仿检索到的技能，而非将其适配到新任务中。对于较强模型，伪规划可能无法在复杂场景中忠实捕捉潜在的环境动态，从而产生反作用。(ii) 功能技能对整体性能提升贡献最大：仅为 K2 和 GLM-4.6 配备功能技能和原子技能就已经产生了可观的增益，凸显了技能作为一种有效经验表示的优势。(iii) 原子技能为关键 API 提供了必要的澄清。当缺失这些技能时，性能会大幅下降，这进一步验证了补充工具模式以及覆盖功能技能中缺失工具的必要性。最后，我们发现 GLM-4.6 从使用所有技能类型中获益最多；K2 在功能技能 + 原子技能组合下表现最佳；而 Qwen3-32B 在仅启用规划技能时达到最佳性能。这进一步证明了多级技能可以全面覆盖不同模型执行智能体任务所需的能力。


Iterative Refinement Strategies Further Enhances SkillX Performance. We evaluate effectiveness of multi-round iterative refinement for the skill library of SkillX on AppWorld (Figure 3 (c)). Overall, multiple iterations further improve performance on both training and test sets. Leveraging existing training data, the process continually improves various aspects of skills, such as documentation and content. Besides, it can slightly expand the size of the skill library (Figure 3 (d)). However, when training data are limited, text-only optimization can lead to overfitting. Thus, selecting an appropriate number of update rounds is crucial to obtain a higher-quality skill library.
迭代优化策略进一步提升了 SkillX 的性能。我们在 AppWorld 上评估了 SkillX 技能库多轮迭代优化的有效性（图 3 (c)）。总体而言，多轮迭代进一步提升了训练集和测试集的表现。利用现有训练数据，该过程持续改进了技能的文档和内容等多个方面。此外，它还能略微扩大技能库的规模（图 3 (d)）。然而，当训练数据有限时，仅基于文本的优化可能导致过拟合。因此，选择合适的更新轮数对于获得高质量技能库至关重要。


Skill Expansion Strategies Improve Generalization. We compare two skill expansion strategies: random exploration and experience-guided expansion. The results are as shown in Figure 3 (d). In terms of skill growth, the experience-guided strategy yields substantially more novel skills, as random exploration treats past executions in isolation and repeatedly rediscovers already identified skills. Empirically, the experience guided strategy yields performance improvement through skill expansion. Overall, our results indicate that in complex environments, particularly under scarce training data, skill expansion is a crucial component of experience learning.
技能扩展策略提升了泛化能力。我们比较了两种技能扩展策略：随机探索和经验引导扩展。结果如图 3 (d) 所示。在技能增长方面，经验引导策略产生了更多新颖技能，而随机探索将过去的执行视为孤立事件，导致重复发现已有的技能。实验证明，经验引导策略通过技能扩展带来了性能提升。总体而言，我们的结果表明，在复杂环境中，特别是在训练数据稀缺的情况下，技能扩展是经验学习的关键组成部分。


Skillx Enhances Agent Execution Efficiency. Learning from experience not only improves the performance of the base model, but also enhances the execution efficiency of the agent. Our experiments further corroborate this effect (see Figure 3 (e) and Figure 3 (f)). Although we do not achieve the minimum number of execution steps or the fewest input tokens, we obtain the best overall performance (see Table 1). These results further highlight the advantages of our multi-level skill design and skills library construction.
SkillX 提升了智能体的执行效率。从经验中学习不仅提高了基座模型的性能，还增强了智能体的执行效率。我们的实验进一步证实了这一效果（见图 3 (e) 和图 3 (f)）。尽管我们并未达到最少的执行步数或最少的输入 Token 数，但我们获得了最佳的整体性能（见表 1）。这些结果进一步凸显了我们多层级技能设计和技能库构建的优势。

## 7. Related Work
## 7. 相关工作


Encoding For Agent Experience. With the advent of the experience era (Sutton, 2025), agents can achieve self-evolving (Gao et al., 2025; Fang et al., 2025b; Xia et al., 2026) by encoding past experience and reusing it in context (Dou et al., 2026) to guide future behavior. Existing approaches to text token-level experience encoding (Zhang et al., 2025b; Hu et al., 2025) can be broadly grouped into three categories: (i) Case-based Experience: Agents directly store successful task-execution trajectories and retrieve them later as few-shot examples to new problem solving (Zhao et al., 2024; Zheng et al., 2024; Zhou et al., 2025). (ii) Strategy-based Experience: By summarizing and contrasting successful versus failed trajectories, agents distill higher-level insights or workflows (Cao et al., 2025; Ouyang et al., 2025; Cai et al., 2025a; Wang et al., 2025c; Tang et al., 2025; Zhang et al., 2025a). (iii) Skill-based Experience: Trajectories are segmented and distilled into modular, reusable skills, such as textual skills or programmatic skills (Wang et al., 2025b;a; 2024; Fang et al., 2025c; Han et al., 2025; Chen et al., 2026; Zheng et al., 2026; Wang et al., 2026a; Zhou et al., 2026a; Zhang et al., 2026b; Ni et al., 2026; Zhou et al., 2026b). However, it remains unclear which unified experience representation is both easily pluggable and consistently effective, especially in diverse and complex agentic tool-use scenarios (Trivedi et al., 2024; Yao et al., 2024; Patil et al., 2025; Barres et al., 2025; He et al., 2025; Li et al., 2025; Zheng et al., 2025; Jiang et al., 2026; Xing et al., 2026; Li, 2026; Li et al., 2026). In this work, we adopt a hybrid representation, high-level planning coupled with textual skills, which yields substantial improvements for the base model.
智能体经验编码。随着经验时代的到来（Sutton, 2025），智能体可以通过对过往经验进行编码并在上下文中重用（Dou et al., 2026）来指导未来行为，从而实现自我进化（Gao et al., 2025; Fang et al., 2025b; Xia et al., 2026）。现有的文本标记级经验编码方法（Zhang et al., 2025b; Hu et al., 2025）大致可分为三类：（i）基于案例的经验：智能体直接存储成功的任务执行轨迹，并在解决新问题时将其作为少样本示例进行检索（Zhao et al., 2024; Zheng et al., 2024; Zhou et al., 2025）。（ii）基于策略的经验：通过总结和对比成功与失败的轨迹，智能体提炼出更高层次的见解或工作流（Cao et al., 2025; Ouyang et al., 2025; Cai et al., 2025a; Wang et al., 2025c; Tang et al., 2025; Zhang et al., 2025a）。（iii）基于技能的经验：轨迹被分割并提炼为模块化、可重用的技能，如文本技能或程序化技能（Wang et al., 2025b;a; 2024; Fang et al., 2025c; Han et al., 2025; Chen et al., 2026; Zheng et al., 2026; Wang et al., 2026a; Zhou et al., 2026a; Zhang et al., 2026b; Ni et al., 2026; Zhou et al., 2026b）。然而，哪种统一的经验表示既易于插件化又始终有效，尤其是在多样且复杂的智能体工具使用场景中，尚不明确（Trivedi et al., 2024; Yao et al., 2024; Patil et al., 2025; Barres et al., 2025; He et al., 2025; Li et al., 2025; Zheng et al., 2025; Jiang et al., 2026; Xing et al., 2026; Li, 2026; Li et al., 2026）。在本文中，我们采用了一种混合表示方法，即高层规划与文本技能相结合，这为基础模型带来了显著的性能提升。


Agent Experience Knowledge Base Construction. The construction pipeline of an experience knowledge base typically consists of two steps: static construction and dynamic updating. (i) Static construction repeatedly attempts tasks on a training set or human-curated information sources, extracts experience, and iteratively refines it until performance plateaus (Zhang et al., 2025c; Cai et al., 2025b; Anthropic, 2025; Wang et al., 2026b; Gallego, 2026; Yang et al., 2026a). (ii) Dynamic updating updates the ExperienceKB immediately after executing new tasks, enabling experience reuse in subsequent tasks (Latimer et al., 2025; Fang et al., 2025a; Cao et al., 2025; Du et al., 2025; Yang et al., 2026b; Yao et al., 2025; Zhang et al., 2026a; Liang et al., 2026).
智能体经验知识库构建。经验知识库的构建流程通常包含两个步骤：静态构建和动态更新。（i）静态构建：在训练集或人工策划的信息源上反复尝试任务，提取经验并迭代精炼，直至性能趋于平稳（Zhang et al., 2025c; Cai et al., 2025b; Anthropic, 2025; Wang et al., 2026b; Gallego, 2026; Yang et al., 2026a）。（ii）动态更新：在执行新任务后立即更新经验知识库（ExperienceKB），从而实现后续任务中的经验重用（Latimer et al., 2025; Fang et al., 2025a; Cao et al., 2025; Du et al., 2025; Yang et al., 2026b; Yao et al., 2025; Zhang et al., 2026a; Liang et al., 2026）。


While dynamic updating is central to continual learning from experience, pre-building a strong static ExperienceKB remains necessary in practice. However, under the task-scarcity challenge in complex agent settings (Patil et al., 2025; Barres et al., 2025; He et al., 2025; Li et al., 2025), we further extend skills by combining task synthesis (Zhai et al., 2025; Mai et al., 2025; Shi et al., 2025; Ramrakhya et al., 2025; Guo et al., 2025) to construct more challenging tasks. To our knowledge, this is the first work to provide a directly reusable skill knowledge base together with an automated pipeline for skill construction.
尽管动态更新对于从经验中进行持续学习至关重要，但在实践中预先构建一个强大的静态经验知识库（ExperienceKB）仍然是必要的。然而，面对复杂智能体设置中的任务稀缺挑战（Patil et al., 2025; Barres et al., 2025; He et al., 2025; Li et al., 2025），我们通过结合任务合成（Zhai et al., 2025; Mai et al., 2025; Shi et al., 2025; Ramrakhya et al., 2025; Guo et al., 2025）来构建更具挑战性的任务，从而进一步扩展了技能。据我们所知，这是首个提供可直接复用的技能知识库以及自动化技能构建流水线的工作。


## 8. Conclusion
## 8. 结论


We introduced SkillX, an automated framework for building a plug-and-play skill library for LLM-based agents. To enable more efficient experience transfer, we design a multi-level skills, including planning skills, functional skills, and atomic skills from the perspective of tool granularity. SkillX iteratively refines and expands the library through three core components: i) skills extraction, which rolls out an agent with the current library and extracts multi-level skills; ii) skills refinement, which iteratively improves skills using execution feedback, while maintaining quality via skill merging and strict filtering; and iii) exploratory skills expansion, which proactively broadens coverage beyond the seed training set. Our experiments demonstrate that SkillX transfers effectively to other models and provides advantages in experience representation. Finally, we will release the optimized skill library constructed by SkillX to facilitate further community exploration.
我们介绍了 SkillX，这是一个用于为基于大语言模型的智能体构建即插即用技能库的自动化框架。为了实现更高效的经验迁移，我们从工具粒度的角度设计了多级技能，包括规划技能、功能技能和原子技能。SkillX 通过三个核心组件迭代地优化和扩展技能库：i) 技能提取，通过在当前库下运行智能体并提取多级技能；ii) 技能精炼，利用执行反馈迭代改进技能，同时通过技能合并和严格过滤来保持质量；以及 iii) 探索性技能扩展，主动拓宽超出种子训练集的覆盖范围。我们的实验表明，SkillX 可有效地迁移至其他模型，并在经验表示方面具有优势。最后，我们将发布由 SkillX 构建的优化技能库，以促进社区的进一步探索。


## Impact Statements
## 影响声明


This work advances generalizable agent learning by transforming isolated trial-and-error experience into a reusable, structured skill knowledge base that can be shared across agents and environments. By enabling weaker agents to benefit from skills distilled by stronger ones, the proposed framework reduces redundant exploration, improves sample efficiency, and lowers the computational and environmental costs of training LLM agents. The plug-and-play design promotes modularity and reproducibility, supporting broader adoption in long-horizon, user-interactive applications. Potential risks include over-reliance on pre-built skills and the propagation of biases present in source agents; however, the automated refinement and expansion mechanisms provide a pathway to mitigate stagnation and encourage continual adaptation.
本工作通过将孤立的试错经验转化为可跨智能体和环境共享的可复用结构化技能知识库，推动了通用智能体学习的发展。通过使较弱的智能体能够受益于较强智能体提炼出的技能，该框架减少了冗余探索，提高了样本效率，并降低了训练大语言模型智能体的计算和环境成本。其即插即用的设计促进了模块化和可复现性，支持在长程、用户交互式应用中得到更广泛的采用。潜在风险包括对预构建技能的过度依赖以及源智能体中固有偏差的传播；然而，自动化的精炼和扩展机制为缓解停滞并鼓励持续适应提供了途径。


## Limitations
## 局限性


Cross-environment transfer. SkillX is currently most naturally applicable when skills can be grounded in a relatively stable tool environment. The extracted skills are associated with specific tool schemas, which makes direct reuse across substantially different domains or tool ecosystems less straightforward.
跨环境迁移。SkillX 目前最自然地适用于技能可以在相对稳定的工具环境中落地的场景。提取的技能与特定的工具模式相关联，这使得在差异巨大的领域或工具生态系统之间直接复用变得不那么直接。


User-interactive settings. The current study focuses mainly on tool-using agent environments. More user interactive scenarios, particularly dialogue scenarios without function calls, are not yet the primary focus of this work.
用户交互设置。目前的研究主要集中在工具使用型智能体环境。更多用户交互场景，特别是没有函数调用的对话场景，尚不是本工作的重点。

### A.1. Benchmark Details
### A.1. 基准测试详情


BFCL-v3 Berkeley Function Calling Leaderboard V3 (BFCL-v3) (Patil et al., 2025) is a benchmark for evaluating function calling and tool use in large language models. It emphasizes multi-turn interaction and multi-step reasoning. The benchmark contains over 1,800 test instances and supports multiple programming languages, including Python, Java, and JavaScript. Models are required to generate valid API calls and handle non-trivial interaction patterns. Evaluation considers both structural validity and functional correctness. We first check whether the generated code is syntactically valid using Abstract Syntax Tree analysis, and then execute it to verify that the outputs match the expected results. A task is considered successful only when the agent produces all required function calls with correct syntax and returns the correct computational outcomes. In this work, we report Avg@4, which measures the average task success rate across four independent trials, and Pass@4, which measures the probability that at least one of the four trials succeeds.
BFCL-v3 Berkeley Function Calling Leaderboard V3 (BFCL-v3) (Patil et al., 2025) 是一个用于评估大语言模型函数调用与工具使用能力的基准测试。它强调多轮交互与多步推理，包含超过 1,800 个测试实例，并支持 Python、Java 和 JavaScript 等多种编程语言。模型需生成有效的 API 调用并处理复杂的交互模式。评估同时考量结构有效性与功能正确性。我们首先通过抽象语法树（AST）分析检查生成代码的语法有效性，随后执行代码以验证输出是否符合预期。仅当智能体生成所有必需的函数调用且语法正确并返回正确的计算结果时，任务才被视为成功。本研究中，我们报告 Avg@4（四次独立试验的平均任务成功率）和 Pass@4（四次试验中至少有一次成功的概率）。


Appworld AppWorld (Trivedi et al., 2024) is a benchmark suite for evaluating function calling agents and interactive coding systems in realistic application environments. It simulates an ecosystem of nine widely used applications, such as email services, music streaming platforms, and payment systems, and provides 457 API endpoints together with activity data from around 100 virtual users. Tasks in AppWorld are typically long-horizon and require executing extended sequences of interdependent actions. Many tasks involve discovering appropriate APIs rather than directly reusing familiar patterns, which places additional demands on exploration and planning. The benchmark also exhibits a noticeable distribution gap between training and test sets, where API usage patterns and task structures in the test set differ from those observed during training. In addition, task execution is tightly coupled with the evolving environment state. Intermediate actions modify the system state and influence future decisions, which increases sensitivity to planning errors and makes robust multi-step reasoning more difficult. Evaluation is based on state-driven unit tests that assess task completion from multiple aspects. AppWorld provides both task-level and scenario-level metrics. In this work, we use Task Goal Completion as the primary measure of performance. Following the standard protocol, we report Avg@4 and Pass@4 across four independent trials.
AppWorld (Trivedi et al., 2024) 是一套用于评估现实应用环境中函数调用智能体与交互式编码系统的基准测试。它模拟了包含电子邮件服务、音乐流媒体平台和支付系统等九种常用应用的生态系统，并提供了 457 个 API 端点以及约 100 名虚拟用户的活动数据。AppWorld 中的任务通常具有长程性，需要执行一系列相互依赖的扩展动作。许多任务涉及发现合适的 API 而非直接复用熟悉模式，这对探索与规划提出了更高要求。该基准测试在训练集与测试集之间存在显著的分布差异，测试集中的 API 使用模式与任务结构与训练时观察到的有所不同。此外，任务执行与不断演变的系统状态紧密耦合。中间动作会修改系统状态并影响后续决策，这增加了对规划错误的敏感性，并加大了稳健多步推理的难度。评估基于状态驱动的单元测试，从多个维度考核任务完成情况。AppWorld 提供任务级和场景级指标。本研究中，我们使用任务目标完成度作为主要性能衡量标准。遵循标准协议，我们报告四次独立试验的 Avg@4 和 Pass@4。


${\tau }^{2}$ -Bench ${\tau }^{2}$ -Bench (Barres et al.,2025) evaluates tool use in conversational agent settings,with a strong emphasis on user-agent interaction. The benchmark simulates multi-turn dialogues between a user and an agent, aiming to reflect realistic conversational behavior. Agents must track dialogue context across turns, interpret user requests, select and invoke APIs appropriately, and follow domain-specific business rules. The tasks cover domains such as airline customer service and retail customer service. The interactive nature of the benchmark requires agents to respond to user feedback, maintain coherent dialogue flow, and coordinate tool use with the ongoing conversation. Performance is assessed based on task completion accuracy, correctness of tool use, and compliance with policies. In this work, we conduct four independent trials per task and report Pass@1 on each of the three domains.
${\tau }^{2}$ -Bench (Barres et al., 2025) 旨在评估对话智能体环境下的工具使用能力，特别强调用户与智能体之间的交互。该基准测试模拟了用户与智能体之间的多轮对话，旨在反映真实的对话行为。智能体必须跨轮次跟踪对话上下文、解读用户请求、选择并适当地调用 API，并遵循特定领域的业务规则。任务涵盖航空客户服务和零售客户服务等领域。该基准测试的交互特性要求智能体能够响应用户反馈、保持连贯的对话流程，并将工具使用与持续进行的对话相协调。性能评估基于任务完成准确性、工具使用正确性以及策略合规性。本研究中，我们对每个任务进行四次独立试验，并报告三个领域各自的 Pass@1。


### A.2. Baseline Details
### A.2. 基线方法详情


A-Mem A-Mem (Xu et al., 2025) is an agentic memory framework that equips LLM-based agents with the ability to maintain and utilize long-term knowledge over extended interactions. The method organizes accumulated experiences into a memory-centric structure, enabling agents to selectively retain, retrieve, and revise stored information according to task objectives and observed outcomes. Rather than treating memory as a passive log, A-Mem emphasizes autonomous memory management driven by the agent's goals and interaction context. In our experiments, we reproduce A-Mem based on its publicly available implementation, with minor prompt adaptations to support memory writing and organization during task interactions.
A-Mem A-Mem (Xu et al., 2025) 是一种智能体记忆框架，旨在赋予基于大语言模型的智能体在长期交互中维护和利用长期知识的能力。该方法将积累的经验组织为以记忆为中心的结构，使智能体能够根据任务目标和观察到的结果，有选择地保留、检索和修订存储的信息。A-Mem 不将记忆视为被动日志，而是强调由智能体目标和交互上下文驱动的自主记忆管理。在实验中，我们基于其公开实现复现了 A-Mem，并进行了微小的提示词调整，以支持任务交互过程中的记忆写入与组织。


AWM AWM (Agent Workflow Memory) (Wang et al., 2025c) is a memory-augmented agent framework that focuses on discovering reusable workflow patterns from past task executions. The method stores completed task trajectories as episodic experiences and derives higher-level procedural knowledge by analyzing multiple successful examples. Experience retrieval follows a lightweight lexical matching strategy. Textual representations of task queries and stored experiences are mapped to sparse term-based vectors, and relevance is measured using cosine similarity. A small set of highly relevant experiences is selected for downstream analysis, with subsampling applied when multiple candidates exhibit comparable similarity. Workflow induction is performed by prompting a language model to analyze the retrieved successful trajectories and summarize recurring action patterns. Rather than relying on explicit symbolic rules or predefined workflow schemas, AWM captures reusable procedural structures directly from empirical task executions. Retrieved experiences are incorporated as conversational message objects (e.g., HumanMessage and AIMessage), enabling the language model to process exemplar interactions naturally within the dialogue context.
AWM AWM (Agent Workflow Memory) (Wang et al., 2025c) 是一种记忆增强型智能体框架，专注于从过往任务执行中发现可复用的工作流模式。该方法将已完成的任务轨迹存储为情境经验，并通过分析多个成功案例推导出更高层级的程序性知识。经验检索遵循轻量级的词法匹配策略：将任务查询和存储经验的文本表示映射为稀疏的基于词项的向量，并使用余弦相似度衡量相关性。系统会选择少量高度相关的经验用于后续分析，当多个候选者相似度相近时进行子采样。工作流归纳通过提示语言模型分析检索到的成功轨迹并总结重复出现的动作模式来完成。AWM 不依赖显式的符号规则或预定义的工作流模式，而是直接从经验性的任务执行中捕获可复用的程序结构。检索到的经验被整合为对话消息对象（如 HumanMessage 和 AIMessage），使语言模型能够在对话上下文中自然地处理示例交互。


ExpeL ExpeL (Zhao et al., 2024) is an experience-driven learning framework that improves agent performance by reflecting on past successes and failures. The method stores task execution trajectories and generates experiential knowledge by contrasting successful and unsuccessful outcomes for the same task. In our experiments, we reproduce ExpeL by collecting both successful trajectories (reward $\geq  {1.0}$ ) and failed trajectories (reward $< {1.0}$ ). For each successful example,a small number of failed trajectories from the same task type are selected for comparative analysis. A large language model is prompted to analyze the paired trajectories and generate natural-language critiques that highlight key decision differences and improvement suggestions. These critiques are retained as unstructured textual experiences and reused as guidance in subsequent tasks.
ExpeL ExpeL (Zhao et al., 2024) 是一种经验驱动的学习框架，通过反思过去的成功与失败来提升智能体性能。该方法存储任务执行轨迹，并通过对比同一任务的成功与失败结果来生成经验性知识。在实验中，我们通过收集成功轨迹（奖励 $\geq  {1.0}$ ）和失败轨迹（奖励 $< {1.0}$ ）来复现 ExpeL。对于每个成功示例，系统会选择少量同一任务类型的失败轨迹进行对比分析。通过提示大语言模型分析配对的轨迹，生成自然语言评论，指出关键决策差异和改进建议。这些评论作为非结构化文本经验被保留，并在后续任务中作为指导复用。


### A.3. Implementation Details
### A.3. 实现细节


Skills Extraction. During the experience extraction stage, which comprises both reasoning and experience extraction, we employ GLM-4.6 with a temperature of 0.9 . For each task in the training set, we independently sample four trajectories. Environment feedback exceeding 1500 tokens is summarized. We cluster the extracted skills using DBSCAN (Density-Based Spatial Clustering of Applications with Noise) with a cosine similarity threshold of 0.9 . For each cluster, we truncate the skill set to at most 15 skills. Skill updates are performed with up to three iterative refinement rounds. During the skill expansion stage, we set the exploration model temperature to 1.0 and perform 1 time to explore environment for each training task.
技能提取。在包含推理和经验提取的经验提取阶段，我们使用 GLM-4.6，温度系数设为 0.9。对于训练集中的每个任务，我们独立采样四条轨迹。超过 1500 个 token 的环境反馈会被总结。我们使用 DBSCAN（基于密度的带噪声空间聚类算法）对提取的技能进行聚类，余弦相似度阈值设为 0.9。对于每个聚类，我们将技能集截断至最多 15 个技能。技能更新执行最多三轮迭代优化。在技能扩展阶段，我们将探索模型的温度系数设为 1.0，并为每个训练任务执行 1 次环境探索。


Skills Usage. We build a skill semantic vector store using FAISS with an HNSW index under cosine similarity (via L2-normalized embeddings and inner-product search). At query time, we first perform a broad retrieval of the Top-100 nearest skills. Candidates are then filtered by a hybrid relevance threshold: we keep only results whose cosine similarity is at least 0.45 , and also within 0.08 of the best match for that query, ensuring both a minimum quality floor and adaptive selectivity. To reduce near-duplicate skills, we apply semantic deduplication by removing items whose pairwise cosine similarity exceeds 0.95, retaining the higher-scoring representative. Finally, we return up to 8 skills after applying Maximal Marginal Relevance (MMR) for diversity-aware selection, using a relevance-diversity trade-off weight of 0.75 to emphasize relevance while mitigating redundancy.
技能使用。我们使用 FAISS 构建技能语义向量存储，并在余弦相似度下使用 HNSW 索引（通过 L2 归一化嵌入和内积搜索）。在查询时，我们首先对前 100 个最相似的技能进行广泛检索。随后，候选者通过混合相关性阈值进行过滤：仅保留余弦相似度至少为 0.45 且与该查询最佳匹配项的差距在 0.08 以内的结果，从而确保最低质量底线并实现自适应选择。为减少近重复技能，我们应用语义去重，移除两两余弦相似度超过 0.95 的项，并保留得分较高的代表性技能。最后，在应用最大边际相关性（MMR）进行多样性感知选择后，我们返回最多 8 个技能，使用 0.75 的相关性-多样性权衡权重，在减轻冗余的同时强调相关性。
