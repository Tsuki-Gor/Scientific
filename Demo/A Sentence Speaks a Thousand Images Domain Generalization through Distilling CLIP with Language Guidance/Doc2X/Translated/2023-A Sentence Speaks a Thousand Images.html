Abstract
摘要

Domain generalization studies the problem of training a model with samples from several domains (or distributions) and then testing the model with samples from a new, unseen domain. In this paper, we propose a novel approach for domain generalization that leverages recent advances in large vision-language models, specifically a CLIP teacher model, to train a smaller model that generalizes to unseen domains. The key technical contribution is a new type of regularization that requires the student's learned image representations to be close to the teacher's learned text representations obtained from encoding the corresponding text descriptions of images. We introduce two designs of the loss function, absolute and relative distance, which provide specific guidance on how the training process of the student model should be regularized. We evaluate our proposed method, dubbed RISE (Regularized Invariance with Semantic Embeddings), on various benchmark datasets, and show that it outperforms several state-of-the-art domain generalization methods. To our knowledge, our work is the first to leverage knowledge distillation using a large vision-language model for domain generalization. By incorporating text-based information, RISE improves the generalization capability of machine learning models.
领域泛化研究的是这样一个问题：使用来自几个领域（或分布）的样本训练模型，然后使用来自一个新未见过的领域的样本测试该模型。在本文中，我们提出了一种新的领域泛化方法，该方法利用了大型视觉-语言模型最近取得的进展，特别是CLIP教师模型，来训练一个能够泛化到未见领域的更小的模型。关键技术贡献是一种新型的正则化，它要求学生学到的图像表示接近教师从编码相应图像的文字描述中获得的学到的文本表示。我们引入了损失函数的两种设计，绝对距离和相对距离，它们为如何正则化学生模型的训练过程提供了具体指导。我们在各种基准数据集上评估了我们提出的方法，名为RISE（带有语义嵌入的正则化不变性），并展示了它优于几种最先进的领域泛化方法。据我们所知，我们的工作是第一个利用大型视觉-语言模型的知识蒸馏进行领域泛化的。通过融入基于文本的信息，RISE提高了机器学习模型的泛化能力。

1. Introduction
1. 引言

An image is worth a thousand words, indeed, because of its power to convey a wealth of information through its visual details. However, a well-written sentence, on the other hand, has the power to concisely capture the essential information that is common to many different images. By describing a scene with a few carefully chosen words, a writer can create a mental image in the reader's mind that conveys the essence of what is being depicted. This perspective is particularly useful when communicating information efficiently, or when emphasizing a specific scene aspect without getting bogged down in extraneous details. Thus, we suggest that a sentence speaks a thousand images.
一幅图像确实值一千个单词，因为它通过视觉细节传达了大量信息。然而，另一方面，一句写得好的句子有能力简洁地捕捉到许多不同图像共有的关键信息。通过用几个精心挑选的词描述一个场景，作者可以在读者的脑海中创造一个心理图像，传达所描绘事物的本质。这种观点在高效传达信息或强调特定场景方面而不陷入冗余细节时尤其有用。因此，我们建议说，一句话可以代表一千个图像。

图1. 我们论点背后的关键直觉。虽然图像可以捕捉更多细节，但文本可以直接总结核心概念来表示感兴趣的物体。
Essential semantic information delivered by an image plays a pivotal role in helping models generalize to shifted distributions, whereas other detailed information (e.g., in the background not relevant to the main object) captured in images may not be as effective for this purpose. The study of domain generalization [38] investigates the problem of training a model with samples from several domains (or distributions) and then testing the model with samples from a new, unseen domain. The training domains are commonly referred to as source domains, and the test domain is referred to as the target domain. Previous studies have identified a challenge in training effective domain generalization models due to the models' tendency to learn domain-specific features [11]. Consequently, numerous works have focused on regularizing the models to learn representations that are invariant to domain-specific features [30,32,82,58,37,4,1,10,40,48,17] . This regularization ensures that the models extract features that are common to multiple domains and are therefore more likely to generalize to unseen domains. By mitigating the influence of domain-specific features, the idea is to improve the generalization capability of these models and ensure that they perform well on a variety of different domains.
图像传达的基本语义信息在帮助模型推广到偏移分布方面起着关键作用，而图像中捕获的其他详细信息（例如，与主要物体不相关的背景）对此目的可能不那么有效。域泛化研究[38]调查了这样一个问题：用来自几个域（或分布）的样本训练模型，然后用来自新的、未见过的域的样本测试模型。训练域通常被称为源域，测试域被称为目标域。先前的研究发现，由于模型倾向于学习特定于域的特征[11]，训练有效的域泛化模型具有挑战性。因此，许多研究集中在规范模型，使其学习对特定于域的特征不变的表示[30,32,82,58,37,4,1,10,40,48,17]。这种规范确保模型提取的特征是多个域共有的，因此更有可能推广到未见过的域。通过减轻特定于域的特征的影响，想法是提高这些模型的泛化能力，并确保它们在各种不同的域上表现良好。

In this paper, we build upon this line of research by investigating methods for learning domain-invariant features in machine learning models. Our proposed method is inspired by a simple intuition: while an image tends to convey rich but sometimes excessive details through its pixels, a corresponding text description can describe the crux of the image content in a highly concise and complementary manner; see Figure 1. Therefore, the most effective regularization might involve incorporating a regularization strategy in which the learned representations need to be close to the representations obtained from encoding the corresponding concise text descriptions of an image.
在本文中，我们在这一研究路线的基础上，通过研究机器学习模型中学习领域不变特征的方法来继续研究。我们提出的方法受到一个简单直觉的启发：虽然图像往往通过其像素传达丰富但有时过多的细节，相应的文本描述却可以以高度简洁和互补的方式描述图像内容的精髓；见图1。因此，最有效的正则化可能涉及将正则化策略与所学表征需要接近于由编码图像对应的简洁文本描述获得的表征相结合。
Building upon this argument, we propose a novel domain generalization approach that leverages recent advances in vision-language models, such as CLIP [47], to train our domain generalization models. We are particularly interested in the setting where our final models are relatively small, and thus, can benefit from a large pre-trained vision-language teacher model through distillation. Our method, dubbed RISE (Regularized Invariance with Semantic Em-beddings), incorporates both the vision and language components of a pre-trained and frozen CLIP teacher, inspired by the importance of the representations encoded by the language component. Specifically, RISE includes three loss functions: the empiricial risk minimization (ERM) loss that follows the standard pipeline of domain generalization, the model distillation loss that leverages the pretrained weights of the image component of CLIP, and the cross-domain (text to image) distance loss that uses the power of text through the language component of CLIP.
基于这个论点，我们提出了一种新颖的领域泛化方法，该方法利用了视觉语言模型最近的进展，如CLIP [47]，来训练我们的领域泛化模型。我们特别关注于我们的最终模型相对较小的情况，因此，可以通过蒸馏从大型预训练的视觉语言教师模型中受益。我们的方法，称为RISE（正则化不变性与语义嵌入），结合了预训练且冻结的CLIP教师模型的视觉和语言两部分，这是受到了语言部分编码的表征重要性的启发。具体来说，RISE包括三个损失函数：遵循领域泛化标准流程的经验风险最小化（ERM）损失，利用CLIP图像部分预训练权重的模型蒸馏损失，以及通过CLIP的语言部分利用文本力量的跨领域（文本到图像）距离损失。
To fully harness the power of language, we introduce two different designs of the cross-domain distance loss function: the absolute distance design pushes the student's learned representation closer to the teacher's domain-invariant representation learned from language, while the relative distance design enforces that the relative domain distances in the teacher's encoded language space are transferred over to the learned representation in the student's encoded image space.
为了充分利用语言的力量，我们引入了两种跨域距离损失函数的不同设计：绝对距离设计将学生学习的表征推向教师从语言中学到的域不变表征，而相对距离设计则确保教师编码语言空间中的相对域距离转移到学生编码图像空间中的学习表征。

2. Related Work
2. 相关工作

2.1. Domain Generalization
2.1. 域泛化

Domain Generalization [38] has been widely studied in recent years. It mainly studies the problem of training a model from the data collected from multiple source distributions and then testing the trained model on a target distribution that is different from the training ones. Because of this problem formulation, a natural assumption to guide the development of the methods is that if the model can learn a representation that is invariant across the multiple training domains, it will generalize well to the unseen test domain. A large number of methods have been invented following this natural assumption, aiming to force the invariance across samples of training distributions, either through explicit regularization based methods [30,32,82,58,37 , 4,1,10,40,48,17,85,9,60,64,36,28,79,71,22,57] or (virtual) data augmentation methods [50,77,12,84,21,63] .
域泛化 [38] 在近年来已经被广泛研究。它主要研究的问题是，从多个源分布收集的数据训练一个模型，然后在该模型上测试一个与训练分布不同的目标分布。由于这种问题设定，一个自然的假设是，如果模型能够学习到一个在多个训练域上不变的表征，它将在未见过的测试域上泛化得很好。许多方法都是遵循这个自然假设而发明的，旨在通过基于显式正则化的方法 [30,32,82,58,37 ,4,1,10,40,48,17,85,9,60,64,36,28,79,71,22,57] 或（虚拟）数据增强方法 [50,77,12,84,21,63] 来强制样本在不同训练分布之间的不变性。
In addition, the assumption above of "invariance across multiple domains" is being challenged in recent years with the argument that a more realistic scenario is when the training datasets are not necessarily partitioned into multiple distributions/domains with clear boundaries during training. As a response to this argument, more powerful methods that do not rely on the domain partitions to force invariance have been introduced [24,54,23] . Our method in this paper in tested in the context of this challenging scenario.
此外，近年来，“跨多个领域不变性”的假设正在受到挑战，有观点认为一个更现实的场景是，在训练过程中，训练数据集不一定会被划分成具有清晰边界的多个分布/领域。作为对这一观点的回应，已经引入了更强大的方法，这些方法不依赖于领域划分来强制不变性 [24,54,23] 。本文中的方法在这种具有挑战性的场景下进行了测试。
Also, in recent years, it seems the community is using the terminology out-of-distribution (OOD) generalization to largely refer to domain generalization. For more detailed discussions on the topics of domain generalization and out-of-distribution (OOD) generalization, we refer the reader to dedicated surveys [59,52] .
此外，近年来，似乎社区正在使用术语“分布外（OOD）泛化”来广泛指代领域泛化。关于领域泛化和分布外（OOD）泛化的更详细讨论，我们建议读者查阅专门的综述 [59,52] 。
More closely related to our contribution in this paper, we notice a prior work that also leverages a pre-trained model to improve domain generalization performance. Specifically, Domain Prompt Learning (DPL) [81] utilizes a lightweight prompt adaptor to automatically generate a prompt that estimates domain-specific features given unlabeled examples from each distribution. The following two works are not closely related to CLIP but leverage CLIP as their pre-trained model for domain generalization: [33] dispatches proper pre-trained models (including CLIP) to each sample based on their generalization ability. [6] reformulates the DG objective by mutual information with oracle models (including CLIP).
与本文贡献更密切相关的是，我们注意到之前的工作也利用了预训练模型来提高领域泛化性能。具体来说，领域提示学习（DPL）[81] 使用轻量级提示适配器自动生成一个提示，用于估计每个分布中未标记样本的领域特定特征。以下两个工作与CLIP没有直接关联，但将CLIP作为它们的预训练模型用于领域泛化：[33] 根据样本的泛化能力将适当的预训练模型（包括CLIP）分派给每个样本。[6] 通过与Oracle模型（包括CLIP）的互信息重新定义了DG目标。
Key novelty: Unlike prior work, we leverage CLIP as a teacher and regularize the student's learned representation through images to be closer to the corresponding text representation of the teacher. Our method includes two loss functions that directly leverage language for learning invariant image representations.
关键新颖之处：与之前的工作不同，我们将CLIP作为教师，通过图像将学生学到的表征正规化，使其更接近教师的相应文本表征。我们的方法包括两个损失函数，它们直接利用语言来学习不变图像表征。

2.2. Knowledge Distillation
2.2. 知识蒸馏

Knowledge distillation is a technique for transferring knowledge from a teacher model to a student model, by optimizing the student model to match the outputs or intermediate features of the teacher model. This technique is used in numerous distinct contexts, such as semi-supervised learning [41] or even self-supervised learning [65].
知识蒸馏是一种将知识从教师模型转移到学生模型的技术，通过优化学生模型以匹配教师模型的输出或中间特征。这种技术在许多不同的上下文中被使用，例如半监督学习 [41] 或甚至自监督学习 [65]。
Ever since the introduction of the term in [20], a plethora of techniques have been developed [13] with improvement in various aspects, centering around the idea of how to align the output of the student model to the teacher model for every input, where the alignment and output are both subject to various concrete definitions. For example, one branch of works varies on how to enforce the alignment, with a particular focus on the loss function design over the outputs between the teacher and the student for every sample, with popular studies such as ℓ1[27],ℓ2[7,45,61] ,MMD [25], KL divergence [8, 44, 43], and cross-entropy losses [66,34] . Another branch studies how to define the output, which, at a high-level, has variants of directly using the embeddings from a certain (or final) layer [69,15,18,51] , or some more structured functions of the (pair-wise) em-beddings of those layers [31,78,74] . There are also other branches such as the student-teacher architecture design or distillation algorithms that are not directly related to our study in this paper; we recommend the reader to refer to a survey for more details [13].
自从术语被引入以来 [20]，已经开发了大量的技术 [13]，各种方面都有所改进，这些技术围绕如何使每个输入的学生模型输出与教师模型对齐的想法展开，其中对齐和输出都受到各种具体定义的约束。例如，一个分支的工作关注于如何强制执行对齐，特别关注于样本间教师和学生输出之间的损失函数设计，如 ℓ1[27],ℓ2[7,45,61] ，MMD [25]，KL散度 [8, 44, 43]，以及交叉熵损失 [66,34] 等流行研究。另一个分支研究如何定义输出，在高级层面，有直接使用某个（或最终）层的嵌入 [69,15,18,51] 的变体，或者这些层的（成对）嵌入的某些更具结构性的函数 [31,78,74] 。还有其他分支，如学生-教师架构设计或与本文研究不直接相关的蒸馏算法；我们建议读者参考综述以获取更多详细信息 [13]。
Among these works, the most relevant technical development to our method is to distill the preservation of the relationship between samples from the teacher model to the student model. For example, [7] distills while the the student also learns the relationship between samples after the relationship is projected to a lower dimensional space, and other works more directly optimize the similarity of the pair-wise distances between embeddings after each pair of samples is fed into the teacher and student models, respectively [76,46,35,42] .
在这些工作中，与我们的方法最相关的技术发展是将样本之间关系的保留从教师模型转移到学生模型。例如，[7] 在蒸馏过程中，学生也学习在关系被投影到低维空间之后样本之间的关系，其他工作则更直接地优化每对样本分别输入教师模型和学生模型后，嵌入之间成对距离的相似性 [76,46,35,42] 。
Key novelty: The objective of prior work KDDG [62] is to distill the knowledge of a pure vision teacher to a student model. In contrast, our approach focuses on distilling the knowledge of large-scale vision and language models (CLIP) to the student model.
关键创新：先前工作 KDDG [62] 的目标是提炼纯视觉教师的知识至学生模型。相比之下，我们的方法专注于将大规模视觉和语言模型（CLIP）的知识提炼至学生模型。

2.3. Large Vision-Language Models
2.3. 大规模视觉-语言模型

Recent advances in vision-language models [47, 26, 68, 70,67,75] have shown promising results in learning generic visual representations and facilitating zero-shot transfer to diverse downstream classification tasks through the use of prompts. These models typically rely on a contrastive loss to align a visual encoder and a text encoder in a shared feature space. Trained on large-scale image-text pairs, these vision-language models demonstrate transferability across a wide range of applications, including object detection [14], semantic segmentation [83], and point cloud classification [80].
近年来视觉-语言模型的研究进展 [47, 26, 68, 70,67,75] 在学习通用视觉表示和通过使用提示促进零样本迁移至多种下游分类任务方面显示出有希望的结果。这些模型通常依赖于对比损失来对齐视觉编码器与文本编码器在共享特征空间中的表示。在大规模图像-文本对上训练后，这些视觉-语言模型在广泛的任务中表现出迁移性，包括目标检测 [14]、语义分割 [83] 和点云分类 [80]。
In particular, Contrastive Language Image Pre-training i.e., CLIP [47] utilizes 400M pretraining image-text pairs to conduct image-caption contrastive pretraining. Empirically, CLIP shows superior zero-shot image classification performance,achieving 76.2% top-1 accuracy on the Ima-geNet validation set, which is on par with the performance of an ImageNet fully-supervised ResNet101 model. Furthermore, CLIP shows potential domain generalization capabilities. For example,it achieves 60.2% accuracy on Ima-geNet Sketch Dataset while the ImageNet supervised training model (ResNet101) can only achieve 25.2% accuracy. This motivates us to answer the following question: What is the best way to distill CLIP's rich domain knowledge to a smaller student network for domain generalization tasks?
特别是，对比性语言图像预训练，即 CLIP [47] 利用 400M 预训练图像-文本对进行图像-标题对比预训练。实证上，CLIP 展现出卓越的零样本图像分类性能，在 ImageNet 验证集上达到 76.2% 的 top-1 准确率，与 ImageNet 全监督训练的 ResNet101 模型的性能相当。此外，CLIP 展现出潜在的域泛化能力。例如，它在 ImageNet 草图数据集上达到 60.2% 的准确率，而 ImageNet 监督训练模型（ResNet101）只能达到 25.2% 的准确率。这激发我们去回答以下问题：对于域泛化任务，将 CLIP 丰富的域知识提炼到更小的学生网络的最佳方式是什么？

3.2. Regularization with Teacher's Language Com- ponent
3.2. 使用教师语言组件的正则化

We use gl(⋅;δ) to denote the CLIP teacher’s language component that takes the input text phrase and generates an embedding. In general, if we have a generic description of the image,such as " z= a photo of a dog",we can directly feed this text phrase into the model to generate the corresponding embedding,following ez(dog)=gl(z;δ) .
我们用 gl(⋅;δ) 来表示CLIP教师的语言组件，该组件接收输入的文本短语并生成一个嵌入向量。通常，如果我们有一个关于图像的通用描述，例如 " z= 一张狗的照片"，我们可以直接将这个文本短语输入模型以生成相应的嵌入向量，遵循 ez(dog)=gl(z;δ) 。
However, in practice, although "a photo of a dog" is recommended by CLIP as a standard text template, this text might not be generic enough as it still indicates the pixel statistics of the image following the typical statistics of what a photo has, which is potentially different from what a sketch or what a painting has.
然而，在实践中，尽管CLIP推荐“一张狗的照片”作为标准的文本模板，但这个文本可能并不足够通用，因为它仍然指示了图像的像素统计信息，遵循典型照片的统计信息，这可能与草图或绘画的统计信息有所不同。
To overcome the potential over-reliance on the pixel statistics of photo, we use the recommended list of eighty templates of text prompts by CLIP [47], including from "a photo of my {} ","an art of {} ",to even "a tattoo of the {} " and consider their averaged representation as the generic teacher's text representation of the depicted object.
为了克服对照片像素统计的潜在过度依赖，我们使用了CLIP [47] 推荐的八十个文本提示模板，包括从“我的 {} 照片”、“{} 的艺术作品”甚至到“{} 的纹身”，并将它们的平均表示视为所描绘对象的通用教师文本表示。
More concretely, we build the generic representation of class i by

其中 Z(i) 表示当类被填充为与对象 i 对应的类名时推荐文本模板的集合。

3.2.1 Absolute Distance Loss
3.2.1 绝对距离损失

The absolute distance loss is designed to directly push the student's learned image representation to be close to the teacher's generic text representation:
绝对距离损失旨在直接将学生学到的图像表示推向接近教师的通用文本表示：

where k(⋅,⋅) is a distance metric, I is the collection of all possible classes,and I[c(x)=i] is simply an identity function that returns 1 if the class of x is i and 0 otherwise.
其中 k(⋅,⋅) 是距离度量，I 是所有可能的类的集合，I[c(x)=i] 是一个简单的恒等函数，如果 x 的类是 i 则返回1，否则返回0。
Ideally, if we can achieve the minimum value from (3), we will train a student model that can learn generic visual representations that are likely to be invariant across the input domains and perform well on target domains.
理想情况下，如果我们能够从（3）中达到最小值，我们将训练出一个学生模型，它能够学习到在输入域之间很可能不变且在目标域上表现良好的通用视觉表示。
However, in practice, due to the difficulties of optimizing deep learning models on real-world data, the optimization cannot easily find such optimal solutions. Therefore, we need to introduce an additional regularization to help shrink the search space.
然而，在实际操作中，由于在现实世界数据上优化深度学习模型的困难，优化过程不能轻易找到这样的最优解。因此，我们需要引入额外的正则化来帮助缩小搜索空间。

3.2.2 Relative Distance Loss
3.2.2 相对距离损失

We introduce a relative distance loss that can better describe where the target generic representation is.
我们引入了一种相对距离损失，它能更好地描述目标通用表示的位置。

3.4. Implementation Details
3.4. 实现细节

In practice,we implement l as cross-entropy loss, l′ as KL divergence, k as CosineSimilarity, k1 as CosineSimilar-ity,and k2 as L2 loss. The KL divergence l′ introduces one more hyperparameter temperature t to control the smoothness of CLIP's predictions. The detail of distance metrics selection is analyzed in the ablation study. We use one linear layer to project the image embedding of the student to the text embedding of the CLIP teacher. During inference, images are passed through the student image encoder and FC layer (or CLIP's text embedding) to make final predictions.
在实际操作中，我们将 l 实现为交叉熵损失，l′ 作为KL散度，k 作为余弦相似度，k1 作为CosineSimilar-ity，以及 k2 作为L2损失。KL散度 l′ 引入了一个额外的超参数温度 t，用于控制CLIP预测的平滑度。距离度量的选择细节在消融研究中进行了分析。我们使用一个线性层将学生的图像嵌入投影到CLIP教师的文本嵌入。在推理过程中，图像通过学生图像编码器和解码层（或CLIP的文本嵌入）进行最终预测。

5. Conclusion
5. 结论

One of the challenges in domain generalization is that machine learning models tend to learn domain-specific features, which can make them less effective at generalizing to new domains. This is because domain-specific features may be highly relevant to the training data but may not be useful for prediction on new domains. To address this challenge, the community has focused on developing methods to regularize the learned representations to become domain-invariant features.
领域泛化的一个挑战是机器学习模型倾向于学习特定领域的特征，这可能会使它们在泛化到新领域时效果不佳。这是因为特定领域的特征可能与训练数据高度相关，但对新领域的预测可能并不有用。为了应对这一挑战，社区专注于开发方法来规范学习到的表征，使其成为领域不变的特征。
In this paper, we build upon this direction by investigating methods for learning domain-invariant features in machine learning models. Our proposed method is inspired by the intuition that while an image tends to convey rich but sometimes excessive details through its pixels, a corresponding text description can describe the crux of the image content in a highly concise and complementary manner.
在本文中，我们基于这一方向，通过研究机器学习模型中学习领域不变特征的方法来继续前进。我们提出的方法受到以下直觉的启发：尽管图像往往通过其像素传达丰富但有时过多的细节，但相应的文本描述可以以高度简洁和补充的方式描述图像内容的精髓。
Following this intuition, we proposed RISE, with main loss functions (the absolute distance and relative distance) to offer specific guidance on how the student model's training process should be regularized, that provides a powerful new direction for domain generalization research by incorporating the power of language to regularize image representations.
沿着这一直觉，我们提出了RISE，主要损失函数（绝对距离和相对距离）为如何规范学生模型的训练过程提供了具体指导，通过结合语言的力量来规范图像表征，为领域泛化研究提供了强大的新方向。
Our results suggest that leveraging language as a regularization strategy can achieve state-of-the-art performance on popular domain generalization benchmarks. We have demonstrated the effectiveness of our approach through a comprehensive set of experiments.
我们的研究结果表明，将语言作为一种正则化策略可以实现在流行的领域泛化基准测试中的最先进性能。我们通过一系列全面的实验证明了我们方法的有效性。
In conclusion, RISE provides a new direction for domain generalization research by incorporating the power of language to regularize image representations. Our results suggest that leveraging language as a regularization strategy can significantly improve the generalization capability of machine learning models, and we believe that our work can motivate further research in this direction.
总之，RISE 通过融入语言的力量来正则化图像表示，为领域泛化研究提供了新的方向。我们的结果表明，将语言作为正则化策略可以显著提高机器学习模型的泛化能力，我们相信我们的工作可以激发这一方向进一步的研究。
Limitations. When facing the downstream task, such as Terra where CLIP shows poor performance during zero-shot inference, finetuning CLIP on the downstream task is recommended before distilling knowledge to students.
局限性。当面临下游任务时，例如 Terra，其中 CLIP 在零样本推理期间表现不佳，建议在将知识蒸馏到学生模型之前，先对下游任务进行 CLIP 微调。
In addition, the quality and relevance of text descriptions used to regularize image representations may impact the effectiveness of our approach. In our experiments, we addressed this limitation by using an average of 80 different text descriptions for each image. However, obtaining a more direct and generic text description might help improve the efficiency of the method. We leave this to future work.
此外，用于正则化图像表示的文本描述的质量和相关性可能会影响我们方法的有效性。在我们的实验中，我们通过为每张图像使用平均80种不同的文本描述来解决这个问题。然而，获取更直接和通用的文本描述可能有助于提高方法的效率。我们将这个问题留给未来的工作。
