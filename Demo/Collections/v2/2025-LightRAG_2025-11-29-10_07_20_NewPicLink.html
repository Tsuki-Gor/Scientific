                                                                                                    ABSTRACT

                                                                                                    摘要

                                                                                                    Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems enhance large language models (LLMs) by integrating external knowledge sources, enabling more accurate and contextually relevant responses tailored to user needs. However, existing RAG systems have significant limitations, including reliance on flat data representations and inadequate contextual awareness, which can lead to fragmented answers that fail to capture complex inter-dependencies. To address these challenges, we propose LightRAG, which incorporates graph structures into text indexing and retrieval processes. This innovative framework employs a dual-level retrieval system that enhances comprehensive information retrieval from both low-level and high-level knowledge discovery. Additionally, the integration of graph structures with vector representations facilitates efficient retrieval of related entities and their relationships, significantly improving response times while maintaining contextual relevance. This capability is further enhanced by an incremental update algorithm that ensures the timely integration of new data, allowing the system to remain effective and responsive in rapidly changing data environments. Extensive experimental validation demonstrates considerable improvements in retrieval accuracy and efficiency compared to existing approaches. We have made our LightRAG open-source and available at the link: https://github.com/HKUDS/LightRAG 检索增强生成（RAG）系统通过整合外部知识源增强大型语言模型（LLMs），使其能够生成更准确、更具上下文相关性的响应以满足用户需求。然而，现有 RAG 系统存在显著局限，包括依赖扁平数据表示和缺乏足够的上下文感知，可能导致回答碎片化，无法捕捉复杂的相互依赖关系。为了解决这些问题，我们提出 LightRAG，将图结构引入文本索引与检索流程。该创新框架采用双层检索系统，增强了从低层次和高层次知识发现中获取全面信息的能力。此外，将图结构与向量表示相结合便于高效检索相关实体及其关系，显著提升响应速度同时保持上下文相关性。通过增量更新算法进一步提升了在不断变化的数据环境中及时整合新数据的能力，使系统保持有效且响应迅速。大量实验证明与现有方法相比在检索准确性和效率上有显著提升。我们已将 LightRAG 开源，地址为：https://github.com/HKUDS/LightRAG

                                                                                                    2 RETRIEVAL-AUGMENTED GENERATION

                                                                                                    2 检索增强生成

                                                                                                    Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) integrates user queries with a collection of pertinent documents sourced from an external knowledge database, incorporating two essential elements: the Retrieval Component and the Generation Component. 1) The retrieval component is responsible for fetching relevant documents or information from the external knowledge database. It identifies and retrieves the most pertinent data based on the input query. 2) After the retrieval process, the generation component takes the retrieved information and generates coherent, contextually relevant responses. It leverages the capabilities of the language model to produce meaningful outputs. Formally, this RAG framework,denoted as M ,can be defined as follows: 检索增强生成（RAG）将用户查询与来自外部知识库的一组相关文档整合，包含两个要素：检索组件与生成组件。1）检索组件负责从外部知识库获取相关文档或信息，基于输入查询识别并检索最相关的数据。2）检索完成后，生成组件利用检索到的信息生成连贯且语境相关的响应，借助语言模型的能力产生有意义的输出。形式上，该 RAG 框架记为 M ，可定义如下：

                                                                                                    (1)M=(G,R=(φ,ψ)),M(q;D)=G(q,ψ(q;D^)),D^=φ(D)

                                                                                                     

                                                                                                    In this framework, G and R represent the generation module and the retrieval module,respectively, while q denotes the input query and D refers to the external database. The retrieval module R includes two key functionalities: i) Data Indexer φ(⋅) : which involves building a specific data structure D^ based on the external database D . ii) Data Retriever ψ(⋅) : The relevant documents are obtained by comparing the query against the indexed data, also denoted as "relevant documents". By leveraging the information retrieved through ψ(⋅) along with the initial query q ,the generative model G(⋅) efficiently produces high-quality,contextually relevant responses. 在此框架中，G 与 R 分别表示生成模块与检索模块，q 表示输入查询，D 指外部数据库。检索模块 R 包含两项关键功能：i）数据索引器 φ(⋅)：基于外部数据库 D 构建特定数据结构 D^。ii）数据检索器 ψ(⋅)：通过将查询与已索引数据比对以获得相关文档，亦称“相关文档”。通过利用由 ψ(⋅) 检索到的信息与初始查询 q，生成模型 G(⋅) 高效地产生高质量、语境相关的响应。

                                                                                                    3.1 GRAPH-BASED TEXT INDEXING

                                                                                                    3.1 基于图的文本索引

                                                                                                    Graph-Enhanced Entity and Relationship Extraction. Our LightRAG enhances the retrieval system by segmenting documents into smaller, more manageable pieces. This strategy allows for quick identification and access to relevant information without analyzing entire documents. Next, we leverage LLMs to identify and extract various entities (e.g., names, dates, locations, and events) along with the relationships between them. The information collected through this process will be used to create a comprehensive knowledge graph that highlights the connections and insights across the entire collection of documents. We formally represent this graph generation module as follows: 图增强的实体与关系抽取。我们的 LightRAG 通过将文档切分为更小、更易处理的片段来增强检索系统。这一策略使得可以快速定位并访问相关信息，而无需分析完整文档。接着，我们利用大模型识别并抽取各类实体（如姓名、日期、地点和事件）及其相互关系。通过此过程收集的信息将用于构建全面的知识图谱，突显整个文档集合中的连接与洞见。我们将该图生成模块形式化表示如下：

                                                                                                    (2)D^=(V^,E^)= Dedupe ∘Prof(V,E),V,E=∪Di∈DRecog(Di)

                                                                                                     

                                                                                                    where D^ represents the resulting knowledge graphs. To generate this data,we apply three main processing steps to the raw text documents Di . These steps utilize a LLM for text analysis and processing. Details about the prompt templates and specific settings for this part can be found in Appendix 7.3.2 The functions used in our graph-based text indexing paradigm are described as: 其中 D^ 表示生成的知识图谱。为生成此数据，我们对原始文本文档 Di 应用三大处理步骤。这些步骤使用大模型进行文本分析与处理。关于提示模板和该部分具体设置的详情见附录 7.3.2。我们图基文本索引范式中使用的函数描述如下：

                                                                                                    • Extracting Entities and Relationships. R(·): This function prompts a LLM to identify entities (nodes) and their relationships (edges) within the text data. For instance, it can extract entities like "Cardiologists" and "Heart Disease," and relationships such as "Cardiologists diagnose Heart Disease" from the text: "Cardiologists assess symptoms to identify potential heart issues." To improve efficiency,the raw text D is segmented into multiple chunks Di .

                                                                                                    • 抽取实体与关系。R(·)：该函数提示大模型识别文本数据中的实体（节点）及其关系（边）。例如，它可以从文本“心脏科医生评估症状以识别潜在心脏问题。”中抽取出“心脏科医生”和“心脏病”等实体，以及“心脏科医生诊断心脏病”之类的关系。为提高效率，原始文本 D 被切分为多个片段 Di。

                                                                                                    • LLM Profiling for Key-Value Pair Generation. P(⋅) : We employ a LLM-empowered profiling function, P(⋅) ,to generate a text key-value pair (K,V) for each entity node in V and relation edge in E . Each index key is a word or short phrase that enables efficient retrieval,while the corresponding value is a text paragraph summarizing relevant snippets from external data to aid in text generation. Entities use their names as the sole index key, whereas relations may have multiple index keys derived from LLM enhancements that include global themes from connected entities.

                                                                                                    • 用于键值对生成的大模型画像。P(⋅)：我们采用大模型支持的画像函数 P(⋅)，为 V 中的每个实体节点和 E 中的关系边生成文本键值对 (K,V)。每个索引键为单词或短语，以便高效检索，对应的值为总结自外部数据相关片段的文本段落以辅助文本生成。实体使用其名称作为唯一索引键，而关系可能具有多个由大模型增强生成的索引键，这些索引键包含来自连接实体的全局主题。

                                                                                                    • Deduplication to Optimize Graph Operations. D(·): Finally, we implement a deduplication function, D(⋅) ,that identifies and merges identical entities and relations from different segments of the raw text Di . This process effectively reduces the overhead associated with graph operations on D^ by minimizing the graph’s size,leading to more efficient data processing.

                                                                                                    • 去重以优化图操作。D(·)：最后，我们实现了去重函数 D(⋅)，该函数识别并合并来自原始文本不同片段 Di 的相同实体和关系。此过程通过最小化图的规模，有效降低对 D^ 上图操作的开销，从而提升数据处理效率。

                                                                                                    Our LightRAG offers two advantages through its graph-based text indexing paradigm. First, Comprehensive Information Understanding. The constructed graph structures enable the extraction of global information from multi-hop subgraphs, greatly enhancing LightRAG's ability to handle complex queries that span multiple document chunks. Second, Enhanced Retrieval Performance. the key-value data structures derived from the graph are optimized for rapid and precise retrieval. This provides a superior alternative to less accurate embedding matching methods (Gao et al., 2023) and inefficient chunk traversal techniques (Edge et al. 2024) commonly used in existing approaches. 我们的 LightRAG 通过其基于图的文本索引范式提供两大优势。其一，全面信息理解。构建的图结构能够从多跳子图中提取全局信息，极大提升 LightRAG 处理跨多个文档片段的复杂查询的能力。其二，增强的检索性能。由图生成的键值数据结构针对快速且精确的检索进行了优化，优于现有方法中常用的较不精确的嵌入匹配方法（Gao et al., 2023）和低效的片段遍历技术（Edge et al. 2024）。

                                                                                                    Fast Adaptation to Incremental Knowledge Base. To efficiently adapt to evolving data changes while ensuring accurate and relevant responses, our LightRAG incrementally updates the knowledge base without the need for complete reprocessing of the entire external database. For a new document D′ ,the incremental update algorithm processes it using the same graph-based indexing steps φ as before,resulting in D^′=(V^′,E^′) . Subsequently,LightRAGcombines the new graph data with the original by taking the union of the node sets V^ and V^′ ,as well as the edge sets E^ and E^′ . 快速适应增量知识库。为在确保准确且相关响应的同时高效适应不断变化的数据，我们的 LightRAG 对知识库进行增量更新，无需对整个外部数据库进行完全重新处理。对于新文档 D′，增量更新算法使用与之前相同的基于图的索引步骤 φ 处理它，生成 D^′=(V^′,E^′)。随后，LightRAG 通过对节点集 V^ 与 V^′ 以及边集 E^ 与 E^′ 取并集，将新图数据与原图合并。

                                                                                                    Two key objectives guide our approach to fast adaptation for the incremental knowledge base: Seamless Integration of New Data. By applying a consistent methodology to new information, the incremental update module allows the LightRAG to integrate new external databases without disrupting the existing graph structure. This approach preserves the integrity of established connections, ensuring that historical data remains accessible while enriching the graph without conflicts or redundancies. Reducing Computational Overhead . By eliminating the need to rebuild the entire index graph, this method reduces computational overhead and facilitates the rapid assimilation of new data. Consequently, LightRAG maintains system accuracy, provides current information, and conserves resources, ensuring users receive timely updates and enhancing the overall RAG effectiveness. 两个关键目标指导我们对增量知识库的快速适应方法：无缝集成新数据。通过对新信息应用一致的方法，增量更新模块使 LightRAG 能在不破坏现有图结构的情况下整合新的外部数据库。这种方法保留已建立连接的完整性，确保历史数据可访问，同时在不产生冲突或冗余的前提下丰富图谱。降低计算开销。通过消除重建整个索引图的需求，该方法降低了计算开销并便于快速同化新数据。因此，LightRAG 保持系统准确性，提供最新信息并节约资源，确保用户及时获得更新，提升整体 RAG 效果。

                                                                                                    3.2 DUAL-LEVEL RETRIEVAL PARADIGM

                                                                                                    3.2 双层检索范式

                                                                                                    To retrieve relevant information from both specific document chunks and their complex interdependencies, our LightRAG proposes generating query keys at both detailed and abstract levels. 为了从具体文档片段及其复杂相互依赖中检索相关信息，我们的 LightRAG 提出在细化与抽象两个层面生成查询键。

                                                                                                    • Specific Queries. These queries are detail-oriented and typically reference specific entities within the graph, requiring precise retrieval of information associated with particular nodes or edges. For example, a specific query might be, "Who wrote 'Pride and Prejudice'?"

                                                                                                    • 具体查询。这类查询面向细节，通常指向图中具体实体，需精确检索与特定节点或边相关的信息。例如，一个具体查询可能是，“谁写了《傲慢与偏见》？”

                                                                                                    • Abstract Queries. In contrast, abstract queries are more conceptual, encompassing broader topics, summaries, or overarching themes that are not directly tied to specific entities. An example of an abstract query is, "How does artificial intelligence influence modern education?"

                                                                                                    • 抽象查询。相反，抽象查询更具概念性，涵盖更广泛的话题、摘要或总体主题，不直接绑定于具体实体。一个抽象查询的例子是，“人工智能如何影响现代教育？”

                                                                                                    To accommodate diverse query types, the LightRAG employs two distinct retrieval strategies within the dual-level retrieval paradigm. This ensures that both specific and abstract inquiries are addressed effectively, allowing the system to deliver relevant responses tailored to user needs. 为适应多样的查询类型，LightRAG 在双层检索范式中采用两种不同的检索策略。这确保具体与抽象查询都能得到有效响应，使系统能够根据用户需求提供相关答案。

                                                                                                    • Low-Level Retrieval. This level is primarily focused on retrieving specific entities along with their associated attributes or relationships. Queries at this level are detail-oriented and aim to extract precise information about particular nodes or edges within the graph.

                                                                                                    • 低层检索。该层主要侧重检索具体实体及其关联属性或关系。此层的查询注重细节，旨在提取图中某些节点或边的精确信息。

                                                                                                    • High-Level Retrieval. This level addresses broader topics and overarching themes. Queries at this level aggregate information across multiple related entities and relationships, providing insights into higher-level concepts and summaries rather than specific details.

                                                                                                    • 高层检索。该层处理更广泛的话题和总体主题。此层的查询在多个相关实体和关系间聚合信息，提供关于高层概念和摘要的见解，而非具体细节。

                                                                                                    Integrating Graph and Vectors for Efficient Retrieval. By combining graph structures with vector representations, the model gains a deeper insight into the interrelationships among entities. This synergy enables the retrieval algorithm to effectively utilize both local and global keywords, streamlining the search process and improving the relevance of results. 整合图与向量以实现高效检索。通过将图结构与向量表示结合，模型能够更深入地理解实体间的相互关系。这种协同使检索算法能够有效利用局部与全局关键词，简化搜索过程并提高结果相关性。

                                                                                                    • (i) Query Keyword Extraction. For a given query q ,the retrieval algorithm of LightRAG begins by extracting both local query keywords k(l) and global query keywords k(g) .

                                                                                                    • (i) 查询关键词提取。对于给定查询 q ，LightRAG 的检索算法首先提取局部查询关键词 k(l) 和全局查询关键词 k(g) 。

                                                                                                    • (ii) Keyword Matching. The algorithm uses an efficient vector database to match local query keywords with candidate entities and global query keywords with relations linked to global keys.

                                                                                                    • (ii) 关键词匹配。该算法使用高效的向量数据库将局部查询关键词与候选实体匹配，将全局查询关键词与与全局键关联的关系匹配。

                                                                                                    • (iii) Incorporating High-Order Relatedness. To enhance the query with higher-order relatedness, LightRAGfurther gathers neighboring nodes within the local subgraphs of the retrieved graph elements. This process involves the set {vi∣vi∈V∧(vi∈Nv∨vi∈Ne)} ,where Nv and Ne represent the one-hop neighboring nodes of the retrieved nodes v and edges e ,respectively.

                                                                                                    • (iii) 纳入高阶相关性。为了用更高阶的相关性增强查询，LightRAG 进一步收集被检索图元素的局部子图内的邻近节点。该过程涉及集合 {vi∣vi∈V∧(vi∈Nv∨vi∈Ne)} ，其中 Nv 和 Ne 分别表示被检索节点 v 和边 e 的一跳邻居节点。

                                                                                                    This dual-level retrieval paradigm not only facilitates efficient retrieval of related entities and relations through keyword matching, but also enhances the comprehensiveness of results by integrating relevant structural information from the constructed knowledge graph. 该双层检索范式不仅通过关键词匹配促进相关实体与关系的高效检索，还通过整合构建的知识图中的相关结构信息提升结果的全面性。

                                                                                                    3.4 COMPLEXITY ANALYSIS OF THE LIGHTRAG FRAMEWORK

                                                                                                    3.4 LIGHTRAG 框架的复杂性分析

                                                                                                    In this section, we analyze the complexity of our proposed LightRAG framework, which can be divided into two main parts. The first part is the graph-based Index phase. During this phase, we use the large language model (LLM) to extract entities and relationships from each chunk of text. As a result, the LLM needs to be called chunk size times. Importantly, there is no additional overhead involved in this process, making our approach highly efficient in managing updates to new text. 在本节中，我们分析所提出的 LightRAG 框架的复杂性，可分为两大部分。第一部分是基于图的索引阶段。在此阶段，我们使用大型语言模型（LLM）从每个文本块中提取实体和关系。因此，LLM 需要被调用与块数相同的次数。重要的是，该过程不涉及额外开销，使我们的方法在管理新文本更新时极为高效。

                                                                                                    The second part of the process involves the graph-based retrieval phase. For each query, we first utilize the large language model (LLM) to generate relevant keywords. Similar to current Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems Gao et al. (2023; 2022); Chan et al. (2024), our retrieval mechanism relies on vector-based search. However, instead of retrieving chunks as in conventional RAG, we concentrate on retrieving entities and relationships. This approach markedly reduces retrieval overhead compared to the community-based traversal method used in GraphRAG. 第二部分是基于图的检索阶段。对于每个查询，我们首先利用大型语言模型（LLM）生成相关关键词。与现有的检索增强生成（RAG）系统 Gao et al. (2023; 2022); Chan et al. (2024) 类似，我们的检索机制依赖于基于向量的搜索。然而，与传统 RAG 检索文本块不同，我们侧重于检索实体和关系。与 GraphRAG 使用的基于社群的遍历方法相比，该方法显著降低了检索开销。

                                                                                                    5.1 与大模型结合的检索增强生成

                                                                                                    Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems enhance LLM inputs by retrieving relevant information from external sources, grounding responses in factual, domain-specific knowledge Ram et al. (2023); Fan et al. (2024). Current RAG approaches Gao et al. (2022); 2023); Chan et al. (2024); Yu et al. (2024) typically embed queries in a vector space to find the nearest context vectors. However, many of these methods rely on fragmented text chunks and only retrieve the top-k contexts, limiting their ability to capture comprehensive global information needed for effective responses. 检索增强生成（RAG）系统通过从外部来源检索相关信息来强化大模型输入，使回答以事实性、领域特定知识为依据 Ram et al. (2023); Fan et al. (2024)。当前的 RAG 方法 Gao et al. (2022); 2023); Chan et al. (2024); Yu et al. (2024) 通常将查询嵌入向量空间以查找最近的上下文向量。然而，许多方法依赖于碎片化的文本块且仅检索 top-k 上下文，限制了其捕捉生成有效回答所需的全面全局信息的能力。

                                                                                                    Although recent studies Edge et al. (2024) have explored using graph structures for knowledge representation, two key limitations persist. First, these approaches often lack the capability for dynamic updates and expansions of the knowledge graph, making it difficult to incorporate new information effectively. In contrast, our proposed model, LightRAG, addresses these challenges by enabling the RAG system to quickly adapt to new information, ensuring the model's timeliness and accuracy. Additionally, existing methods often rely on brute-force searches for each generated community, which are inefficient for large-scale queries. Our LightRAG framework overcomes this limitation by facilitating rapid retrieval of relevant information from the graph through our proposed dual-level retrieval paradigm, significantly enhancing both retrieval efficiency and response speed. 尽管近期研究 Edge et al. (2024) 探讨了使用图结构进行知识表示，但仍存在两大关键局限。首先，这些方法往往缺乏对知识图进行动态更新与扩展的能力，难以有效纳入新信息。与之相对，我们提出的 LightRAG 通过使 RAG 系统能够快速适应新信息来解决这些挑战，确保模型的时效性与准确性。此外，现有方法常对每个生成的社区依赖暴力搜索，这对于大规模查询效率低下。我们的 LightRAG 框架通过提出的双层检索范式，使得从图中快速检索相关信息成为可能，显著提升了检索效率与响应速度。

                                                                                                    5.2 LARGE LANGUAGE MODEL FOR GRAPHS

                                                                                                    5.2 面向图的巨型语言模型

                                                                                                    Graphs are a powerful framework for representing complex relationships and find applications in numerous fields. As Large Language Models (LLMs) continue to evolve, researchers have increasingly focused on enhancing their capability to interpret graph-structured data. This body of work can be divided into three primary categories: i) GNNs as Prefix where Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) are utilized as the initial processing layer for graph data, generating structure-aware tokens that LLMs can use during inference. Notable examples include GraphGPT Tang et al. (2024) and LLaGA Chen et al. (2024). ii) LLMs as Prefix involves LLMs processing graph data enriched with textual information to produce node embeddings or labels, ultimately refining the training process for GNNs, as demonstrated in systems like GALM Xie et al. (2023) and OFA Liu et al. (2024). iii) LLMs-Graphs Integration focuses on achieving a seamless interaction between LLMs and graph data, employing techniques such as fusion training and GNN alignment, and developing LLM-based agents capable of engaging with graph information directly Li et al. (2023); Brannon et al. (2023). 图是表示复杂关系的强大框架，并在众多领域中得到应用。随着巨型语言模型（LLMs）的不断发展，研究者越来越关注增强其解读图结构数据的能力。这类工作可分为三类：i) 将图神经网络（GNNs）作为前缀，即将 GNN 作为图数据的初始处理层，生成结构感知的标记供 LLM 在推理时使用，代表性例子包括 GraphGPT Tang et al. (2024) 与 LLaGA Chen et al. (2024)。ii) 将 LLMs 作为前缀，指 LLM 处理富含文本信息的图数据以生成节点嵌入或标签，进而优化 GNN 的训练流程，如 GALM Xie et al. (2023) 与 OFA Liu et al. (2024) 等系统所示。iii) LLMs-图集成，侧重实现 LLM 与图数据的无缝交互，采用融合训练与 GNN 对齐等技术，并开发能够直接与图信息交互的基于 LLM 的智能体 Li et al. (2023); Brannon et al. (2023)。

                                                                                                    6 结论

                                                                                                    This work introduces an advancement in Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) through the integration of a graph-based indexing approach that enhances both efficiency and comprehension in information retrieval. LightRAG utilizes a comprehensive knowledge graph to facilitate rapid and relevant document retrieval, enabling a deeper understanding of complex queries. Its dual-level retrieval paradigm allows for the extraction of both specific and abstract information, catering to diverse user needs. Furthermore, LightRAG's seamless incremental update capability ensures that the system remains current and responsive to new information, thereby maintaining its effectiveness over time. Overall, LightRAG excels in both efficiency and effectiveness, significantly improving the speed and quality of information retrieval and generation while reducing costs for LLM inference. 本工作提出通过引入基于图的索引方法来改进检索增强生成（RAG），从而提升信息检索的效率与理解能力。LightRAG 利用全面的知识图谱实现快速且相关的文档检索，促进对复杂查询的更深刻理解。其双层检索范式兼顾具体与抽象信息的提取，以满足多样化用户需求。此外，LightRAG 无缝的增量更新能力确保系统对新信息保持同步与响应，从而随时间维持其有效性。总体而言，LightRAG 在效率与效果上表现卓越，显著提升信息检索与生成的速度与质量，同时降低 LLM 推理成本。
