Abstract. The task of open-set domain generalization (OSDG) involves recognizing novel classes within unseen domains, which becomes more challenging with multiple modalities as input. Existing works have only addressed unimodal OSDG within the meta-learning framework, without considering multimodal scenarios. In this work, we introduce a novel approach to address Multimodal Open-Set Domain Generalization (MM-OSDG) for the first time, utilizing self-supervision. To this end, we introduce two innovative multimodal self-supervised pretext tasks: Masked Cross-modal Translation and Multimodal Jigsaw Puzzles. These tasks facilitate the learning of multimodal representative features, thereby enhancing generalization and open-class detection capabilities. Additionally, we propose a novel entropy weighting mechanism to balance the loss across different modalities. Furthermore, we extend our approach to tackle also the Multimodal Open-Set Domain Adaptation (MM-OSDA) problem, especially in scenarios where unlabeled data from the target domain is available. Extensive experiments conducted under MM-OSDG, MM-OSDA, and Multimodal Closed-Set DG settings on the EPIC-Kitchens and HAC datasets demonstrate the efficacy and versatility of the proposed approach. Our source code is publicly available 1 .

An inherent assumption in both DG and multimodal DG is the alignment of label spaces between source and target domains. However, real-world applications, such as autonomous driving [3], often feature target domains with novel categories not present in the source label space. As a result, the learned model may struggle with samples from these novel categories, significantly degrading the robustness of existing DG and multimodal DG methods. This setup, where the target domain contains unknown or open classes not seen in source domains, is referred to as open-set DG. Several unimodal open-set DG approaches, including DAML [57] and MEDIC [62], have been developed within the meta-learning framework to tackle this issue. CrossMatch [67] utilizes an adversarial data augmentation strategy to generate auxiliary samples beyond the source label space. Yet, none specifically address the challenge of Multimodal Open-Set DG (MM-OSDG), which is the primary focus of this paper. The goal of MM-OSDG is to train a model using data from several source domains across two or more modalities, enabling it to effectively generalize to previously unseen target domains with the same modalities and including samples from unknown classes. The key challenge of MM-OSDG is to efficiently leverage complementary information from diverse modalities to improve generalization and open-class detection performance, areas where current unimodal open-set DG approaches fall short. To address the challenge of domain shift in unimodal setups, several studies have explored the integration of self-supervision techniques for DG and DA [4,6] . This approach involves addressing a self-supervised pretext task concurrently with the primary supervised problem, which leads to the learning of resilient cross-domain features conducive to robust generalization. Additionally, recent research has demonstrated the utility of leveraging outputs from self-supervised models for anomaly detection, facilitating the discrimination between normal and anomalous samples [2,21] - a scenario similar to open-class detection. In particular, Rotation-based Open Set (ROS) [4] explores rotation recognition as a self-supervised task for both unknown class detection and domain adaptation. Meanwhile, results from MOOD [39] illustrate that a reconstruction-based pretext task forces the network to learn the real data distribution of the in-distribution (ID) samples, thereby enlarging the divergence between the out-of-distribution (OOD) and ID samples.
为了解决单模态设置中领域偏移的挑战，许多研究探讨了将自监督技术融入域适应（DG）和领域自适应（DA）[4,6] 的方法。这种方法涉及同时解决一个自监督的辅助任务和主要的监督问题，从而学习到有助于稳健泛化的弹性跨领域特征。此外，近期研究已经证明了利用自监督模型的输出进行异常检测的效用，这有助于区分正常样本和异常样本 [2,21] ——这与开放类检测的情景相似。特别是，基于旋转的开集（ROS）[4] 探索了将旋转识别作为一个自监督任务，用于未知类检测和领域自适应。同时，MOOD [39] 的结果表明，基于重建的辅助任务迫使网络学习分布内（ID）样本的真实数据分布，从而增大了分布外（OOD）样本与ID样本之间的差异。
Inspired by the success of the self-supervised pretext tasks in robust feature learning and OOD sample detection in unimodal setups, we propose a novel approach referred to as MOOSA to address the Multimodal Open-Set Domain Generalization and Adaptation problems using multimodal self-supervised tasks. Specifically, we propose a generative task, termed Masked Cross-modal Translation, and a contrastive task, denoted as Multimodal Jigsaw Puzzles. These tasks are complementary to each other to effectively learn multimodal representative features conducive to both generalization and open-class detection. An entropy weighting mechanism is further introduced to balance the loss across different modalities. Additionally, our methodology is extended to handle the Multimodal Open-Set Domain Adaptation (MM-OSDA) problem in scenarios where unlabeled target data is available. The efficacy of the proposed approach is thoroughly validated through extensive experiments conducted on two multimodal DG benchmark datasets: EPIC-Kitchens [10] and Human-Animal-Cartoon (HAC) [14]. 2 Related Work
2 相关工作

Self-supervised Learning generates supervisory signals based on the input data, alleviating the need for expensive manual labeling and improving performance across almost all types of downstream tasks, including domain generalization [6] and anomaly detection [21]. The majority of self-supervised tasks have so far focused on a single modality. Examples of such self-supervised tasks include predicting the relative position of image patches [12], colorizing grayscale images [34], recognizing the rotation of an image [20], or inpainting removed patches [51]. Recently, some approaches have been proposed to tackle the specific challenges of multimodal data, such as multimodal alignment [47], relative norm alignment [53], and cross-modal translation [14].
自监督学习基于输入数据生成监督信号，减轻了对昂贵的人工标注的依赖，并在几乎所有类型的下游任务中提高了性能，包括域泛化 [6] 和异常检测 [21]。到目前为止，大多数自监督任务都集中在单一模态上。这类自监督任务的例子包括预测图像补丁的相对位置 [12]、为灰度图像上色 [34]、识别图像的旋转 [20] 或修复移除的补丁 [51]。最近，一些方法被提出以解决多模态数据的特定挑战，例如多模态对齐 [47]、相对范数对齐 [53] 和跨模态翻译 [14]。
Domain Generalization entails training a model on multiple source domains to achieve robust generalization to previously unseen target domains. This is different from domain adaptation, where unlabeled target domain data is available during training. Prior research [61] has identified three main categories of domain generalization methods: data manipulation, representation learning, and learning strategies. Strategies involving data manipulation aim to enhance generalization performance by augmenting the diversity of training data [58,64,66] . Representation learning methods strive to acquire domain-invariant representations employing techniques such as domain-adversarial neural networks [19,38] , explicit feature distribution alignment [59], and instance normalization [49]. Additionally, various approaches leverage learning strategies to improve generalization performance, including meta-learning [37], gradient operation [28], and self-supervised learning [6].
域泛化指的是在多个源域上训练模型以实现到之前未见过的目标域的稳健泛化。这与域适应不同，后者在训练期间有来自目标域的无标签数据可用。先前的研究 [61] 将域泛化方法分为三大类：数据操作、表征学习和学习策略。涉及数据操作策略的目的是通过增加训练数据的多样性来提高泛化性能 [58,64,66]。表征学习方法努力获取域不变表征，采用诸如域对抗性神经网络 [19,38]、显式特征分布对齐 [59] 和实例归一化 [49] 等技术。此外，各种方法利用学习策略来提高泛化性能，包括元学习 [37]、梯度操作 [28] 和自监督学习 [6]。
Open-Set DA and DG address scenarios where the target domain, with domain shifts, encompasses classes not present in the source domain [50]. In the context of open-set DA, several approaches have been proposed to tackle this challenge, such as assigning target domain images to source categories while discarding unrelated target domain images [50], and employing adversarial training to separate unknown target samples [56]. Separate To Adapt (STA) [41] introduces a progressive separation mechanism for unknown and known class samples. ROS [4] investigates the utility of rotation recognition for unknown class detection. Adjustment and Alignment (ANNA) [40] addresses semantic-level bias by incorporating front-door adjustment and decoupled causal alignment modules. For open-set DG, Domain-Augmented Meta-Learning (DAML) [57] adopts a meta-learning framework to learn open-domain generalizable representations and augment domains at both feature and label levels. CrossMatch [67] employs an adversarial data augmentation strategy to generate auxiliary samples beyond the source label space. MEDIC [62] considers gradient matching towards inter-domain and inter-class splits simultaneously to find a generalizable boundary balanced for all tasks. However, none of these approaches tackle the challenge posed by multiple modalities.
开集域自适应（Open-Set DA）和开集域泛化（Open-Set DG）处理的是目标域中包含源域中不存在类别的场景，这些场景存在域偏移 [50]。在开集域自适应的背景下，已经提出了几种方法来应对这一挑战，例如将目标域图像分配到源类别，同时丢弃无关的目标域图像 [50]，以及采用对抗训练来分离未知的目标样本 [56]。Separate To Adapt (STA) [41] 引入了一种渐进式分离机制，用于未知和已知类别的样本。ROS [4] 调查了旋转识别在未知类别检测中的效用。Adjustment and Alignment (ANNA) [40] 通过结合前门调整和去耦因果对齐模块来解决语义级别的偏差。对于开集域泛化，Domain-Augmented Meta-Learning (DAML) [57] 采用元学习框架来学习开域可泛化的表示，并在特征和标签层面增强域。CrossMatch [67] 采用对抗性数据增强策略来生成超出源标签空间的辅助样本。MEDIC [62] 同时考虑了针对跨域和跨类分割的梯度匹配，以找到适用于所有任务的平衡泛化边界。然而，这些方法中没有一种解决多模态带来的挑战。
Multimodal DA and DG tackle the challenges of DA and DG involving multiple modalities. For instance, MM-SADA [47] proposes a self-supervised alignment approach coupled with adversarial alignment for multimodal DA, while Kim et al. [32] leverage cross-modal contrastive learning to align cross-modal and cross-domain representations. Zhang et al. [65] introduce an audio-adaptive encoder and an audio-infused recognizer to address domain shifts. RNA-Net [53] addresses the multimodal DG problem by introducing a relative norm alignment loss to balance audio and video feature norms. SimMMDG [14] presents a universal framework for multimodal domain generalization, involving the separation of features within each modality into modality-specific and modality-shared components, along with constraints to facilitate meaningful representation learning. Different from prior efforts, our work represents the first attempt to address the Multimodal Open-Set DG and DA, scenarios that are both more practical and significantly more challenging.
多模态DA和DG解决了涉及多种模态的DA和DG的挑战。例如，MM-SADA [47] 提出了一个自监督对齐方法，结合对抗性对齐进行多模态DA，而Kim等人 [32] 利用跨模态对比学习来对齐跨模态和跨域表示。Zhang等人 [65] 引入了一种音频自适应编码器和一个音频增强识别器来解决域偏移问题。RNA-Net [53] 通过引入相对范数对齐损失来平衡音频和视频特征范数，解决了多模态DG问题。SimMMDG [14] 提出了一个用于多模态域泛化的通用框架，涉及将每种模态内的特征分离为模态特定和模态共享组件，以及施加约束以促进有意义的表示学习。与之前的努力不同，我们的工作是首次尝试解决多模态开放集DG和DA问题，这些场景既更具实际性，也更具挑战性。

3.1 Multimodal Open-Set Domain Generalization
3.1 多模态开放集域泛化

In the context of MM-OSDG, we work with several source domains, denoted as S={D1,D2,…,DS} ,each sharing a common label space C . We also consider an unseen target domain T={DT} that introduces an extended label space C∪U ,with the stipulation that C and U do not overlap C∩U=∅ . Each source domain,indexed by s and comprising Ns samples,is represented as Ds= {(xis,yis)}i=1Ns following the distribution PXYs . Here, xis denotes the i -th sample from the sample space X and yis is the corresponding label,which belongs to the shared label space C . Each sample instance xis is comprised of M modalities, denoted as xis={(xis)k∣k=1,⋯,M} . The joint distributions between each pair of domains differ: PXYi≠PXYj,1≤i≠j≤S . The objective of MM-OSDG is to develop a model f:X→Y that leverages data from multiple source domains S with a label space C and M modalities. This model aims to seamlessly adapt to unseen target domains DT that include both known classes from C and unknown classes from U ,with the combined label space being C∪U . Consequently, the model is expected to accurately classify each target sample into the appropriate class from C ,or designate it as "unknown" if it falls within U . Importantly,during training,data from the target domain DT is inaccessible, and there is a distribution shift between the source and the target domains, PXYT≠PXYs for all s∈{1,⋯,S} .
在MM-OSDG的背景下，我们处理多个源域，记为 S={D1,D2,…,DS}，每个源域共享一个公共标签空间 C。我们还考虑了一个不可见的目标域 T={DT}，它引入了一个扩展的标签空间 C∪U，规定 C 和 U 不重叠 C∩U=∅。每个源域，由索引 s 表示并包含 Ns 个样本，表示为 Ds= {(xis,yis)}i=1Ns 遵循分布 PXYs。在这里，xis 表示样本空间 X 中的第 i 个样本，yis 是相应的标签，属于共享的标签空间 C。每个样本实例 xis 由 M 模态组成，记为 xis={(xis)k∣k=1,⋯,M}。每对域之间的联合分布不同：PXYi≠PXYj,1≤i≠j≤S。MM-OSDG的目标是开发一个模型 f:X→Y，该模型利用来自多个源域 S 的数据以及 C 和 M 模态。该模型旨在无缝适应包含 C 中的已知类别和 U 中的未知类别的未见目标域 DT，组合的标签空间为 C∪U。因此，模型预计能够准确地将每个目标样本分类到 C 中的适当类别，或者如果它落在 U 中，则将其指定为“未知”。重要的是，在训练过程中，目标域 DT 的数据是无法访问的，并且源域和目标域之间存在分布偏移 PXYT≠PXYs 对于所有 s∈{1,⋯,S}。

3.2 Motivation: Self-supervised Pretext Tasks
3.2 动机：自监督预训练任务

Self-supervised pretext tasks are commonly classified into generative and contrastive methods [43]. Contrastive approaches, such as context spatial relations [20,45] and instance discrimination [8,24] ,focus on encouraging class-invariance between different instances [43], thereby contributing to generalization. However, recent findings [55] indicate that contrastive methods may tend to learn specific patterns of categories, which are beneficial for classification, but less conducive to understanding intrinsic data distributions of in-distribution samples. In contrast,generative methods [11,23] force the network to learn the real data distribution of in-distribution samples during training, enhancing the divergence between in-distribution and out-of-distribution samples [39], making them particularly valuable for open-class detection. Motivated by these observations, we propose using both generative and contrastive tasks to achieve robust generalization and reliable open-class detection. Accordingly, we propose two novel multimodal self-supervised pretext tasks aligned with these two objectives. Multimodal self-supervised tasks are challenging, requiring the joint consideration of all modality properties to ensure meaningful outcomes.
自监督预训练任务通常被分类为生成方法和对比方法 [43]。对比方法，如上下文空间关系 [20,45] 和实例区分 [8,24]，专注于促进不同实例之间的类不变性 [43]，从而有助于泛化。然而，最近的研究发现 [55] 表明，对比方法可能倾向于学习对分类有益的特定类别模式，但不太有助于理解分布内样本的内在数据分布。相比之下，生成方法 [11,23] 在训练期间强制网络学习分布内样本的真实数据分布，增强分布内和分布外样本之间的差异 [39]，使其对于开放类检测特别有价值。受到这些观察的启发，我们提出同时使用生成和对比任务来实现稳健的泛化和可靠的开放类检测。因此，我们提出了两个新颖的多模态自监督预训练任务，与这两个目标保持一致。多模态自监督任务具有挑战性，需要同时考虑所有模态的属性以确保有意义的成果。

3.4 Contrastive Task: Multimodal Jigsaw Puzzles
3.4 对比任务：多模态拼图游戏

Context-based spatial relation prediction [12,20] falls within the category of contrastive objectives [43]. Previous studies [6,48] have explored learning visual representations through solving Jigsaw puzzles, a task that involves recovering an original image from its shuffled parts. In this work, we extend the Jigsaw puzzle-solving task to multimodal scenarios. Given the heterogeneity of different modalities, shuffling them in the input space and then randomly combining them poses a challenge. Therefore, our approach involves shuffling in the embedding space for all modalities. Given the embedding Ei of the i -th modality,we split it into P parts as Ei=[Ei,1,Ei,2,…,Ei,P] with equal length. Similarly,for the j -th modality,we have Ej=[Ej,1,Ej,2,…,Ej,P] . Combining all M⋅P split parts from M modalities,we randomly shuffle them to generate different permutations. For example,with two modalities i and j and by setting P to 2,one possible permutation is E~p=[Ej,1,Ei,2,Ej,2,Ei,1] . The shuffling is performed across modalities,which makes the task particularly challenging. Out of the (M⋅P) ! possible permutations,we randomly select a set of Q elements and assign an index to each entry as the label. Then, we can define an auxiliary classification task on each sample instance as {(E~p,p)}p=1Q ,where E~p indicates the recomposed embeddings and p∈{1,…,Q} denotes the corresponding permutation index. To achieve this,we aim to minimize the multimodal jigsaw loss LMulJig (hp(E~p),p) , were hp is the classifier for permutation recognition,and LMulJig  is the standard cross-entropy loss. Fig. 1 (c) provides an illustration of this process.
基于上下文的空间关系预测 [12,20] 属于对比目标 [43] 的范畴。之前的研究 [6,48] 已经探讨了通过解决拼图难题来学习视觉表示，这是一项涉及从打乱的部分恢复原始图像的任务。在这项工作中，我们将拼图解谜任务扩展到多模态场景。鉴于不同模态的异质性，将它们在输入空间中打乱然后随机组合构成了一项挑战。因此，我们的方法包括对所有模态的嵌入空间进行打乱。给定 i -th 模态的嵌入 Ei，我们将其分为 P 个长度相等的部分，表示为 Ei=[Ei,1,Ei,2,…,Ei,P]。类似地，对于 j -th 模态，我们有 Ej=[Ej,1,Ej,2,…,Ej,P]。将所有 M 模态的 M⋅P 分割部分组合起来，然后随机打乱它们以生成不同的排列。例如，如果有两种模态 i 和 j，并且将 P 设置为 2，则一种可能的排列是 E~p=[Ej,1,Ei,2,Ej,2,Ei,1]。这种打乱是在模态之间进行的，这使得任务特别具有挑战性。在所有可能的 (M⋅P) ! 排列中，我们随机选择一组 Q 元素并为每个条目分配一个索引作为标签。然后，我们可以将每个样本实例定义为一个辅助分类任务 {(E~p,p)}p=1Q，其中 E~p 表示重新组合的嵌入，p∈{1,…,Q} 表示相应的排列索引。为了实现这一点，我们旨在最小化多模态拼图损失 LMulJig (hp(E~p),p)，其中 hp 是用于排列识别的分类器，LMulJig  是标准交叉熵损失。图 1 (c) 提供了此过程的示意图。
By solving the multimodal Jigsaw puzzles problem, the model must not only to recover the correct order of the shuffled embeddings for each modality but also discern the source modality of each shuffled part. This process enables the model to learn multimodal representative features conducive to robust generalization.
通过解决多模态拼图难题，模型不仅必须恢复每种模态的打乱嵌入的正确顺序，还要辨别每个打乱部分的来源模态。这个过程使模型能够学习到有利于稳健泛化的多模态表征特征。

Evaluation Metrics. For performance evaluation, we employ three widely recognized evaluation metrics commonly used in OSDA literature [4,40,41] . In OSDA,rather than using average per-class accuracy OS ,we present results using the harmonic mean HOS. The HOS is calculated as 2×OS∗×UNKOS∗+UNK ,where OS∗ represents the accuracy in known categories,and UNK represents the accuracy in unknown classes. The HOS metric offers a balance between accuracy in known and unknown categories, providing a more nuanced evaluation than OS alone. This distinction is particularly important in scenarios where the accuracy of unknown classes is notably low, emphasizing the significance of effective detection of unknown categories. The HOS is alternatively denoted as H-score in some paper [57,62] .
评估指标。为了性能评估，我们采用了三种在OSDA文献中广泛认可的评估指标 [4,40,41] 。在OSDA中，我们并没有使用每类平均准确度 OS ，而是使用调和平均数HOS来呈现结果。HOS的计算方式为 2×OS∗×UNKOS∗+UNK ，其中 OS∗ 代表已知类别的准确度，UNK 代表未知类别的准确度。HOS指标在已知和未知类别准确度之间提供了平衡，比单独使用OS提供了更细致的评估。在未知类别准确度特别低的情况下，这种区分尤其重要，强调了有效检测未知类别的重要性。在某些论文中，HOS也被称作H-score [57,62] 。

5 Conclusion
5 结论

In this paper, we introduce the Multimodal Open-Set Domain Generalization problem and propose a novel solution MOOSA employing self-supervised pretext tasks. Our approach involves the development of two complementary multimodal self-supervised tasks, namely Masked Cross-modal Translation and Multimodal Jigsaw Puzzles, aimed at learning multimodal representative features for robust generalization and effective open-class detection. To maintain a balance in the loss across different modalities, we introduce an Entropy Weighting mechanism during training. Furthermore, we extend our framework to address Multimodal Open-Set Domain Adaptation. Through extensive evaluations on the EPIC-Kitchens and HAC datasets, our method demonstrates superior performance compared to competing baselines. Notably, our approach exhibits robustness across variations in label sets, openness levels, and parameter choices. In future work, methods proposed in Multimodal OOD Detection [16] could be good alternatives for open-class detection.
在本文中，我们介绍了多模态开集领域泛化问题，并提出了一个新颖的解决方案 MOOSA，该方案采用自监督的前置任务。我们的方法包括开发两种互补的多模态自监督任务，即遮蔽跨模态翻译和多模态拼图游戏，旨在学习多模态表征特征以实现鲁棒泛化和有效的开类检测。为了在不同模态之间保持损失的平衡，我们在训练过程中引入了熵加权机制。此外，我们还扩展了我们的框架以解决多模态开集领域适应问题。通过对 EPIC-Kitchens 和 HAC 数据集的广泛评估，我们的方法与竞争基线相比展示了优越的性能。值得注意的是，我们的方法在标签集、开放性水平以及参数选择的变化上表现出鲁棒性。在未来的工作中，多模态OOD检测[16]中提出的方法可能成为开类检测的良好替代方案。

B Implementation Details
B 实施细节

Our experiments encompass three modalities: video, audio, and optical flow. We adopt SimMMDG [14] as the base framework by splitting the features within each modality into modality-specific and modality-shared components due to its demonstrated efficacy in multimodal DG tasks. In line with [14], we employ the MMAction2 [9] toolkit for experiments. For encoding visual information, we utilize the SlowFast network [17] initialized with Kinetics-400 [30] pre-trained weights. The audio encoder employs ResNet-18 [25], initialized with weights from the VGGSound pre-trained checkpoint [7]. Similarly, the optical flow encoder employs the SlowFast network with a slow-only pathway, initialized with Kinetics-400 [30] pre-trained weights. The dimensions of the unimodal embedding E for video,audio,and optical flow are 2304,512,and 2048,respectively. We employ a multi-layer perceptron with two hidden layers of size 2048 to instantiate the masked cross-modal translation MLPEi→Ej and select a mask ratio β of 0.7 . For multimodal Jigsaw puzzles,we set P to 4 and Q to 128 . The classifier hp is an MLP with two layers and hidden size 512. The Adam optimizer [33] is utilized with a learning rate of 0.0001 and a batch size of 16 . The scalar temperature parameter T in entropy weighting is set to 1.0 . Additionally,we set α1=0.1,α2=1.0 ,and α3=0.1 . Finally,the network is trained for 20 epochs on an RTX 3090 GPU, requiring approximately 20 hours. The model with the best performance on the validation dataset is selected.

E Motivation behind MOOSA for Open-set DG Setting
E 开放集域泛化设置下MOOSA背后的动机

For Open-set DG setting, we need to address both domain shift and category shift. Self-supervised tasks (i.e., solving Jigsaw puzzles [6], predicting image rotations [4]) have shown their efficacy in mitigating domain shift by capturing invariance to bridge domain gaps. Inspired by solving unimodal Jigsaw puzzles on images, we propose the novel Multimodal Jigsaw Puzzles (MulJig) in multimodal scenarios to learn domain-invariant features and address the high heterogeneity across modalities by random shuffling in the embedding space. For category shift, the key lies in detecting unknown classes in target domain. Masked image modeling [39] has shown its efficacy in unknown class detection by forcing the network to learn the real data distribution of known class samples during training, enhancing the divergence between known and unknown samples. Inspired by this, we introduce the novel Masked Cross-modal Translation (MaskedTrans) to facilitate the model in learning intrinsic data distributions for known classes across diverse modalities. MaskedTrans and MulJig are inherently complementary in addressing both domain and category shifts effectively.
在开放集DG设置中，我们需要解决领域偏移和类别偏移的问题。自监督任务（例如，解决拼图游戏 [6]，预测图像旋转 [4]）通过捕获不变性来桥接领域差异，已证明其在减轻领域偏移方面的有效性。受到在图像上解决单模态拼图游戏的启发，我们在多模态场景中提出了新颖的多模态拼图游戏（MulJig），以学习领域不变特征，并通过在嵌入空间中的随机洗牌来解决模态之间的高度异质性。对于类别偏移，关键在于检测目标领域中的未知类别。遮蔽图像建模 [39] 通过在训练期间强制网络学习已知类别样本的真实数据分布，增强了已知样本与未知样本之间的差异，已证明其在未知类别检测方面的有效性。受到这一点的启发，我们引入了新颖的遮蔽跨模态翻译（MaskedTrans），以帮助模型跨不同模态学习已知类别的内在数据分布。MaskedTrans和MulJig在解决领域和类别偏移方面本质上是互补的。
