Flow matching (FM) is a recent generative modelling paradigm which has rapidly been gaining popularity in the deep probabilistic ML community. Flow matching combines aspects from Continuous Normalising Flows (CNFs) and Diffusion Models (DMs), alleviating key issues both methods have. In this blogpost we' II cover the main ideas and unique properties of FM models starting from the basics.

流匹配（Flow Matching，FM）是一种新兴的生成建模范式，近年来在深度概率机器学习社区迅速流行起来。流匹配结合了连续归一化流（Continuous Normalising Flows，CNFs）和扩散模型（Diffusion Models，DMs）的特点，缓解了两者的关键问题。在这篇博客中，我们将从基础开始，介绍FM模型的主要思想和独特性质。

Generative Modelling

生成建模

Let's assume we have data samples from a distribution of interest, whose density is unknown. We're interested in using these samples to learn a probabilistic model approximating. In particular, we want efficient generation of new samples (approximately) distributed from. This task is referred to as generative modelling.

假设我们拥有来自某个感兴趣分布的数据样本，该分布的密度未知。我们希望利用这些样本学习一个概率模型来近似该分布。特别地，我们希望能够高效地生成新的样本，这些样本（近似地）服从该分布。这个任务被称为生成建模。

The advancement in generative modelling methods over the past decade has been nothing short of revolutionary. In 2012, Restricted Boltzmann Machines, then the leading generative model, were just about able to generate MNIST digits . Today, state-of-the-art methods are capable of generating high-quality images, audio and language, as well as model complex biological and physical systems. Unsurprisingly, these methods are now venturing into video generation .

过去十年生成建模方法的进展堪称革命性。2012年，当时领先的生成模型受限玻尔兹曼机（Restricted Boltzmann Machines）仅能生成MNIST数字。如今，最先进的方法能够生成高质量的图像、音频和语言，并能模拟复杂的生物和物理系统。不出意外，这些方法现已开始涉足视频生成领域。

Transforming a base distribution into another via a transformation is interesting, yet its direct application in generative modelling is limited. In generative modelling, the aim is to approximate a distribution using only the available samples. Therefore, this task requires the transformation to map samples from a "simple" distribution, such as, to approximately the data distribution. However, a straightforward linear transformation, as in the previous example, is inadequate due to the highly non-Gaussian nature of the data distribution. This brings us to a neural network as a flexible transformation. The key task then becomes optimising the neural net's parameters.

通过变换将基础分布转化为另一分布是有趣的，但其在生成建模中的直接应用有限。生成建模的目标是仅利用可用样本来近似一个分布。因此，该任务要求变换将来自“简单”分布（如）的样本映射到数据分布的近似上。然而，由于数据分布高度非高斯，前述的简单线性变换不足以胜任。这就引入了神经网络作为灵活的变换工具。关键任务转为优化神经网络的参数。

One may legitimately wonder why should we bother with such time-continuous flows versus discrete residual flows. There are a couple of benefits:

有人可能会合理地质疑，为什么我们要使用这种连续时间流而非离散残差流。这里有几个好处：

  1. CNFs can be seen as an automatic way of choosing the number of residual flows to use, which would otherwise be a hyperparameter we would have to tune. In the time-continuous setting, we can choose an error threshold and the adapative solver would give us a the discretisation step size , effectively yielding steps. Using an explicit first order solver, each step is of the form , akin to a residual flow, where the residual connection parameters are shared for each discretisation step, since is amortised over , instead of having a different for each layer.
  1. CNF（连续归一化流）可以看作是自动选择残差流数量的方法，否则这将是我们必须调节的超参数。在连续时间设置中，我们可以选择误差阈值，自适应求解器会给出离散化步长，有效地产生步数。使用显式一阶求解器，每一步形式为，类似于残差流，其中残差连接参数在每个离散步骤中共享，因为被摊销在上，而不是每层有不同的。
  1. In residual flows, during training we need to ensure that is Lipschitz; otherwise the resulting flow will not be invertible and thus not a valid normalising flow. With CNFs, we still require the vector field to be Lipschitz in , but we don't have to worry about exactly what this Lipschitz constant is, which is obviously much easier to satisfy and enforce in the neural architecture.
  1. 在残差流中，训练时我们需要确保其是Lipschitz连续的；否则生成的流将不可逆，从而不构成有效的归一化流。对于连续正则流（CNFs），我们仍然要求向量场在中是Lipschitz连续的，但不必过于担心具体的Lipschitz常数，这显然更容易在神经网络架构中满足和强制执行。

CNFs are very expressive as they parametrise a large class of flows, and therefore of probability distributions. Yet training can be extremely slow due to the ODE integration at each iteration. One may wonder whether a 'simulation-free' , i.e. not requiring any integration, training procedure exists for training these CNFs.

CNFs表达能力极强，因为它们参数化了大量的流及概率分布。然而，由于每次迭代都需进行ODE积分，训练可能非常缓慢。有人可能会想是否存在一种“无模拟”的训练方法，即不需要任何积分的训练程序来训练这些CNFs。

And that is exactly where Flow Matching (FM) comes in!

这正是流匹配（Flow Matching，FM）发挥作用的地方！

In practice, we cannot compute the optimal coupling between and, as algorithms solving this problem are only known for finite distributions. In fact, finding a map from to is the generative modelling problem that we are trying to solve in the first place!

实际上，我们无法计算和之间的最优耦合，因为解决该问题的算法仅适用于有限分布。事实上，寻找从到的映射正是我们试图解决的生成建模问题！

Tong et al. (2023) and Pooladian et al. (2023) propose to approximate the OT coupling by computing such optimal coupling only over each mini-batch of data and noise samples, coined mini-batch OT (Fatras et al., 2020). This is scalable as for finite collection of samples the OT problem can be computed with quadratic complexity via the Sinkhorn algorithm (Peyre and Cuturi, 2020). This results in a joint distribution over "inputs" and "outputs" such that the expected distance is (approximately) minimised. Finally, to construct a mini-batch from this which we can subsequently use for training, we can either compute the expectation wrt. by considering all pairs (in practice, this can often boil down to only needing to consider disjoint pairs 11 ) or sample a new collection of training pairs with12.

Tong 等人（2023）和 Pooladian 等人（2023）提出通过仅在每个小批量数据和噪声样本上计算最优耦合来近似最优传输耦合，称为小批量最优传输（mini-batch OT）（Fatras 等人，2020）。该方法具有可扩展性，因为对于有限样本集合，最优传输问题可通过 Sinkhorn 算法以二次复杂度计算（Peyré 和 Cuturi，2020）。这产生了“输入”和“输出”的联合分布，使得期望距离（近似）最小化。最后，为构造可用于训练的小批量，我们可以通过考虑所有配对计算关于的期望（实际上，通常只需考虑不相交的配对11），或用12采样新的训练配对集合。

It's worth noting that in we only considered choosing the coupling such that we minimize the expected squared Euclidean distance. This works well in the examples and, but we could also replace squared Euclidean distance with some other distance metric when constructing the coupling . For example, if we were modeling molecules using CNFs, it might also make sense to pick such that and are also rotationally aligned as is done in the work of Klein et al. (2023).

值得注意的是，在中我们仅考虑选择耦合以最小化期望平方欧氏距离。这在示例中效果良好，但我们也可以在构造耦合时用其他距离度量替代平方欧氏距离。例如，在使用连续归一化流（CNFs）建模分子时，选择使和旋转对齐的耦合也是合理的，正如 Klein 等人（2023）的工作所示。

In short, we' ve shown that flow matching is an efficient approach to training continuous normalising flows (CNFs), by directly regressing over the vector field instead of explicitly training by maximum likelihood. This is enabled by constructing the target vector field as the marginalisation of simple conditional vector fields which (marginally) interpolate between the reference and data distribution, but crucially for which we can evaluate and integrate over time. A neural network parameterising the vector field can then be trained by regressing over these conditional vector fields. Similarly to CNFs, samples can be obtained at inference time by solving the ODE associated with the neural vector field.

简而言之，我们展示了流匹配（flow matching）是一种高效训练连续归一化流（CNFs）的方法，通过直接对向量场进行回归，而非显式地通过最大似然训练。这得益于将目标向量场构造为简单条件向量场的边缘化，这些条件向量场（边缘上）在参考分布和数据分布之间插值，且关键是我们可以对其进行时间上的评估和积分。参数化向量场的神经网络随后可通过对这些条件向量场进行回归来训练。与 CNFs 类似，推理时可通过求解与神经向量场相关的常微分方程（ODE）获得样本。

In this post we have not talked about diffusion (i.e. score based) models on purpose as they are not necessary for understanding flow matching. Yet these are deeply related and even exactly the same in some settings. We are planning to explore these connections, along with generalisations in a follow-up post!

本文特意未讨论扩散（即基于得分的）模型，因为它们对理解流匹配并非必要。然而，这两者关系密切，甚至在某些设置下完全相同。我们计划在后续文章中探讨这些联系及其推广！
