Abstract

摘要

Vision-and-Language Pre-training (VLP) has improved performance on various joint vision-and-language downstream tasks. Current approaches to VLP heavily rely on image feature extraction processes, most of which involve region supervision (e.g., object detection) and the convolutional architecture (e.g., ResNet). Although disregarded in the literature, we find it problematic in terms of both (1) efficiency/speed, that simply extracting input features requires much more computation than the multimodal interaction steps; and (2) expressive power, as it is upper bounded to the expressive power of the visual embedder and its predefined visual vocabulary. In this paper, we present a minimal VLP model, Vision-and-Language Transformer (ViLT), monolithic in the sense that the processing of visual inputs is drastically simplified to just the same convolution-free manner that we process textual inputs. We show that ViLT is up to tens of times faster than previous VLP models, yet with competitive or better downstream task performance. Our code and pre-trained weights are available at https://github.com/dandelin/vilt.

视觉与语言预训练（Vision-and-Language Pre-training，VLP）提升了多种视觉与语言联合下游任务的性能。当前的VLP方法高度依赖图像特征提取过程，其中大多数涉及区域监督（如目标检测）和卷积架构（如ResNet）。尽管文献中未被重视，我们发现这在两个方面存在问题：(1) 效率/速度，仅提取输入特征所需的计算量远大于多模态交互步骤；(2) 表达能力，受限于视觉嵌入器及其预定义视觉词汇的表达上限。本文提出了一种极简的VLP模型——视觉与语言变换器（Vision-and-Language Transformer，ViLT），其单一结构极大简化了视觉输入的处理，采用与文本输入相同的无卷积方式。我们展示了ViLT在速度上比以往VLP模型快数十倍，同时在下游任务中表现出竞争力甚至更优的性能。我们的代码和预训练权重可在https://github.com/dandelin/vilt获取。

1. Introduction

1. 引言

The pre-train-and-fine-tune scheme has been expanded to a joint domain of vision and language, giving birth to the category of Vision-and-Language Pre-training (VLP) models (Lu et al., 2019; Chen et al., 2019; Su et al., 2019; Li et al., 2019; Tan & Bansal, 2019; Li et al., 2020a; Lu et al., 2020; Cho et al., 2020; Qi et al., 2020; Zhou et al., 2020; Huang et al., 2020; Li et al., 2020b; Gan et al., 2020; Yu et al., 2020; Zhang et al., 2021). These models are pre-trained with image text matching and masked language modeling objectives 1 on images and their aligned descriptions,and are fine-tuned on vision-and-language downstream tasks where the inputs involve two modalities.

预训练加微调方案已扩展至视觉与语言的联合领域，催生了视觉与语言预训练（VLP）模型类别（Lu et al., 2019；Chen et al., 2019；Su et al., 2019；Li et al., 2019；Tan & Bansal, 2019；Li et al., 2020a；Lu et al., 2020；Cho et al., 2020；Qi et al., 2020；Zhou et al., 2020；Huang et al., 2020；Li et al., 2020b；Gan et al., 2020；Yu et al., 2020；Zhang et al., 2021）。这些模型通过图像文本匹配和掩码语言建模目标1在图像及其对应描述上进行预训练，并在涉及两种模态输入的视觉与语言下游任务上进行微调。

To be fed into VLP models, image pixels need to be initially embedded in a dense form alongside language tokens. Since the seminal work of Krizhevsky et al. (2012), deep convolutional networks have been regarded as essential for this visual embedding step. Most VLP models employ an object detector pre-trained on the Visual Genome dataset (Krishna et al., 2017) annotated with 1,600 object classes and 400 attribute classes as in Anderson et al. (2018). Pixel-BERT (Huang et al., 2020) is one exception of this trend, as it uses ResNet variants (He et al., 2016; Xie et al., 2017) pre-trained on ImageNet classification (Russakovsky et al., 2015) embedding pixels in place of object detection modules.

为了输入VLP模型，图像像素需与语言标记一同初步嵌入为密集形式。自Krizhevsky等人（2012）的开创性工作以来，深度卷积网络被视为视觉嵌入步骤的关键。大多数VLP模型采用在Visual Genome数据集（Krishna等，2017）上预训练的目标检测器，该数据集标注了1600个目标类别和400个属性类别，如Anderson等人（2018）所示。Pixel-BERT（Huang等，2020）是此趋势的例外，它使用在ImageNet分类（Russakovsky等，2015）上预训练的ResNet变体（He等，2016；Xie等，2017）来嵌入像素，替代目标检测模块。

To this date, most VLP studies have focused on improving performance by increasing the power of visual embedders. The shortcomings of having a heavy visual embedder are often disregarded in academic experiments because region features are commonly cached in advance at training time to ease the burden of feature extraction. However, the limitations are still evident in real-world applications as the queries in the wild have to undergo a slow extraction process.

迄今为止，大多数视觉语言预训练（VLP）研究都集中在通过增强视觉嵌入器的能力来提升性能。由于区域特征通常在训练时预先缓存以减轻特征提取的负担，学术实验中常忽视了重型视觉嵌入器的缺点。然而，在实际应用中，这些限制依然明显，因为实际查询必须经历缓慢的特征提取过程。

To this end, we shift our attention to the lightweight and fast embedding of visual inputs. Recent work (Dosovitskiy et al., 2020; Touvron et al., 2020) demonstrated that using a simple linear projection of a patch is effective enough to embed pixels before feeding them into transformers. Whereas being the solid mainstream for text (Devlin et al., 2019), it is only recently that transformers (Vaswani et al., 2017) are used for images as well. We presume that the transformer module-used for modality interaction in VLP models- can also manage to process visual features in place of a convolutional visual embedder, just as it processes textual features.

为此，我们将注意力转向轻量且快速的视觉输入嵌入。近期工作（Dosovitskiy 等，2020；Touvron 等，2020）表明，使用简单的线性投影对图像块进行嵌入，在送入Transformer之前对像素进行编码已足够有效。虽然Transformer（Vaswani 等，2017）作为文本处理的主流方法（Devlin 等，2019）已久，但其在图像领域的应用尚属近年。我们推测，Transformer模块——在VLP模型中用于模态交互——同样可以替代卷积视觉嵌入器处理视觉特征，就像处理文本特征一样。

This paper proposes the Vision-and-Language Transformer (ViLT) that handles two modalities in a single unified manner. It mainly differs from previous VLP models in its shallow, convolution-free embedding of pixel-level inputs. Removing deep embedders solely dedicated to visual inputs significantly cuts down the model size and running time by design. Figure 1 shows that our parameter-efficient model is tens of times faster than VLP models with region features and at least four times faster than those with grid features while exhibiting similar or even better performance on vision-and-language downstream tasks.

本文提出了视觉语言Transformer（ViLT），以统一方式处理两种模态。其主要区别在于对像素级输入采用浅层、无卷积的嵌入方式。去除专门针对视觉输入的深层嵌入器，设计上显著减少了模型规模和运行时间。图1显示，我们的参数高效模型比使用区域特征的VLP模型快数十倍，比使用网格特征的模型至少快四倍，同时在视觉语言下游任务上表现相当甚至更优。

  • ViLT是迄今为止最简洁的视觉语言模型架构，它委托Transformer模块提取和处理视觉特征，取代了独立的深层视觉嵌入器。这一设计本质上带来了显著的运行时和参数效率提升。
  • For the first time, we achieve competent performance on vision-and-language tasks without using region features or deep convolutional visual embedders in general.

2. Background

2. 背景

2.1. Taxonomy of Vision-and-Language Models

2.1. 视觉语言模型的分类法

We propose a taxonomy of vision-and-language models based on two points: (1) whether the two modalities have an even level of expressiveness in terms of dedicated parameters and/or computation; and (2) whether the two modalities interact in a deep network. A combination of these points leads to four archetypes in Figure 2.

我们基于两个方面提出视觉语言模型的分类法：（1）两种模态在专用参数和/或计算上的表达能力是否均衡；（2）两种模态是否在深层网络中交互。结合这两点，形成图2中的四种典型模型。

The visual semantic embedding (VSE) models such as VSE++ (Faghri et al., 2017) and SCAN (Lee et al., 2018) belong to Figure 2a. They use separate embedders for image and text, with the former being much heavier. Then, they represent the similarity of the embedded features from the two modalities with simple dot products or shallow attention layers.

视觉语义嵌入（VSE）模型，如VSE++（Faghri 等，2017）和SCAN（Lee 等，2018），属于图2a。它们为图像和文本使用独立的嵌入器，且图像嵌入器更为复杂。随后，通过简单的点积或浅层注意力层表示两种模态嵌入特征的相似度。

CLIP (Radford et al., 2021) belongs to Figure 2b as it uses separate but equally expensive transformer embedders for each modality. Interaction between the pooled image vector and text vector is still shallow (dot product). Despite CLIP's remarkable zero-shot performance on image-to-text retrieval, we could not observe the same level of performance on other vision-and-language downstream tasks. For instance, fine-tuning the MLP head on NLVR2 (Suhr et al., 2018) with the dot product of pooled visual and textual vectors from CLIP as the multimodal representation gives a low dev accuracy of 50.99±0.38 (ran with three different seeds); as chance level accuracy is 0.5 , we conclude that the representations are incapable of learning this task. It also matches the findings of Suhr et al. (2018) that all models with simply fused multimodal representation failed to learn NLVR2.

CLIP（Radford 等，2021）属于图2b，因为它为每种模态使用独立但同等复杂的Transformer嵌入器。图像和文本的池化向量之间的交互仍然是浅层的（点积）。尽管CLIP在图像到文本的零样本检索上表现卓越，但我们未观察到其在其他视觉语言下游任务上的同等表现。例如，在NLVR2（Suhr 等，2018）上微调MLP头，使用CLIP池化的视觉和文本向量点积作为多模态表示，开发集准确率低于50.99±0.38（使用三个不同随机种子运行）；鉴于随机准确率为0.5，我们认为该表示无法学习此任务。这也与Suhr 等（2018）的发现一致，即所有简单融合多模态表示的模型均未能学会NLVR2。

This result backs up our speculation that simple fusion of outputs even from high-performing unimodal embedders may not be sufficient to learn complex vision-and-language tasks, bolstering the need for a more rigorous inter-modal interaction scheme.

这一结果支持我们的推测：即使是高性能的单模态嵌入器输出的简单融合，也可能不足以学习复杂的视觉语言任务，强调了更严谨的模态间交互方案的必要性。

Unlike models with shallow interaction, the more recent VLP models that fall under Figure 2c use a deep transformer to model the interaction of image and text features. Aside from the interaction module, however, convolutional networks are still involved in extracting and embedding image features, which accounts for most of the computation as depicted in Figure 1. Modulation-based vision-and-language models (Perez et al., 2018; Nguyen et al., 2020) also fall under Figure 2c, with their visual CNN stems corresponding to visual embedder, RNNs producing the modulation parameters to textual embedder, and modulated CNNs to modality interaction.

与浅层交互模型不同，图2c中的最新VLP模型使用深层Transformer来建模图像和文本特征的交互。然而，除交互模块外，卷积网络仍参与图像特征的提取和嵌入，这占据了大部分计算量，如图1所示。基于调制的视觉语言模型（Perez 等，2018；Nguyen 等，2020）也属于图2c，其视觉CNN主干对应视觉嵌入器，RNN生成调制参数用于文本嵌入器，调制后的CNN实现模态交互。

Our proposed ViLT is the first model of type Figure 2d where the embedding layers of raw pixels are shallow and computationally light as of text tokens. This architecture thereby concentrates most of the computation on modeling modality interactions.

我们提出的ViLT是图2d类型的首个模型，其中原始像素的嵌入层与文本标记的嵌入层一样浅且计算量轻。因此，该架构将大部分计算集中在建模模态交互上。

2.2. Modality Interaction Schema

2.2. 模态交互方案

At the very core of contemporary VLP models lie transformers. They get visual and textual embedding sequences as input, model inter-modal and optionally intra-modal interactions throughout layers, then output a contextualized feature sequence.

当代视觉语言预训练（VLP）模型的核心是transformer。它们以视觉和文本嵌入序列为输入，在各层中建模模态间及可选的模态内交互，最终输出上下文化的特征序列。

Bugliarello et al. (2020) classifies interaction schema into two categories: (1) single-stream approaches (e.g., Visual-BERT (Li et al., 2019), UNITER (Chen et al., 2019)) where layers collectively operate on a concatenation of image and text inputs; and (2) dual-stream approaches (e.g., ViLBERT (Lu et al., 2019), LXMERT (Tan & Bansal, 2019)) where the two modalities are not concatenated at the input level. We follow the single-stream approach for our interaction transformer module because the dual-stream approach introduces additional parameters.

Bugliarello等（2020）将交互方案分为两类：（1）单流方法（如Visual-BERT（Li等，2019）、UNITER（Chen等，2019）），其层共同作用于图像和文本输入的拼接；（2）双流方法（如ViLBERT（Lu等，2019）、LXMERT（Tan & Bansal，2019）），两种模态在输入层不拼接。我们在交互transformer模块中采用单流方法，因为双流方法会引入额外参数。

2.3. Visual Embedding Schema

2.3. 视觉嵌入方案

Whereas all performant VLP models share the same textual embedder- tokenizer from pre-trained BERT, word and position embeddings resembling those of BERT- they differ on visual embedders. Still, in most (if not all) cases, visual embedding is the bottleneck of existing VLP models. We focus on cutting corners on this step by introducing patch projection instead of using region or grid features for which heavy extraction modules are used.

虽然所有高性能VLP模型共享相同的文本嵌入器——来自预训练BERT的分词器、词嵌入和位置嵌入，类似于BERT，但它们在视觉嵌入器上存在差异。然而，在大多数（如果不是全部）情况下，视觉嵌入是现有VLP模型的瓶颈。我们通过引入patch投影，替代使用需要重型提取模块的区域或网格特征，来简化这一步骤。

Region Feature. VLP models dominantly utilize region features, also known as bottom-up features (Anderson et al., 2018). They are obtained from an off-the-shelf object detector like Faster R-CNN (Ren et al., 2016).

区域特征。VLP模型主要使用区域特征，也称为自底向上特征（Anderson等，2018）。这些特征来自现成的目标检测器，如Faster R-CNN（Ren等，2016）。

The general pipeline of generating region features is as follows. First, a region proposal network (RPN) proposes regions of interest (RoI) based on the grid features pooled from the CNN backbone. Non-maximum suppression (NMS) then reduces the number of RoIs to a few thousand. After being pooled by operations such as RoI Align (He et al., 2017), the RoIs go through RoI heads and become region features. NMS is again applied to every class, finally reducing the number of features under a hundred.

生成区域特征的一般流程如下。首先，区域提议网络（RPN）基于从CNN主干网络池化的网格特征提出感兴趣区域（RoI）。非极大值抑制（NMS）随后将RoI数量减少到几千个。经过RoI Align（He等，2017）等操作池化后，RoI通过RoI头部，成为区域特征。NMS再次应用于每个类别，最终将特征数量减少到不足百个。

The above process involves several factors that affect the performance and runtime: the backbone, the style of NMS, the RoI heads. Previous works were lenient with controlling these factors, making varying choices from each other as listed in Table 7. 2

上述过程涉及多个影响性能和运行时间的因素：主干网络、NMS风格、RoI头部。以往工作对这些因素控制宽松，选择各异，如表7所示。2

  • Backbone: ResNet-101 (Lu et al., 2019; Tan & Bansal, 2019; Su et al., 2019) and ResNext-152 (Li et al., 2019; 2020a; Zhang et al., 2021) are two commonly used backbones.
  • 主干网络：ResNet-101（Lu等，2019；Tan & Bansal，2019；Su等，2019）和ResNext-152（Li等，2019；2020a；Zhang等，2021）是两种常用主干网络。
  • NMS: NMS is typically done in a per-class fashion. Applying NMS to each and every class becomes a major runtime bottleneck with a large number of classes, e.g. 1.6K in the VG dataset (Jiang et al., 2020). Class-agnostic NMS was recently introduced to tackle this issue (Zhang et al., 2021).
  • NMS：NMS通常按类别进行。对每个类别应用NMS在类别数众多时（如VG数据集（Jiang等，2020）中的1.6K类）成为主要运行瓶颈。近期引入了类别无关NMS以解决此问题（Zhang等，2021）。
  • RoI head: C4 heads were initially used (Anderson et al., 2018). FPN-MLP heads were introduced later (Jiang et al., 2018). As heads operate for each and every RoI, they pose a substantial runtime burden.
  • RoI头部：最初使用C4头部（Anderson等，2018），后引入FPN-MLP头部（Jiang等，2018）。由于头部对每个RoI操作，带来显著运行负担。

However lightweight, object detectors are less likely to be faster than the backbone or a single-layer convolution. Freezing the visual backbone and caching the region features in advance only helps at training time and not during inference, not to mention that it could hold performance back.

尽管轻量，目标检测器通常不比主干网络或单层卷积更快。冻结视觉主干并提前缓存区域特征仅在训练时有益，推理时无效，且可能限制性能。

Grid Feature. Besides detector heads, the output feature grid of convolutional neural networks such as ResNets can also be used as visual features for vision-and-language pretraining. Direct use of grid features was first proposed by VQA-specific models (Jiang et al., 2020; Nguyen et al., 2020), mainly to avoid using severely slow region selection operations.

网格特征。除检测头外，卷积神经网络（如ResNets）的输出特征网格也可用作视觉语言预训练的视觉特征。直接使用网格特征最早由针对VQA的模型提出（Jiang等，2020；Nguyen等，2020），主要为避免使用极慢的区域选择操作。

X-LXMERT (Cho et al., 2020) revisited grid features by fixing the region proposals to grids instead of those from the region proposal networks. However, their caching of features excluded further tuning of the backbone.

X-LXMERT（Cho 等，2020）通过将区域提议固定为网格而非来自区域提议网络的提议，重新审视了网格特征。然而，他们对特征的缓存排除了对骨干网络的进一步微调。

Pixel-BERT is the only VLP model that replaces the VG-pre-trained object detector with a ResNet variant backbone pre-trained with ImageNet classification. Unlike frozen detectors in region-feature-based VLP models, the backbone of Pixel-BERT is tuned during vision-and-language pretraining. The downstream performance of Pixel-BERT with ResNet-50 falls below region-feature-based VLP models, but it matches that of other competitors with the use of a much heavier ResNeXt-152.

Pixel-BERT 是唯一用预训练于 ImageNet 分类任务的 ResNet 变体骨干网络替代了 Visual Genome（VG）预训练目标检测器的视觉语言预训练（VLP）模型。与基于区域特征的 VLP 模型中冻结的检测器不同，Pixel-BERT 的骨干网络在视觉语言预训练过程中是可调的。使用 ResNet-50 时，Pixel-BERT 的下游性能低于基于区域特征的 VLP 模型，但使用更重的 ResNeXt-152 时，其性能与其他竞争者相当。

We claim that grid features are not the go-to option, however, since deep CNNs are still expensive that they account for a large portion of the whole computation as in Figure 1.

我们认为网格特征并非首选方案，因为深度卷积神经网络（CNN）仍然计算开销巨大，占据了整体计算的大部分，如图1所示。

Patch Projection. To minimize overhead, we adopt the simplest visual embedding scheme: linear projection that operates on image patches. The patch projection embedding was introduced by ViT (Dosovitskiy et al., 2020) for image classification tasks. Patch projection drastically simplifies the visual embedding step to the level of textual embedding, which also consists of simple projection (lookup) operations.

Patch Projection（图像块投影）。为了最小化开销，我们采用最简单的视觉嵌入方案：对图像块进行线性投影。图像块投影嵌入由 ViT（Dosovitskiy 等，2020）为图像分类任务引入。图像块投影极大简化了视觉嵌入步骤，使其与文本嵌入的简单投影（查找）操作相当。

We use a 32×32 patch projection which only requires 2.4M parameters. This is in sharp contrast to complex ResNe(X)t backbones 3 and detection components. Its running time is also ignorable as shown in Figure 1. We make a detailed runtime analysis in Section 4.6.

我们使用了一个32×32图像块投影，仅需2.4M参数。这与复杂的 ResNe(X)t 骨干网络3和检测组件形成鲜明对比。其运行时间也可忽略不计，如图1所示。我们在第4.6节进行了详细的运行时分析。

3. Vision-and-Language Transformer

3. 视觉与语言变换器

3.1. Model Overview

3.1. 模型概述

ViLT has a succinct architecture as a VLP model with a minimal visual embedding pipeline and following the single-stream approach.

ViLT 作为视觉语言预训练模型，架构简洁，视觉嵌入流程极简，采用单流（single-stream）方法。

We deviate from the literature that we initialize the interaction transformer weights from pre-trained ViT instead of BERT. Such initialization exploits the power of the interaction layers to process visual features while lacking a separate deep visual embedder. 4

我们不同于文献中做法，采用预训练的 ViT 权重初始化交互变换器（interaction transformer）权重，而非 BERT。此初始化利用交互层处理视觉特征的能力，同时缺少单独的深度视觉嵌入器。4

(1)t¯=[tclass ;t1T;⋯;tLT]+Tpos 

(2)v¯=[vclass ;v1V;⋯;vNV]+Vpos 

(3)z0=[t¯+ttype ;v¯+vtype ]

(4)z^d=MSA(LN(zd−1))+zd−1,d=1…D

(5)zd=MLP(LN(z^d))+z^d,d=1…D

(6)p=tanh⁡(z0DWpool )

ViT consists of stacked blocks that include a multiheaded self-attention (MSA) layer and an MLP layer. The position of layer normalization (LN) in ViT is the only difference from BERT: LN comes after MSA and MLP in BERT ("post-norm") and before in ViT ("pre-norm"). The input text t∈RL×|V| is embedded to t¯∈RL×H with a word embedding matrix T∈R|V|×H and a position embedding matrix Tpos ∈R(L+1)×H .

ViT 由堆叠的模块组成，每个模块包含多头自注意力（MSA）层和多层感知机（MLP）层。ViT 与 BERT 唯一的区别在于层归一化（LN）的位置：BERT 中 LN 位于 MSA 和 MLP 之后（“后归一化”），而 ViT 中 LN 位于之前（“前归一化”）。输入文本t∈RL×|V|通过词嵌入矩阵T∈R|V|×H和位置嵌入矩阵Tpos ∈R(L+1)×H嵌入为t¯∈RL×H。

The input image I∈RC×H×W is sliced into patches and flattened to v∈RN×(P2⋅C) where(P,P)is the patch resolution and N=HW/P2 . Followed by linear projection V∈R(P2⋅C)×H and position embedding Vpos ∈R(N+1)×H,v is embedded into v¯∈RN×H .

输入图像I∈RC×H×W被切分为图像块并展平成v∈RN×(P2⋅C)，其中 (P,P) 是图像块分辨率，N=HW/P2。随后通过线性投影V∈R(P2⋅C)×H和位置嵌入Vpos ∈R(N+1)×H,v嵌入为v¯∈RN×H。

The text and image embeddings are summed with their corresponding modal-type embedding vectors ttype ,vtype ∈RH , then are concatenated into a combined sequence z0 . The contextualized vector z is iteratively updated through D - depth transformer layers up until the final contextualized sequence zD.p is a pooled representation of the whole multimodal input, and is obtained by applying linear projection Wpool ∈RH×H and hyperbolic tangent upon the first index of sequence zD .

文本和图像的嵌入向量与对应的模态类型嵌入向量ttype ,vtype ∈RH相加，然后拼接成一个组合序列z0。上下文化向量z通过D层深的Transformer层迭代更新，直到最终的上下文化序列zD.p成为整个多模态输入的池化表示，该表示通过对序列的第一个索引应用线性投影Wpool ∈RH×H和双曲正切函数获得zD。

For all experiments, we use weights from ViT-B/32 pre-trained on ImageNet,hence the name ViLT-B/32. 5 Hidden size H is 768,layer depth D is 12,patch size P is 32,MLP size is 3,072, and the number of attention heads is 12 .

在所有实验中，我们使用在ImageNet上预训练的ViT-B/32权重，因此命名为ViLT-B/32。5隐藏层大小H为768，层深D为12，patch大小P为32，MLP大小为3072，注意力头数为12。

3.2. Pre-training Objectives

3.2. 预训练目标

We train ViLT with two objectives commonly used to train VLP models: image text matching (ITM) and masked language modeling (MLM).

我们使用两种常用于训练视觉语言预训练（VLP）模型的目标训练ViLT：图文匹配（ITM）和掩码语言建模（MLM）。

Image Text Matching. We randomly replace the aligned image with a different image with the probability of 0.5 . A single linear layer ITM head projects the pooled output feature p to logits over binary class,and we compute negative log-likelihood loss as our ITM loss.

图文匹配。我们以0.5的概率随机用不同的图像替换对齐的图像。单层线性ITM头将池化输出特征p投影为二分类的logits，并计算负对数似然损失作为ITM损失。

Plus, inspired by the word region alignment objective in Chen et al. (2019), we design word patch alignment (WPA) that computes the alignment score between two subsets of zD:zD|t (textual subset) and zD|v (visual subset),using the inexact proximal point method for optimal transports (IPOT) (Xie et al., 2020). We set the hyperparameters of IPOT following Chen et al. (2019) (β=0.5,N=50) ,and add the approximate wasserstein distance multiplied by 0.1 to the ITM loss.

此外，受Chen等人（2019）中词区域对齐目标的启发，我们设计了词块对齐（WPA），计算文本子集zD:zD|t和视觉子集zD|v之间的对齐分数，使用不精确近端点法（IPOT）（Xie等，2020）进行最优传输。我们按照Chen等人（2019）(β=0.5,N=50)设置IPOT的超参数，并将近似Wasserstein距离乘以0.1后加入ITM损失。

Masked Language Modeling. This objective is to predict the ground truth labels of masked text tokens tmasked  from its contextualized vector zmasked D|t . Following the heuristics of Devlin et al. (2019),we randomly mask t with the probability of 0.15 . We use a two-layer MLP MLM head that inputs zmasked D|t and outputs logits over vocabulary, just as the MLM objective of BERT. The MLM loss is then computed as the negative log-likelihood loss for the masked tokens.

掩码语言建模。该目标是从上下文化向量zmasked D|t预测被掩码文本标记tmasked 的真实标签。遵循Devlin等人（2019）的启发式方法，我们以0.15的概率随机掩码t。我们使用两层MLP的MLM头，输入为zmasked D|t，输出词汇表上的logits，类似于BERT的MLM目标。MLM损失计算为掩码标记的负对数似然损失。

3.3. Whole Word Masking

3.3. 整词掩码

Whole word masking is a masking technique that masks all consecutive subword tokens that compose a whole word. It is shown to be effective on downstream tasks when applied to original and Chinese BERT (Cui et al., 2019).

整词掩码是一种掩码技术，掩盖组成整个词的所有连续子词标记。实验证明，该方法在原始BERT和中文BERT（Cui等，2019）上对下游任务有效。

We hypothesize that whole word masking is particularly crucial for VLP in order to make full use of information from the other modality. For example, the word "giraffe" is to-kenized into three wordpiece tokens ["gi", "##raf", "##fe"] with the pre-trained bert-base-uncased tokenizer. If not all tokens are masked, say, ["gi", " [MASK] ", " # # f e"], the model may solely rely on the nearby two language tokens ["gi", "##fe"] to predict the masked " # # raf " rather than using the information from the image.

我们假设整词掩码对视觉语言预训练尤为重要，以充分利用来自另一模态的信息。例如，单词“giraffe”被预训练的bert-base-uncased分词器分成三个词片段["gi", "##raf", "##fe"]。如果不是全部掩码，比如掩码为["gi", "[MASK]", "##fe"]，模型可能仅依赖附近的两个语言标记["gi", "##fe"]来预测被掩码的"##raf"，而不是利用图像信息。

We mask whole words with a mask probability of 0.15 during pre-training. We discuss its impact in Section 4.5.

我们在预训练期间以0.15的掩码概率掩盖整词。其影响将在第4.5节讨论。

3.4. Image Augmentation

3.4. 图像增强

Image augmentation reportedly improves the generalization power of vision models (Shorten & Khoshgoftaar, 2019). DeiT (Touvron et al., 2020) that builds on ViT experimented with various augmentation techniques (Zhang et al., 2017; Yun et al., 2019; Berman et al., 2019; Hoffer et al., 2020; Cubuk et al., 2020), and found them beneficial for ViT training. However, the effects of image augmentation have not been explored within VLP models. Caching visual features restrains region-feature-based VLP models from using image augmentation. Notwithstanding its applicability, neither did Pixel-BERT study its effects.

据报道，图像增强能够提升视觉模型的泛化能力（Shorten & Khoshgoftaar, 2019）。基于ViT（视觉变换器，Vision Transformer）的DeiT（Touvron等，2020）尝试了多种增强技术（Zhang等，2017；Yun等，2019；Berman等，2019；Hoffer等，2020；Cubuk等，2020），并发现这些技术对ViT训练有益。然而，图像增强在视觉语言预训练（VLP，Vision-Language Pre-training）模型中的效果尚未被探讨。缓存视觉特征限制了基于区域特征的VLP模型使用图像增强。尽管适用，Pixel-BERT也未研究其影响。

To this end, we apply RandAugment (Cubuk et al., 2020) during fine-tuning. We use all the original policies except two: color inversion, because texts often contain color information as well, and cutout, as it may clear out small but important objects dispersed throughout the whole image. We use N=2,M=9 as the hyperparameters. We discuss its impact in Section 4.5 and Section 5.

为此，我们在微调阶段应用了RandAugment（Cubuk等，2020）。我们使用了所有原始策略，除了两项：颜色反转，因为文本中通常也包含颜色信息；以及遮挡（cutout），因为它可能会清除分布在整张图像中的小而重要的物体。我们使用N=2,M=9作为超参数。其影响将在第4.5节和第5节中讨论。

4.2. Implementation Details

4.2. 实现细节

For all experiments, we use AdamW optimizer (Loshchilov &Hutter,2018) with base learning rate of 10−4 and weight decay of 10−2 . The learning rate was warmed up for 10% of the total training steps and was decayed linearly to zero for the rest of the training. Note that downstream performance may be further improved if we customize the hyperparame-ters to each task.

所有实验中，我们使用AdamW优化器（Loshchilov & Hutter，2018），基础学习率为10−4，权重衰减为10−2。学习率在总训练步骤的10%内进行预热，随后线性衰减至零。注意，如果针对每个任务定制超参数，下游性能可能进一步提升。

We resize the shorter edge of input images to 384 and limit the longer edge to under 640 while preserving the aspect ratio. This resizing scheme is also used during object detection in other VLP models, but with a larger size of the shorter edge (800). Patch projection of ViLT-B/32 yields 12 ×20=240 patches for an image with a resolution of 384× 640. As this is a rarely reached upper limit, we sample 200 patches at maximum during pre-training. We interpolate Vpos  of ViT-B/32 to fit the size of each image and pad the patches for batch training. Note that the resulting image resolution is four times smaller than 800×1,333 ,which is the size that all other VLP models use for inputs to their visual embedders.

我们将输入图像的短边调整为384，长边限制在640以内，同时保持宽高比。该调整方案也被其他视觉语言预训练（VLP）模型在目标检测中采用，但短边尺寸更大（800）。ViLT-B/32的patch投影在分辨率为384××640的图像上产生12××20=240个patch。由于这是一个很少达到的上限，我们在预训练时最多采样200个patch。我们对ViT-B/32的Vpos 进行插值以适应每张图像的尺寸，并对patch进行填充以支持批量训练。注意，最终图像分辨率比800×1,333小四倍，而后者是所有其他VLP模型输入视觉嵌入器时使用的尺寸。

We use the bert-base-uncased tokenizer to tokenize text inputs. Instead of fine-tuning from pre-trained BERT, we learn the textual embedding-related parameters tclass ,T , and Tpos  from scratch. Although beneficial prima facie, employing a pre-trained text-only BERT does not guarantee performance gain for vision and language downstream tasks. Counterevidence has already been reported by Tan & Bansal

Visual Question Answering. The VQAv2 task asks for answers given pairs of an image and a question in natural language. The annotated answers are originally in free-form natural language, but it is a common practice to convert the task to a classification task with 3,129 answer classes. Following this practice, we fine-tune ViLT-B/32 on the VQAv2 train and validation sets while reserving 1,000 validation images and their related questions for internal validation.

Natural Language for Visual Reasoning. The NLVR2 task is a binary classification task given triplets of two images and a question in natural language. As there are two input images unlike the pre-training setup, multiple strategies exist 7 . Following OSCAR (Li et al.,2020b) and VinVL (Zhang et al., 2021), we use the pair method. Here, the triplet input is reformulated into two pairs (question, image 1) and (question, image 2), and each pair goes through the ViLT. The head takes the concatenation of two pooled representations(p)as input and outputs the binary prediction.

用于视觉推理的自然语言。NLVR2任务是一个二分类任务，输入为两张图像和一个自然语言问题的三元组。由于输入包含两张图像，这与预训练设置不同，因此存在多种策略7。继OSCAR（Li et al.,2020b）和VinVL（Zhang et al., 2021）之后，我们采用配对方法。这里，三元组输入被重新构造成两个配对（问题，图像1）和（问题，图像2），每个配对分别通过ViLT。分类头以两个池化表示(p)的拼接作为输入，输出二分类预测。

Table 2 shows the results. ViLT-B/32 maintains competitive performance on both datasets considering its remarkable inference speed.

表2展示了结果。考虑到其显著的推理速度，ViLT-B/32在两个数据集上均保持了有竞争力的性能。

4.4. Retrieval Tasks

4.4. 检索任务

We fine-tune ViLT-B/32 on the Karpathy & Fei-Fei (2015) split of MSCOCO and F30K. For image-to-text and text-to-image retrieval, we measure both zero-shot and fine-tuned performance 8 . We initialize the similarity score head from the pre-trained ITM head, particularly the part that computes the true-pair logits. We sample 15 random texts as negative samples and tune the model with cross-entropy loss that maximizes the scores on positive pairs.

我们在Karpathy & Fei-Fei (2015)划分的MSCOCO和F30K数据集上微调ViLT-B/32。对于图像到文本和文本到图像的检索，我们测量零样本和微调后的性能8。我们从预训练的ITM头初始化相似度评分头，特别是计算真实配对logits的部分。我们随机采样15个文本作为负样本，并使用交叉熵损失调优模型，以最大化正样本对的得分。

At zero-shot retrieval, ViLT-B/32 performs better in general than ImageBERT despite Image-BERT's pre-training on a larger (14M) dataset. At fine-tuned retrieval, recalls for ViLT-B/32 are higher by a large margin than the second fastest model (Pixel-BERT-R50).

5. 结论与未来工作

In this paper, we present a minimal VLP architecture, Vision-and-Langauge Transformer (ViLT). ViLT is competent to competitors which are heavily equipped with convolutional visual embedding networks (e.g., Faster R-CNN and ResNets). We ask for future work on VLP to focus more on the modality interactions inside the transformer module rather than engaging in an arms race that merely powers up unimodal embedders.

本文提出了一种极简的VLP架构——视觉与语言变换器（Vision-and-Language Transformer，ViLT）。ViLT的性能可与那些配备了大量卷积视觉嵌入网络（如Faster R-CNN和ResNets）的竞争模型相媲美。我们建议未来的VLP研究更多关注变换器模块内部的模态交互，而非仅仅在单模态嵌入器上进行性能竞赛。

Although remarkable as it is, ViLT-B/32 is more of a proof of concept that efficient VLP models free of convolution and region supervision can still be competent. We wrap up by pointing out a few factors that may add to the ViLT family.

尽管表现出色，ViLT-B/32更像是一个概念验证，证明了无需卷积和区域监督的高效VLP模型依然具备竞争力。我们最后指出一些可能丰富ViLT系列的因素。

Cho et al. (2020) proposed to train their grid RoIs on masked object classification (MOC) tasks. We encourage future work that does not use region supervision to devise a more sophisticated masking objective for the visual modality.

我们鼓励未来的工作在不使用区域监督的情况下，为视觉模态设计更复杂的掩码目标。

Previous work on contrastive visual representation learning (Chen et al., 2020a;b) showed that gaussian blur, not employed by RandAugment, brings noticeable gains to downstream performance compared with a simpler augmentation strategy (He et al., 2020).
