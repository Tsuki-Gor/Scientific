Abstract
摘要

Fine-tuning pretrained models is a common practice in domain generalization (DG) tasks. However, fine-tuning is usually computationally expensive due to the ever-growing size of pretrained models. More importantly, it may cause over-fitting on source domain and compromise their generalization ability as shown in recent works. Generally, pretrained models possess some level of generalization ability and can achieve decent performance regarding specific domains and samples. However, the generalization performance of pretrained models could vary significantly over different test domains even samples, which raises challenges for us to best leverage pretrained models in DG tasks. In this paper, we propose a novel domain generalization paradigm to better leverage various pretrained models, named specialized ensemble learning for domain generalization (SEDGE). It first trains a linear label space adapter upon fixed pretrained models, which transforms the outputs of the pretrained model to the label space of the target domain. Then, an ensemble network aware of model specialty is proposed to dynamically dispatch proper pretrained models to predict each test sample. Experimental studies on several benchmarks show that SEDGE achieves significant performance improvements comparing to strong baselines including state-of-the-art method in DG tasks and reduces the trainable parameters by ∼99% and the training time by ∼99.5% .
在领域泛化（DG）任务中，微调预训练模型是一种常见做法。然而，由于预训练模型的大小持续增长，微调通常是计算成本高昂的。更重要的是，正如近期研究所示，微调可能会导致源域的过拟合并损害其泛化能力。通常，预训练模型具有某种程度的泛化能力，并且针对特定领域和样本可以取得令人满意的表现。但是，预训练模型在不同测试领域的泛化性能可能会有显著差异，这给我们如何在DG任务中最好地利用预训练模型带来了挑战。在本文中，我们提出了一个新颖的领域泛化范式，以更好地利用各种预训练模型，名为针对领域泛化的专用集成学习（SEDGE）。它首先在固定的预训练模型上训练一个线性标签空间适配器，将预训练模型的输出转换为目标领域的标签空间。然后，提出一个了解模型特性的集成网络，动态地分派适当的预训练模型来预测每个测试样本。在几个基准上的实验研究表明，SEDGE与包括领域泛化任务中最先进方法在内的强基线相比，实现了显著的性能提升，并减少了 ∼99% 的可训练参数和 ∼99.5% 的训练时间。

1 Introduction
1 引言

Distribution shift is a common problem caused by physical or psychological factors of the real-world applications, which breaks the independent and identically distributional (i.i.d.) assumption of machine learning algorithms. Thus, generalization becomes increasingly important when training and applying machine learning models in practice.
分布偏移是由现实世界应用中的物理或心理因素引起的常见问题，它打破了机器学习算法的独立同分布（i.i.d.）假设。因此，在实际训练和应用机器学习模型时，泛化变得越来越重要。
The task of domain generalization and the corresponding benchmark [Gulrajani and Lopez-Paz, 2020] have been proposed for studying and improving model generalization by training on source domains and test on target domains. These methods focus on generalizable model training following a fine-tuning paradigm which often leverages pretrained models like ResNet [He et al., 2016] as an initialization and fine-tunes that with some elaborate training algorithms on the source domains. Then, the trained models would be evaluated on the unseen target domains. One common assumption
领域泛化任务及其相应的基准 [Gulrajani和Lopez-Paz，2020] 已经被提出，用于研究通过在源域训练并在目标域测试来提高模型泛化性。这些方法关注于遵循微调范式的泛化模型训练，通常利用预训练模型如ResNet [He等，2016] 作为初始化，并在源域上使用一些精心设计的训练算法进行微调。之后，训练好的模型将在未见过的目标域上进行评估。这个常用范式中隐含的一个普遍假设是微调能够带来更好的性能。然而，由于预训练模型的规模不断增长，微调通常是计算成本高昂的，并且据证实可能会损害预训练模型的泛化能力，并在分布外场景下表现不佳 [Kumar等，2021，Yu等，2021]。

Here we propose a novel learning paradigm that dispatches proper pretrained models to each sample based on their generalization ability, named specialized ensemble learning for domain generalization (SEDGE). As shown in Figure 1 (C), specifically, in addition to the label adapter that projects the pretrained domain to the target domain upon the model with the fixed parameters, we further incorporate a model specialty aware ensemble network that selects a set of proper pretrained models and aggregate together to conduct predictions for each specific sample.

The advantages of our proposed learning paradigm lie in three aspects. First, it shows a significant improvement over the existing state-of-the-art (SOTA) result using the model pool pretrained only on ImageNet [Krizhevsky et al., 2012] dataset and gains even larger using the relatively larger model pool pretrained with additional datasets. Second, it exhibits significantly higher training efficiency. The only parameters trained on the source domains contain (1) a linear adapter transforming model outputs to the target label space and (2) a lightweight ensemble network that has largely reduced the training cost on the source domains comparing with that fine-tuning the pretrained models. We visualize the comparison of the performance w.r.t. to training parameter size and cost of training time in Figure 2. Last, this method illustrates a flexible way to utilize pretrained models, making it easier to exploit the abundant resource of pretrained models.
我们提出的学习范式的优势在于三个方面。首先，它在仅使用ImageNet [Krizhevsky et al., 2012]数据集预训练的模型池上显著优于现有的最新技术水平（SOTA）结果，并在使用相对较大的模型池（额外数据集预训练）时获得更大的提升。其次，它表现出显著更高的训练效率。在源领域上训练的唯一参数包括（1）将模型输出转换为目标标签空间的线性适配器；（2）一个轻量级集成网络，它在源领域上的训练成本比微调预训练模型要低得多。我们在图2中可视化了性能与训练参数大小和训练时间成本的关系。最后，该方法展示了一种利用预训练模型的灵活方式，使得利用丰富的预训练模型资源变得更加容易。

2 Related work
2 相关工作

2.1 Domain Generalization
2.1 领域泛化

Mainstream domain generalization research can be divided into following categories. (1) Domain alignment. In order to find the invariant representation across various domains, Ganin et al. [2016] adversarially train a generator and discriminator to reach the equilibrium of optimal invariant features across domains, hence the classifier trained on multiple source domains would generalize well to target unseen domains. Gong et al. [2019] consider reducing domain discrepancy in a manifold space. Some works resort to explicit feature distribution alignment on maximum mean discrepancy (MMD) [Pan et al., 2010, Tzeng et al., 2014, Wang et al., 2018], second order correlation [Sun et al., 2016, Sun and Saenko, 2016, Peng and Saenko, 2018], moment matching [Peng et al., 2019] and Wasserstein distance [Zhou et al., 2020, Lyu et al., 2021], etc. Besides learning invariant representation, Arjovsky et al. [2019] consider to learn an optimal invariant classifier on top of the representation space, and enforce the learned classifier predicts according with causal mechanism. (2) Data manipulation. Tobin et al. [2017] first introduce this idea, which aims to create diverse training data to simulate unseen target domain. Besides, Peng et al. [2018] and Tremblay et al. [2018] strengthen the generalization capability of the models via domain randomization, while other works consider using self-supervised learning [Carlucci et al., 2019, Kim et al., 2021] to match representation of an image with various augmentations. (3) Meta-learning. Inspired by Finn et al. [2017] and with the expectation to capture the most transferable representations across domains, MLDG [Li et al., 2018a] split the multiple source domains data into meta-train and meta-test set to simulate domain shifts to learn more generalizable representations. Dou et al. [2019] introduce additional losses to explicitly pertain to the semantic structure in representations. Balaji et al. [2018] consider learning a regularization function on classifier to avoid biasing to domain-specific information, while Du et al. [2020] resort to regularize Kullback-Leibler (KL) divergence between distributions of latent representations within samples from different domains.
主流领域泛化研究可以分为以下几类：(1) 领域对齐。为了找到跨不同领域的不变表示，Ganin等人[2016]通过对抗性训练生成器和判别器以达到跨领域最优不变特征的平衡，因此，在多个源领域上训练的分类器可以很好地泛化到未见过的目标领域。Gong等人[2019]考虑在流形空间中减少领域差异。一些工作求助于在最大平均差异(MMD) [Pan等人，2010，Tzeng等人，2014，Wang等人，2018]、二阶相关 [Sun等人，2016，Sun和Saenko，2016，Peng和Saenko，2018]、矩匹配 [Peng等人，2019] 和Wasserstein距离 [Zhou等人，2020，Lyu等人，2021] 等上的显式特征分布对齐。除了学习不变表示，Arjovsky等人[2019]考虑在表示空间之上学习最优不变分类器，并强制学习到的分类器按照因果机制进行预测。(2) 数据操作。Tobin等人[2017]首先提出这种想法，旨在创建多样化的训练数据以模拟未见过的目标领域。此外，Peng等人[2018]和Tremblay等人[2018]通过领域随机化增强了模型的泛化能力，而其他工作考虑使用自监督学习 [Carlucci等人，2019，Kim等人，2021] 来匹配图像的各种增强表示。(3) 元学习。受到Finn等人[2017]的启发，并期望捕捉到跨领域最具迁移性的表示，MLDG [Li等人，2018a] 将多个源领域数据分为元训练集和元测试集以模拟领域转移，以学习更通用的表示。Dou等人[2019]引入了额外的损失，明确关联到表示中的语义结构。Balaji等人[2018]考虑在分类器上学习正则化函数以避免偏向领域特定信息，而Du等人[2020]求助于正则化不同领域样本的潜在表示分布之间的Kullback-Leibler (KL)散度。
While above categories more focus on algorithmic improvements, our proposed method SEDGE emphasizes the innovation of a learning paradigm based on a specialized pretrained model ensemble.
虽然上述类别更多地关注算法改进，我们提出的方法 SEDGE 强调基于专用预训练模型集成的学习范式的创新。

2.2 Ensemble Learning
2.2 集成学习

Ensemble learning methods [Hansen and Salamon, 1990, Zhou et al., 2018] exploit multiple models to produce prediction results and combine the results with various techniques, e.g., boosting [Schapire, 1990, Freund, 1995, Moghimi et al., 2016] or mean aggregation [Zhou et al., 2018, Zhang et al., 2020], etc., to achieve better performance than individual model alone. These methods combine base model learning and ensemble as a whole and focus more on training diverse base models [Zhou et al., 2018].
集成学习方法 [Hansen 和 Salamon, 1990, Zhou 等, 2018] 利用多个模型产生预测结果，并通过各种技术（例如，提升 [Schapire, 1990, Freund, 1995, Moghimi 等, 2016] 或均值聚合 [Zhou 等, 2018, Zhang 等, 2020] 等）组合这些结果，以实现比单个模型单独更好的性能。这些方法将基础模型学习和集成作为一个整体，并更多地关注训练多样化的基础模型 [Zhou 等, 2018]。
In DG, specifically, ensemble methods are used to exploit the relationship between source domains and the overall prediction results are composed of the superposition of the multiple networks on each domain. Mancini et al. [2018] proposed to aggregate different predictions from specific trained source models. Segu et al. [2020] proposed domain specific batch-norm statistics for each source domain. Zhou et al. [2021] proposed one shared CNN feature extractor with domain specific classifiers and each classifier is an expert to its own domain but non-expert to other domains. MulDEns [Thopalli et al., 2021] relaxes the requirement for domain-specific models and uses a model-domain relevance matrix to define the relations between models and domains. Besides aggregating different domain expert models, other works consider combining model weights in different runs. SWAD [Cha et al., 2021] avoids overfitting models to local sharp minima by averaging model weights below a validation loss threshold. EoA [Arpit et al., 2021] further lessens the frequent computations on validation set by averaging model weights simply from start to the end.
在 DG 中，特别是，集成方法被用来利用源域之间的关系，整体预测结果是由每个域上的多个网络的叠加组成的。Mancini 等 [2018] 提出了从特定的训练源模型中聚合不同的预测。Segu 等 [2020] 为每个源域提出了域特定的批量归一化统计量。Zhou 等 [2021] 提出了一个共享的 CNN 特征提取器，带有域特定的分类器，每个分类器对其自己的域是专家，但对其他域不是专家。MulDEns [Thopalli 等, 2021] 放松了对域特定模型的要求，并使用模型-域相关性矩阵来定义模型与域之间的关系。除了聚合不同的域专家模型外，其他工作还考虑了在不同运行中组合模型权重。SWAD [Cha 等, 2021] 通过在验证损失阈值以下平均模型权重来避免模型过度拟合到局部尖锐最小值。EoA [Arpit 等, 2021] 进一步减少了在验证集上的频繁计算，通过从头到尾简单地平均模型权重。
These ensemble learning methods rely on training or fine-tuning from a pretrained model, share the same limitation of training cost and initialization model selection. They did not consider the model specialty in different domains, classes or even samples. We start from a novel perspective that incorporates various pretrained models without fine-tuning and builds a lightweight specialty-aware ensemble network, which illustrates better generalization performance and largely reduces training costs.
这些集成学习方法依赖于从预训练模型进行训练或微调，共享训练成本和初始化模型选择的相同局限性。它们没有考虑到不同领域、类别甚至样本的模型专业性。我们从一个新的视角出发，整合了多种预训练模型而不进行微调，构建了一个轻量级的专业性感知集成网络，该网络展示了更好的泛化性能并大幅降低了训练成本。

3 Preliminaries
3 初步探讨

3.1 Problem Formulation
3.1 问题公式化

Domain generalization aims to tackle the shift of data distribution among different domains by zero-shot transferring knowledge from seen to unseen domains. Specifically, unlike domain adaptation, samples from unseen target domain(s) are inaccessible in domain generalization. For a domain, its input and label space can be denoted as X∈Rd and Y∈RC ,and its samples are observed constructing a dataset D={(xi,yi)}i=1N with N sample points. Consider that we have S source domains Ds={D1,…,DS} and T target domains Dt={D1,…,DT} with different distributions on X×Y and sharing the label space. Given instances drawn from source domains,the task is to learn a predictor parameterized by θ as fθ∈M:Rd↦RC ,where d is the dimension of input and C is the number of classes in Y . We can define a population loss as ED(θ)=1|D|∑j=1|D|⁡Exi∼Dj[l(fθ(xi),yi)] over the given domain D . The objective is to minimize the task-specific loss l (e.g.,cross-entropy loss for classification) over both source domains Ds and target domains Dt by only minimizing the empirical risk E^Ds(θ) w.r.t. model parameter θ . The performance on the target domains,then, measures the generalization ability of the learned model.
领域泛化旨在通过从已观察到的领域向未观察到的领域进行零样本迁移知识来解决不同领域数据分布的偏移问题。具体来说，与领域适应不同，领域泛化中无法访问未观察到的目标领域样本。对于一个领域，其输入和标签空间可以表示为 X∈Rd 和 Y∈RC，其样本被观察到并构成一个包含 N 个样本点的数据集 D={(xi,yi)}i=1N。考虑到我们有 S 个源领域 Ds={D1,…,DS} 和 T 个目标领域 Dt={D1,…,DT}，它们在 X×Y 上有不同的分布并共享标签空间。给定从源领域抽取的实例，任务是学习一个由 θ 参数化的预测器，表示为 fθ∈M:Rd↦RC，其中 d 是输入的维度，C 是 Y 中的类别数。我们可以定义一个在给定领域 D 上的总体损失 ED(θ)=1|D|∑j=1|D|⁡Exi∼Dj[l(fθ(xi),yi)]。目标是仅通过最小化关于模型参数 θ 的经验风险 E^Ds(θ)，来最小化源领域 Ds 和目标领域 Dt 上的特定任务损失 l（例如，用于分类的交叉熵损失）。然后，目标领域的性能衡量了学习模型的泛化能力。

3.2 Preliminary Analysis
3.2 初步分析

In this section, we want to investigate the generalization ability of various pretrained models, to gain some insights to motivate our method. As suggested by preliminary work [Kumar et al., 2021], a pretrained model with a linear probing layer (i.e., replacing the last layer of the pretrained model and retraining that) may achieve better accuracy in out-of-distribution scenarios than fine-tuning the whole model. However, linear probing is not feasible due to different pretrained models having different penultimate layer output feature dimensions. Instead, as shown in Figure 1 (B), we only train a label space adapter which learns the mapping function parameterized with ϕ as hϕ∈A:RCo↦RC , where Co is the dimension of the label space of the original pretraining dataset (pretraining domain). Thus, all the pretrained models on the same pretraining dataset (e.g., ImageNet) share the same label adapter, through which it largely reduces the adaptation cost of the pretrained models on the new domains.
在本节中，我们希望研究各种预训练模型的一般化能力，以获得一些洞见来激发我们的方法。如初步工作 [Kumar et al., 2021] 所示，一个带有线性探测层的预训练模型（即替换预训练模型的最后一层并重新训练该层）可能在分布外场景中比微调整个模型达到更好的准确度。然而，由于不同的预训练模型具有不同的倒数第二层输出特征维度，线性探测不可行。相反，如图1（B）所示，我们只训练一个标签空间适配器，它学习由 ϕ 参数化的映射函数作为 hϕ∈A:RCo↦RC ，其中 Co 是原始预训练数据集（预训练领域）的标签空间维度。因此，所有在相同预训练数据集（例如，ImageNet）上预训练的模型共享相同的标签适配器，这极大地降低了预训练模型在新领域上的适应成本。
Given the pretrained model pool {fk}k=1K with K pretrained models each of which is parameterized as θk ,we further parameterize the adapted model hϕ(fk(⋅)) as θk′=[ϕ;θk] . 4.1 Framework: Pretrained Model without Fine-tuning
4.1 框架：无需微调的预训练模型

Recall that the focus of the paper is on leveraging pretrained models without fine-tuning to cope with domain generalization. As motivated in Section 3.2, each model has its own specialty and each sample may require to choose a specific set of models to give a good prediction. As a result, we learn the matching of pretrained models and samples from the source domains' training data.
回顾一下，本文的重点是利用无需微调的预训练模型来应对域泛化。正如3.2节中所述，每个模型都有自己的专长，每个样本可能需要选择一组特定的模型以给出良好的预测。因此，我们从源域的训练数据中学习预训练模型和样本的匹配。
Based on this idea, as illustrated in Figure 4, we propose a novel specialty-aware domain generalization framework to dispatch an ensemble of specialized models for each sample. Specifically, a label space adapter described in Section 3.2 is trained to transform the prediction of the pretrained models. And then, an ensemble network is learned to dispatch the models in a model pool to each sample according to their estimated specialty at sample level, and aggregate their outputs as an ensemble to output the final prediction for each sample.
基于这一想法，如图4所示，我们提出了一个新颖的面向专业的领域泛化框架，用于为每个样本分派一组专用模型。具体来说，第3.2节中描述的标签空间适配器被训练以转换预训练模型的预测。然后，学习一个集成网络，根据样本级别的估计专业度将模型池中的模型分派给每个样本，并聚合它们的输出作为集成，为每个样本输出最终预测。

5.3 Ablation Study
5.3 剥夺研究

We want to verify the effectiveness of SEDGE design by answering two research questions: (Q1) Is grafting a label space adapter on top of model outputs sufficient, for utilizing pretrained models to generalize to novel domains? (Q2) Is specialty-aware ensemble necessary, compared to an average ensemble method?
我们希望通过回答两个研究问题来验证 SEDGE 设计的有效性：(Q1) 在模型输出之上嫁接一个标签空间适配器是否足以使预训练模型泛化到新颖领域？(Q2) 与平均集成方法相比，是否需要专有感知集成？
To verify whether a single model with an adapter can perform well, we train an individual adapter on source domains for each pretrained model in the model pool and compare their performance on target domain with state-of-the-art DG algorithm. To show the "cheating" upper bound of performance under this ablation study, we report the best single model performance on test set as best single model + adapter. As shown in Table 3, the best single model + adapter among Model Pool-A can outperform SWAD only on OfficeHome. It first indicates that the generalization ability of the fixed pretrained models may be more promising than model with fine-tuning on specific domains. However, in other four datasets, it lags behind SWAD by a large margin. This demonstrates a single pretrained model with a label space adapter is not sufficient to generalize to unseen domains, which motivates the main contribution of our method of introducing ensemble learning.
为了验证单个模型加上适配器是否能表现良好，我们在每个预训练模型的源领域上分别训练一个单独的适配器，并将其在目标领域的性能与最先进的域泛化算法进行比较。为了展示在这种消融研究下的性能“作弊”上限，我们报告了测试集上最佳单个模型加上适配器的最佳性能。如表3所示，模型池A中的最佳单个模型+适配器仅在 OfficeHome 上超过 SWAD。首先，这表明固定预训练模型的泛化能力可能比特定领域上的微调模型更有前景。然而，在其他四个数据集上，它落后于 SWAD 很大差距。这证明了单个预训练模型加上一个标签空间适配器不足以泛化到未见过的领域，这激发了我们的方法的主要贡献，即引入集成学习。
DG algorithms that combine ensemble learning, such as SWAD and EoA, demonstrate promising performance. A natural question is whether using an ensemble of pretrained models rather than a single model can improve performance. Following the ensemble approaches [Lakshminarayanan et al., 2017] using mean average, we experiment a random ensemble over the fixed pretrained models, i.e.,randomly sampling k models for each sample and averaging their outputs for final prediction. The results are shown in Table 3. As can be seen, random ensemble results in worse performance than the single model, while SEDGE with specialty-aware ensemble boosts the final performance significantly, albeit with strong or weak individual model performance, which verifies the necessity to select and ensemble the pretrained models based on their specialty over samples as mentioned in Section 3.2 (Q2).
DG算法结合了集成学习，如SWAD和EoA，表现出有希望的性能。一个自然的问题是，使用预训练模型的集成而不是单个模型是否能提高性能。遵循[Lakshminarayanan等人，2017]使用平均值的集成方法，我们对固定的预训练模型进行随机集成实验，即对每个样本随机抽取k模型并平均它们的输出以进行最终预测。结果如表3所示。可以看出，随机集成比单个模型的性能更差，而具有专长感知集成的SEDGE显著提高了最终性能，尽管单个模型的性能或强或弱，这验证了在第3.2节（Q2）中提到的根据样本的专长选择和集成预训练模型的必要性。

6 Conclusions
6 结论

Domain generalization algorithms use the pretrained model as initialization for fine-tuning, while a few works have found that fine-tuning may lead to out-of-distribution performance degradation. Different from the previous fine-tuning paradigm, this paper proposes a novel paradigm for domain generalization, specialized ensemble learning which learns to dispatch an ensemble of fixed pretrained models for each sample based on the model specialty on it. Experiments on five benchmark datasets show that our proposed method has achieved state-of-the-art performance with significant training cost reduction.
领域泛化算法使用预训练模型作为微调的初始化，而一些研究已经发现微调可能导致分布外性能下降。与之前的微调范式不同，本文提出了一个用于领域泛化的新范式，即专门的集成学习，它学习根据模型在样本上的专长为每个样本分配一组固定的预训练模型。在五个基准数据集上的实验表明，我们提出的方法在不显著的训练成本降低的同时，实现了最先进的表现。
