Abstract

摘要

Large pre-trained vision-language models like CLIP have shown great potential in learning representations that are transferable across a wide range of downstream tasks. Different from the traditional representation learning that is based mostly on discretized labels, vision-language pre-training aligns images and texts in a common feature space, which allows zero-shot transfer to a downstream task via prompting, i.e., classification weights are synthesized from natural language describing classes of interest. In this work, we show that a major challenge for deploying such models in practice is prompt engineering, which requires domain expertise and is extremely time-consuming-one needs to spend a significant amount of time on words tuning since a slight change in wording could have a huge impact on performance. Inspired by recent advances in prompt learning research in natural language processing (NLP), we propose Context Optimization (CoOp), a simple approach specifically for adapting CLIP-like vision-language models for downstream image recognition. Concretely, CoOp models a prompt's context words with learnable vectors while the entire pre-trained parameters are kept fixed. To handle different image recognition tasks, we provide two implementations of CoOp: unified context and class-specific context. Through extensive experiments on 11 datasets, we demonstrate that CoOp requires as few as one or two shots to beat hand-crafted prompts with a decent margin and is able to gain significant improvements over prompt engineering with more shots, e.g., with 16 shots the average gain is around 15% (with the highest reaching over 45%). Despite being a learning-based approach, CoOp achieves superb domain generalization performance compared with the zero-shot model using hand-crafted prompts.

大型预训练视觉语言模型如CLIP在学习可迁移到多种下游任务的表征方面展现出巨大潜力。不同于主要基于离散标签的传统表征学习，视觉语言预训练将图像和文本对齐到一个共同的特征空间，从而允许通过提示（prompting）实现零样本迁移，即通过描述感兴趣类别的自然语言合成分类权重。本文指出，实际部署此类模型的主要挑战是提示工程，这需要领域专业知识且极其耗时——因为措辞的细微变化可能对性能产生巨大影响。受自然语言处理（NLP）中提示学习最新进展的启发，我们提出了上下文优化（Context Optimization，CoOp），一种专门用于适配CLIP类视觉语言模型进行下游图像识别的简单方法。具体而言，CoOp用可学习向量建模提示的上下文词，同时保持整个预训练参数不变。为应对不同图像识别任务，我们提供了两种CoOp实现：统一上下文和类别特定上下文。通过在11个数据集上的大量实验，我们证明CoOp仅需一两张样本即可明显优于手工设计的提示，且随着样本数增加，性能提升显著，例如16张样本时平均提升约15%（最高超过45%）。尽管是基于学习的方法，CoOp在领域泛化性能上也优于使用手工提示的零样本模型。

1 Introduction

1 引言

A common approach for building state-of-the-art visual recognition systems is to train vision models to predict for a fixed set of object categories using discrete labels (He et al., 2016; Dosovitskiy et al., 2021). From a technical point of view, this is achieved by matching image features-produced by a vision model like ResNet (He et al., 2016) or ViT (Doso-vitskiy et al., 2021)-with a fixed set of weights that are seen as visual concepts and initialized randomly. Although training categories often have a textual form, such as "goldfish" or "toilet paper," they will be converted into discrete labels just for easing the computation of the cross-entropy loss, leaving the semantics encapsulated in texts largely unexploited. Such a learning paradigm limits visual recognition systems to closed-set visual concepts, making them unable to deal with new categories since additional data are required for learning a new classifier.

构建最先进视觉识别系统的常用方法是训练视觉模型预测固定对象类别集合，使用离散标签（He等，2016；Dosovitskiy等，2021）。从技术角度看，这通过将视觉模型（如ResNet（He等，2016）或ViT（Dosovitskiy等，2021））产生的图像特征与一组被视为视觉概念且随机初始化的固定权重匹配实现。尽管训练类别通常有文本形式，如“金鱼”或“卫生纸”，但它们会被转换为离散标签以简化交叉熵损失计算，文本中蕴含的语义信息大多未被利用。这种学习范式限制了视觉识别系统只能识别封闭集视觉概念，无法处理新类别，因为学习新分类器需要额外数据。

Recently, vision-language pre-training such as CLIP (Radford et al., 2021) and ALIGN (Jia et al., 2021) has emerged as a promising alternative for visual representation learning. The main idea is to align images and raw texts using two separate encoders-one for each modality. For instance, both CLIP and ALIGN formulate the learning objective as a contrastive loss, which pulls together images and their textual descriptions while pushes away unmatched pairs in the feature space. By pre-training at a large scale, models can learn diverse visual concepts and can readily be transferred to any downstream task through prompting (Radford et al., 2021; Jia et al., 2021; Fürst et al., 2021; Li et al., 2021; Singh et al., 2021; Yuan et al., 2021). In particular, for any new classification task one can first synthesize the classification weights by giving sentences describing task-relevant categories to the text encoder, and then compare with image features produced by the image encoder.

近年来，视觉语言预训练如CLIP（Radford等，2021）和ALIGN（Jia等，2021）成为视觉表征学习的有前景替代方案。其核心思想是使用两个独立编码器分别对图像和原始文本进行编码，实现图像与文本的对齐。例如，CLIP和ALIGN都将学习目标设为对比损失，拉近图像与其文本描述的特征距离，同时推远不匹配的图文对。通过大规模预训练，模型能够学习多样的视觉概念，并可通过提示轻松迁移到任何下游任务（Radford等，2021；Jia等，2021；Fürst等，2021；Li等，2021；Singh等，2021；Yuan等，2021）。特别地，对于任何新分类任务，可以先通过向文本编码器输入描述任务相关类别的句子合成分类权重，再与图像编码器产生的图像特征进行比较。

We observe that for pre-trained vision-language models, the text input, known as prompt, plays a key role in downstream datasets. However, identifying the right prompt is a non-trivial task, which often takes a significant amount of time for words tuning-a slight change in wording could make a huge difference in performance. For instance, for Caltech101 (Figure 1(a), 2nd vs 3rd prompt), adding "a" before the class token brings more than 5% increase in accuracy. Moreover, prompt engineering also requires prior knowledge about the task and ideally the language model's underlying mechanism. This is exemplified in Figure 1(b-d) where adding task-relevant context can lead to significant improvements, i.e., "flower" for Flowers102, "texture" for DTD and "satellite" for EuroSAT. Tuning the sentence structure could bring further improvements, e.g., putting "a type of flower" after the class token for Flowers102, keeping only "texture" in the context for DTD, and adding "centered" before "satellite photo" for EuroSAT. However, even with extensive tuning, the resulting prompts are by no means guaranteed to be optimal for these downstream tasks.

我们观察到，对于预训练的视觉-语言模型，文本输入，即提示（prompt），在下游数据集中起着关键作用。然而，确定合适的提示并非易事，通常需要大量时间进行词语调优——措辞的细微变化可能导致性能的巨大差异。例如，对于Caltech101（图1(a)，第2与第3个提示），在类别标记前加上“a”能使准确率提升超过5%。此外，提示工程还需要对任务有先验知识，理想情况下还需了解语言模型的底层机制。如图1(b-d)所示，添加与任务相关的上下文能显著提升性能，即Flowers102的“flower”，DTD的“texture”，以及EuroSAT的“satellite”。调整句子结构还能带来进一步提升，例如在Flowers102的类别标记后加上“a type of flower”，在DTD的上下文中仅保留“texture”，以及在EuroSAT中在“satellite photo”前加上“centered”。然而，即使经过大量调优，所得提示也绝不能保证对这些下游任务是最优的。

Inspired by recent prompt learning research in natural language processing (NLP) (Shin et al., 2020; Jiang et al., 2020; Zhong et al., 2021), we propose a simple approach called Context Optimization (CoOp)1 to automate prompt engineering, specifically for pre-trained vision-language models. Concretely, CoOp models a prompt’s context words with learnable vectors, which could be initialized with either random values or pre-trained word embeddings (see Figure 2). Two implementations are provided to handle tasks of different natures: one is based on unified context, which shares the same context with all classes and works well on most categories; while the other is based on class-specific context, which learns a specific set of context tokens for each class and is found to be more suitable for some fine-grained categories. During training, we simply minimize prediction errors using the cross-entropy loss with respect to the learnable context vectors while keeping the entire pre-trained parameters fixed. The gradients can be back-propagated all the way through the text encoder, distilling the rich knowledge encoded in the parameters for learning task-relevant context.

受自然语言处理（NLP）中近期提示学习研究（Shin et al., 2020；Jiang et al., 2020；Zhong et al., 2021）的启发，我们提出了一种名为上下文优化（Context Optimization）(CoOp)1的简单方法，用于自动化提示工程，专门针对预训练的视觉-语言模型。具体而言，CoOp用可学习的向量对提示的上下文词进行建模，这些向量可以用随机值或预训练词嵌入初始化（见图2）。我们提供了两种实现以应对不同性质的任务：一种基于统一上下文，所有类别共享相同上下文，适用于大多数类别；另一种基于类别特定上下文，为每个类别学习一组特定的上下文标记，发现更适合某些细粒度类别。在训练过程中，我们仅通过交叉熵损失最小化预测误差，针对可学习的上下文向量，同时保持整个预训练参数不变。梯度可以一直反向传播至文本编码器，提炼参数中编码的丰富知识以学习与任务相关的上下文。

To demonstrate the effectiveness of CoOp ,we benchmark on 11 datasets, which cover a diverse set of visual recognition tasks including classification on generic objects, scenes, actions and fine-grained categories, as well as specialized tasks like recognizing textures and satellite imagery. The results show that CoOp effectively turns pre-trained vision-language models into data-efficient visual learners, requiring as few as one or two shots to beat hand-crafted prompts with a decent margin. The performance can be further boosted by using more shots, e.g., with 16 shots the margin over hand-crafted prompts averages at around 15% and reaches over 45% for the highest. CoOp also outperforms the linear probe model, which is known as a strong few-shot learning baseline (Tian et al., 2020). Furthermore, CoOp demonstrates much stronger robustness than the zero-shot model (which uses manual prompts) to domain shifts, despite being a learning-based approach.

为了验证CoOp的有效性，我们在11个数据集上进行了基准测试，涵盖了多样的视觉识别任务，包括通用物体、场景、动作和细粒度类别的分类，以及识别纹理和卫星影像等专业任务。结果表明，CoOp有效地将预训练视觉-语言模型转变为数据高效的视觉学习者，仅需一两次样本即可显著超越手工设计的提示。使用更多样本可进一步提升性能，例如16次样本时，相较手工提示的平均提升约为15%，最高提升超过45%。CoOp还优于线性探测模型，该模型被认为是强有力的少样本学习基线（Tian et al., 2020）。此外，尽管是基于学习的方法，CoOp在面对领域转移时表现出比零样本模型（使用手工提示）更强的鲁棒性。

2 Related Work

2 相关工作

2.1 Vision-Language Models

2.1 视觉-语言模型

Vision-language models have recently demonstrated great potential in learning generic visual representations and allowing zero-shot transfer to a variety of downstream classification tasks via prompting (Radford et al., 2021; Jia et al., 2021; Zhang et al., 2020; Singh et al., 2021; Yuan et al., 2021).

视觉-语言模型近期展现出在学习通用视觉表征方面的巨大潜力，并通过提示实现对多种下游分类任务的零样本迁移（Radford et al., 2021；Jia et al., 2021；Zhang et al., 2020；Singh et al., 2021；Yuan et al., 2021）。

To our knowledge, the recent developments in vision-language learning, particularly CLIP (Radford et al., 2021) and ALIGN (Jia et al., 2021), are largely driven by advances in the following three areas: i) text representation learning with Transformers (Vaswani et al., 2017), ii) large-minibatch contrastive representation learning (Chen et al., 2020; He et al., 2020; Hénaff et al., 2020), and iii) web-scale training datasets-CLIP benefits from 400 million curated image-text pairs while ALIGN exploits 1.8 billion noisy image-text pairs.

据我们所知，近期视觉-语言学习的发展，特别是CLIP（Radford等，2021）和ALIGN（Jia等，2021），主要得益于以下三个方面的进展：i）基于Transformer（Vaswani等，2017）的文本表示学习，ii）大批量对比表示学习（Chen等，2020；He等，2020；Hénaff等，2020），以及iii）网络规模的训练数据集——CLIP利用了4亿条精心筛选的图文对，而ALIGN则利用了18亿条噪声较多的图文对。

The idea of mapping images and text onto a common embedding space has been studied since nearly a decade ago (Socher et al., 2013; Frome et al., 2013; Elhoseiny et al., 2013), but with drastically different technologies. For text features extraction, early work has mainly utilized pre-trained word vectors (Socher et al., 2013; Frome et al., 2013) or the hand-crafted TF-IDF features (Elhoseiny et al., 2013; Lei Ba et al., 2015). Matching images and text features has been formulated as metric learning (Frome et al., 2013), multi-label classification (Joulin et al., 2016; Gomez et al., 2017), n-gram language learning (Li et al., 2017), and the recently proposed captioning (Desai and Johnson, 2021).

将图像和文本映射到共同嵌入空间的想法已研究近十年（Socher等，2013；Frome等，2013；Elhoseiny等，2013），但所用技术迥异。文本特征提取方面，早期工作主要利用预训练词向量（Socher等，2013；Frome等，2013）或手工设计的TF-IDF特征（Elhoseiny等，2013；Lei Ba等，2015）。图文特征匹配被形式化为度量学习（Frome等，2013）、多标签分类（Joulin等，2016；Gomez等，2017）、n-gram语言学习（Li等，2017）以及最近提出的图像描述生成（Desai和Johnson，2021）。

Our work is orthogonal to recent research in vision-language models, aiming to facilitate the adaptation and deployment of such models in downstream datasets.

我们的工作与近期视觉-语言模型研究相辅相成，旨在促进此类模型在下游数据集上的适应与部署。

2.2 Prompt Learning in NLP

2.2 自然语言处理中的提示学习

Knowledge probing for large pre-trained language models, formally defined by Petroni et al. (2019) as "fill-in-the-blank" cloze tests, has recently sparked interest in prompt learning research in NLP (Shin et al., 2020; Jiang et al., 2020; Li and Liang, 2021; Zhong et al., 2021; Lester et al., 2021; Gao et al., 2020; Liu et al., 2021b).

大型预训练语言模型的知识探测，Petroni等（2019）正式定义为“填空”测试，近期激发了自然语言处理领域对提示学习的兴趣（Shin等，2020；Jiang等，2020；Li和Liang，2021；Zhong等，2021；Lester等，2021；Gao等，2020；Liu等，2021b）。

The basic idea of knowledge probing is to induce pre-trained language models to generate answers given cloze-style prompts, which can benefit a number of downstream tasks, such as sentiment analysis. Jiang et al. (2020) propose to generate candidate prompts through text mining and paraphrasing, and identify the optimal ones that give the highest training accuracy. Shin et al. (2020) introduce a gradient-based approach, which searches for tokens with the largest gradient changes in the label likelihood.

知识探测的基本思路是引导预训练语言模型在填空式提示下生成答案，这对诸多下游任务如情感分析有益。Jiang等（2020）提出通过文本挖掘和释义生成候选提示，并筛选出训练准确率最高的提示。Shin等（2020）引入基于梯度的方法，搜索标签似然中梯度变化最大的词元。

Most related to our work are continuous prompt learning methods (Zhong et al., 2021; Li and Liang, 2021; Lester et al., 2021) which optimize continuous vectors in the word embedding space. A drawback of such methods compared to searching discrete tokens is the lack of a clear way to visualize what "words" are learned for the vectors. We refer readers to Liu et al. (2021a) for a comprehensive survey in the topic of prompt learning in NLP.

与我们工作最相关的是连续提示学习方法（Zhong等，2021；Li和Liang，2021；Lester等，2021），它们在词嵌入空间中优化连续向量。相比于搜索离散词元，这类方法的缺点是缺乏直观方式来可视化向量所学的“词语”。我们建议读者参考Liu等（2021a）对自然语言处理领域提示学习的全面综述。

It is worth noting that we are the first to apply prompt learning to the adaptation of large vision-language models in computer vision-which we view as an important topic for democratizing foundation models (Bommasani et al., 2021)—and justify that prompt learning not only brings significant improvements to computer vision tasks in terms of transfer learning performance but also produces robust models that can handle domain shifts.

值得注意的是，我们首次将提示学习应用于大型视觉-语言模型在计算机视觉中的适应，这被视为普及基础模型（Bommasani等，2021）的重要议题——并证明提示学习不仅显著提升了计算机视觉任务的迁移学习性能，还能生成能够应对领域变化的鲁棒模型。

3 Methodology

3 方法论

3.1 Vision-Language Pre-Training

3.1 视觉-语言预训练

We briefly introduce vision-language pre-training with a particular focus on CLIP (Radford et al., 2021). Our approach is applicable to broader CLIP-like vision-language models.

我们简要介绍视觉-语言预训练，特别聚焦于CLIP（Radford等，2021）。我们的方法适用于更广泛的类似CLIP的视觉-语言模型。

Models CLIP consists of two encoders, one for images and the other for text. The image encoder aims to map high-dimensional images into a low-dimensional embedding space. The architecture of the image encoder can take the form of a CNN like ResNet-50 (He et al., 2016) or a ViT (Dosovitskiy et al., 2021). On the other hand, the text encoder is built on top of a Transformer (Vaswani et al., 2017) and aims to generate text representations from natural language.

CLIP模型由两个编码器组成，一个用于图像，另一个用于文本。图像编码器旨在将高维图像映射到低维嵌入空间。图像编码器的架构可以采用类似ResNet-50（He等，2016）的卷积神经网络（CNN）或ViT（Dosovitskiy等，2021）。另一方面，文本编码器基于Transformer（Vaswani等，2017）构建，旨在从自然语言生成文本表示。

Specifically, given a sequence of words (tokens), such as "a photo of a dog," CLIP first converts each one of the token (including punctuation) into a lower-cased byte pair encoding (BPE) representation (Sennrich et al., 2016), which is essentially a unique numeric ID. The vocabulary size in CLIP is 49,152. To facilitate minibatch processing, each text sequence is encompassed with the [SOS] and [EOS] tokens and capped at a fixed length of 77 . After that, the IDs are mapped to 512-D word embedding vectors, which are then passed on to the Transformer. Finally, the features at the [EOS] token position are layer normalized and further processed by a linear projection layer.

具体来说，给定一个单词（标记）序列，例如“a photo of a dog”，CLIP首先将每个标记（包括标点符号）转换为小写的字节对编码（BPE）表示（Sennrich等，2016），本质上是一个唯一的数字ID。CLIP的词汇表大小为49,152。为了便于小批量处理，每个文本序列都被包裹在[SOS]和[EOS]标记之间，并限制在固定长度77以内。之后，这些ID被映射为512维的词嵌入向量，随后传入Transformer。最后，对[EOS]标记位置的特征进行层归一化，并通过线性投影层进一步处理。

Training CLIP is trained to align the two embedding spaces learned for images and text respectively. Specifically, the learning objective is formulated as a contrastive loss. Given a batch of image-text pairs, CLIP maximizes the cosine similarity for matched pairs while minimizes the cosine similarity for all other unmatched pairs. To learn diverse visual concepts that are more transferable to downstream tasks, CLIP's team collects a large training dataset consisting of 400 million image-text pairs.

训练 CLIP旨在对齐分别为图像和文本学习的两个嵌入空间。具体而言，学习目标被定义为对比损失。给定一批图像-文本对，CLIP最大化匹配对的余弦相似度，同时最小化所有不匹配对的余弦相似度。为了学习更多样化且更易迁移到下游任务的视觉概念，CLIP团队收集了包含4亿图像-文本对的大规模训练数据集。

Zero-Shot Inference Since CLIP is pre-trained to predict whether an image matches a textual description, it naturally fits zero-shot recognition. This is achieved by comparing image features with the classification weights synthesized by the text encoder, which takes as input textual descriptions specifying classes of interest. Formally,let f be image features extracted by the image encoder for an image x and {wi}i=1K a set of weight vectors generated by the text encoder. K denotes the number of classes and each wi is derived from a prompt that could have the form of "a photo of a [CLASS]." where the class token is replaced by the specific class name, such as "cat," "dog" or "car." The prediction probability is then computed as

零样本推理 由于CLIP经过预训练以预测图像是否与文本描述匹配，因此天然适合零样本识别。这是通过将图像特征与由文本编码器合成的分类权重进行比较实现的，文本编码器以指定感兴趣类别的文本描述作为输入。形式上，设f为图像编码器提取的图像x的特征，{wi}i=1K为文本编码器生成的一组权重向量。K表示类别数，每个wi由一个提示语生成，形式如“a photo of a [CLASS]”，其中类别标记被具体类别名称替换，如“cat”、“dog”或“car”。预测概率计算如下

(1)p(y=i∣x)=exp⁡(cos⁡(wi,f)/τ)∑j=1K⁡exp⁡(cos⁡(wj,f)/τ),

where τ is a temperature parameter learned by CLIP and cos⁡(⋅,⋅) denotes cosine similarity.

其中τ是CLIP学习的温度参数，cos⁡(⋅,⋅)表示余弦相似度。

Compared with the traditional classifier learning approach where closed-set visual concepts are learned from random vectors, vision-language pre-training allows open-set visual concepts to be explored through a high-capacity text encoder, leading to a broader semantic space and in turn making the learned representations more transferable to downstream tasks.

与传统分类器学习方法从随机向量学习封闭集视觉概念不同，视觉-语言预训练通过高容量的文本编码器探索开放集视觉概念，导致更广泛的语义空间，从而使学习到的表示更易迁移到下游任务。

3.2 Context Optimization

3.2 上下文优化

We propose Context Optimization (CoOp), which avoids manual prompt tuning by modeling context words with continuous vectors that are end-to-end learned from data while the massive pre-trained parameters are frozen. An overview is shown in Figure 2. Below we provide several different implementations.

我们提出了上下文优化（Context Optimization，CoOp），通过用连续向量建模上下文词汇，避免了手动提示调优，这些向量从数据中端到端学习，而庞大的预训练参数保持冻结。概览见图2。以下我们提供几种不同的实现方式。

Unified Context We first introduce the unified context version, which shares the same context with all classes. Specifically,the prompt given to the text encoder g(⋅) is designed with the following form,

统一上下文 我们首先介绍统一上下文版本，该版本对所有类别共享相同的上下文。具体来说，给文本编码器g(⋅)的提示设计为以下形式，

(2)t=[V]1[V]2…[V]M[ CLASS ],

where each [V]m(m∈{1,…,M}) is a vector with the same dimension as word embeddings (i.e.,512 for CLIP),and M is a hyperparameter specifying the number of context tokens.

其中每个[V]m(m∈{1,…,M})是与词嵌入维度相同的向量（即CLIP为512维），M是指定上下文标记数量的超参数。

By forwarding a prompt t to the text encoder g(⋅) ,we can obtain a classification weight vector representing a visual concept (still from the [EOS] token position). The prediction probability is computed as

通过将提示t传入文本编码器g(⋅)，我们可以获得表示视觉概念的分类权重向量（仍来自[EOS]标记位置）。预测概率计算如下

(3)p(y=i∣x)=exp⁡(cos⁡(g(ti),f)/τ)∑j=1K⁡exp⁡(cos⁡(g(tj),f)/τ),

where the class token within each prompt ti is replaced by the corresponding word embedding vector(s) of the i -th class name.

其中每个提示ti中的类别标记被对应第i类名称的词嵌入向量替换。

Other than placing the class token at the end of a sequence as in Equation (2), we can also put it in the middle like

除了像公式（2）中那样将类别标记放在序列末尾，我们也可以将其放在中间，如下所示

(4)t=[V]1…[V]M2[CLASS][V]M2+1…[V]M,

which increases flexibility for learning-the prompt is allowed to either fill the latter cells with supplementary descriptions or cut off the sentence earlier by using a termination signal such as full stop.

这增加了学习的灵活性——提示可以用补充描述填充后续位置，或者通过使用终止信号（如句号）提前截断句子。

Class-Specific Context Another option is to design class-specific context (CSC) where context vectors are independent to each class,i.e., [V]1i[V]2i…[V]Mi≠[V]1j[V]2j…[V]Mj for i≠j and i,j∈{1,…,K} . As an alternative to unified context, we find that CSC is particularly useful for some fine-grained classification tasks.

类别特定上下文 另一种选择是设计类别特定上下文（CSC），其中上下文向量对每个类别独立，即，[V]1i[V]2i…[V]Mi≠[V]1j[V]2j…[V]Mj 对于 i≠j 和 i,j∈{1,…,K} 。作为统一上下文的替代方案，我们发现CSC在某些细粒度分类任务中特别有用。

Training is performed to minimize the standard classification loss based on the cross-entropy, and the gradients can be back-propagated all the way through the text encoder g(⋅) , making use of the rich knowledge encoded in the parameters to optimize the context. The design of continuous representations also allows full exploration in the word embedding space, which facilitates the learning of task-relevant context.

训练通过最小化基于交叉熵的标准分类损失进行，梯度可以一直反向传播通过文本编码器 g(⋅) ，利用参数中编码的丰富知识来优化上下文。连续表示的设计也允许在词嵌入空间中充分探索，有助于学习与任务相关的上下文。

3.3 Discussion

3.3 讨论

Our approach specifically addresses the emerging problem of the adaptation of recently proposed large vision-language models such as CLIP (Radford et al., 2021). There are some differences that distinguish our approach from the prompt learning methods developed in NLP for language models (e.g., GPT-3 (Brown et al., 2020)). First, the backbone architectures are clearly different for CLIP-like models and language models-the former take both visual and textual data as input and produce alignment scores used for image recognition, while the latter are tailored to handle textual data only. Second, the pre-training objectives are different: contrastive learning vs autoregressive learning. This would lead to different model behaviors and thus require different module designs.

我们的方法专门针对最近提出的大型视觉-语言模型（如CLIP（Radford等，2021））的适应性问题。我们的方案与NLP中为语言模型（如GPT-3（Brown等，2020））开发的提示学习方法存在一些区别。首先，CLIP类模型和语言模型的骨干架构明显不同——前者同时接受视觉和文本数据作为输入，输出用于图像识别的对齐分数，而后者仅处理文本数据。其次，预训练目标不同：对比学习与自回归学习。这导致模型行为不同，因此需要不同的模块设计。

4.2 Domain Generalization

4.2 域泛化

Since CoOp requires training on a specific data distribution, it risks learning spurious correlations that are detrimental to generalization in unseen distributions (domains), as suggested in recent studies (Taori et al., 2020; Zhou et al., 2021). On the contrary, zero-shot CLIP is not tied to a specific data distribution and has exhibited strong robustness to distribution shifts (Radford et al., 2021). In this section, we aim to unveil how robust CoOp is to distribution shifts,in comparison to zero-shot CLIP and the linear probe model.

由于CoOp需要在特定数据分布上训练，存在学习到对泛化不利的虚假相关性的风险，正如近期研究（Taori等，2020；Zhou等，2021）所指出的。相反，零样本CLIP不依赖于特定数据分布，且已表现出对分布变化的强鲁棒性（Radford等，2021）。本节旨在揭示CoOp相较于零样本CLIP和线性探测模型在分布变化下的鲁棒性。

Datasets The source dataset is ImageNet. The target datasets are ImageNetV2 (Recht et al., 2019), ImageNet-Sketch (Wang et al., 2019), ImageNet-A (Hendrycks et al., 2021b) and ImageNet-R (Hendrycks et al., 2021a), all of which have compatible class names with ImageNet allowing seamless transfer for the prompts learned by CoOp. ImageNetV2 is a reproduced test set using different sources while following ImageNet's data collection process. ImageNet-Sketch contains sketch images belonging to the same 1,000 ImageNet classes. Both ImageNet-A and -R contain 200 classes derived from a subset of ImageNet's 1,000 classes. The former consists of real-world adversarially filtered images that cause current ImageNet classifiers to produce low results, whereas the latter features a rendition of the ImageNet classes in diverse image styles such as paintings, cartoons and sculptures.

4.3 Further Analysis

4.3 进一步分析

Context Length How many context tokens should be used? And is it better to have more context tokens? The results in Section 4.2 suggest having a shorter context length benefits domain generalization (probably due to less over-fitting as fewer parameters are learned). Here we study this hyperparameter for source datasets. Specifically, we repeat experiments on the 11 datasets by varying the context length from 4 to 8 to 16 . The average results are shown in Figure 5(a), which indicate that having more context tokens leads to better performance and that positioning the class token in the middle gains more momentum with longer context length. To sum up, there is no golden rule for selecting perfect context length since one needs to balance between performance and robustness to distribution shift.

上下文长度 应该使用多少上下文标记？更多的上下文标记是否更好？第4.2节的结果表明，较短的上下文长度有利于领域泛化（可能是因为学习的参数较少，过拟合较少）。这里我们研究源数据集的该超参数。具体来说，我们在11个数据集上重复实验，将上下文长度从4变为8再到16。平均结果如图5(a)所示，表明更多的上下文标记带来更好的性能，且将类别标记置于中间位置在较长上下文长度时效果提升更明显。总之，选择完美的上下文长度没有黄金法则，需要在性能和对分布偏移的鲁棒性之间权衡。

Vision Backbones Figure 5(b) summarizes the results on the 11 datasets using a variety of vision backbones covering both CNNs and ViTs. The results are expected: the more advanced the backbone, the better the performance. The gap between CoOp and hand-crafted prompts is significant across all architectures.

视觉骨干网络 图5(b)总结了在11个数据集上使用多种视觉骨干网络（涵盖CNN和ViT）的结果。结果符合预期：骨干网络越先进，性能越好。CoOp与手工设计提示词之间的差距在所有架构中均显著。

Comparison with Prompt Ensembling The authors of CLIP (Radford et al., 2021) have suggested that additional improvements can be obtained by ensembling over multiple zero-shot classifiers generated using different hand-crafted prompts, such as "a photo of the large [CLASS].", "a bad photo of the [CLASS]." and "a origami [CLASS].", which reflect a different scale, view and abstraction respectively for an image. We are interested to know whether the prompts learned by CoOp can still maintain advantages when compared with prompt ensembling. For fair comparison, we use the select prompts from Radford et al. (2021), which have been extensively tuned on ImageNet, to construct the ensemble classifier. Table 2 shows the comparison and justifies the superiority of CoOp. Given the potential of prompt ensem-bling, future work could investigate how to improve CoOp from the ensembling perspective.

与提示词集成的比较 CLIP的作者（Radford等，2021）提出，通过对使用不同手工设计提示词生成的多个零样本分类器进行集成，可以获得额外提升，例如“a photo of the large [CLASS].”、“a bad photo of the [CLASS].”和“a origami [CLASS].”，分别反映图像的不同尺度、视角和抽象层次。我们关注CoOp学习的提示词在与提示词集成比较时是否仍保持优势。为公平比较，我们使用Radford等（2021）中经过ImageNet广泛调优的精选提示词构建集成分类器。表2展示了比较结果，证明了CoOp的优越性。鉴于提示词集成的潜力，未来工作可从集成角度探讨如何提升CoOp。

Comparison with Other Fine-tuning Methods We further compare CoOp with other fine-tuning methods: i) fine-tuning CLIP’s image encoder; ii) optimizing a transformation layer added to the text encoder's output; iii) optimizing a bias term added to the text encoder's output. The results are shown in Table 5. Obviously, fine-tuning the image encoder does not work well. Adding a transformation layer slightly improves upon the zero-shot model. Adding a bias term shows promising results, but still largely underperforms CoOp, which suggests that the gradients that went through the text encoder provide more useful information.

初始化 我们比较了随机初始化与手动初始化。后者使用“a photo of a”的嵌入向量来初始化11个数据集的上下文向量。为公平比较，使用随机初始化时我们也将上下文长度设置为4。表3表明，“良好”的初始化并不会带来显著差异。尽管进一步调整初始化词可能有帮助，但在实际中我们建议使用简单的随机初始化方法。

Interpreting the Learned Prompts is difficult because the context vectors are optimized in a continuous space. We resort to an indirect way by searching within the vocabulary for words that are closest to the learned vectors based on the Euclidean distance. Note that CLIP (Radford et al., 2021) uses the BPE representation (Sennrich et al., 2016) for tokenization, so the vocabulary includes subwords that frequently appear in text, such as "hu" (subsumed by many words like "hug" and "human"). Table 4 shows the searched results on some datasets. We observe that a few words are somewhat relevant to the tasks, such as "enjoyed" for Food101, "fluffy" and "paw" for OxfordPets, and "pretty" for DTD. But when connecting all the nearest words together, the prompts do not make much sense. We also observe that when using manual initialization (like "a photo of a"), the nearest words for the converged vectors are mostly the ones used for initialization. We conjecture that the learned vectors might encode meanings that are beyond the existing vocabulary. Overall, we are unable to draw any firm conclusion based on the observations because using nearest words to interpret the learned prompts could be inaccurate-the semantics of the vectors is not necessarily correlated with the nearest words.

解释学习到的提示较为困难，因为上下文向量是在连续空间中优化的。我们采用一种间接方法，通过在词汇表中搜索与学习向量基于欧氏距离最接近的词语。注意，CLIP（Radford等，2021）使用BPE表示法（Sennrich等，2016）进行分词，因此词汇表包含频繁出现的子词，如“hu”（包含于“hug”和“human”等多个词中）。表4展示了部分数据集的搜索结果。我们观察到一些词与任务有一定相关性，如Food101的“enjoyed”，OxfordPets的“fluffy”和“paw”，以及DTD的“pretty”。但将所有最近词连接起来，提示语并无太大意义。我们还发现，使用手动初始化（如“a photo of a”）时，收敛向量的最近词大多是初始化时使用的词。我们推测学习到的向量可能编码了超出现有词汇的含义。总体而言，基于观察我们无法得出确切结论，因为用最近词解释学习提示可能不准确——向量的语义不一定与最近词相关联。

5 Conclusion, Limitations and Future Work

5 结论、局限性与未来工作

Large pre-trained vision-language models have shown surprisingly powerful capabilities in diverse downstream applications. However, these models, also called vision foundation models given their "critically central yet incomplete" nature (Bommasani et al., 2021), need to be adapted using automated techniques for better downstream performance and efficiency.

大型预训练视觉-语言模型在多样的下游应用中展现出惊人的强大能力。然而，这些模型也被称为视觉基础模型，鉴于其“关键核心但不完整”的特性（Bommasani等，2021），需要通过自动化技术进行适配，以提升下游性能和效率。

Our research provides timely insights on how CLIP-like models can be turned into a data-efficient learner by using prompt learning, and reveals that despite being a learning-based approach, CoOp performs much better in domain generalization than manual prompts. The results serve as strong evidence that prompt learning has potential for large vision models. It is worth noting that our paper presents the first comprehensive study about adapting large vision models with prompt learning.

我们的研究及时揭示了如何通过提示学习将类似CLIP的模型转变为数据高效的学习者，并表明尽管是一种基于学习的方法，CoOp在领域泛化上远优于手动提示。结果有力证明了提示学习在大型视觉模型中的潜力。值得注意的是，本文首次对使用提示学习适配大型视觉模型进行了全面研究。

Though the performance is excellent,the results of CoOp are relatively difficult to interpret, like other continuous prompt learning methods in NLP. The experiments also reveal that CoOp is sensitive to noisy labels given the weak performance on Food101 (Table 5).

尽管性能优异，CoOp的结果相对难以解释，类似于NLP中其他连续提示学习方法。实验还显示，CoOp对噪声标签较为敏感，正如在Food101上的较弱表现（表5）所示。

Nevertheless, the simplicity of CoOp allows easy extension for future work and there remain many interesting questions to explore, such as cross-dataset transfer (Zhou et al., 2022) and test-time adaptation (Wang et al., 2020). It would also be interesting to investigate more generic adaptation methods for mega-size vision models (Jia et al., 2022; Bahng et al., 2022; Gao et al., 2021). In summary, we hope the empirical findings and insights presented in this work could pave the way for future research on efficient adaptation methods for emerging foundation models, which is still a nascent research topic.

然而，CoOp的简洁性便于未来工作扩展，仍有许多有趣问题待探索，如跨数据集迁移（Zhou等，2022）和测试时适配（Wang等，2020）。研究更通用的超大规模视觉模型适配方法（Jia等，2022；Bahng等，2022；Gao等，2021）也颇具意义。总之，我们希望本文的实证发现和见解能为未来高效适配新兴基础模型的研究铺路，该领域仍处于起步阶段。
