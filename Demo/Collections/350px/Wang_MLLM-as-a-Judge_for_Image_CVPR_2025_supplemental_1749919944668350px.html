7. Details for Constructing Objective Safety Bench (OS Bench)

7. 构建目标安全基准（OS基准）的细节

These seed prompts serve as a foundation, and we then use LLMs to rewrite and expand on them, enriching the content to create a diverse set of prompts. This process increases the variety and depth of the prompts. The detailed "seed prompts" used for the unsafe images violating different rules and that for corresponding borderline safe images are shown in Table 12.

正如我们在第4.1节中介绍的，我们使用最先进的文本到图像扩散模型创建不安全/安全图像。具体而言，我们首先收集一组初始的“种子提示”。这些种子提示作为基础，然后我们使用LLM对其进行重写和扩展，丰富内容以创建多样化的提示集。这个过程增加了提示的多样性和深度。用于违反不同规则的不安全图像的详细“种子提示”和对应的边界安全图像的提示在表12中展示。

9. More Results on Ablation Study

9. 关于消融研究的更多结果

In this section, we provide more results on ablation study. More Results for the Relevance Scanning. We first show more results of the relevance scanning module described in Section 3.2. Besides the results with relevance scanning encoder clip-vit-base-patch16 [28], we demonstrate the results on siglip-so400m-patch14-384 [37] in Figure 11. The results indicate that the relevance scanning module is effective on different relevance scanning encoder. We also tested more relevance scanning models. Our results show that Align [16] filtered 69.1% of rules with a 3.1% recall reduction on finding ground-truth violated rules, while LongCLIP [38] filtered 73.7% rules with only a 1.7% recall reduction. Our preliminary results show that LongCLIP works best as it is optimized for long text, aligning with our rule characteristics. Optimizing the performance of the relevance scanning module further will be our future direction.

10. Robustness

10. 鲁棒性

In this paper, we focus on the scenario where the judgment models are close-sourced and the attackers can not directly access the parameters of the judgment models, which is practical for the real-world image safety judgment tasks. As our approach is compatible to different MLLMs, and the attacker does not have the knowledge about detailed model parameters. Note that our approach is compatible to different models, and the attacker does not have the knowledge about detailed model parameters and exact rule prompts. Therefore, we focus on the transferable adversarial attacks described in Hu et al. [14]. The attack success rates on InternVL2-8B-AWQ with a denoising input preprocessing under \({l}_{\infty }\) attack bound 8/255 are only 5.7%. The results demonstrate our method is robust to the practical adversarial examples and stealthy transferable adversarial attacks on MLLMs are still challenging.

在本文中，我们关注判断模型为闭源且攻击者无法直接访问判断模型参数的场景，这在现实世界的图像安全判断任务中是实用的。由于我们的方法与不同的多模态大语言模型（MLLMs）兼容，且攻击者对详细的模型参数没有了解。请注意，我们的方法与不同模型兼容，攻击者对详细的模型参数和确切的规则提示没有了解。因此，我们关注Hu等人[14]描述的可转移对抗攻击。在\({l}_{\infty }\)攻击界限8/255下，使用去噪输入预处理的InternVL2-8B-AWQ的攻击成功率仅为5.7%。结果表明，我们的方法对实际的对抗示例具有鲁棒性，而对MLLMs的隐蔽可转移对抗攻击仍然具有挑战性。

11. Efficiency

11. 效率

In this section, we evaluate the efficiency of our method. Table 11 summarizes the average runtime per image for our approach across different MLLM models, using the specified inference engine and devices. While our method is slower than the approach of inputting the entire constitution into a single query, similar to OpenAI's O1, it achieves significantly better safety judgment performance, aligning with the inference time scaling law of LLMs. To improve efficiency, we propose various enhancements, including relevance scanning and token-probability-based judgments for cases with high confidence. Furthermore, our method can function as an auto-labeler for safety judgment tasks. Its predictions can be directly used as training or fine-tuning labels for a smaller MLLM, effectively distilling our method into a simpler one-step model without adding overhead during inference. Importantly, the cost shown in Table 11 is substantially lower than the expense of human labeling, highlighting the practicality of our approach.

在本节中，我们评估我们方法的效率。表11总结了我们的方法在不同MLLM模型上每张图像的平均运行时间，使用指定的推理引擎和设备。虽然我们的方法比将整个构成输入到单个查询中的方法（类似于OpenAI的O1）要慢，但它在安全判断性能上显著更好，符合LLM的推理时间缩放法则。为了提高效率，我们提出了多种增强措施，包括相关性扫描和基于令牌概率的高置信度案例判断。此外，我们的方法可以作为安全判断任务的自动标注器。其预测可以直接用作较小MLLM的训练或微调标签，有效地将我们的方法提炼为一个更简单的一步模型，而不会在推理过程中增加开销。重要的是，表11中显示的成本远低于人工标注的费用，突显了我们方法的实用性。
