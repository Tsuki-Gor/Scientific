ABSTRACT
摘要

The success of Computer Vision (CV) relies heavily on manually annotated data. However, it is prohibitively expensive to annotate images in key domains such as healthcare, where data labeling requires significant domain expertise and cannot be easily delegated to crowd workers. To address this challenge, we propose a neuro-symbolic approach called RAPID, which infers image labeling rules from a small amount of labeled data provided by domain experts and automatically labels unannotated data using the rules. Specifically, RAPID combines pre-trained CV models and inductive logic learning to infer the logic-based labeling rules. RAPID achieves a labeling accuracy of 83.33% to 88.33% on four image labeling tasks with only 12 to 39 labeled samples. In particular, RAPID significantly outperforms finetuned CV models in two highly specialized tasks. These results demonstrate the effectiveness of RAPID in learning from small data and its capability to generalize among different tasks. Code and our dataset are publicly available at https://github.com/Neural-Symbolic-Image-Labeling/Rapid/
计算机视觉（CV）的成功严重依赖于手动注释的数据。然而，在医疗等关键领域对图像进行注释成本高昂，因为数据标注需要大量的领域专业知识，不能轻易地委托给众包工作者。为了应对这一挑战，我们提出了一种神经符号方法，称为RAPID，它从领域专家提供的少量标记数据中推断图像标注规则，并使用这些规则自动标注未注释的数据。具体来说，RAPID结合了预训练的CV模型和归纳逻辑学习来推断基于逻辑的标注规则。RAPID在四个图像标注任务上仅用12至39个标记样本就达到了83.33%到88.33%的标注精度。特别是，RAPID在两个高度专业化任务中显著优于微调的CV模型。这些结果证明了RAPID在小数据学习中的有效性及其在不同任务之间泛化的能力。代码和我们的数据集已公开提供在 https://github.com/Neural-Symbolic-Image-Labeling/Rapid/

1 INTRODUCTION
1 引言

Deep learning methods have shown great power in challenging computer vision tasks,such as traffic scene detection [5,19] and disease diagnosis [41,51] . These methods often require a vast amount of labeled image data to achieve good performance. Labeling these data is laborious and expensive. This challenge is exacerbated in highly specialized domains such as healthcare, where data labeling requires significant domain expertise.
深度学习方法在具有挑战性的计算机视觉任务中展示了强大的能力，例如交通场景检测 [5,19] 和疾病诊断 [41,51] 。这些方法通常需要大量的标记图像数据才能达到良好的性能。标记这些数据既费力又昂贵。在数据标记需要大量领域专业知识的高度专业化领域（如医疗保健）中，这一挑战进一步加剧。
Many data labeling methods have been proposed to address this challenge with a small amount of labeled data (e.g., less than 100 labeled samples). One mainstream line of research is to develop models for automated data labeling [8,24,29,39] . Existing approaches in this category often learn class prototypes from the training data samples and infer the class of unlabeled data by assigning the class of its nearest class prototype. To adopt these approaches in a low resource setting, the distance between data samples is often designed to depend on task-specific information such as meta-data or other task-specific insights. However, the task-specific nature of these approaches restricts their generalizability to other tasks. Besides, the need for designing specific models for a specific task requires extensive human efforts, which is against the motivation of saving human efforts for data labeling in the first place.
已经提出了许多数据标记方法，以少量标记数据（例如，少于100个标记样本）应对这一挑战。其中一个主流研究方向是开发用于自动数据标记的模型 [8,24,29,39] 。现有此类方法通常从训练数据样本中学习类原型，并通过分配其最近类原型的类别来推断未标记数据的类别。在资源匮乏的环境中采用这些方法时，数据样本之间的距离通常被设计为依赖于任务特定信息，如元数据或其他任务特定见解。然而，这些方法的任务特定性质限制了它们在其他任务上的泛化性。此外，为特定任务设计特定模型需要大量的人力投入，这违背了最初节省数据标记人力投入的动机。
To address the aforementioned limitations, a new data labeling paradigm called data programming [32] has been proposed for rapid data labeling. For example, Snorkel [31] asks domain experts to create labeling functions and uses a generative model to combine those labeling functions to provide probabilistic labels. However, those labeling functions must be written in a programming language such as Python. This requirement not only incurs an overhead of manually composing labeling functions but also incurs a steep learning curve for domain experts and end users who typically do not have any programming experience.
为了解决上述局限性，已经提出了一个新的数据标注范式，称为数据编程 [32]，用于快速数据标注。例如，Snorkel [31] 要求领域专家创建标注函数，并使用生成模型将这些标注函数结合起来提供概率标签。然而，这些标注函数必须使用如Python之类的编程语言编写。这一要求不仅产生了手动编写标注函数的开销，而且对于通常没有编程经验的领域专家和最终用户来说，还产生了陡峭的学习曲线。
In this paper, we propose a new neuro-symbolic approach called RAPID for image labeling in low-resource settings (e.g., less than 100 labeled images). The novelty of this framework lies in synergizing the strength of neural models (i.e., handling rich, complex image data) and the strength of inductive logic learning (i.e., learning from small datasets) to handle the image labeling challenge. Specifically, RAPID automatically infers logic rules from a small amount of labeled data, applies the inferred rules to label the unlabeled data, and solicits user feedback to refine the rules iteratively.
在本文中，我们提出了一种新的神经符号方法RAPID，用于资源匮乏环境下的图像标注（例如，少于100个标注图像）。这个框架的新颖之处在于结合了神经模型（即处理丰富、复杂的图像数据）的力量和归纳逻辑学习（即从小数据集中学习）的力量来应对图像标注挑战。具体来说，RAPID自动从未标注数据的小量数据中推断逻辑规则，将推断的规则应用于未标注数据，并征求用户反馈以迭代优化这些规则。
Unlike Snorkel, RAPID infers labeling rules automatically rather than requiring users to manually construct these rules. RAPID leverages the First-Order Inductive Learner (FOIL) algorithm to infer logic rules based on the low-level visual attributes extracted by pre-trained models. This way, our approach disentangles the perception and the learning process, making it more transparent and explainable to human labelers. Furthermore, to maximize the efficiency of data usage, we develop a multi-criteria active learning method to iteratively elicit human feedback to refine the labeling rules.
与Snorkel不同，RAPID自动推断标注规则，而不是要求用户手动构建这些规则。RAPID利用第一顺序归纳学习器（FOIL）算法，根据预训练模型提取的低级视觉属性推断逻辑规则。这种方式使我们的方法将感知和学习过程分离，使其对人类标注者更加透明和可解释。此外，为了最大化数据使用效率，我们开发了一种多标准主动学习方法，以迭代地征求人类反馈来优化标注规则。
We conduct extensive experiments on datasets from two highly specialized domains and two common domains. Our method achieves significantly higher labeling accuracy on the two highly specialized domains ( 85.52% on disease diagnosis and 86.11% on bird species labeling, respectively) compared to the baseline models. We demonstrate that by actively refining labeling rules with rapid, incremental human feedback, RAPID can effectively embed expert knowledge and achieve high image labeling accuracy with a limited amount of training data.
我们在两个高度专业化的领域和两个常见领域的数据集上进行了广泛的实验。我们的方法在这两个高度专业化的领域中（85.52% 在疾病诊断上，86.11% 在鸟类种类标记上）相比于基线模型显著提高了标记准确性。我们证明了通过积极 refined 标记规则，并利用快速、增量的人类反馈，RAPID 能够有效嵌入专业知识，并在有限的训练数据量下实现高图像标记准确性。
Overall, this work makes the following contributions:
总的来说，这项工作做出了以下贡献：
  • We proposed a new neuro-symbolic learning framework that synergizes pre-trained computer vision models with inductive logic learning for rapid image labeling with a limited amount of training data;
    - 我们提出了一个新的神经符号学习框架，该框架将预训练的计算机视觉模型与归纳逻辑学习相结合，用于在有限的训练数据下快速图像标记；
  • We designed a new conflict-based informativeness metric for data selection in active learning;
    - 我们为主动学习中的数据选择设计了一种新的基于冲突的信息量度量；
  • We conducted comprehensive evaluations on four labeling tasks from different domains with user simulation and multiple baselines.
    - 我们在来自不同领域的四个标记任务上进行了综合评估，包括用户模拟和多个基线。

2 RELATED WORK
2 相关工作

2.1 Image Labeling
2.1 图像标记

Reducing the human effort in image labeling has become increasingly important in recent years with the advent of deep learning, which requires a massive amount of labeled data to train a model. There has been a continuous effort to develop automated solutions for image labeling. The basic idea is to train a model with some labeled data and automatically assign labels to new data samples without further human involvement. Among these existing methods, fully-automated methods have attracted significant attention and achieved promising performance. Some methods exploit the similarity between unlabeled and label images [8]. Some methods resolve the problem of data scarcity by creating representations of images using auxiliary information such as corresponding captions [29], meta-data [24], or pseudo-labels generated by other models [39]. Despite the promising performance of these methods, they often lack generalizability to domains where data acquisition is challenging, as the models usually require a substantial amount of data to learn the knowledge. Thus, some semi-automated labeling approaches use human efforts in the training or inference process to provide the information needed for training [37] or a coarse initial label [13]. Compared with existing work, our approach uses inductive logic learning to infer logic rules from a small amount of human-annotated data to label images.
随着深度学习的出现，近年来减少人力在图像标注上的投入变得越来越重要，因为深度学习训练模型需要大量标记过的数据。一直在持续努力开发图像标注的自动化解决方案。基本思路是使用一些标记过的数据训练模型，然后自动为新数据样本分配标签，无需进一步的人力参与。在这些现有方法中，完全自动化的方法引起了广泛关注并取得了有希望的性能。一些方法利用未标记图像和标记图像之间的相似性 [8]。一些方法通过使用辅助信息（如相应的标题 [29]、元数据 [24] 或其他模型生成的伪标签 [39]）创建图像表示来解决数据稀缺问题。尽管这些方法表现出有希望的性能，但它们通常缺乏泛化到数据获取具有挑战性的领域的特性，因为模型通常需要大量数据来学习知识。因此，一些半自动化标注方法在训练或推理过程中使用人力提供训练所需的信息 [37] 或粗略的初始标签 [13]。与现有工作相比，我们的方法使用归纳逻辑学习从少量人工标注数据中推断逻辑规则来标记图像。

2.2 Active Learning
2.2 主动学习

Active learning is widely adopted to get humans involved in the labeling process iteratively while minimizing the amount of data to be labeled by humans. Active learning methods select the data samples that can benefit the model most in each iteration and request humans to label them to push the usage of human efforts to the minimum. To determine which data sample to be labeled by humans, some approaches use probability models and prioritize the data samples with high prediction inconsistency [12,14,26,52] , some depend on the vectorized representation from deep learning models [16,34,53] ,and some calculate the low-rank matrix representation for both labeled and unlabeled data to calculate the informativeness [45].
主动学习被广泛采用，以迭代地引入人力参与标注过程，同时最小化人力需要标记的数据量。主动学习方法在每次迭代中选择对模型最有益的数据样本，并请求人力标记它们，以将人力投入降至最低。为了确定哪些数据样本应该由人力标记，一些方法使用概率模型并优先考虑预测一致性高的数据样本 [12,14,26,52]，一些依赖深度学习模型的向量表示 [16,34,53]，还有一些计算标记和未标记数据的低秩矩阵表示来计算信息量 [45]。
Though the active learning strategy can optimize data selection to reduce human effort, it often requires many iterations to achieve a reasonable labeling accuracy. Thus, it remains too expensive for labeling tasks where time is precious for domain experts such as clinicians. Snorkel [31] enables users to explicitly embed their domain knowledge by creating labeling functions. However, the labeling functions are either written in programming languages or special declarative functions defined by the author of Snorkel, causing huge overhead effort in learning the labeling function grammar. Our work combines interactive learning with an inductive logic learner, generating logic rules to classify images. The generated rules are represented with simple logic and descriptive predicates, which are easy for users to read and edit.
尽管主动学习策略可以通过优化数据选择来减少人力投入，但通常需要许多迭代才能达到合理的标注精度。因此，对于时间对于领域专家（如临床医生）来说非常宝贵的标注任务来说，它仍然过于昂贵。Snorkel [31] 使用户能够通过创建标注函数明确地嵌入他们的领域知识。然而，标注函数要么是用编程语言编写的，要么是由Snorkel的作者定义的特殊声明性函数，这导致了在学习标注函数语法上的巨大开销。我们的工作将交互式学习与归纳逻辑学习器相结合，生成用于分类图像的逻辑规则。生成的规则用简单的逻辑和描述性谓词表示，用户易于阅读和编辑。

2.3 Neuro-Symbolic Learning
2.3 神经符号学习

There has been a growing interest in combining neural networks with symbolic methods [1,17,23,25,49] . Here,the term neuro refers to artificial neural networks or connectionist systems, while the term symbolic refers to AI approaches that perform explicit symbol manipulation, such as term rewriting, graph algorithms, and formal logic. There are different ways of combining neural network modules with symbolic learning. Following the categorization in a recent survey [33], our method belongs to a cascading neuro-symbolic paradigm that extracts latent patterns from input data using a neural system and then feeds them into a symbolic reasoner for final prediction.
人们对于将神经网络与符号方法相结合的兴趣日益浓厚 [1,17,23,25,49]。在这里，“神经”一词指的是人工神经网络或连接主义系统，而“符号”一词指的是执行显式符号操作的人工智能方法，如项重写、图算法和形式逻辑。将神经网络模块与符号学习相结合的方式有多种。根据最近一篇综述 [33] 中的分类，我们的方法属于级联神经符号范式，它使用一个神经网络从输入数据中提取潜在模式，然后将它们输入到符号推理器中进行最终预测。
Existing approaches in this category include NS-VQA [50], NS-CL [23], and FO-SL [25]. NS-VQA [50] firstly parses an image to a structural scene representation with Mask R-CNN and ResNet-34 and converts a natural language question into a query program with an LSTM model. Then it uses a symbolic executor to run the program on the scene representation to obtain the answer to the given question. NS-CL [23] adopts a similar approach as NL-VQA but learns the feature vector representation of an object from question-answer pairs, instead of extracting them directly with pre-trained models. FO-SL [25] represents images in first-order logic and uses an SAT solver to solve visual discrimination puzzles.
现有此类方法包括 NS-VQA [50]、NS-CL [23] 和 FO-SL [25]。NS-VQA [50] 首先将图像解析为结构化场景表示，使用 Mask R-CNN 和 ResNet-34，并将自然语言问题转换为一个 LSTM 模型的查询程序。然后它使用符号执行器在场景表示上运行程序以获取给定问题的答案。NS-CL [23] 采用与 NL-VQA 类似的方法，但是从问题-答案对中学习对象的特征向量表示，而不是直接使用预训练模型提取它们。FO-SL [25] 将图像表示为一阶逻辑，并使用 SAT 解算器解决视觉区分谜题。
Unlike existing neuro-symbolic learning approaches in this category, we are the first to use inductive logic learning as the symbolic method for rule inference. In this way, we can explicitly model the logic of labeling rules. Furthermore, our approach is also the first to apply neuro-symbolic learning to image labeling.
与此类现有的神经符号学习方法不同，我们是第一个使用归纳逻辑学习作为规则推理的符号方法。这样，我们可以显式地建模标签规则的逻辑。此外，我们的方法也是第一个将神经符号学习应用于图像标签的。

2.4 Human Feedback in Inductive Logic Learning
2.4 人类反馈在归纳逻辑学习中的使用

To the best of our knowledge, our work is the first that integrates active learning with Inductive Logic Learning (ILL). Existing research in ILL has focused on improving the learning algorithm for better efficiency and scalability. There are only a few that investigates the interactivity of ILL in the 1990s [4,9,10] . Specifically,De Raedt et al. [9,10] propose an interactive paradigm in which the inductive learner asks a yes/no question about the correctness of a learned rule. If a user answers no, the learner will backtrack and learn a new rule. Bergadano et al. [4] propose to prompt users for new counter-examples to refute an incorrect logic program, but users need to manually design counter-examples from scratch. More recently, Sivaraman et al. [35] present an interactive inductive logic programming approach to infer rule-based code patterns for code search. However, this approach only allows users to mark some search results as correct or incorrect for pattern refinement. None of these four approaches have an active learning component (i.e., a data selection algorithm) to carefully rank and select data samples for user inspection and correction.
就我们的知识所及，我们的工作是第一个将主动学习与归纳逻辑学习（ILL）相结合的研究。现有的ILL研究主要集中在提高学习算法的效率和可扩展性。只有少数研究在20世纪90年代探讨了ILL的交互性 [4,9,10]。具体来说，De Raedt等人 [9,10] 提出了一个交互范式，其中归纳学习者会询问关于所学规则正确性的是非问题。如果用户回答否定，学习者将会回溯并学习新的规则。Bergadano等人 [4] 提出提示用户提供新的反例来反驳错误的逻辑程序，但用户需要从头开始手动设计反例。最近，Sivaraman等人 [35] 提出了一种交互式归纳逻辑编程方法，用于推断基于规则的代码搜索模式。然而，这种方法只允许用户将某些搜索结果标记为正确或错误，以进行模式细化。这四种方法中没有一种包含主动学习组件（即数据选择算法）来仔细排序和选择数据样本供用户检查和纠正。

3 METHOD
3 方法

Figure 2 gives an overview of our approach called RAPID. RAPID consists of three parts-(1) a pre-trained visual attribute extractor to extract basic, low-level visual attributes from images, (2) an inductive learner to infer logic labeling rules from the relationship between the visual attributes and the target classes, and (3) a multi-criteria data selection module to select a small set of informative and diverse automatically labeled data for users to inspect and fix. RAPID works iteratively. It starts with a small initial set of training images created by human users. In each iteration, RAPID first extracts the visual attributes of each labeled image. Then, it takes as input the visual attributes and the corresponding class label of the images to infer a set of labeling rules for each class. The inferred rules are then applied to automatically generate labels for unannotated images. In case of contradicting labels, RAPID selects the corresponding label of the rule with the highest Clause Satisfaction Ratio (detailed in Section 3.3.2, Equation 7). Next, RAPID adopts a multi-criteria data selection strategy to compute the informativeness score for each unannotated data and select a diverse set of data samples for users to inspect. Users can fix the incorrect labels, which will be used to refine the labeling rules by RAPID.

3.1 Visual Attribute Extraction with Pre-trained Models
3.1 使用预训练模型进行视觉属性提取

A visual attribute extractor processes a given image and extracts the basic, low-level attributes of the image that are useful and relevant to the labeling task. The visual attributes can be object types in an image, the relationships between the objects, and an object's properties (e.g., size, number). This work mainly uses pre-trained perception models as the visual attribute extractors, detailed in Section 4.3. But one can also use a traditional feature extractor such as SIFT [21] to extract visual attributes. The visual attribute extractors are designed as pluggable components in our approach. For different labeling tasks, we use different pre-trained models to extract visual attributes related to the labeling task. This design increases the flexibility of our approach to be reused for new labeling tasks.
视觉属性提取器处理给定图像，并提取图像的基本、低级属性，这些属性对于标注任务是有用和相关的。视觉属性可以是图像中的对象类型、对象之间的关系以及对象的属性（例如，大小、数量）。本工作主要使用预训练的感知模型作为视觉属性提取器，具体在4.3节中详细说明。但也可以使用传统的特征提取器，如SIFT [21]，来提取视觉属性。在我们的方法中，视觉属性提取器被设计为可插拔的组件。对于不同的标注任务，我们使用不同的预训练模型来提取与标注任务相关的视觉属性。这种设计增加了我们的方法用于新标注任务的灵活性。

3.2 Rule Inference via Inductive Logic Learning
3.2 通过归纳逻辑学习进行规则推理

Given its capability to learn from a small amount of data, we use First Order Inductive Learner (FOIL) [30] to infer labeling rules. Furthermore, the declarative nature of logic rules makes them easy to be understood and refined by human labelers based on their domain knowledge. FOIL is initially designed to learn a logic rule with pre-defined predicates to distinguish a set of positive and negative examples. In our design, each predicate represents one trait of a visual attribute. The original FOIL algorithm can only infer logic rules with variables, which lacks the expressiveness for logic rules with constant values. Therefore, we extend FOIL to support the inference of constant values. As a consequence, this increases the search space exponentially. To address this challenge, we design several inductive biases, such as a TF-IDF-based heuristic, to improve search efficiency (detailed in Appendix A).
鉴于其从小量数据中学习的能力，我们使用一阶归纳学习器（FOIL）[30]来推理标注规则。此外，逻辑规则的声明性质使它们易于被人类标注者基于他们的领域知识理解和优化。FOIL最初被设计为学习一个具有预定义谓词的逻辑规则，以区分一组正例和反例。在我们的设计中，每个谓词代表一个视觉属性的特质。原始的FOIL算法只能推理带有变量的逻辑规则，这缺乏对具有常量值逻辑规则的表达性。因此，我们扩展了FOIL以支持常量值的推理。结果，这使搜索空间指数增长。为了应对这一挑战，我们设计了几个归纳偏置，如基于TF-IDF的启发式方法，以提高搜索效率（具体见附录A）。

由于少量训练数据的不明确性和不完整性，归纳学习模块可能无法在一次遍历中学习到最佳的标签规则。我们提出使用主动学习来通过迭代地征求更多的人工标签来提高归纳学习的性能。

3.3.3 Diversity. The robustness of image labeling models largely depends on the variety of labeled data. Therefore, our data selection algorithm also accounts for the diversity of images to maximize the variety of a group of data samples when selecting the data to label. We propose to cluster all the unlabeled images into k clusters and choose one sample from each cluster to form the group of data to label. We use k-Means to cluster the data samples and choose the final centroids to generate a diverse group of samples. In k-Means, each image is represented with a vector, and the similarity between every two images is measured by the cosine similarity of the two vectors. The feature vector for an image has d dimensions, which represent the total d types of objects we consider for this task (or dataset). The value in each dimension is the number of the corresponding objects detected in the image.

Specifically, the hit rate is defined as the percentage of selected data samples that RAPID mislabels in the current iteration and thus is worth fixing. A higher hit rate indicates better effectiveness of data selection.
为了回答RQ4，我们将RAPID与三种替代数据选择策略进行了比较—随机选择、仅基于信息性标准的选择和仅基于多样性标准的选择。我们使用图像标注准确度以及误分类数据的命中率作为评估指标。具体来说，命中率定义为在当前迭代中RAPID错误标注的选定数据样本的百分比，因此值得修复。更高的命中率表明数据选择的有效性更好。

4.2 User Simulation
4.2 用户模拟

Since RAPID is designed as a human-in-the-loop approach, RAPID needs to keep soliciting feedback from human experts to refine the labeling rules. It is expensive to recruit human participants to provide feedback, especially in the two highly specialized labeling tasks that require domain experts such as ophthalmologists and ornithologists. Therefore, we develop an automated script to simulate human feedback based on the ground truth data. To simulate label corrections, our script compares the ground truth label of each image with the labels inferred by RAPID and automatically fixes the incorrect labels. To simulate human edits to labeling rules, the authors first manually constructed a set of high-quality labeling rules based on their own knowledge and the information shared on professional websites. In each iteration of the training process, our script compares the labeling rule inferred by RAPID with the corresponding manually curated rule. Our script then replaces the first inconsistent clause with the clause in the manually curated rule. In all experiments, we restrict the simulation script to only edit one clause per iteration to simulate the incremental editing process of human labelers.
由于RAPID被设计为一种人在循环的方法，RAPID需要不断征求人类专家的反馈来优化标记规则。招募人类参与者提供反馈的成本高昂，特别是在需要领域专家如眼科医生和鸟类学家的高度专业化的两个标记任务中。因此，我们开发了一个自动化脚本，基于真实数据模拟人类反馈。为了模拟标签修正，我们的脚本比较每张图像的真实标签与RAPID推断的标签，并自动修复错误的标签。为了模拟人类对标记规则的编辑，作者首先基于自己的知识和专业网站上共享的信息手动构建了一套高质量的标记规则。在训练过程的每次迭代中，我们的脚本比较RAPID推断的标记规则与相应的手动审查的规则。然后，我们的脚本将第一个不一致的条款替换为手动审查规则中的条款。在所有实验中，我们限制模拟脚本每次迭代只编辑一个条款，以模拟人类标签者的逐步编辑过程。

4.3 Imagle Labeling Tasks and Datasets
4.3 图像标记任务和数据集

RAPID is designed for image labeling tasks in highly specialized domains. Therefore, we first select two datasets-Glaucoma Diagnosis and Bird Species Labeling-from highly specialized domains. To test the generalizability of RAPID, we construct the two datasets on general domains, including traffic scene labeling and occupation labeling, by searching on Google and Flickr.
RAPID是为高度专业化领域的图像标记任务而设计的。因此，我们首先从高度专业化的领域中选择了两个数据集——青光眼诊断和鸟类种类标记。为了测试RAPID的泛化性，我们通过在Google和Flickr上搜索，在通用领域中构建了两个数据集，包括交通场景标记和职业标记。
Glaucoma Diagnosis. Given a color fundus image, this task requires labeling the eye in the image to be either normal or diseased. We combine the color fundus images from three datasets, Drishti-GS [36], RIM-ONE_r3 [3], and REFUGE [28]. We have 116 images of glaucomatous eyes and 189 images of normal eyes with both glaucoma diagnosis and structure segmentation. We use a pre-trained model called BEAL [43] as the visual attribute extractor to obtain the segmentation of eye fundus structures in the images. The visual attributes designed for this task are the diameter, area, and cup-to-disk ratio calculated based on the segmentation results.
青光眼诊断。给定一幅彩色眼底图像，此任务需要将图像中的眼睛标注为正常或患病。我们整合了来自三个数据集的彩色眼底图像，分别是 Drishti-GS [36]、RIM-ONE_r3 [3] 和 REFUGE [28]。我们拥有 116 张青光眼眼睛图像和 189 张正常眼睛图像，这些图像同时用于青光眼诊断和结构分割。我们使用了一个名为 BEAL [43] 的预训练模型作为视觉属性提取器，以获取图像中眼底结构分割的结果。为此任务设计的视觉属性是基于分割结果计算出的直径、面积和杯盘比。
Bird Species Labeling. Given a bird image, this task requires labeling the bird species. We use the Caltech-UCSD Birds-200-2011 (CUB 200-2011) dataset [40], containing 11, 788 bird images annotated with 200 bird species and 312 attributes that describe each body part of a bird, e.g., wing color, tail shape, etc. Following the experiment settings in Koh et al. [20], we use 112 out of the 312 attributes, and randomly choose three bird species to label in our experiments. We use the pre-trained concept models from Koh et al. [20] to extract the visual attributes.
鸟类物种标注。给定一幅鸟类图像，此任务需要标注鸟类的物种。我们使用了 Caltech-UCSD 鸟类-200-2011（CUB 200-2011）数据集 [40]，包含 11,788 张鸟类图像，注释有 200 个鸟类物种和 312 个描述鸟类每个身体部位的属性，例如翅膀颜色、尾巴形状等。遵循 Koh 等人 [20] 的实验设置，我们使用了 312 个属性中的 112 个，并随机选择三种鸟类物种在我们的实验中进行标注。我们使用 Koh 等人 [20] 的预训练概念模型来提取视觉属性。
Occupation Labeling. Given an image of a person, this task requires labeling the occupation of the person. For this task, we build a dataset containing 300 images of three occupations-chef, farmer, and teacher. Each occupation has 100 labeled images. We use a pre-trained object detection model [2] to detect objects in the images and use the type (glasses, long hair, kitchen, etc.), color, and overlapping relationship between them as visual attributes.
职业标注。给定一个人的图像，此任务需要标注这个人的职业。为此任务，我们构建了一个包含 300 张三种职业的图像数据集——厨师、农民和教师。每个职业有 100 张标注图像。我们使用一个预训练的对象检测模型 [2] 来检测图像中的对象，并使用对象的类型（眼镜、长发、厨房等）、颜色以及它们之间的重叠关系作为视觉属性。
Traffic Scene Labeling. Given a road image, this task requires labeling the traffic scene of the image. For this task, we build a dataset containing 420 images of three traffic scenes-mountain road, highway, and downtown. Each traffic scene has 140 labeled images. We use a pre-trained object detection model called DETR [6] to detect the objects in the images and use the position, color, and type (e.g., pedestrian, truck, car, etc.) of the objects and the overlapping relationship between them as visual attributes.
交通场景标注。给定一幅道路图像，这项任务需要标注图像中的交通场景。为此，我们构建了一个包含三种交通场景的420幅图像数据集——山路、高速公路和市区。每个交通场景都有140幅标注图像。我们使用了一个名为DETR [6]的预训练目标检测模型来检测图像中的目标，并使用目标的位置、颜色和类型（例如，行人、卡车、汽车等）以及它们之间的重叠关系作为视觉属性。

4.4 Comparison Baselines
4.4 基线对比

For RQ1, we compare RAPID with four image classification neural network baselines-ResNet-18 [15], ResNet-34, ResNeXt-32 [46] and Inception-V3 [38])-and an active learning baseline called CEAL [42]. We further compare RAPID with GARDNet [22] on the Glaucoma diagnosis task since GARDNet is specially designed for this task. To represent the condition of learning from limited training data, in each task, we randomly sample 30 training data per class label to finetune the baseline models. That is a total of 60 training samples for the Glaucoma Diagnosis task since it only has two class labels and 90 for the other three tasks since they have three class labels. For the first five baselines, we first pre-train them on ImageNet [11] and then fine-tune them on the four datasets, with training sets in the same size as training RAPID. For GARDNet, we obtained the trained model from its original paper and then fine-tuned it on our Glaucoma diagnosis dataset.
对于RQ1，我们将RAPID与四个图像分类神经网络基线——ResNet-18 [15]、ResNet-34、ResNeXt-32 [46]和Inception-V3 [38]——以及一个名为CEAL [42]的主动学习基线进行了比较。我们还在青光眼诊断任务上进一步将RAPID与专门为此任务设计的GARDNet [22]进行了比较。为了表示从有限训练数据中学习的条件，在每个任务中，我们随机抽取每个类标签30个训练数据来微调基线模型。也就是说，在青光眼诊断任务中，因为有两个类标签，所以总共有60个训练样本，而在其他三个任务中，因为它们有三个类标签，所以有90个。对于前五个基线，我们首先在ImageNet [11]上预训练它们，然后在与训练RAPID相同大小的训练集上微调。对于GARDNet，我们从其原始论文中获取了训练好的模型，然后在我们自己的青光眼诊断数据集上进行了微调。
For RQ2, we build three variants of RAPID-RAPID without editing rules by users (RAPIDno-edit ) ,RAPID without labeling correction or new labels (RAPIDno-al ) ,and RAPIDno-feedback  ,RAPID without feedback when selecting training samples. Similar to the training setting of RAPID,both RAPIDno-edit  and RAPIDno-al  are initially trained with 3 randomly selected images. In the following interactions, RAPIDno-edit  only fixes incorrect labels in the 3 images selected by the multi-criteria active learning algorithm per iteration but does not apply any direct edits to the inferred rules. By contrast, RAPIDno-al  only makes direct edits to one clause of the inferred rules per iteration but does not fix any incorrect labels. RAPIDno-feedback  does not use any active feedback from users. It is trained with randomly sampled images in various numbers (e.g., 3, 6,9 ,etc.) ahead of time without soliciting further human feedback.
对于 RQ2，我们构建了三种 RAPID 变体，无需用户编辑规则 (RAPIDno-edit )，不进行标签校正或新标签的 RAPID (RAPIDno-al )，以及 RAPIDno-feedback ，在选择训练样本时不进行反馈的 RAPID。与 RAPID 的训练设置相似，RAPIDno-edit  和 RAPIDno-al  初始时均使用随机选取的 3 张图像进行训练。在随后的交互中，RAPIDno-edit  只修复每次迭代中多标准主动学习算法选定的 3 张图像中的错误标签，而不对推断的规则进行任何直接编辑。相比之下，RAPIDno-al  每次迭代只对推断规则的一个条款进行直接编辑，而不修复任何错误标签。RAPIDno-feedback  不使用任何用户的主动反馈。它通过随机抽取不同数量（例如，3，6,9 等）的图像进行预训练，而不征求进一步的人工反馈。
For RQ3, we create four variants of RAPID by replacing the inductive logic learner with other machine learning methods, including SVM, random forest, gradient boosting, and neural network. For these variants, we use a feature vector generated with the extracted visual attributes to represent each image. The feature vector is constructed in the same way as calculating the diversity criterion in Section 3.3.3. We repeat each training three times and compute the average performance of each baseline.
对于 RQ3，我们通过将归纳逻辑学习器替换为其他机器学习方法创建了四种 RAPID 变体，包括支持向量机（SVM）、随机森林、梯度提升和神经网络。对于这些变体，我们使用由提取的视觉属性生成的特征向量来表示每张图像。特征向量的构建方式与第 3.3.3 节中计算多样性准则的方式相同。我们重复训练三次，并计算每个基线的平均性能。

6 DISCUSSION
6 讨论

The experiment results demonstrate the effectiveness of RAPID in learning accurate labeling rules with a small amount of training data. Based on the ablation studies in RQ3 and RQ4, we found that both inductive logic learning and multi-criteria active learning play vital roles in the success of RAPID. Furthermore, given the inherent transparency and explainability of logic-based labeling rules, RAPID provides affordance for users to directly embed their expertise into the labeling rules via rule editing. This further improves the efficiency of rule inference, leading to significantly fewer feedback iterations compared with using active learning alone.
实验结果证明了RAPID在学习精确标记规则方面的有效性，只需少量训练数据即可。基于RQ3和RQ4的消融研究，我们发现归纳逻辑学习和多标准主动学习在RAPID的成功中发挥着至关重要的作用。此外，由于基于逻辑的标记规则具有固有的透明性和可解释性，RAPID使用户能够通过规则编辑直接将他们的专业知识嵌入到标记规则中。这进一步提高了规则推理的效率，与仅使用主动学习相比，显著减少了反馈迭代次数。
Our work is the first to apply inductive logic learning to neuro-symbolic learning. The experiment for RQ3 demonstrates the learning capability of our FOIL-based inductive logic learner. Specifically, our logic learner outperforms alternative statistical and neural network models. It is interesting to observe that when replacing the inductive logic learner with Random Forest, the resulting model can still achieve better performance than the fine-tuned models in Table 2 in highly specialized domains. This implies that simply disentangling the perception process from the reasoning process can still be beneficial when learning highly specialized tasks in a low-resource setting (i.e., training with a small amount of data).
我们的工作是首次将归纳逻辑学习应用于神经符号学习。RQ3的实验证明了我们基于FOIL的归纳逻辑学习器的学习能力。具体来说，我们的逻辑学习器优于其他统计和神经网络模型。有趣的是，当用随机森林替换归纳逻辑学习器时，得到的模型仍然能够在高度专业化的领域中实现比表2中的微调模型更好的性能。这意味着在资源匮乏的环境下（即使用少量数据进行训练）学习高度专业化的任务时，仅仅将感知过程与推理过程分离仍然可能是有益的。
Compared with Snorkel [31], our approach has two significant advantages. First, RAPID can learn an initial set of labeling rules from a small amount of labeled data (e.g., 3 training samples in our case) as a starting point for expert users to refine. In contrast, Snorkel requires users to manually write labeling functions from scratch, which can be effortful for expressing complex knowledge. Second, to use Snorkel, users need to be familiar with a programming language such as Python to write labeling functions. Thus, it comes with a steep learning curve for domain experts and end-users who do not have programming background. By contrast, the logic labeling rules in our approach are readable and intuitive. Thus, our approach comes with a more gentle learning curve compared with Snorkel.
与Snorkel [31]相比，我们的方法有两个显著优势。首先，RAPID可以从少量的标记数据（例如，在我们的案例中是3个训练样本）中学习一组初始的标记规则，作为专家用户进行细化的起点。相比之下，Snorkel要求用户从头开始手动编写标记函数，这对于表达复杂知识可能需要付出较大努力。其次，使用Snorkel时，用户需要熟悉如Python之类的编程语言来编写标记函数。因此，对于没有编程背景的领域专家和最终用户来说，它有一个陡峭的学习曲线。相比之下，我们的方法中的逻辑标记规则是可读的且直观的。因此，与Snorkel相比，我们的方法具有更平缓的学习曲线。
This work is also a good demonstration of effective human-AI collaboration in challenging tasks. In our case, RAPID infers an initial set of labeling rules and continuously refines them based on human feedback in the form of direct edits or label corrections. Our experiment in RQ2 has shown that incorporating human feedback is critical to infer accurate labeling rules. Without humans in the loop, RAPID has significantly lower accuracy even when trained with the same amount of data as training with many iterations.
这项工作也很好地展示了在挑战性任务中有效的人机协作。在我们的案例中，RAPID推断一组初始的标记规则，并基于人类的反馈（以直接编辑或标签修正的形式）持续优化这些规则。我们在RQ2的实验已经证明，融入人类反馈对于推断准确的标记规则至关重要。没有人类参与，即使经过多次迭代训练，RAPID的准确度也显著低于使用相同数量数据训练的情况。
Despite the promising results, the final image labeling accuracy of RAPID may still not be on par with human experts. Our approach achieves an average of 85% in the four labeling tasks,while the accuracy of human labelers on ImageNet is about 95% [27]. Similar to Snorkel [31], we also want to argue that such relatively noisy data can still provide valuable supervision signals for model training, especially when used together with noise-robust learning [44,48] and weak supervision [31,47] . This can be extremely beneficial in highly specialized domains such as medical imaging, where human labelers are expensive and hard to acquire.
尽管结果令人鼓舞，RAPID最终的图像标记准确度可能仍然无法与人类专家相媲美。我们的方法在四个标记任务中平均达到了 85% 的准确度，而人类标记者在ImageNet上的准确度大约为95% [27]。与Snorkel [31]类似，我们还想争辩说，这种相对噪声较大的数据仍然可以为模型训练提供有价值的监督信号，特别是当与噪声鲁棒学习 [44,48] 和弱监督 [31,47] 结合使用时。这在医学成像等高度专业化的领域可能极为有益，在这些领域中，获取人类标记者的成本高昂且难以获得。
The current design of the inductive logic learner in RAPID has a rigid objective of learning a rule that matches all positive samples while rejecting all negative samples. Consequently, our approach is sensitive to user mistakes (e.g., incorrect labels and edits). A single incorrect training sample can lead to a labeling rule that makes no sense to users. In future work, we will improve the inductive logic learning method by providing the flexibility to relax the rigid rule satisfaction requirement, which is expected to increase the noisy label tolerance and robustness. Besides, when training RAPID in this work, we follow the FOIL algorithm and use an information gain-based search method. In future work, We will explore improving the search mechanism by adding heuristics using visual attributes.
RAPID中的归纳逻辑学习器当前设计具有严格的目标，即学习一个规则以匹配所有正样本并拒绝所有负样本。因此，我们的方法对用户错误（例如，错误的标签和编辑）敏感。单个错误的训练样本可能导致对用户来说毫无意义的标签规则。在未来的工作中，我们将通过提供灵活性来放宽严格的规则满足要求，改进归纳逻辑学习方法，预计这将提高对噪声标签的容忍度和鲁棒性。此外，在本工作中训练RAPID时，我们遵循FOIL算法并使用基于信息增益的搜索方法。在未来的工作中，我们将探索通过添加基于视觉属性的启发式方法来改进搜索机制。
The performance of RAPID highly depends on the visual attributes extracted by pre-trained computer vision models. For specialized domains, it is possible that no pre-trained computer vision model can extract meaningful visual attributes. However, we think this situation will not occur very often. There are two reasons. First, numerous large computer vision models have been developed and made available online (e.g., HuggingFace, GitHub) these days. These models can recognize a wide range of objects, shapes, colors, and other basic visual attributes that generally apply to different domains, including many highly specialized domains. Second, for rare visual attributes, the pre-trained models can be substituted with conventional handcrafted computer vision techniques, such as SIFT. Our inductive logic learning component can still act on those low-level attributes and synthesize meaningful labeling rules.
RAPID的性能高度依赖于预训练计算机视觉模型提取的视觉属性。对于专业领域，可能不存在能够提取有意义视觉属性的预训练计算机视觉模型。然而，我们认为这种情况不会经常发生。有两个原因。首先，目前已经有大量的大型计算机视觉模型被开发并在线提供（例如，HuggingFace、GitHub）。这些模型能够识别各种对象、形状、颜色以及其他基本视觉属性，这些属性通常适用于不同的领域，包括许多高度专业化的领域。其次，对于罕见的视觉属性，可以采用传统的手工制作的计算机视觉技术（如SIFT）来替代预训练模型。我们的归纳逻辑学习组件仍然可以作用于这些低级属性并合成有意义的标签规则。

7 CONCLUSION
7 结论

We present a rapid image labeling method based on neuro-symbolic learning. The proposed method uses pre-trained neural network models to extract visual attributes and use first-order inductive learning to infer labeling rules based on the visual attributes. This architecture disentangles perception from learning, enabling our method to be applied to new tasks by easily changing the pre-trained visual attribute extractor. Besides, the declarative nature of logic rules enables users to directly inspect and edit the inferred labeling rules, explicitly embedding human expertise into the rules. The experiments show that our method can achieve outstanding performance on highly specialized domains under an extremely low resource setting while generalizing to other general domains with reasonable performance.
我们提出了一种基于神经符号学习的快速图像标注方法。该方法使用预训练的神经网络模型提取视觉属性，并利用一阶归纳学习根据视觉属性推断标注规则。这种架构将感知与学习分离，使得我们的方法可以通过简单地更改预训练的视觉属性提取器来应用于新任务。此外，逻辑规则的声明性质使用户能够直接检查和编辑推断的标注规则，将人类专业知识显式地嵌入到规则中。实验表明，我们的方法在资源极低的情况下，能够在高度专业化的领域实现出色的性能，同时还能在其它一般性领域实现合理的性能。

A. 2 Predicate Preference

By analyzing the predicates in each rule, we find that the predicates about object type appear much more frequently than any other predicate. Therefore, we design an inductive bias that the algorithm prioritizes searching for those predicates about object type. Only when the number of predicates about object type in the clause exceeds the threshold Φ will the algorithm search for other possible predicates. Besides, we also set separate thresholds for separate predicates to generate a rule which can result in a higher labeling accuracy.
