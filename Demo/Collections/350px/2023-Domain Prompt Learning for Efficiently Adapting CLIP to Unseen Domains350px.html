Summary
摘要

Domain generalization (DG) is a difficult transfer learning problem aiming to learn a generalizable model for unseen domains. Recent foundation models (FMs) are robust to many distribution shifts and, therefore, should substantially improve the performance of DG. In this work, we study generic ways to adopt contrastive language-image pre-training (CLIP), a visual-language foundation model, for DG problems in image classification. While empirical risk minimization (ERM) greatly improves the accuracy with bigger backbones and training datasets using standard DG benchmarks, fine-tuning FMs is not practical in many real-world situations. We propose Domain Prompt Learning (DPL) as a novel approach for domain inference in the form of conditional prompt generation. DPL achieved a significant accuracy improvement with only training a lightweight prompt generator (a three-layer MLP), whose parameter is of equivalent scale to the classification projector in the previous DG literature. Combining DPL with CLIP provides surprising performance,raising the accuracy of zero-shot CLIP from 73.7% to 79.3% on several standard datasets, namely PACS, VLCS, OfficeHome, and Terralncognita. We hope the simplicity and success of our approach lead to broader adoption and analysis of foundation models in the domain generalization field.
领域泛化（DG）是一个旨在学习适用于未见领域的一般化模型的困难的迁移学习问题。最近的基础模型（FMs）对许多分布偏移具有鲁棒性，因此，应该显著提高DG的性能。在这项工作中，我们研究了将对比性语言-图像预训练（CLIP），一种视觉-语言基础模型，用于图像分类中的DG问题的通用方法。虽然经验风险最小化（ERM）通过使用更大的主干网络和训练数据集在标准DG基准上大大提高了准确性，但在许多现实世界情况下，微调FMs并不实用。我们提出了领域提示学习（DPL）作为一种新颖的领域推理方法，以条件提示生成形式。DPL仅通过训练一个轻量级提示生成器（一个三层MLP），其参数规模与前人DG文献中的分类投影器相当，实现了显著的准确性提升。结合DPL与CLIP提供了令人惊讶的性能，将零样本CLIP的准确性从 73.7% 提高到 79.3% ，在几个标准数据集上，即PACS、VLCS、OfficeHome和Terralncognita。我们希望我们的方法的简单性和成功能够促进在领域泛化领域中更广泛地采用和分析基础模型。

1. Introduction
1. 引言

Pre-training large vision models using web-scale images is an essential ingredient of recent success in computer vision. Fine-tuning pre-trained models, such as ResNet and Vision Transformer (ViT) is the most popular paradigm for many downstream tasks [He 15, Dosovitskiy 20]. However, domain shifts pose a substantial challenge in real-world scenarios for successfully transferring models. Over the past decade, various studies on domain generalization (DG) have sought a systematic way to narrow the gap between source and target domains [Zhou 22a, Wang 21, Shen 21] aiming to build a model that generalizes to unseen domains. Despite the significant work on this front, machine learning systems are still vulnerable to domain shifts even after using DG methods [Gulrajani 20].
使用网络规模图像对大型视觉模型进行预训练是计算机视觉近期成功的关键因素。对预训练模型如ResNet和Vision Transformer（ViT）进行微调是许多下游任务中最流行的范例 [He 15, Dosovitskiy 20]。然而，在现实世界场景中，域偏移对成功迁移模型提出了重大挑战。在过去的十年里，关于域泛化（DG）的各种研究试图找到一种系统性的方法来缩小源域和目标域之间的差距 [Zhou 22a, Wang 21, Shen 21]，旨在构建一个能够泛化到未见域的模型。尽管在这方面取得了显著的工作成果，但机器学习系统在使用DG方法后仍然容易受到域偏移的影响 [Gulrajani 20]。
Large pre-trained vision-language models like CLIP are an emerging category of models showing great potential in learning transferable representation across many vision tasks. At the core of CLIP is to learn image representations by contrasting them with the representations of text description of the image,such as ’a photo of a {class name}’. The text description is often called prompt, and its design is vital in enhancing CLIP performance. Notably, CLIP can handle unseen classes without fine-tuning them by adequately changing the text description using the target class name.
大型预训练的视觉语言模型如CLIP是新兴的模型类别，它们在跨多个视觉任务学习迁移性表征方面显示出巨大的潜力。CLIP的核心是通过对图像的文本描述（如“一张{类名}的照片”）的表征与图像表征进行对比来学习图像表征。文本描述通常被称为提示语，其设计对于提高CLIP性能至关重要。值得注意的是，CLIP可以通过充分更改文本描述来使用目标类名，从而在不进行微调的情况下处理未见过的类别。
This paper investigates the robustness of CLIP against various distribution shifts and proposes an efficiently adapting method using a recently proposed DomainBed benchmark [Gulrajani 20] (Figure 1).
本文研究了CLIP在各种分布偏移下的鲁棒性，并提出了一种使用最近提出的DomainBed基准 [Gulrajani 20]（图1）的高效适应方法。
While prior works test various DG methods in the benchmark, the most studied only focused on medium-scale pre-trained models, such as ResNet18 or ResNet50. There are two naïve approaches to leveraging CLIP in the DG setup (Figure 2). The first approach is fine-tuning the image encoder trained by CLIP, similar to the other vision models such as ResNet and ViT. We show that the backbone networks trained by CLIP substantially outperform many backbone networks trained solely on images, such as ResNet, big transfer [Kolesnikov 20], and vision transformer. At the same time, however, fine-tuning sometimes degraded the performance on some domains, suggesting that fine-tuning possibly distorts good properties of pre-trained features [Kumar 22]. Another naïve approach is designing the template prompt, such as 'a photo of a {class name}’. The clear merit of this approach is that it does not require optimizing any network and, therefore, keeps the representations learned via pre-training. Despite its simplicity, we show that zero-shot CLIP is still more robust on many DG benchmarks than the vision backbones (e.g., ResNet18, ResNet50, ViT-B16) fine-tuned on source domains, while it is inferior to fine-tuning vision backbone trained by CLIP.
虽然先前的工作在基准测试中测试了各种DG方法，但研究最多的是仅关注中等规模的预训练模型，例如ResNet18或ResNet50。有两种天真地在DG设置中利用CLIP的方法（图2）。第一种方法是微调由CLIP训练的图像编码器，类似于其他视觉模型如ResNet和ViT。我们展示了由CLIP训练的骨干网络在性能上显著优于仅基于图像训练的许多骨干网络，如ResNet、big transfer [Kolesnikov 20]和视觉变换器。然而，同时，微调有时会降低某些领域的性能，这表明微调可能扭曲了预训练特征的优良属性[Kumar 22]。另一种天真方法是设计模板提示，例如“一张{类名}的照片”。这种方法的一个明显优点是它不需要优化任何网络，因此保持了通过预训练学到的表示。尽管这种方法简单，但我们展示了zero-shot CLIP在许多DG基准测试中仍然比在源领域微调的视觉骨干（例如ResNet18、ResNet50、ViT-B16）更稳健，而它比由CLIP训练的视觉骨干的微调性能差。
Fig. 1 Bigger backbone (from ResNet18 to ViT-B16) and bigger pre-train dataset (from ImageNet to YMC1000) improve the performance of ERM on VLCS, PACS, OfficeHome, TerraIncognita. Even without fine-tuning the image encoder, our DPL effectively improves the performance of CLIP and outperforms the baseline ERM by a large margin (3.7%).
图1 更大的骨干（从ResNet18到ViT-B16）和更大的预训练数据集（从ImageNet到YMC1000）提高了ERM在VLCS、PACS、OfficeHome、TerraIncognita上的性能。即使在不对图像编码器进行微调的情况下，我们的DPL也有效地提高了CLIP的性能，并以很大的幅度（3.7%）超过了基线ERM。
Based on the observations, we propose Domain Prompt Learning (DPL), a simple yet effective extension of CLIP in the DG setup. A natural way to adapt the model is to add domain-specific features to the prompt template. However, manually designing a prompt template is challenging in many cases due to its ambiguity. Instead, we propose DPL for automatically generating a prompt that estimates domain-specific features given unlabeled examples from each distribution. More specifically, DPL trains a lightweight prompt generator using source domains, which outputs fixed-length continuous domain prompts given input images of each distribution while freezing other networks. During test-time, the prompt generator generates domain prompt given input images from the target distribution and adds them to the label prompts. Since the entire networks are frozen, the core properties of the pre-training would remain in DPL and are expected to improve CLIP performance in DG stably, as shown in our experiments.
基于观察结果，我们提出了域提示学习（DPL），这是在DG设置下对CLIP的一个简单而有效的扩展。一种自然的模型适应方式是将特定域的特征添加到提示模板中。然而，在许多情况下，手动设计提示模板是具有挑战性的，因为其模糊性。相反，我们提出了DPL，用于自动生成一个估计每个分布中特定域特征的提示，给定每个分布的无标签示例。更具体地说，DPL使用源域训练一个轻量级提示生成器，给定每个分布的输入图像时输出固定长度的连续域提示，同时冻结其他网络。在测试时，提示生成器根据目标分布的输入图像生成域提示，并将其添加到标签提示中。由于整个网络都被冻结，预训练的核心属性在DPL中得以保留，并且如我们的实验所示，预期可以稳定地提高CLIP在DG中的性能。
It is worth noting our work is not the first attempt to tune the prompt of CLIP. For example, [Gao 23, Zhou 21] have proposed optimizing continuous prompts on the target datasets, effectively improving CLIP performance. CoCoOp [Zhou 22b], as a contemporary work, trains a meta-net to generate a meta token for adapting to each instance. CoCoOp focuses on unseen classes and demonstrates its performance by transferring from ImageNet to the four specially designed ImageNet variants. CoCoOp generate a prompt for each input image and, relatively, we generate a prompt for a batch of input images. Our work focuses on the robustness of CLIP against distribution shifts, and proposes a generic way to extract a domain-specific features and improve performance on the target domain at test-time.
值得注意的是，我们的工作并非首次尝试调整CLIP的提示。例如，[Gao 23, Zhou 21] 已经提出在目标数据集上优化连续提示，有效提高了CLIP的性能。作为一项当代工作，CoCoOp [Zhou 22b] 训练了一个元网络来生成一个适应每个实例的元标记。CoCoOp专注于未见过的类别，并通过从ImageNet转移到四个特别设计的ImageNet变体来展示其性能。CoCoOp为每个输入图像生成一个提示，而相对地，我们为一批输入图像生成一个提示。我们的工作关注于CLIP对分布偏移的鲁棒性，并提出了一种通用方法来提取特定域特征，并在测试时提高目标域的性能。
We conduct experiments on four standard datasets included in DomainBed to evaluate DPL, following the experiment setup in[Gulrajani 20, Iwasawa 21], such as parameter tuning and model selection. We show that CLIP with DPL outperforms the strong baselines by a large margin,raising the accuracy from 73.7% to 79.6% (Table 1). Moreover, since DPL can be seen as a kind of Test-Time Adaptation (TTA) method, we compare it with a series of SoTA TTA methods and demonstrate the efficiency of DPL (Table 2). And lastly, through various ablation studies, we surprisingly found that frozen backbone outperforms fine-tuning on OfficeHome datasets for all of ResNet, DeiT [Tou-vron 21], HViT, and ViT-B16 (Table 4). These results prove that DPL is effective, and more importantly, they provide many insights for future works that apply CLIP on DG.
我们在 DomainBed 包含的四个标准数据集上对 DPL 进行实验，以评估 DPL，遵循 [Gulrajani 20, Iwasawa 21] 中的实验设置，例如参数调整和模型选择。我们展示了带有 DPL 的 CLIP 相比于强大的基线有着显著的优势，将准确度从 73.7% 提升到 79.6%（表 1）。此外，由于 DPL 可以看作是一种测试时适应（TTA）方法，我们将其与一系列最先进的 TTA 方法进行比较，并证明了 DPL 的高效性（表 2）。最后，通过多种消融研究，我们惊讶地发现，在 OfficeHome 数据集上，对于 ResNet、DeiT [Tou-vron 21]、HViT 和 ViT-B16，冻结主干网络的表现超过了微调。这些结果证明了 DPL 的有效性，更重要的是，它们为未来将 CLIP 应用于领域泛化的工作提供了许多洞见。
In summary, our main contributions are:
总结来说，我们的主要贡献有：

(3) We demonstrate the impressive empirical performance of DPL by comparing with strong DG baselines and a series of state-of-the-art (SoTA) TTA methods.
（3）我们通过与传统领域泛化基线和一系列最先进（SoTA）的 TTA 方法进行比较，展示了 DPL 的显著实证性能。

2. Preliminaries
2. 基本概念

2.1 Domain Generalization
2.1 领域泛化

Over the past decade, various approaches have been proposed to solve DG. Most prior works have focused on regularizing the model using the knowledge from multiple source domains. For example, domain-invariant representation learning [Ganin 16] is a major branch of domain generalization, aiming to reduce the domain gaps in the space of latent representations. There are many different approaches to measures the domain gaps, including adversarial classifier [Li 18b, Ganin 15, Ganin 16], kernel mapping [Blanchard 11, Grubinger 15], metric learning [Moti-ian 17, Jin 20], and invariant risk minimization [Arjovsky 19]. Similarly, several researchers have sought to generate samples with diverse styles so that models can learn domain-invariant features through them [Shankar 18, Zhou 20, Borlino 21]. Other methods use meta learning to learn how to regularize the model to improve robustness [Dou 19, Li 18a].
在过去的十年里，人们提出了各种方法来解决 DG 问题。大多数先前的工作都集中在使用来自多个源域的知识来规范模型。例如，领域不变性表征学习 [Ganin 16] 是领域泛化的一个主要分支，旨在减少潜在表征空间中的领域差距。有许多不同的方法来衡量领域差距，包括对抗性分类器 [Li 18b, Ganin 15, Ganin 16]、核映射 [Blanchard 11, Grubinger 15]、度量学习 [Moti-ian 17, Jin 20] 和不变风险最小化 [Arjovsky 19]。同样，一些研究者试图生成具有不同风格的样本，以便模型可以通过它们学习领域不变特征 [Shankar 18, Zhou 20, Borlino 21]。其他方法使用元学习来学习如何规范模型以提高鲁棒性 [Dou 19, Li 18a]。
Our work investigates the importance of the CLIP [Radford 21] in DG, and proposes a lightweight way to adapt the CLIP for unseen domains. There are several recent observations to motivate us to benchmark CLIP in the DG setup. First, [Gulrajani 20] shows that many prior approaches do not provide significant improvement compared to simple supervised learning. The results imply that regularizing the model is not sufficient to achieve high performance in DG. Secondly, despite significant related works, most studies have focused on medium-scale pre-trained models, such as ResNet18 or ResNet50, although very large-scale models often lead to substantial improvements. Notably, the latest work [Iwasawa 21] compares more large-scale backbone networks, including big transfer [Kolesnikov 20] (BiT-MR50x3, BiT-M-R101x3, and BiT-M-R152x4), vision transformer (ViTB16 and ViT-L16 [Dosovitskiy 20], Hybrid ViT, DeiT [Touvron 21]), and MLP-Mixer [Tol-stikhin 21] (Mixer-L16), and shows that the selection of backbone networks is important in DG. In contrast with [Iwa-sawa 21], we herein demonstrate that CLIP performs surprisingly well without fine-tuning the entire model in source domains, which is time-consuming in practice.
我们的工作研究了CLIP [Radford 21]在DG中的重要性，并提出了一个轻量级的方法来适应未见过的领域。有几项最近的观察结果激励我们在DG设置中对CLIP进行基准测试。首先，[Gulrajani 20]表明，许多先前的方法与简单的监督学习相比并没有提供显著的改进。结果表明，仅对模型进行正则化不足以在DG中实现高性能。其次，尽管有许多相关研究，但大多数研究集中在中等规模的预训练模型上，如ResNet18或ResNet50，尽管非常大的模型往往会导致实质性的改进。值得注意的是，最新的工作 [Iwasawa 21]比较了更多的大型主干网络，包括big transfer [Kolesnikov 20]（BiT-MR50x3、BiT-M-R101x3和BiT-M-R152x4）、视觉转换器（ViTB16和ViT-L16 [Dosovitskiy 20]、Hybrid ViT、DeiT [Touvron 21]）以及MLP-Mixer [Tol-stikhin 21]（Mixer-L16），并表明在选择主干网络在DG中很重要。与[Iwa-sawa 21]不同，我们在本文中证明了CLIP在没有对整个模型在源领域进行微调的情况下表现得出奇的好，这在实际中是耗时的。
From the methodological perspective, our work relates to several prior works that have attempted leveraging domain features rather than discarding them [Ganin 16, Zhou 20, Borlino 21]. While these works focused on the standard vision backbone, we propose a CLIP-specific approach to leverage the domain features by combining these features with prompt tuning.
从方法论的角度来看，我们的工作与先前尝试利用领域特征而不是丢弃它们的多项工作有关 [Ganin 16, Zhou 20, Borlino 21]。虽然这些工作关注的是标准的视觉主干网络，但我们提出了一个针对CLIP的特定方法，通过将领域特征与提示调整相结合来利用这些特征。

2.2 Test Time Adaptation
2.2 测试时间适应

Regarding the problem setup, our work can also be seen as Test-Time Adaptation (TTA). The concept of TTA is updating a part of networks to minimize the prediction entropy for adapting the model to an unseen domain robustly at the test time. Pseudo Label [Lee 13] updates entire networks and Tent [Wang 20] updates the BN parameters. SHOT[Liang 20] update feature extractor and minimizes a diversity regularizer and pseudo-label loss, not only prediction entropy. Instead of minimizing prediction entropy at the test time, we infer domain information and generate a domain-specific prompt to adapt CLIP to an unseen target domain.
关于问题设置，我们的工作也可以视为测试时适应（Test-Time Adaptation，TTA）。TTA的概念是更新网络的一部分，以最小化预测熵，使模型在测试时能够稳健地适应未见过的领域。伪标签 [Lee 13] 更新整个网络，Tent [Wang 20] 更新批量归一化（BN）参数。SHOT [Liang 20] 更新特征提取器并最小化多样性正则化和伪标签损失，而不仅仅是预测熵。我们在测试时不是最小化预测熵，而是推断领域信息并生成特定领域的提示，以适应未见过的目标领域。
Our work also relates to [Iwasawa 21] in that both approaches modulate their prediction given the unlabeled data available at test time. Specifically, [Iwasawa 21] proposes T3A that replaces the linear classifier using pseudo-labeling and prototypical classification and shows that it stably improves the performance in unseen domains. However, T3A cannot be directly applied to CLIP, as it assumes a simple linear classifier that CLIP does not employ.
我们的工作也与 [Iwasawa 21] 相关，因为这两种方法都根据测试时可用的大量未标记数据调整其预测。具体来说，[Iwasawa 21] 提出了 T3A，该算法使用伪标签和原型分类代替线性分类器，并证明它可以在未见过的领域中稳定地提高性能。然而，由于 T3A 假设了一个 CLIP 没有使用的简单线性分类器，因此它不能直接应用于 CLIP。

2.3 Prompt Learning
2.3 提示学习

The success of GPT-3 demonstrated the importance of prompt tuning. There are various prompting strategies, such as discrete natural language prompts and continuous prompts [Liu 23]. PADA [Ben-David 22] proposed a domain adaptation algorithm that trains T5 [Raffel 19], a language foundation model, to generate unique domain-relevant features for each input. PADA uses discrete prompts for the NLP applications and differs from our DPL with continuous prompts in computer vision. On the other hand, many recent works [Li 21, Lester 21] directly tuning prompts in continuous vector forms, and P-Tuning v2 [Liu 21] showed that continuous prompt tuning achieves the same performance as fine-tuning in various settings.
GPT-3 的成功展示了提示调整的重要性。存在各种提示策略，例如离散的自然语言提示和连续提示 [Liu 23]。PADA [Ben-David 22] 提出了一个领域适应算法，该算法训练语言基础模型 T5 [Raffel 19]，为每个输入生成独特的领域相关特征。PADA 在 NLP 应用中使用离散提示，并与我们计算机视觉中的连续提示 DPL 不同。另一方面，许多近期工作 [Li 21, Lester 21] 直接在连续向量形式中调整提示，P-Tuning v2 [Liu 21] 显示了连续提示调整在各种设置下实现了与微调相同的性能。
Because of the successful applications of CLIP, prompt tuning is also of great interest in computer vision. Context Optimization (CoOp) [Zhou 21] demonstrated that CLIP performance is susceptible to prompts and that a suitable prompt can improve performance for the image recognition task. CLIP-Adapter [Gao 23] was proposed to learn with an additional adapter network. [Ge 22] adapts CLIP using contrastive learning in the Unsupervised Domain Adaption setup. Unlike these works, which need to access the image or class labels in the target domain, we adapt CLIP to an unseen domain with a generated domain prompt inferred from input images.

3.2 Naïve Approaches for Using CLIP in DG

CLIP consists of two parts: an image encoder fclip  and a language model gclip  . CLIP classifies the image features based on the similarity between embedding of a text prompt p ,such as ’dog’ or ’a photo of a class label,’ rather than initially using the classification head trained from scratch. Specifically,given an image x and k class prompt pk ,CLIP output a prediction using both fclip  and gclip  :
(2)y^clip =arg⁡maxk⟨fclip (x),gclip (pk)⟩

To demonstrate how powerful the representation of massively pre-trained models (CLIP) for DG setup, we tested following two naïve approaches to use CLIP in DG setups: fine-tuning and zero-shot. Firstly, we evaluated CLIP in a zero-shot manner; i.e., we freeze both the image encoder and the language model, and substitute the class labels used in each dataset for the text prompt p .
Secondly,we can use the image encoder fclip  as an alternative to the standard image backbones, such as ResNet and ViT. In this setup,we train fclip  by using the datasets Si from multiple source domains i ,similar to the standard DG setup. We can use any algorithms tailored for DG setup, such as DANN [Ganin 16] and CORAL [Sun 16] during fine-tuning. While it is powerful as shown in experiments, it requires additional computational costs to re-train such large models entirely. Besides, good properties of massive pre-training might be distorted during fine-tuning, as highlighted by the performance degradation compared to zero-shot approach.
In summary, zero-shot approach is computationally effective yet less expressive, and fine-tuning can leverage the knowledge of source datasets but it is computationally heavy and possibly distort good representations learned during pre-training. Based on the observation, we propose a novel approach to design the prompt p to improve the performance in an unseen domain without fine-tuning the entire model.
总结来说，零样本方法在计算上有效但表达性不足，而微调可以利用源数据集的知识，但计算量大且可能会扭曲预训练期间学到的良好表示。基于这一观察，我们提出了一种新颖的方法来设计提示 p ，以在不微调整个模型的情况下提高未见领域的性能。

5. Conclusions
5. 结论

We introduce CLIP to DG on DomainBed. For this purpose, we proposed a novel approach called Domain Prompt Learning (DPL) for efficiently adapting CLIP to an unseen domain. By generating the domain prompt conditional on input images, CLIP + DPL brings substantial improvements over strong DG baselines and several effective TTA methods on DomainBed. Then, we conducted ablation experiments with various backbones and frozen ERM. We verified that DPL can stabilize performance and present meaningful insights about existing datasets and fine-tuning strategy of backbones. We hope that our research will broaden and inspire the roles of prompt learning in domain transfer learning.
我们将CLIP引入到DomainBed上的DG。为此，我们提出了一种名为域提示学习（DPL）的新方法，以高效地将CLIP适应到未见过的域。通过在输入图像上生成域提示，CLIP + DPL在DomainBed上的强DG基线以及几种有效的TTA方法上带来了实质性改进。然后，我们使用各种主干网络和冻结ERM进行了消融实验。我们验证了DPL可以稳定性能，并提供了关于现有数据集和主干网络微调策略的有意义见解。我们希望我们的研究能够拓宽并激发提示学习在域迁移学习中的角色。

5.1 Limitation
5.1 局限性

i. Interpretability of Domain Prompt
i. 领域提示的可解释性

To better perform, our DPL is directly represented in a continuous vector form, which lacks interpretability. However, improving interpretability is an important research direction in both FM applications and Domain Generalization. We consider producing discrete semantically informative prompts by some means is an exciting extension of DPL, even with some loss of precision.
为了更好地表现，我们的DPL直接表示为连续向量形式，这缺乏可解释性。然而，提高可解释性是FM应用和领域泛化中的重要研究方向。我们认为通过某种方式生成离散的语义信息提示是DPL的一个令人兴奋的扩展，即使会有些许精度损失。

ii. Label Shift
ii. 标签偏移

From the technical perspective, DPL cannot capture the domain shift outside of the images because DPL uses domain features extracted from only images. As a result, DPL has no idea how to record such non-visual domain shift. Unfortunately, the label shift exists in the actual-world applications [Azizzadenesheli 19]. An innovative question is whether adding appropriate information to the Domain Prompt Learning can help solve the label shift problem, such as a detailed textual description of the target domain.
从技术角度来看，DPL无法捕获图像之外的领域偏移，因为DPL只使用从图像中提取的领域特征。因此，DPL不知道如何记录这种非视觉领域偏移。不幸的是，实际应用中存在着标签偏移 [Azizzadenesheli 19]。一个创新性的问题是，向领域提示学习中添加适当的信息是否可以帮助解决标签偏移问题，例如目标领域的详细文本描述。

iii. Social impact perspective
iii. 社会影响视角

Many images and text descriptions of web data are used directly to train CLIP. Though CLIP benefits from low-cost data that do not require manual labeling, it inevitably includes a lot of bias and privacy in CLIP and other foundation models [Bommasani 21]. This requires us to spend more time paying attention to the opportunities and risks of Foundation Models.
许多网络数据的图像和文本描述被直接用来训练CLIP。尽管CLIP从低成本且无需手动标记的数据中获益，但它不可避免地在CLIP和其他基础模型中包含了大量的偏见和隐私问题 [Bommasani 21]。这要求我们花更多的时间关注基础模型的机会和风险。

5.2 Future Work
5.2 未来工作

First and foremost, interpretability is critical in both Domain Transfer Learning and the Foundation Model. As discussed in Section 5⋅1 ,DPL introduce the possibility of using a large language model in DG in the form of prompt. We will investigate this direction in our future work.
首先最重要的是，解释性在领域迁移学习和基础模型中至关重要。如第 5⋅1 节所述，DPL 引入了在 DG 中以提示的形式使用大型语言模型的可能性。我们将在未来的工作中研究这个方向。
There are two simple and critical approaches to improving the performance of DG. One is to apply visual prompt tuning [Jia 22] on the pure visual backbones, which can be used to more previous methods. Another is focusing on a data-centric approach since we observe uneven data quality on the widely used datasets.
有两种简单且关键的方法可以提高 DG 的性能。一种是应用视觉提示调整 [Jia 22] 在纯视觉主干网络上，这可以用于改进之前的方法。另一种是关注数据驱动的方法，因为我们观察到在广泛使用的数据集上数据质量不均。
Finally, several recent studies systematically analyze the performance and shortcomings of large-scale pre-train models in the Out-of-Distribution generalization [Cha 22, Wenzel 22]. We hope that our results will inspire more research in this direction.
最近几项研究系统地分析了大规模预训练模型在分布外泛化 [Cha 22, Wenzel 22] 中的性能和不足。我们希望我们的结果将激发更多这方面的研究。
