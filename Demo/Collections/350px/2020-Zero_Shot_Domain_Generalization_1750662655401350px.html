1 Introduction

1 引言

Generalization is a key desideratum for machine learning models to scale to the dynamic nature of the real world. The standard supervised learning framework assumes that train and test data are from the same distribution (domain). Domain generalization techniques [B, [C], [D], [D], [D] demand to train a model in such a way that it can generalize to a novel domain at inference, by gracefully handling domain shift. However, current domain generalization methods assume the same classes to be present in all domains (including unseen test domains), which is a restriction on the application of such methods. Our work attempts to relax this assumption, and allow novel test domains to have new classes that were not present in any training domain. We introduce this harder problem as Zero-Shot Domain Generalization, and to the best of our knowledge, is the first such effort (Fig 1 illustrates the setting). We hypothesize that learning a domain-invariant feature representation, with explicit class information, would help address Zero-Shot Domain Generalization. Encoding class information in the feature space ensures smooth transition of information from the classes that are seen during training on multiple source domains, to the unseen set of classes in the new domain. To this end, we adapt state-of-the-art domain generalization methods - in particular, Feature Critic Network (FC) [22] and Multi-Task Auto Encoder (MTAE) [14] - to ensure that their intermediate feature representations are semantically consistent. Our experimental evaluation on domain generalization variants of CIFAR-10 [CIFAR-100 [CI], F-MNIST [BI] and PACS [B] provide promise, and establish baselines for this new research problem.

2 Related Work

2 相关工作

Domain Generalization: Multi-Domain Learning, Domain Generalization, Domain Adaptation are closely related topics. All of these deal with scenarios in which a model is trained on a source distribution and is used in the context of a different (but related) target distribution. In Multi-Domain Learning the target domain and train domains are the same. In Domain Generalization the target domain is different from that of train domains. In Domain Adaptation also the target domain is different from the train domain but we have access to unlabeled data from the target domains during training.

领域泛化：多领域学习、领域泛化、领域适应是密切相关的主题。所有这些都涉及模型在源分布上训练并应用于不同（但相关）目标分布的场景。在多领域学习中，目标域和训练域相同。在领域泛化中，目标域与训练域不同。在领域适应中，目标域也不同于训练域，但训练时可以访问目标域的无标签数据。

In domain generalization (DG), we are given training data from different domains and the objective is to generalize to a novel domain. Blanchard et al. 2011 [B, II ] proposed a kernel mean embedding based algorithm to get similarity between different domains and transfer the learning. The same kernel mean embedding idea was used to extend this DG framework to a multiclass setting in [8]. Ghifary et al. 2015 [14] proposed an autoencoder based method called Multi-Task Auto Encoder (MTAE) to learn domain-invariant features. In MTAE, encoder for all domains is same but decoder is different which forces encoder to learn a common feature space for all domains. Ghifary et al. 2017 [B] proposes scatter component analysis (SCA) to solve domain generalization problem. SCA finds representation that trades between maximizing the separability of classes and data, and minimizing the mismatch between domains through scatter. Motiian et al. 2017 [26] proposed classification and contrastive semantic alignment (CCSA) to learn a domain-invariant embedding by minimizing the sum of classification loss, confusion alignment loss, and semantic alignment loss. Confusion alignment loss is a distance between distribution and semantic alignment loss makes sure that samples from different domains and with different labels are mapped as far apart as possible in the embedding space. Li et al. 2017 [B] proposed a method that takes advantage of the robustness of deep learning models to domain shift, and developed a low-rank parameterized CNN model for end-to-end DG learning. Li et al. 2017 [20] proposed a meta-learning approach to solve for DG by simulating train-test domain shifts during training by synthesizing virtual test domains. Li et al. 2019 [12] extended this approach to produce a more general feature extractor that can be used with any classifier, by simultaneously learning an auxiliary loss function that trains the feature extractor for improved domain invariance. None of these efforts attempted Zero-Shot DG, setting our work apart.

在领域泛化（DG）中，我们获得来自不同领域的训练数据，目标是泛化到一个新领域。Blanchard等人2011年[B, II]提出了一种基于核均值嵌入的算法，用于计算不同领域间的相似性并实现迁移学习。相同的核均值嵌入思想被用于将该DG框架扩展到多类别设置[8]。Ghifary等人2015年[14]提出了一种基于自编码器的方法，称为多任务自编码器（MTAE），用于学习领域不变特征。在MTAE中，所有领域共享同一编码器，但解码器不同，这迫使编码器学习一个所有领域通用的特征空间。Ghifary等人2017年[B]提出了散射成分分析（Scatter Component Analysis, SCA）来解决领域泛化问题。SCA通过散射找到一种表示，权衡最大化类别和数据的可分性与最小化领域间不匹配。Motiian等人2017年[26]提出了分类与对比语义对齐（Classification and Contrastive Semantic Alignment, CCSA），通过最小化分类损失、混淆对齐损失和语义对齐损失之和，学习领域不变嵌入。混淆对齐损失是分布间的距离，语义对齐损失确保不同领域且标签不同的样本在嵌入空间中尽可能远离。Li等人2017年[B]提出了一种利用深度学习模型对领域偏移的鲁棒性，开发了低秩参数化卷积神经网络模型用于端到端的DG学习。Li等人2017年[20]提出了一种元学习方法，通过在训练中模拟训练-测试领域偏移，合成虚拟测试域来解决DG问题。Li等人2019年[12]扩展了该方法，生成一个更通用的特征提取器，可与任何分类器配合使用，同时学习辅助损失函数以提升特征提取器的领域不变性。这些工作均未涉及零样本领域泛化，凸显了我们的工作特色。

Zero-Shot Learning (ZSL): On the other hand, Zero-Shot learning is a supervised learning method, in which the classes of images seen during training is disjoint from the classes present during testing. Successful zero-shot models should maintain very high accuracy when presented test examples from those unseen classes. In generalized zero-shot setting, the model should maintain good performance on all the classes which includes both the base classes and new zero-shot classes. The main idea in most zero-shot algorithms is to learn a semantically consistent correlation between seen and unseen classes, using semantic information between classes. Semantic information can be either manually specified as attributes [16, 16, 16] or imparted from word embeddings such as Word2Vec [24] or GloVe vectors[28]. Since attribute based methods use binary mapping they result in loss of information and hence have been shown to be suboptimal[B]. Semantic embedding based algorithms first learn an embedding function which maps visual space to semantic space, to aid in this task. Socher et al. 2013 [29] minimizes Euclidean distance loss with word vectors in semantic space as objective, and learns a two-layer neural network for classification. Attribute Label Embedding (ALE) [8], Deep Visual Semantic Embedding (DEVISE) [12] and Structured Joint Embedding (SJE) [11] learn a bilinear compatibility function to map image to semantic space. To learn the compatibility function, different objectives functions have been explored: DEVISE[[12] uses pairwise ranking objective, [2] takes a dot product between the embedded visual feature and semantic vectors considering three training losses, including a binary cross entropy loss, hinge loss and Euclidean distance loss. Yongqin et al. 2017 [60] presents a survey of zero-shot learning methods.

零样本学习（Zero-Shot Learning, ZSL）：另一方面，零样本学习是一种监督学习方法，其中训练时见到的图像类别与测试时出现的类别不重合。成功的零样本模型在面对这些未见类别的测试样本时应保持极高的准确率。在广义零样本设置中，模型应在包括基础类别和新的零样本类别的所有类别上保持良好性能。大多数零样本算法的核心思想是利用类别间的语义信息，学习已见类别与未见类别之间的语义一致相关性。语义信息可以是手动指定的属性[16, 16, 16]，也可以是从词嵌入（如Word2Vec[24]或GloVe向量[28]）中获得。由于基于属性的方法采用二元映射，导致信息损失，因此被证明效果不佳[B]。基于语义嵌入的算法首先学习一个嵌入函数，将视觉空间映射到语义空间，以辅助该任务。Socher等人2013年[29]以语义空间中的词向量为目标，最小化欧氏距离损失，并学习一个两层神经网络进行分类。属性标签嵌入（Attribute Label Embedding, ALE）[8]、深度视觉语义嵌入（Deep Visual Semantic Embedding, DEVISE）[12]和结构化联合嵌入（Structured Joint Embedding, SJE）[11]学习一个双线性兼容函数，将图像映射到语义空间。为学习兼容函数，探索了不同的目标函数：DEVISE[12]使用成对排序目标，[2]通过计算嵌入视觉特征与语义向量的点积，考虑三种训练损失，包括二元交叉熵损失、铰链损失和欧氏距离损失。Yongqin等人2017年[60]对零样本学习方法进行了综述。

Even though both DG and ZSL have been explored by the community in isolation, to the best of our knowledge, no existing work in literature has attempted to solve the Zero-Shot DG problem. This setting can have practical use in applications such as robotics, medical image analysis and general scene understanding. We identify this problem in our work, formalize the same and provide a methodology that can serve as a baseline.

尽管领域泛化（DG）和零样本学习（ZSL）均已被学界分别研究，据我们所知，文献中尚无工作尝试解决零样本领域泛化问题。该设置在机器人技术、医学图像分析和通用场景理解等应用中具有实际价值。我们在本工作中识别该问题，形式化定义，并提出一种可作为基线的方法论。

3 Zero-Shot Domain Generalization

3 零样本领域泛化

3.1 Problem Definition

3.1 问题定义

We define a domain as the joint distribution of feature and label space. Let the training data for the ith domain be: (Xij,Yij)∼PXYi and PXYi∼μ . In a DG setting,the test data comes from the same distribution μ:(XjT,YjT)∼PT and PT∼μ ,while in zero-shot domain generalization,it comes from a different distribution v:(XjT,YjT)∼PT and PT∼v ,where v contains additional unseen labels compared to μ (note that feature space and common label space follow the same data generation structure as μ but it differs only for and when unseen classes are seen).

我们将域定义为特征空间与标签空间的联合分布。设ith域的训练数据为：(Xij,Yij)∼PXYi和PXYi∼μ。在领域泛化设置中，测试数据来自相同分布μ:(XjT,YjT)∼PT和PT∼μ，而在零样本领域泛化中，测试数据来自不同分布v:(XjT,YjT)∼PT和PT∼v，其中v包含相较于μ的额外未见标签（注意特征空间和公共标签空间遵循与μ相同的数据生成结构，仅在遇到未见类别时有所不同）。

We assume all(X,Y)pairs are drawn i.i.d. from their respective distributions. In particular,let Ytr and Yts represent the set of classes in training and test data respectively,such that Ytr∩Yts=∅ . The training data for the ith domain is given by Di={Xij,Yij}1≤j≤ni and Yij∈Ytr ,and the test data set be DT={XjT,YjT}1≤j≤nT and YjT∈Yts where ni is the number of images in the ith domain. The main objective of ZSDG problem is to train a model on all training domains D={D1,D2,…DN} to perform well on DT .

我们假设所有(X,Y)对均独立同分布地从各自的分布中抽取。特别地，令Ytr和Yts分别表示训练和测试数据中的类别集合，满足Ytr∩Yts=∅。ith域的训练数据由Di={Xij,Yij}1≤j≤ni和Yij∈Ytr给出，测试数据集为DT={XjT,YjT}1≤j≤nT和YjT∈Yts，其中ni是ith域中的图像数量。ZSDG问题的主要目标是在所有训练域D={D1,D2,…DN}上训练模型，以在DT上表现良好。

3.2 Proposed Approach

3.2 提出的方法

We propose a generic approach to extend the state-of-the-art DG techniques to solve zero-shot domain generalization. The key insight is to bind the intermediate domain-invariant feature representations to a semantic space that is shared across the seen classes of the old domains and the unseen classes of the new domain. Such use of semantic space has been successfully used in recent ZSL methodologies [121, 29, 30].

我们提出了一种通用方法，将最先进的域泛化（DG）技术扩展以解决零样本域泛化问题。关键见解是将中间的域不变特征表示绑定到一个语义空间，该空间在旧域的已见类别和新域的未见类别之间共享。语义空间的这种使用已在近期的零样本学习（ZSL）方法[121, 29, 30]中成功应用。

Existing DG methods [14, 12] can be considered as a composition of a feature extractor function fθ and classifier function gϕ:(g∘f)(I) ,where I is the input data-point. In this paper, we only consider DG methods which generate domain invariant features and train a common classifier on those features. We restrict domain invariant features to semantic space, forcing the model to be semantically consistent along with domain invariance. Our proposed method can be extended to any DG method which learn domain invariant features but does not apply to methods which learn different features for different domains. While training the proposed semantically constrained DG approach,the features generated by fθ are projected to a semantic space of labels. Images of similar classes are grouped together, and dissimilar classes spaced apart by a semantic alignment loss given in Equation 1. By using the semantic embedding and restricting lower-level features of the model to a semantic space, a shared invariant representation is learned where semantic alignment is accounted for. We make an inherent assumption that classes which appear visually similar should also be semantically similar (as in other ZSL methods). Thus using semantic space helps us in the visual classification task. We use word embeddings of classes - in particular, simple GloVe embeddings [23] trained on Common Crawl corpus - as the semantic space in this work. One could use more complex embedding functions to study this even further.

现有的DG方法[14, 12]可视为特征提取函数fθ和分类器函数gϕ:(g∘f)(I)的组合，其中I是输入数据点。本文仅考虑生成域不变特征并在这些特征上训练通用分类器的DG方法。我们将域不变特征限制在语义空间内，强制模型在保持域不变性的同时语义一致。我们提出的方法可扩展到任何学习域不变特征的DG方法，但不适用于为不同域学习不同特征的方法。在训练所提的语义约束DG方法时，fθ生成的特征被投影到标签的语义空间。相似类别的图像被聚集在一起，不相似类别通过公式1中的语义对齐损失分开。通过使用语义嵌入并将模型的低层特征限制在语义空间，学习到一个共享的不变表示，同时考虑语义对齐。我们内在假设视觉上相似的类别在语义上也应相似（如其他ZSL方法）。因此，使用语义空间有助于视觉分类任务。本文使用类别的词嵌入——特别是基于Common Crawl语料训练的简单GloVe嵌入[23]——作为语义空间。也可以使用更复杂的嵌入函数进行更深入研究。

During inference, we depart from traditional DG methods which train a separate classifier (neural network or SVM) on the domain-invariant features. We instead use nearest neighbour (NN) search in the semantic space. Our method is lightweight and can scale to large numbers of unseen classes using existing efficient NN search methods. We now describe how we infuse semantic information into three DG methods, to solve zero-shot DG.

推理阶段，我们不同于传统DG方法在域不变特征上训练单独分类器（神经网络或SVM）。我们改用语义空间中的最近邻（NN）搜索。我们的方法轻量且可利用现有高效NN搜索方法扩展到大量未见类别。下面描述如何将语义信息注入三种DG方法，以解决零样本域泛化问题。

Our approach focuses on DG methods which learn domain-invariant features and cannot be any combination of DG + ZSL methods. For example existing SOTA ZSL methods, based on generative models, cannot be directly extended this way. The proposed method is novel in terms of how one does training + inference in ZSDG, diff from existing DG methods.

我们的方法聚焦于学习域不变特征的DG方法，且不能是DG与ZSL方法的任意组合。例如，基于生成模型的现有最先进ZSL方法不能直接以此方式扩展。所提方法在ZSDG的训练与推理方式上具有创新性，区别于现有DG方法。

3.2.2 Semantic MTAE (S-MTAE)

3.2.2 语义多任务自编码器（S-MTAE）

Multi-task Auto Encoder (MTAE) [12] learns domain-invariant features using an autoencod-ing framework. In MTAE,the encoder acts as a feature extractor. A single encoder (hΘ) is maintained across domains, which projects each image to a latent representation. Domain-specific decoders (gΦ1,gΦ2,…gΦN) take these representations and regenerate the image back to each domain. This implicitly forces the encoder to learn an unbiased, domain-agnostic projection function. The final classification function is learned using these domain-invariant features with an SVM or a simple MLP. We adapt MTAE for Zero-Shot DG by restricting the latent representation to the semantic embedding of the class labels.

多任务自编码器（MTAE）[12]利用自编码框架学习域不变特征。在MTAE中，编码器充当特征提取器。一个单一的编码器(hΘ)跨域保持不变，将每张图像投影到潜在表示空间。域特定的解码器(gΦ1,gΦ2,…gΦN)利用这些表示将图像重建回各自域。这隐式地促使编码器学习一个无偏的、域无关的投影函数。最终的分类函数使用这些域不变特征，通过支持向量机（SVM）或简单的多层感知机（MLP）进行学习。我们通过将潜在表示限制为类别标签的语义嵌入，调整MTAE以适应零样本域泛化（Zero-Shot DG）。

3.2.3 Semantic FC (S-FC)

3.2.3 语义特征批评网络（S-FC）

Feature Critic Networks (FC) [22] provides a meta-learning approach to DG. It learns a domain-invariant feature extractor by meta-learning an auxiliary loss that 'criticizes' the effectiveness of features generated by the feature extractor, when dealing with an unseen domain. The network is trained by simulating training-to-testing domain shift by splitting the source domains into virtual training and testing meta-domains, following standard meta-learning practice. The goal is to train a model which can perform well both across new domains and also across zero-shot classes. We achieve both these objectives in a novel way i.e model learns to generalize horizontally(across domains) by simulating domain shift and generalize vertically(across zero-shot classes) by exploiting the semantic information of classes.

特征批评网络（FC）[22]提供了一种基于元学习的域泛化方法。它通过元学习一个辅助损失函数来“批评”特征提取器生成的特征在处理未见域时的有效性，从而学习域不变的特征提取器。网络通过模拟训练到测试的域转移，将源域划分为虚拟的训练和测试元域，遵循标准的元学习实践进行训练。目标是训练一个模型，使其在新域和零样本类别上均能表现良好。我们以一种新颖的方式实现这两个目标，即模型通过模拟域转移实现横向泛化（跨域），并通过利用类别的语义信息实现纵向泛化（跨零样本类别）。

4.2 Implementation Details

4.2 实现细节

The partition size of Dtr and Dts in Semantic FC is 3:2; i.e three domains are chosen as meta-train and two as meta-test. We compare the performance across 6 different methods: AGG, S-AGG, FC, S-FC, MTAE, S-MTAE. Here AGG, FC and MTAE, we mean the vanilla method without semantic loss and S- {AGG,MTAE,FC} denote their semantic counterpart. The ZSDG accuracies are computed using distance in the semantic space to the class vectors. The vanilla method helps us in understanding the usefulness of adding the semantic loss. The reported results are averaged across 5 seeds. All the codes, videos and other resources will be available at https://github.com/aniketde/ZeroShotDG.

语义全连接（Semantic FC）中Dtr和Dts的划分比例为3:2；即选择三个域作为元训练(meta-train)，两个域作为元测试(meta-test)。我们比较了6种不同方法的性能：AGG、S-AGG、FC、S-FC、MTAE、S-MTAE。这里AGG、FC和MTAE指的是不带语义损失的基础方法，而带S-{AGG,MTAE,FC}的则表示它们的语义对应版本。零样本域泛化（ZSDG）准确率是通过语义空间中与类别向量的距离计算得出。基础方法帮助我们理解添加语义损失的作用。报告的结果是5个随机种子平均值。所有代码、视频及其他资源将发布于https://github.com/aniketde/ZeroShotDG。

We meticulously designed fair experiments for this new setting. As defined in Sec 4.1, we remove some classes (chosen randomly) from domains used for training, from known DG datasets. Trained ZSDG models are evaluated on these unseen classes in unseen domains. Vanilla DG methods form the baseline (which is fair, considering lack of any other methods at this time). The network, dataset, training strategies in vanilla methods and semantic counterparts are all kept consistent for fair comparison.

我们为这一新设定精心设计了公平的实验。如第4.1节所述，我们从已知的域泛化（DG）数据集中随机移除部分类别，这些类别来自用于训练的域。训练好的ZSDG模型将在这些未见类别和未见域上进行评估。基础DG方法作为基线（考虑到目前缺乏其他方法，这样设定是公平的）。基础方法和其语义对应版本在网络结构、数据集和训练策略上均保持一致，以确保公平比较。

Choice of Word Vectors: Word2Vec[23] uses a two-layer neural net that processes text by vectorizing words. GloVe[23] is an unsupervised learning algorithm which uses word-word co-occurrence statistics from a corpus to obtain word vectors. We use the pre-extracted Word2Vec and GloVe vectors from wikipedia provided by [2]. After comparing with different semantic vectors we found GloVe embeddings [23] to be much more meaningful and hence help in getting good results.

词向量选择：Word2Vec[23]使用两层神经网络将文本中的词向量化。GloVe[23]是一种无监督学习算法，通过语料库中的词共现统计来获得词向量。我们使用[2]提供的预先提取的维基百科Word2Vec和GloVe向量。经过与不同语义向量的比较，我们发现GloVe嵌入[23]更具语义意义，因此有助于获得更好的结果。

6 Conclusion

6 结论

We introduce a novel Zero-Shot Domain Generalization (ZSDG) problem, where a model is expected to generalize to new classes in an unseen domain. This realistic problem setting is harder than Domain Generalization [8, 14, 12, 16], Domain Adaptation [1, 13, 12] and Zero-Shot Learning [2, 1, 2, 2, 3] as ZSDG models are expected to generalize over novel classes in novel domains, without access to corresponding training data-points. We find that learning semantically consistent domain-invariant features helps address this challenging problem. We adapt current state-of-the-art DG methods [124, 122] to this setting to reveal the efficacy of our proposed approach, as well as provide a baseline for further efforts on this problem.

我们提出了一个新颖的零样本域泛化（ZSDG）问题，模型需在未见域中对新类别进行泛化。该现实问题设置比域泛化[8,14,12,16]、域适应[1,13,12]和零样本学习[2,1,2,2,3]更具挑战性，因为ZSDG模型需在未见域中对新类别泛化，且无法访问对应的训练数据。我们发现学习语义一致的域不变特征有助于解决这一难题。我们将当前最先进的域泛化方法[124,122]适配到该设置，验证了所提方法的有效性，并为后续研究提供基线。

A.1.1 Domain Generalization

A.1.1 域泛化

In domain generalization we are given training data from different domains and the objective is to generalize over a novel domain. Let the training dataset be Di={Xij,Yij}1≤j≤ni and the test dataset be Dt={Xjt,Yjt}1≤j≤nt where ni refers to the number of examples in ith domain. In domain generalization framework the label space of all Di and Dt is same.

在域泛化中，我们获得来自不同域的训练数据，目标是在新颖域上实现泛化。设训练数据集为Di={Xij,Yij}1≤j≤ni，测试数据集为Dt={Xjt,Yjt}1≤j≤nt，其中ni表示ith域中的样本数量。在域泛化框架中，所有Di和Dt的标签空间相同。

We define a domain as the joint distribution of feature and label space such that (Xij,Yij)∼ PXYi and PXYi∼μ . The test data set is (XjT,YjT)∼PT and PT∼μ . We assume all(X,Y) pairs are drawn iid from their respective distributions,and that P1,…,PN,PT are iid from μ . The main objective of domain generalization is to train a model on all the training domains D={D1,D2,…DN} which performs well on DT .

我们将域定义为特征空间与标签空间的联合分布，使得(Xij,Yij)∼ PXYi 和 PXYi∼μ。测试数据集为(XjT,YjT)∼PT和PT∼μ。假设所有(X,Y)对均独立同分布(iid)采样自各自的分布，且P1,…,PN,PT独立同分布于μ。域泛化的主要目标是在所有训练域D={D1,D2,…DN}上训练模型，使其在DT上表现良好。

A.1.2 Zero Shot Learning

A.1.2 零样本学习

In zero-shot learning the classes seen during training is disjoint from the classes present in the test phase. Let Ytr and Yts represent the set of classes in the training and testing phase. Let the training dataset be Dtr={xi,yi}1≤i≤ntr,yi∈Ytr . The test dataset be Dts={xi,yi}1≤i≤nts , yi∈Yts . In zero-shot learning framework the objective is to learn a model on Dtr which can generalize well on novel classes from Dts .

在零样本学习中，训练阶段见到的类别与测试阶段出现的类别不相交。设Ytr和Yts分别表示训练和测试阶段的类别集合。训练数据集为Dtr={xi,yi}1≤i≤ntr,yi∈Ytr，测试数据集为Dts={xi,yi}1≤i≤nts，yi∈Yts。零样本学习框架的目标是在Dtr上学习模型，能够在来自Dts的新类别上实现良好泛化。
