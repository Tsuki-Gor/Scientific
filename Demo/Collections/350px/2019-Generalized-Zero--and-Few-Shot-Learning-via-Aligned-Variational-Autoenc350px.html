Abstract
摘要


Many approaches in generalized zero-shot learning rely on cross-modal mapping between the image feature space and the class embedding space. As labeled images are expensive, one direction is to augment the dataset by generating either images or image features. However, the former misses fine-grained details and the latter requires learning a mapping associated with class embeddings. In this work, we take feature generation one step further and propose a model where a shared latent space of image features and class embeddings is learned by modality-specific aligned variational autoencoders. This leaves us with the required discriminative information about the image and classes in the latent features, on which we train a softmax classifier. The key to our approach is that we align the distributions learned from images and from side-information to construct latent features that contain the essential multi-modal information associated with unseen classes. We evaluate our learned latent features on several benchmark datasets, i.e. CUB, SUN, AWA1 and AWA2, and establish a new state of the art on generalized zero-shot as well as on few-shot learning. Moreover, our results on ImageNet with various zero-shot splits show that our latent features generalize well in large-scale settings.
广义零样本学习中的许多方法依赖于图像特征空间和类别嵌入空间之间的跨模态映射。由于带标签的图像成本高昂，一种方法是通过生成图像或图像特征来扩充数据集。然而，前者会丢失细粒度细节，而后者需要学习与类别嵌入相关的映射。在这项工作中，我们将特征生成向前推进了一步，提出了一个模型，其中图像特征和类别嵌入的共享潜在空间由特定模态的对齐变分自编码器学习得到。这使得我们在潜在特征中保留了关于图像和类别的必要判别信息，并在此基础上训练了一个softmax分类器。我们方法的关键在于，我们将从图像和辅助信息中学习到的分布进行对齐，以构建包含与未见类别相关的基本多模态信息的潜在特征。我们在几个基准数据集上评估了我们学习到的潜在特征，即加州理工学院鸟类数据集（CUB）、斯坦福大学场景数据集（SUN）、动物属性数据集1（AWA1）和动物属性数据集2（AWA2），并在广义零样本学习和少样本学习方面建立了新的最优水平。此外，我们在具有各种零样本划分的ImageNet数据集上的实验结果表明，我们的潜在特征在大规模场景下具有良好的泛化能力。

1. Introduction
1. 引言


Generalized zero-shot learning (GZSL) is a challenging task especially for unbalanced and large datasets such as ImageNet [5]. Although at training time no visual data of some classes, i.e. unseen classes, are provided the classifier must learn to differentiate between all classes, i.e. seen and unseen classes. As visual data of unseen classes is not available at training time, typically knowledge transfer from seen to unseen classes is achieved via some form of side information that encode semantic relationship between classes, i.e. class embeddings.
广义零样本学习（GZSL）是一项具有挑战性的任务，特别是对于像ImageNet [5] 这样不平衡的大型数据集。尽管在训练时没有提供某些类别的视觉数据，即未见类别，但分类器必须学会区分所有类别，即已见类别和未见类别。由于在训练时无法获取未见类别的视觉数据，通常通过某种形式的辅助信息（即类别嵌入）来实现从已见类别到未见类别的知识迁移，这些辅助信息编码了类别之间的语义关系。

Most approaches to GZSL [6,1,20,34,2] learn a mapping between images and their class embeddings. An orthogonal approach is to augment data by generating artificial images [23]. However, due to the level of detail missing in the synthetic images, CNN features extracted from them do not improve classification accuracy. To alleviate this issue, [36] proposed to generate image features via a conditional WGAN, which simplifies the task of the generative model and directly optimizes the loss on image features. Although the features generated by [36] improved GZSL significantly, GAN-based loss functions suffer from instability in training. Hence, recently conditional variational au-toencoders (VAE) [18,14] have been employed for this purpose. As GZSL is inherently a multi-modal learning task, [29] proposed to transform both modalities to the latent spaces of autoencoders and match the corresponding distributions by minimizing the Maximum Mean Discrepancy (MMD). Learning such cross-modal embeddings is beneficial for potential downstream tasks that require multimodal fusion, e.g. visual question answering. In this domain, [22] recently used a cross-modal autoencoder to extend visual question answering to previously unseen objects.
大多数广义零样本学习的方法 [6,1,20,34,2] 是学习图像与其类别嵌入之间的映射。另一种不同的方法是通过生成人工图像来扩充数据 [23]。然而，由于合成图像缺少细节，从中提取的卷积神经网络（CNN）特征并不能提高分类准确率。为了缓解这个问题，[36] 提出通过条件生成对抗网络（WGAN）生成图像特征，这简化了生成模型的任务，并直接优化图像特征上的损失。尽管 [36] 生成的特征显著提高了广义零样本学习的性能，但基于生成对抗网络（GAN）的损失函数在训练过程中存在不稳定性。因此，最近条件变分自编码器（VAE） [18,14] 被用于此目的。由于广义零样本学习本质上是一个多模态学习任务，[29] 提出将两种模态都转换到自编码器的潜在空间，并通过最小化最大均值差异（MMD）来匹配相应的分布。学习这种跨模态嵌入对于需要多模态融合的潜在下游任务（如视觉问答）是有益的。在这个领域，[22] 最近使用跨模态自编码器将视觉问答扩展到以前未见的对象。

In this work, we train VAEs to encode and decode features from different modalities, e.g. images and class attributes, and use the learned latent features to train a generalized zero-shot learning classifier. Our latent representations are aligned by matching their parametrized distributions and by enforcing a cross-modal reconstruction criterion. Consequently, by explicitly enforcing alignment both in the latent features and in the distributions of latent features learned using different modalities, the VAEs enable knowledge transfer to unseen classes without forgetting the previously seen classes.
在这项工作中，我们训练变分自编码器来对来自不同模态（如图像和类别属性）的特征进行编码和解码，并使用学习到的潜在特征来训练广义零样本学习分类器。我们的潜在表示通过匹配其参数化分布并强制执行跨模态重建准则来进行对齐。因此，通过在潜在特征和使用不同模态学习到的潜在特征分布中明确强制执行对齐，变分自编码器能够在不遗忘先前已见类别的情况下将知识迁移到未见类别。

Our contributions are as follows. (1) We propose the CADA-VAE model that learns shared cross-modal latent representations of multiple data modalities using VAEs via distribution alignment and cross alignment objectives. (2) We extensively evaluate our model using conventional benchmark datasets, i.e. CUB, SUN, AWA1 and AWA2, on zero-shot and few-shot learning settings. Our model establishes the new state-of-the-art performance on generalized zero-shot and few-shot learning settings on all these datasets. Furthermore, we show that our model can be extended easily to more than two modalities that are trained simultaneously. (3) Finally, we show that the latent features learned by our model improve the state of the art in the truly large-scale ImageNet dataset in all splits for the generalized zero-shot learning task.
我们的贡献如下。(1) 我们提出了CADA - VAE模型，该模型通过分布对齐和交叉对齐目标，使用变分自编码器（VAE）学习多种数据模态的共享跨模态潜在表示。(2) 我们使用传统基准数据集，即CUB、SUN、AWA1和AWA2，在零样本和少样本学习设置下对我们的模型进行了广泛评估。我们的模型在所有这些数据集的广义零样本和少样本学习设置上建立了新的最优性能。此外，我们表明我们的模型可以轻松扩展到同时训练的两种以上的模态。(3) 最后，我们表明我们的模型学习到的潜在特征在广义零样本学习任务的所有分割中，提高了真正大规模的ImageNet数据集的现有最优水平。

2. Related Work
2. 相关工作


In this section, we present related work on generalized zero-shot learning, few-shot learning and cross-modal reconstruction.
在本节中，我们介绍广义零样本学习、少样本学习和跨模态重建的相关工作。

Generalized Zero-and Few-Shot Learning. In zero-shot learning, training and test classes are disjoint with shared attributes annotated on class level, and the performance of the method is solely judged on its classification accuracy on the novel, i.e. unseen classes. Generalized zero-shot learning is a more realistic variant of zero-shot learning, since the same information is available at training time, but the performance of the model is judged on the harmonic mean of the classification accuracy on seen and unseen classes.
广义零样本和少样本学习。在零样本学习中，训练类和测试类是不相交的，且在类级别上标注了共享属性，该方法的性能仅根据其在新的（即未见类）上的分类准确率来判断。广义零样本学习是零样本学习的一种更现实的变体，因为在训练时可获得相同的信息，但模型的性能是根据在已见类和未见类上的分类准确率的调和平均值来判断的。

In few-shot learning,there are k examples provided at training time for the previously unseen classes [30,26,10 , 32]. Using auxiliary information for few-shot learning was introduced in [30], where attributes related to images were used to improve the performance of the model. The use of auxiliary information was also explored in ReViSE [29], in which a common image-label semantic space for transduc-tive few-shot learning is learned. Analogous to the relation between ZSL and GZSL, we extend few-shot to the generalized few-shot learning (GFSL) setting, in which we evaluate the model on both seen and unseen classes.
在少样本学习中，在训练时为先前未见的类k提供了示例 [30,26,10 , 32]。[30]中引入了使用辅助信息进行少样本学习的方法，其中使用与图像相关的属性来提高模型的性能。在ReViSE [29]中也探索了辅助信息的使用，该方法学习了一个用于直推式少样本学习的通用图像 - 标签语义空间。类似于零样本学习（ZSL）和广义零样本学习（GZSL）之间的关系，我们将少样本学习扩展到广义少样本学习（GFSL）设置，在该设置中，我们在已见类和未见类上评估模型。

Data-Generating Models for GZSL. Generative models are used for generating images or image feature as a data-augmentation [36,18,14] mechanism in GZSL. These approaches treat GZSL as a missing data problem and train conditional GANs or conditional VAEs to generate image features for unseen classes from semantic side-information. In this work, we create latent space features instead.
用于广义零样本学习（GZSL）的数据生成模型。生成模型用于在广义零样本学习中生成图像或图像特征作为数据增强 [36,18,14] 机制。这些方法将广义零样本学习视为一个缺失数据问题，并训练条件生成对抗网络（GAN）或条件变分自编码器（VAE），以从语义侧信息中为未见类生成图像特征。在这项工作中，我们改为创建潜在空间特征。

Cross-Modal Embedding Models. Recent cross-modal embeddings for GZSL are based on autoencoders, such as ReViSE [29] and DMAE [19], which learn to jointly represent features from different modalities in their latent space. By making use of autoencoders, it is possible to learn representations of visual and semantic information in a semi-supervised fashion. Learning a joint representation for visual and semantic data is achieved by aligning the latent distributions between different data types. ReViSE implements this distribution alignment by minimizing the maximum mean discrepancy between the two latent distributions [9]. DMAE aligns distributions by means of minimizing the Squared-Loss Mutual Information [28]. In this work, we use Variational Autoencoders instead, and align the latent distributions by minimizing their Wasserstein distance. In contrast to [19] and [29], we also enforce a cross-reconstruction loss, by decoding every encoded feature into every other modality.
跨模态嵌入模型。最近用于广义零样本学习的跨模态嵌入基于自编码器，如ReViSE [29]和DMAE [19]，它们学习在其潜在空间中联合表示来自不同模态的特征。通过使用自编码器，可以以半监督的方式学习视觉和语义信息的表示。通过对齐不同数据类型之间的潜在分布，可以实现视觉和语义数据的联合表示学习。ReViSE通过最小化两个潜在分布之间的最大均值差异来实现这种分布对齐 [9]。DMAE通过最小化平方损失互信息来对齐分布 [28]。在这项工作中，我们改为使用变分自编码器，并通过最小化它们的Wasserstein距离来对齐潜在分布。与[19]和[29]不同，我们还通过将每个编码特征解码为其他每种模态来施加交叉重建损失。

Cross-Reconstruction in Generative models. Reconstructing data across domains, referred to as cross-alignment, is commonly used in the field of domain adaptation. While models like CycleGAN [37] learn to generate data across domains directly, latent space models use cross-reconstruction to capture the common information contained in both domains in their intermediate latent representations [31]. In this regard, cross-aligned VAE's have been used previously for text-style transfer [25] and image-to-image translation [16]. In [25] a cross-aligned VAE ensures that the latent representations of texts from different input domains are similar, while in [16] a comparable approach matches the latent representations of images from different domains. Both methods have in common that they use a different variant of VAEs with an adversarial loss. Additionally, [25] makes use of conditional encoders and decoders, while [16] enforces cycle consistency and weight sharing. Similarly, better generalization can be achieved if the shared representation space is amenable to class-interpolation, sentence interpolation [3] and image interpolation [11]. In this paper, our building blocks are unconditional VAEs and we achieve multi-modal alignment via cross-reconstruction and latent distribution alignment in a highly reduced space.
生成模型中的交叉重建。跨领域重建数据，即交叉对齐，常用于领域适应领域。像CycleGAN [37]这样的模型直接学习跨领域生成数据，而潜在空间模型使用交叉重建来在其中间潜在表示中捕获两个领域中包含的共同信息 [31]。在这方面，交叉对齐的变分自编码器（VAE）以前曾用于文本风格迁移 [25]和图像到图像的翻译 [16]。在[25]中，交叉对齐的变分自编码器确保来自不同输入领域的文本的潜在表示相似，而在[16]中，一种类似的方法匹配来自不同领域的图像的潜在表示。这两种方法的共同点是它们使用具有对抗损失的不同变体的变分自编码器。此外，[25]使用条件编码器和解码器，而[16]强制循环一致性和权重共享。同样，如果共享表示空间适用于类插值、句子插值 [3]和图像插值 [11]，则可以实现更好的泛化。在本文中，我们的构建块是无条件变分自编码器，我们通过在高度缩减的空间中进行交叉重建和潜在分布对齐来实现多模态对齐。

3. CADA-VAE Model
3. CADA - VAE模型


Of the existing GZSL models, recent data generating approaches [36,14,18] achieve superior performance over other methods on disjoint datasets. Classifying generated image features from GANS [36] or conditional VAEs [14,18] runs at risk of being compromised by the curse of dimensionality. On the other hand, CADA-VAE has a control over the dimensionality and structure (via a prior) of the features to be classified. The main insight of our proposed model is that instead of generating images or image features, we generate low-dimensional latent features and achieve both stable training and state-of-the-art performance. Hence, the key to our approach is the choice of a VAE latent-space, a reconstruction and cross-reconstruction criterion to preserve class-discriminative information in lower dimensions, as well as explicit distribution alignment to encourage domain-agnostic representations.
在现有的广义零样本学习（GZSL）模型中，近期的数据生成方法 [36,14,18] 在不相交数据集上的表现优于其他方法。对来自生成对抗网络（GANs）[36]或条件变分自编码器（VAEs） [14,18] 的生成图像特征进行分类，存在受维度灾难影响的风险。另一方面，跨模态与分布对齐的变分自编码器（CADA - VAE）能够控制待分类特征的维度和结构（通过先验）。我们提出的模型的主要见解是，我们不生成图像或图像特征，而是生成低维潜在特征，从而实现稳定训练和达到当前最优性能。因此，我们方法的关键在于选择变分自编码器（VAE）的潜在空间、一种重建和交叉重建准则以在低维度中保留类别判别信息，以及进行显式的分布对齐以促进与领域无关的表示。

3.1. Background
3.1. 背景


We first provide the background as the task (GZSL) and as the model building blocks (variational autoencoders).
我们首先介绍广义零样本学习（GZSL）任务和模型构建模块（变分自编码器）的背景知识。

Generalized Zero-shot Learning. The task definition of GZSL is as follows. Let S={(x,y,c(y))∣x∈X,y∈ YS,c(y)∈C} be a set of training examples,consisting of image-features x ,e.g. extracted by a CNN,class labels y available during training and class-embeddings c(y) . Typical class-embeddings are vectors of hand-annotated continuous attributes or Word2Vec features [17]. In addition, an auxiliary training set U={(u,c(u))∣u∈Yu,c(u)∈C} is used,where u denote unseen classes from a set Yu ,which is disjoint from YS . Here, C(U)={c(u1),…,c(uL)} is the set of class-embeddings of unseen classes. In the legacy challenge of ZSL, the task is to learn a classifier fZSL:X→YU . However,in this work,we focus on the more realistic and challenging setup of GZSL where the aim is to learn a classifier fGZSL:X→YU∪YS .
广义零样本学习。广义零样本学习（GZSL）的任务定义如下。设 S={(x,y,c(y))∣x∈X,y∈ YS,c(y)∈C} 为一组训练示例，由图像特征 x（例如由卷积神经网络（CNN）提取）、训练期间可用的类别标签 y 和类别嵌入 c(y) 组成。典型的类别嵌入是手动标注的连续属性向量或词向量（Word2Vec）特征 [17]。此外，使用辅助训练集 U={(u,c(u))∣u∈Yu,c(u)∈C}，其中 u 表示来自集合 Yu 的未见类别，该集合与 YS 不相交。这里，C(U)={c(u1),…,c(uL)} 是未见类别的类别嵌入集合。在传统的零样本学习（ZSL）挑战中，任务是学习一个分类器 fZSL:X→YU。然而，在这项工作中，我们专注于更现实且更具挑战性的广义零样本学习（GZSL）设置，其目标是学习一个分类器 fGZSL:X→YU∪YS。

Variational Autoencoder (VAE). The basic building block of our model is the variational autoencoder (VAE) [13]. Variational inference aims at finding the true conditional probability distribution over the latent variables, pϕ(z∣x) . Due to interactibility of this distribution, it can be approximated by finding its closest proxy posterior, qθ(z∣x) , through minimizing their distance using a variational lower bound limit. The objective function of a VAE is the variational lower bound on the marginal likelihood of a given datapoint and can be formulated as:
变分自编码器（VAE）。我们模型的基本构建模块是变分自编码器（VAE）[13]。变分推断旨在找到潜在变量上的真实条件概率分布 pϕ(z∣x)。由于该分布难以处理，可以通过使用变分下界最小化其与最接近的代理后验 qθ(z∣x) 之间的距离来近似它。变分自编码器（VAE）的目标函数是给定数据点边缘似然的变分下界，可以表述为：

(1)L=Eqϕ(z∣x)[log⁡pθ(x∣z)]−DKL(qϕ(z∣x)∥pθ(z))

where the first term is the reconstruction error and the second term is the unpacked Kullback-Leibler divergence between the inference model q(z∣x) ,and p(z) . A common choice for the prior is a multivariate standard Gaussian distribution. The encoder predicts μ and ∑ such that qϕ(z∣x)=N(μ,∑) ,from which a latent vector z is generated via the reparametrization trick [13].
其中第一项是重建误差，第二项是推理模型 q(z∣x) 和 p(z) 之间展开的库尔贝克 - 莱布勒散度（Kullback - Leibler divergence）。先验分布的常见选择是多元标准高斯分布。编码器预测 μ 和 ∑，使得 qϕ(z∣x)=N(μ,∑)，通过重参数化技巧 [13] 从中生成一个潜在向量 z。

Implementation Details. All encoders and decoders are multilayer perceptrons with one hidden layer. More hidden layers degraded performance as CNN features and attributes are already very high-level representations. We use 1560 hidden units for the image feature encoder and 1660 for the decoder. The attribute encoder and decoder have 1450 and 660 hidden units, respectively.
实现细节。所有编码器和解码器都是具有一个隐藏层的多层感知机。更多的隐藏层会降低性能，因为卷积神经网络（CNN）特征和属性已经是非常高层的表示。我们为图像特征编码器使用 1560 个隐藏单元，为解码器使用 1660 个。属性编码器和解码器分别有 1450 个和 660 个隐藏单元。

The latent embedding size is 64. For ImageNet, we chose a size of 128 , and use two hidden layers of identical size for the encoder, with the number of hidden units specified above and the image feature decoder layers are of size 1160 and 1660, while the attribute decoder uses 460 and 660 units. The model is trained for 100 epochs by stochastic gradient descent using the Adam optimizer [12] and a batch size of 128 for ImageNet and 50 for all other datasets. Each training batch consists of pairs of CNN features and matching attributes, from different seen classes. A pair of data always belongs to the same class. After individual VAEs learn to encode features of only their specific datatype for some epochs, we also start to compute cross- and distribution alignment losses. δ is increased from epoch 6 to epoch 22 by a rate of 0.54 per epoch,while γ is increased from epoch 21 to 75 by 0.044 per epoch. For the KL-divergence we use an annealing scheme [3], in which we increase the weight β of the KL-divergence by a rate of 0.0026 per epoch until epoch 90. A KL-annealing scheme serves the purpose of first letting the VAE learn "useful" representations before they are "smoothed" out, since the KL-divergence would be otherwise a very strong regularizer [3].
潜在嵌入大小为64。对于ImageNet数据集，我们选择的大小为128，并为编码器使用两个相同大小的隐藏层，隐藏单元数量如上文所述，图像特征解码器层的大小分别为1160和1660，而属性解码器使用460和660个单元。该模型通过随机梯度下降法使用Adam优化器[12]进行100个训练周期的训练，对于ImageNet数据集，批量大小为128，对于其他所有数据集，批量大小为50。每个训练批次由来自不同已见类别的卷积神经网络（CNN）特征对和匹配属性组成。一对数据始终属于同一类别。在各个变分自编码器（VAE）经过若干个训练周期学会仅对其特定数据类型的特征进行编码后，我们也开始计算交叉和分布对齐损失。δ从第6个训练周期到第22个训练周期以每个周期0.54的速率增加，而γ从第21个训练周期到第75个训练周期以每个周期0.044的速率增加。对于KL散度，我们采用退火方案[3]，在该方案中，我们以每个周期0.0026的速率增加KL散度的权重β，直到第90个训练周期。KL退火方案的目的是先让变分自编码器学习“有用”的表示，然后再进行“平滑”处理，因为否则KL散度将是一个非常强的正则化项[3]。

We empirically found that it is useful to use a variant of the reparametrization trick [13], in which all dimensions of the noise vector are sampled from a single unimodal Gaussian. Also, using the L1 distance as reconstruction error appeared to yield slightly better results than L2. After training, the VAE encoders transform the training and test set of the final linear classifier into the latent space 1 .
我们通过实验发现，使用重参数化技巧[13]的一种变体很有用，在这种变体中，噪声向量的所有维度都从单个单峰高斯分布中采样。此外，使用L1距离作为重建误差似乎比使用L2距离能产生略好的结果。训练完成后，变分自编码器编码器将最终线性分类器的训练集和测试集转换到潜在空间1中。

As for our results in Figure 5 on the right, we build a dynamic training set by generating latent features continuously at every iteration and do not use any sample more than once. Hence, we eliminate one of the tunable parameters, i.e. the number of latent features to generate. Because of the non-deterministic mapping of the VAE encoder, every latent feature of a different class is unique. Our results indicate that the best accuracy is achieved when unseen and seen class samples are equally balanced. In CUB, using a dynamic training set reaches the same performance as using a fixed dataset with 100 unseen examples and 50 seen examples. On the other hand, using a fixed dataset leads to a faster training procedure. Hence, we use a fixed dataset with 200 examples per seen class and 400 examples per unseen class in every benchmark reported in this paper.
至于右侧图5中的结果，我们通过在每次迭代时持续生成潜在特征来构建动态训练集，且每个样本仅使用一次。因此，我们消除了一个可调参数，即要生成的潜在特征数量。由于变分自编码器（VAE）编码器的非确定性映射，不同类别的每个潜在特征都是唯一的。我们的结果表明，当未见类别和已见类别样本达到平衡时，可获得最佳准确率。在加州大学伯克利分校鸟类数据集（CUB）中，使用动态训练集与使用包含100个未见示例和50个已见示例的固定数据集可达到相同的性能。另一方面，使用固定数据集可加快训练过程。因此，在本文报告的每个基准测试中，我们使用每个已见类别包含200个示例、每个未见类别包含400个示例的固定数据集。

Since our model does not use any CNN features, i.e. we generate 64-dimensional latent features for all classes, it achieves a balance between seen and unseen class accuracies better than CNN feature-generating approaches especially on CUB. In addition, CADA-VAE learns a shared representation in a weakly-supervised fashion, through a cross-reconstruction objective. Since the latent features have to be decoded into every involved modality, and since every modality encodes complementary information, the model is encouraged to learn an encoding that retains the information contained in all used modalities. In doing so, our method is less biased towards learning the distribution of the seen class image features, which is known as the projection domain shift problem [7]. As we generate a certain number of latent features per class using non-deterministic encoders, our method is also akin to data-generating approaches. However, the learned representations lie in a lower dimensional space, i.e. only 64, and therefore, are less prone to bias towards the training set of image features. In effect, our training is more stable than the adversarial training schemes used for data generation [36]. In fact, we did not conduct any dataset specific parameter tuning and use the same parameters for all datasets.
由于我们的模型不使用任何卷积神经网络（CNN）特征，即我们为所有类别生成64维的潜在特征，因此它在可见和不可见类别准确率之间实现了比CNN特征生成方法更好的平衡，尤其是在CUB数据集上。此外，CADA - VAE通过交叉重建目标以弱监督的方式学习共享表示。由于潜在特征必须被解码为每个涉及的模态，并且每个模态都编码互补信息，因此鼓励模型学习一种保留所有使用模态中包含信息的编码。这样，我们的方法在学习可见类别图像特征的分布时偏差较小，这就是所谓的投影域偏移问题[7]。由于我们使用非确定性编码器为每个类别生成一定数量的潜在特征，我们的方法也类似于数据生成方法。然而，学习到的表示位于较低维度的空间中，即仅为64维，因此对图像特征训练集的偏差较小。实际上，我们的训练比用于数据生成的对抗训练方案[36]更稳定。事实上，我们没有进行任何特定于数据集的参数调整，而是对所有数据集使用相同的参数。

4.3. ImageNet Experiments
4.3. ImageNet实验


The ImageNet dataset serves as a challenging testbed for GZSL. In [35] several evaluation splits were proposed with increasing granularity and size both in terms of the number of classes and the number of images. Note that since all the images of 1K classes are used to train ResNet-101, measuring seen class accuracies would be biased. However, we can still evaluate the accuracy of unseen class images in the GZSL search space that contains both seen and unseen classes. Hence,at test time the 1K seen classes act as distractors. This way, we can measure the transferability of our latent representations to completely unseen classes, i.e. classes that are not seen either during ResNet training nor CADA-VAE training. For ImageNet, since attributes are not available, we use Word2Vec features as class em-beddings provided by [4]. We compare our model with f-CLSWGAN [36], i.e. an image feature generating framework which currently achieves the state of the art on Ima-geNet. We use the same evaluation protocol on all the splits. Among the splits, 2H and 3H are the classes 2 or 3 hops away from the 1K seen training classes of ImageNet according to the ImageNet hierarchy. M500, M1K and M5K are the 500,1000 and 5000 most populated classes, while L500, L1K and L5K are the 500, 1000 and 5000 least populated classes that come from the rest of the 21K classes. Finally,’All ’denotes the remaining 20K classes.
ImageNet数据集是广义零样本学习（GZSL）的一个具有挑战性的测试平台。在文献[35]中，提出了几种评估划分方式，这些划分在类别数量和图像数量方面的粒度和规模都在不断增加。请注意，由于1K类别的所有图像都用于训练ResNet - 101，因此衡量已见类别的准确率会存在偏差。然而，我们仍然可以评估广义零样本学习搜索空间中未见类别图像的准确率，该搜索空间同时包含已见和未见类别。因此，在测试时，1K个已见类别会起到干扰作用。通过这种方式，我们可以衡量我们的潜在表示对完全未见类别的可迁移性，即那些在ResNet训练和CADA - VAE训练期间都未见过的类别。对于ImageNet，由于没有可用的属性信息，我们使用文献[4]提供的Word2Vec特征作为类别嵌入。我们将我们的模型与f - CLSWGAN[36]进行比较，f - CLSWGAN是一个图像特征生成框架，目前在ImageNet上达到了最先进水平。我们在所有划分上使用相同的评估协议。在这些划分中，根据ImageNet的层次结构，2H和3H是与ImageNet的1K个已见训练类别相隔2跳或3跳的类别。M500、M1K和M5K分别是图像数量最多的500、1000和5000个类别，而L500、L1K和L5K则是来自其余21K个类别中图像数量最少的500、1000和5000个类别。最后，“All”表示剩余的20K个类别。

As shown in Figure 7, our model significantly improves the state of the art in all splits. The accuracy improvement is significant especially on M500 and M1K splits, i.e. for M500 the search space is 1.5K classes,for M1K ,the search space consists of 2K classes. For the L500,L1K and L5K splits, there are on average only 1,3 and 5 images per class available [35]. Since the test time search space in the 'All' split is 22K dimensional,even a small improvement in accuracy is considered to be compelling. The achieved substantial increase in performance by CADA-VAE shows that our 128-dim latent feature space constitutes a robust generalizable representation, surpassing the current state-of-the-art image feature generating framework f -CLSWGAN.
如图7所示，我们的模型在所有划分中都显著提升了当前的最先进水平。准确率的提升在M500和M1K划分上尤为显著，即对于M500，搜索空间有1.5K个类别；对于M1K，搜索空间由2K个类别组成。对于L500,L1K和L5K划分，每个类别平均只有1、3和5张可用图像[35]。由于“All”划分的测试时搜索空间是22K维的，即使准确率有微小的提升也被认为是很有说服力的。CADA - VAE在性能上实现的大幅提升表明，我们的128维潜在特征空间构成了一种强大的可泛化表示，超越了当前最先进的图像特征生成框架f - CLSWGAN。

5. Conclusion
5. 结论


In this work, we propose CADA-VAE, a cross-modal embedding framework for generalized zero- and few-shot learning. In CADA-VAE, we train a VAE for both visual and semantic modalities. The VAE of each modality has to jointly represent the information embodied by all modalities in its latent space. The corresponding latent distributions are aligned by minimizing their Wasserstein distance and by enforcing cross-reconstruction. This procedure leaves us with encoders that can encode features from different modalities into one cross-modal embedding space, in which a linear softmax classifier can be trained. We present different variants of cross-aligned and distribution aligned VAEs and establish new state-of-the-art results in generalized zero-shot learning for four medium-scale benchmark datasets as well as the large-scale ImageNet. We further show that a cross-modal embedding model for generalized zero-shot learning achieves better performance than data-generating methods, establishing the new state of the art.
在这项工作中，我们提出了CADA - VAE，这是一个用于广义零样本和少样本学习的跨模态嵌入框架。在CADA - VAE中，我们为视觉和语义模态分别训练一个变分自编码器（VAE）。每个模态的VAE必须在其潜在空间中联合表示所有模态所包含的信息。通过最小化它们的Wasserstein距离并强制进行交叉重建来对齐相应的潜在分布。这个过程为我们提供了编码器，这些编码器可以将来自不同模态的特征编码到一个跨模态嵌入空间中，在这个空间中可以训练一个线性softmax分类器。我们提出了跨对齐和分布对齐VAE的不同变体，并在四个中等规模的基准数据集以及大规模的ImageNet上的广义零样本学习中建立了新的最先进结果。我们进一步表明，用于广义零样本学习的跨模态嵌入模型比数据生成方法具有更好的性能，确立了新的最先进水平。
