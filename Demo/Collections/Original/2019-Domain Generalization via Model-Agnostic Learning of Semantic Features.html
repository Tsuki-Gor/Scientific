Abstract
摘要

Generalization capability to unseen domains is crucial for machine learning models when deploying to real-world conditions. We investigate the challenging problem of domain generalization, i.e., training a model on multi-domain source data such that it can directly generalize to target domains with unknown statistics. We adopt a model-agnostic learning paradigm with gradient-based meta-train and meta-test procedures to expose the optimization to domain shift. Further, we introduce two complementary losses which explicitly regularize the semantic structure of the feature space. Globally, we align a derived soft confusion matrix to preserve general knowledge about inter-class relationships. Locally, we promote domain-independent class-specific cohesion and separation of sample features with a metric-learning component. The effectiveness of our method is demonstrated with new state-of-the-art results on two common object recognition benchmarks. Our method also shows consistent improvement on a medical image segmentation task.
在将机器学习模型部署到现实世界条件时，对未见领域的泛化能力至关重要。我们研究了领域泛化的挑战性问题，即在多领域源数据上训练模型，使其能够直接泛化到具有未知统计特征的目标领域。我们采用了一种模型无关的学习范式，使用基于梯度的元训练和元测试程序来暴露优化过程中的领域转移。此外，我们引入了两个互补的损失函数，明确地规范特征空间的语义结构。总体上，我们对派生的软混淆矩阵进行对齐，以保持关于类间关系的通用知识。在局部，我们通过一个度量学习组件促进领域无关的类特定的凝聚性和样本特征的分离性。我们的方法在两个常见的物体识别基准上展示了新的最先进的结果，证明了其有效性。我们的方法在医学图像分割任务上也显示出一致的改进。

1 Introduction
1 引言

Machine learning methods have achieved remarkable success, under the assumption that training and test data are sampled from the same distribution. In real-world applications, this assumption is often violated as conditions for data acquisition may change, and a trained system may fail to produce accurate predictions for unseen data with domain shift. To tackle this issue, domain adaptation algorithms normally learn to align source and target data in a domain-invariant discriminative feature space [6,11,19,32,33,42,43,50,51] . These methods rely on access to a few labelled [6,42,50] or unlabelled [11,19,32,33,43,51] data samples from the target distribution during training.
机器学习方法在假设训练数据和测试数据来自同一分布的前提下取得了显著成功。在现实应用中，这一假设常常被违反，因为数据获取的条件可能会变化，训练好的系统可能无法对具有领域转移的未见数据做出准确预测。为了解决这个问题，领域适应算法通常学习在领域不变的判别特征空间中对齐源数据和目标数据 [6,11,19,32,33,42,43,50,51] 。这些方法在训练期间依赖于从目标分布中获取少量标记 [6,42,50] 或未标记 [11,19,32,33,43,51] 数据样本。
An arguably harder problem is domain generalization, which aims to train a model using multi-domain source data, such that it can directly generalize to new domains without need of retraining. This setting is very different to domain adaptation as no information about the new domains is available, a scenario that is encountered in real-world applications. In the field of healthcare, for example, medical images acquired at different sites can differ significantly in their data distribution, due to varying scanners, imaging protocols or patient cohorts. At deployment, each new hospital can be regarded as a new domain but it is impractical to collect data each time to adapt a trained system. Learning a model which directly generalizes to new clinical sites would be of great practical value.
一个可以说更困难的问题是领域泛化，其目标是使用多领域源数据训练模型，使其能够直接泛化到新领域，而无需重新训练。这个设置与领域适应非常不同，因为没有关于新领域的信息，这是在现实世界应用中遇到的场景。例如，在医疗保健领域，不同地点获取的医学图像在数据分布上可能会显著不同，这可能是由于扫描仪、成像协议或患者群体的差异。在部署时，每个新的医院可以被视为一个新领域，但每次收集数据以适应已训练系统是不切实际的。学习一个能够直接泛化到新的临床场所的模型将具有很大的实际价值。
Domain generalization is an active research area with a number of approaches being proposed. As no a priori knowledge of the target distribution is available, the key question is how to guide the model learning to capture information which is discriminative for the specific task but insensitive to changes of domain-specific statistics. For computer vision applications, the aim is to capture general semantic features for object recognition. Previous work has demonstrated that this can be investigated through regularization of the feature space, e.g., by minimizing divergence between marginal distributions of data sources [35], or joint consideration of the class conditional distributions [30]. Li et al. [28] use adversarial feature alignment via maximum mean discrepancy. Leveraging distance metrics of feature vectors is another method [20, 34]. Model-agnostic meta-learning [10] is a recent gradient-based method for fast adaptation of models to new conditions, e.g., a new task at few-shot learning. Meta-learning has been introduced to address domain generalization [1, 26, 31], by adopting an episodic training paradigm, i.e., splitting the available source domains into meta-train and meta-test at each iteration, to simulate domain shift. Promising performance has been demonstrated by deriving the loss from a task error [26], a classifier regularizer [1], or a predictive feature-critic module [31].
域泛化是一个活跃的研究领域，提出了多种方法。由于没有可用的目标分布的先验知识，关键问题是如何引导模型学习，以捕捉对特定任务具有区分性的但对领域特定统计变化不敏感的信息。对于计算机视觉应用，目标是捕捉用于物体识别的一般语义特征。先前的研究表明，这可以通过对特征空间的正则化来研究，例如，通过最小化数据源的边际分布之间的散度 [35]，或联合考虑类条件分布 [30]。Li 等人 [28] 通过最大均值差异使用对抗特征对齐。利用特征向量的距离度量是另一种方法 [20, 34]。模型无关的元学习 [10] 是一种最近的基于梯度的方法，用于快速适应模型到新条件，例如，在少样本学习中的新任务。元学习已被引入以解决域泛化问题 [1, 26, 31]，通过采用情节训练范式，即在每次迭代中将可用的源域分为元训练和元测试，以模拟领域转移。通过从任务误差 [26]、分类器正则化器 [1] 或预测特征-评论模块 [31] 中推导损失，展示了有希望的性能。
We introduce two complementary losses which explicitly regularize the semantic structure of the feature space via a model-agnostic episodic learning procedure. Our optimization objective encourages the model to learn semantically consistent features across training domains that may generalize better to unseen domains. Globally, we align a derived soft confusion matrix to preserve inter-class relationships. Locally, we use a metric-learning component to encourage domain-independent while class-specific cohesion and separation of sample features. The effectiveness of our approach is demonstrated with new state-of-the-art performance on two common object recognition benchmarks. Our method also shows consistent improvement on a medical image segmentation task. Code for our proposed method is available at: https://github.com/biomedia-mira/masf.
我们引入了两种互补损失，通过一种模型无关的情景学习过程，明确地规范特征空间的语义结构。我们的优化目标鼓励模型学习在训练域之间具有语义一致性的特征，这些特征可能更好地泛化到未见域。总体上，我们对一个派生的软混淆矩阵进行对齐，以保持类间关系。在局部，我们使用一个度量学习组件来鼓励样本特征的领域独立性，同时保持类特异性的凝聚和分离。我们的方法在两个常见的物体识别基准上展示了新的最先进的性能，证明了其有效性。我们的方案在医学图像分割任务上也显示出一致的改进。我们提出的方法的代码可在以下网址获取：https://github.com/biomedia-mira/masf。

2 Related Work
2 相关工作

Domain adaptation is based on the central theme of bounding the target error by the source error plus a discrepancy metric between the target and the source [2]. This is practically performed by narrowing the domain shift between the target and source either in input space [19], feature space [6, 11,32,42,51] ,or output space [33,43,49] ,generally using maximum mean discrepancy [15,46] or adversarial learning [14]. The success of methods operating on feature representations motivates us to optimize the semantic feature space for domain generalization in this paper.
领域适应的核心主题是通过源错误加上目标与源之间的差异度量来界定目标错误 [2]。这通常通过缩小目标和源之间的领域偏移来实现，无论是在输入空间 [19]、特征空间 [6, 11,32,42,51]，还是输出空间 [33,43,49]，通常使用最大均值差异 [15,46] 或对抗学习 [14]。在特征表示上运作的方法的成功激励我们在本文中优化语义特征空间以实现领域泛化。
Domain generalization aims to generalize models to unseen domains without knowledge about the target distribution during training. Different methods have been proposed for learning generalizable and transferable representations. A promising direction is to extract task-specific but domain-invariant features [12,28,30,34,35] . Muandet et al. [35] propose a domain-invariant component analysis method with a kernel-based optimization algorithm to minimize the dissimilarity across domains. Ghifary et al. [12] learn multi-task auto-encoders to extract invariant features which are robust to domain variations. Li et al. [30] consider the conditional distribution of label space over input space, and minimize discrepancy of a joint distribution. Motiian et al. [34] use contrastive loss to guide samples from the same class being embedded nearby in latent space across data sources. Li et al. [28] extend adversarial autoencoders by imposing maximum mean discrepancy measure to align multi-domain distributions. Instead of harmonizing the feature space, others use low-rank parameterized CNNs [25] or decompose network parameters to domain-specific/-invariant components [22]. Data augmentation strategies, such as gradient-based domain perturbation [47] or adversarially perturbed samples [53] demonstrate effectiveness for model generalization. A recent method with state-of-the-art performance is JiGen [3], which leverages self-supervised signals by solving jigsaw puzzles.
域泛化旨在将模型推广到未见过的领域，而在训练过程中对目标分布没有任何知识。已经提出了不同的方法来学习可泛化和可转移的表示。一种有前景的方向是提取任务特定但与域无关的特征 [12,28,30,34,35]。Muandet 等人 [35] 提出了一种域不变成分分析方法，结合基于核的优化算法，以最小化不同域之间的差异。Ghifary 等人 [12] 学习多任务自编码器，以提取对域变化具有鲁棒性的无变特征。Li 等人 [30] 考虑输入空间上标签空间的条件分布，并最小化联合分布的差异。Motiian 等人 [34] 使用对比损失来指导来自同一类别的样本在潜在空间中相互靠近。Li 等人 [28] 通过施加最大均值差异度量来扩展对抗自编码器，以对齐多域分布。除了协调特征空间外，其他方法使用低秩参数化的卷积神经网络 [25] 或将网络参数分解为域特定/无关的成分 [22]。数据增强策略，如基于梯度的域扰动 [47] 或对抗性扰动样本 [53]，在模型泛化中表现出有效性。最近一种具有最先进性能的方法是 JiGen [3]，它通过解决拼图来利用自监督信号。
Meta-learning (a.k.a. learning to learn [44,48] ) is a long standing topic exploring the training of a meta-learner that learns how to train particular models [10,29,36,37]. Recently, gradient-based meta-learning methods [10,36] have been successfully applied to few-shot learning,with a procedure purely leveraging gradient descent. The episodic training paradigm, originated from model-agnostic meta-learning (MAML) [10], has been introduced to address domain generalization [1, 26, 27, 31]. Epi-FCR [27] alternates domain-specific feature extractors and classifiers across domains via episodic training, but without using inner gradient descent update. The method of MLDG [26] closely follows the update rule of MAML, back-propagating the gradients from an ordinary task loss on meta-test data. A limitation is that using the task objective might be sub-optimal, as it is highly abstracted from the feature representations (only using class probabilities). Moreover, it may not well fit the scenario where target data are unavailable (as pointed out by Balaji et al. [1]). A recent method, MetaReg [1],learns a regularization function (e.g.,weighted L1 loss) particularly for the network’s classification layer, excluding the feature extractor. Instead, Li et al. [31] propose a feature-critic network which learns an auxiliary meta loss (producing a non-negative scalar) depending on output of the feature extractor. Both [1] and [31] lack notable guidance from semantics of feature space, which may contain crucial domain-independent 'general knowledge' for model generalization. Our method is orthogonal to previous work, proposing to enforce semantic features via global class alignment and local sample clustering, with losses explicitly derived in an episodic learning procedure.
元学习（即学习如何学习 [44,48]）是一个长期存在的话题，探讨如何训练一个元学习者，使其能够学习如何训练特定模型 [10,29,36,37]。最近，基于梯度的元学习方法 [10,36] 已成功应用于少样本学习，整个过程纯粹依赖于梯度下降。源自模型无关元学习（MAML）[10] 的情节训练范式已被引入以解决领域泛化问题 [1, 26, 27, 31]。Epi-FCR [27] 通过情节训练在不同领域之间交替使用特定领域的特征提取器和分类器，但没有使用内部梯度下降更新。MLDG [26] 的方法紧密遵循 MAML 的更新规则，从元测试数据上的普通任务损失反向传播梯度。一个局限性是，使用任务目标可能不是最优的，因为它与特征表示高度抽象（仅使用类别概率）。此外，它可能不适合目标数据不可用的情况（正如 Balaji 等人所指出的 [1]）。最近的一种方法，MetaReg [1]，为网络的分类层学习一个正则化函数（例如，加权 L1 损失），而不包括特征提取器。相反，Li 等人 [31] 提出了一个特征-评论网络，该网络学习一个辅助元损失（产生一个非负标量），依赖于特征提取器的输出。[1] 和 [31] 都缺乏来自特征空间语义的显著指导，而特征空间可能包含对模型泛化至关重要的领域无关的“通用知识”。我们的方法与之前的工作正交，提出通过全局类别对齐和局部样本聚类来强化语义特征，损失在情节学习过程中明确推导。

3 Method
3 方法

In the following,we denote input and label spaces by X and Y ,the domains D={D1,D2,…,DK} are different distributions on the joint space X×Y . Since domain generalization involves a common predictive task, the label space is shared by all domains. In each domain, samples are drawn from a dataset Dk={(xn(k),yn(k))}n=1Nk where Nk is the number of labeled data points in the k -th domain. The domain generalization (DG) setting further assumes the existence of domain-invariant patterns in the inputs (e.g. semantic features), which can be extracted to learn a label predictor that performs well across seen and unseen domains. Unlike domain adaptation, DG assumes no access to observations from or explicit knowledge about the target distribution.
在以下内容中，我们用 X 和 Y 表示输入和标签空间，领域 D={D1,D2,…,DK} 是联合空间 X×Y 上的不同分布。由于领域泛化涉及一个共同的预测任务，标签空间被所有领域共享。在每个领域中，样本来自数据集 Dk={(xn(k),yn(k))}n=1Nk，其中 Nk 是第 k 个领域中标记数据点的数量。领域泛化（DG）设置进一步假设输入中存在领域不变的模式（例如语义特征），这些模式可以被提取以学习一个在已见和未见领域中表现良好的标签预测器。与领域适应不同，DG 假设无法访问目标分布的观察数据或明确知识。
In this work,we consider a classification model composed of a feature extractor, Fψ:X→Z ,where Z is a feature space (typically much lower-dimensional than X ),and a task network, Tθ:Z→RC , where C is the number of classes in Y . The final class predictions are given by p(y∣x;ψ,θ)=y^= softmax(Tθ(Fψ(x))) ,where softmax(a)=ea/∑r⁡ear.1 The parameters (ψ,θ) are optimized with respect to a task-specific loss Ltask  ,e.g. cross-entropy: ℓtask (y,y^)=−∑c⁡1[y=c]log⁡y^c .
在这项工作中，我们考虑一个由特征提取器组成的分类模型 Fψ:X→Z ，其中 Z 是一个特征空间（通常维度远低于 X ），以及一个任务网络 Tθ:Z→RC ，其中 C 是 Y 中的类别数量。最终的类别预测由 p(y∣x;ψ,θ)=y^= softmax(Tθ(Fψ(x))) 给出，其中 softmax(a)=ea/∑r⁡ear.1 参数 (ψ,θ) 是相对于特定任务损失 Ltask  进行优化的，例如交叉熵：ℓtask (y,y^)=−∑c⁡1[y=c]log⁡y^c 。
Although the minimization of Ltask  may produce highly discriminative features z=Fψ(x) ,and hence an excellent predictor for data from the training domains, nothing in this process prevents the model from overfitting to the source domains and suffering from degradation on unseen test domains. We therefore propose to optimize the feature space such that its semantic structure is insensitive to different training domains, and generalize better to new unseen domains. 3.1 Model-Agnostic Learning with Episodic Training
3.1 使用情节训练的模型无关学习

In line with our goal of domain generalization, the model is trained on a sequence of simulated episodes with domain shift. Specifically,at each iteration,the available domains D are randomly split into sets of meta-train Dtr  and meta-test Dte  domains. The model is trained to semantically perform well on held-out Dte  after being optimized with one or more steps of gradient descent with Dtr  domains. In other words, parameters are updated such that future updates with given source domains also improve the model regarding some generalizable aspects on unseen target domains.
量化这些属性的目标函数 Lmeta  是基于更新后的参数计算的 (ψ′,θ′)，并且梯度是相对于原始参数计算的 (ψ,θ)。直观地说，除了任务本身，训练过程还在学习如何在领域转移下进行泛化。换句话说，参数被更新，以便在给定源领域的未来更新中，也能改善模型在未见目标领域的一些可泛化方面。
In particular, we desire the feature space to encode semantically relevant properties: features from different domains should respect inter-class relationships, and they should be compactly clustered by class labels regardless of domains (cf. Alg. 1). In the remainder of this section we describe the design of our semantic meta-objective, Lmeta =β1Lglobal +β2Llocal  ,composed of a global class alignment term and a local sample clustering term,with weighting coefficients β1,β2>0 .
特别是，我们希望特征空间能够编码语义相关的属性：来自不同领域的特征应尊重类间关系，并且无论领域如何，它们应根据类标签紧凑地聚类（参见算法 1）。在本节的其余部分，我们描述我们的语义元目标的设计 Lmeta =β1Lglobal +β2Llocal ，该目标由全局类对齐项和局部样本聚类项组成，权重系数为 β1,β2>0。

3.2 Global Class Alignment Objective
3.2 全局类对齐目标

Relationships between class concepts exist in purely semantic space, independent of changes in the observation domain. In light of this, compared with individual hard label prediction, aligning class relationships can promote more transferable knowledge towards model generalization. This is also noted by Tzeng et al. [50] in the context of domain adaptation, by aggregating the output probability distribution when fine-tuning the model on a few labelled target data. In contrast to their work, our goal is to structure the feature space itself to preserve learned class relationships on unseen data, by means of explicit regularization.
类概念之间的关系存在于纯语义空间中，与观察领域的变化无关。考虑到这一点，与单独的硬标签预测相比，对齐类关系可以促进更可转移的知识，从而实现模型泛化。Tzeng 等人 [50] 在领域适应的背景下指出，通过在少量标记目标数据上微调模型时聚合输出概率分布，可以实现这一点。与他们的工作相比，我们的目标是通过显式正则化来构建特征空间本身，以保持在未见数据上学习到的类关系。
Specifically, we formulate this objective in a manner that imposes a global layout of extracted features, such that the relative locations of features from different classes embody the inherent similarity in semantic structures. Inspired by knowledge distillation from neural networks [18], we exploit what the model has learned about class ambiguities-in the form of per-class soft labels- and enforce them to be consistent between Dtr  and Dte  domains. For each domain k ,we summarize the model’s current ’concept’ of each class c by computing the class-specific mean feature vectors z―c(k) :
具体来说，我们以一种方式制定这一目标，强加提取特征的全局布局，使得来自不同类别的特征的相对位置体现语义结构的内在相似性。受到神经网络知识蒸馏的启发 [18]，我们利用模型对类别模糊性的学习——以每类软标签的形式——并强制它们在 Dtr  和 Dte  域之间保持一致。对于每个域 k，我们通过计算特定类别的均值特征向量 z―c(k) 来总结模型当前对每个类别的“概念” c：
(2)z―c(k)=1Nk(c)∑n:yn(k)=c⁡Fψ′(xn(k))≈EDk[Fψ′(x)∣y=c],
where Nk(c) is the number of samples in domain Dk labelled as class c . The obtained z―c(k) conveys how samples from a particular class are generally represented. It is then forwarded to the task network Tθ′ ,for computing soft label distributions sc(k) with a ’softened’ softmax at temperature τ>1 [18]:
其中 Nk(c) 是标记为类别 c 的域 Dk 中样本的数量。获得的 z―c(k) 表示特定类别样本的一般表示方式。然后将其转发到任务网络 Tθ′，以计算具有温度 τ>1 的“软化”softmax的软标签分布 sc(k) [18]：
(3)sc(k)=softmax(Tθ′(z―c(k))/τ).
The collection of soft labels [sc(k)]c=1C represents a kind of ’soft confusion matrix’ associated with a particular domain, encoding the inter-class relationships learned by the model. Such relationships should be preserved as general semantics on meta-test after updating the classification model on meta-train (e.g., cartoon dogs are more easily misclassified as horses than as houses, which likely holds in unseen domains). Standard supervised training with Ltask  focuses only on the dominant hard label prediction, there is no reason a priori for consistency of such inter-class alignment. We therefore propose to align the soft class confusion matrix between two domains Di∈Dtr and Dj∈Dte ,by minimising their symmetrized Kullback-Leibler (KL) divergence,averaged over all C classes:
软标签的集合 [sc(k)]c=1C 表示与特定域相关的一种“软混淆矩阵”，编码模型学习到的类间关系。这些关系在更新分类模型后应作为元测试上的一般语义得以保留（例如，卡通狗更容易被错误分类为马而不是房子，这在未见过的域中可能也成立）。标准的监督训练 Ltask  仅关注主导的硬标签预测，因此没有理由事先保证这种类间对齐的一致性。因此，我们建议通过最小化两个域 Di∈Dtr 和 Dj∈Dte 之间的对称化Kullback-Leibler (KL) 散度来对齐软类混淆矩阵，该散度在所有 C 类上进行平均：
(4)ℓglobal (Di,Dj;ψ′,θ′)=1C∑c=1C⁡12[DKL(sc(i)∥sc(j))+DKL(sc(j)∥sc(i))],
where DKL(p∥q)=∑r⁡prlog⁡prqr . Finally,the global class alignment loss, Lglobal (Dtr ,Dte ;ψ′,θ′) ,is calculated as the average of ℓglobal (Di,Dj;ψ′,θ′) over all pairs of available meta-train and meta-test domains, (Di,Dj)∈Dtr ×Dte  (cf. Alg. 1). The complexity of this computation is not problematic in practice, since the number of domains selected in a training mini-batch is limited (as with the form in MAML [10]),and in our experiments we took |Dtr|=2 and |Dte|=1 .
在这里 DKL(p∥q)=∑r⁡prlog⁡prqr 。其他对称散度，如詹森-香农（JS），也可以考虑，尽管我们的初步实验显示与对称KL相比，JS没有显著差异。最后，全局类别对齐损失 Lglobal (Dtr ,Dte ;ψ′,θ′) 被计算为所有可用的元训练和元测试域的 ℓglobal (Di,Dj;ψ′,θ′) 的平均值 (Di,Dj)∈Dtr ×Dte  （参见算法1）。在实践中，这个计算的复杂性并不成问题，因为在训练小批量中选择的域数量是有限的（与MAML [10]中的形式相同），在我们的实验中我们采用了 |Dtr|=2 和 |Dte|=1 。

3.3 Local Sample Clustering Objective
3.3 本地样本聚类目标

In addition to promoting the alignment of class relationships across domains with Lglobal  as defined above, we further encourage robust semantic features that locally cluster according to class regardless of the domain. This is crucial,as neither of the class-prediction-based losses (Ltask  or Lglobal  ) ensure that features of samples in the same class will lie close to each other and away from those of different classes, a.k.a. feature compactness [21]. If the model cannot project the inputs to the semantic feature clusters with domain-independent class-specific cohesion and separation, the predictions may suffer from ambiguous decision boundaries, and still be sensitive to unseen kinds of domain shift. We therefore propose a local regularization objective Llocal  to boost robustness,by increasing the compactness of class-specific clusters while reducing their overlap. Note how this is complementary to the global class alignment of semantically structuring the relative locations among class clusters.
除了促进跨域类别关系的对齐，使用上述定义的 Lglobal  外，我们进一步鼓励根据类别进行本地聚类的稳健语义特征，而不考虑域。这一点至关重要，因为基于类别预测的损失 (Ltask  或 Lglobal  并不能确保同一类别的样本特征彼此接近，而与不同类别的样本特征保持距离，即特征紧凑性 [21]。如果模型无法将输入投影到具有域独立类别特定凝聚力和分离性的语义特征聚类中，预测可能会受到模糊决策边界的影响，并且仍然对未见过的域转移类型敏感。因此，我们提出了一种本地正则化目标 Llocal  来增强稳健性，通过增加类别特定聚类的紧凑性，同时减少它们的重叠。请注意，这与全局类别对齐在语义上构建类别聚类之间相对位置的方式是互补的。
Our preliminary experiments revealed that applying such regularization explicitly onto the features may constrain the optimization for Ltask  and Lglobal  too heavily,hurting generalization performance on unseen domain. We thus take a metric-learning approach,introducing an embedding network Mϕ that operates on the extracted features, z=Fψ′(x) . This component represents a learnable distance function [5] between feature vectors (rather than between raw inputs):
我们的初步实验显示，将这种正则化显式应用于特征可能会对 Ltask  和 Lglobal  的优化施加过重的限制，从而损害在未见领域上的泛化性能。因此，我们采用了一种度量学习的方法，引入了一个嵌入网络 Mϕ，该网络在提取的特征上进行操作 z=Fψ′(x)。这个组件表示了一个可学习的距离函数 [5]，用于特征向量之间（而不是原始输入之间）的比较：
(5)dϕ(zn,zm)=‖en−em‖2=‖Mϕ(zn)−Mϕ(zm)‖2.
The sample pairs(n,m)are randomly drawn from all source domains D ,because we expect the updated Fψ′ will harmonize the semantic feature space of Dte  with that of Dtr  ,in terms of class-specific clustering regardless of domains. The computed embeddings, e=Mϕ(z) ,can then be optimized with any suitable metric-learning loss Llocal (D;ψ′,ϕ) to regularize the local sample clustering. Under mild domain shift, the contrastive loss [16] is a sensible choice, as it attempts to separately collapse each group of same-class exemplars to a distinct single point. It might however be over-restrictive for more extreme situations, wherein domains are related rather semantically, but with wildly distinct low-level statistics. For such cases, we propose instead to employ the triplet loss [45].
样本对 (n,m) 是从所有源领域 D 中随机抽取的，因为我们期望更新后的 Fψ′ 将在类特定聚类方面使 Dte  的语义特征空间与 Dtr  的语义特征空间协调一致，而不考虑领域的差异。计算得到的嵌入 e=Mϕ(z) 可以使用任何合适的度量学习损失 Llocal (D;ψ′,ϕ) 进行优化，以规范化局部样本聚类。在轻微的领域偏移下，对比损失 [16] 是一个合理的选择，因为它试图将同类样本的每个组单独收缩到一个独特的单点。然而，对于更极端的情况，这可能会过于限制，其中领域在语义上相关，但具有截然不同的低级统计特征。对于这种情况，我们建议改用三元组损失 [45]。
Contrastive loss is computed for pairs of samples, attracting samples of the same class and repelling samples of different classes [16]. The total loss for a training mini-batch, Llocal  ,is normally averaged over all pairs of samples. In cases where full O(N2) enumeration is intractable-e.g. image segmentation,which would involve all pairs of pixels in all images-we can obtain an unbiased O(N) estimator of the loss by e.g. shuffling the samples and iterating over(2i - 1,2i)pairs with i=1,…,⌊N/2⌋ .
训练小批量的总损失 Llocal  通常在所有样本对上进行平均。在全 O(N2) 枚举不可行的情况下，例如图像分割，这将涉及所有图像中所有像素对，我们可以通过例如打乱样本并迭代 (2i - 1, 2i) 对来获得损失的无偏 O(N) 估计。

Schroff et al. [45] argue that judicious triplet selection is essential for good convergence, as many triplets may already satisfy this constraint and others may be too hard to contribute meaningfully to the learning process. Here we adopt their proposed online 'semi-hard' triplet mining strategy, and Llocal  is the average over all selected triplet pairs.

4 Experiments
4 实验

We evaluate and compare our method on three datasets: 1) the classic VLCS domain generalization benchmark for image classification, 2) the recently introduced PACS benchmark for object recognition with challenging domain shift, 3) a real-world medical imaging task of tissue segmentation in brain MRI. Results with an in-depth analysis and ablation study are presented in the following.

4.1 VLCS Dataset
4.1 VLCS 数据集

VLCS [8] is a classic benchmark for domain generalization, which includes images from four datasets: PASCAL VOC2007 (V) [7], LabelMe (L) [41], Caltech (C) [9], and SUN09 (S) [4]. The multi-class object recognition task includes five classes: bird, car, chair, dog and person. We follow previous work [3,27,34] of using the publicly available pre-extracted DeCAF6 features (4096-dimensional vector) for leave-one-domain-out validation with randomly dividing each domain into 70% training and 30% test,inputting to two fully connected layers with output size of 1024 and 128 with ReLU activation. For our metric embedding Mϕ (inputting the 128-dimensional vector),we use two fully connected layers with output size of 128 and 64 . The triplet loss is adopted for computing Llocal  ,with coefficient β2=0.005 ,such that it is in a similar scale to Ltask  and Lglobal (β1=1) . We use the Adam optimizer [23] with η initialized to 10−3 and exponentially decayed by 2% every 1k iterations. For the inner optimization to obtain (ψ′,θ′) ,we clip the gradients by norm (threshold by 2.0) to prevent them from exploding, since this step uses plain, non-adaptive gradient descent (with learning rate α=10−5 ). Note that,although performing gradient descent on Lmeta  involves second-order gradients on (ψ,θ) ,their computation does not incur a substantial overhead in training time [10]. We also employ an Adam optimizer for the meta-updates of ϕ with learning rate γ=10−5 without decay. The batch size is 128 for each source domain, with an Nvidia TITAN Xp 12 GB GPU. The metric-learning margin hyperparameter ξ was chosen heuristically based on observing the distances within and between the clusters of class features. For our results, we report the average and standard deviation over three independent runs.

4.3 Tissue Segmentation in Multi-site Brain MRI
4.3 多站点脑 MRI 中的组织分割

We evaluate our method on a real-world medical imaging task of brain tissue segmentation in T1- weighted MRI. Data was acquired from four clinical centers (denoted as Set-A/B/C/D). Domain shift occurs due to differences in scanners, acquisition protocols and many other factors, posing severe limitations for translating learning-based methods to clinical practice [13]. Figure 4 shows example images and intensity histograms. We adapt MASF for the segmentation of four classes: background, grey matter (GM), white matter (WM), cerebrospinal fluid (CSF). We employ a U-Net [38],commonly used for this task. For Lglobal  ,the z―c(k) is computed by averaging over all pixels of a class. Our metric-embedding has two layers of 1×1 convolutions,with contrastive loss for Llocal  . We randomly split each domain to 80% for training and 20% for testing in experimental settings.
我们在 T1 加权 MRI 的脑组织分割这一真实世界医学影像任务上评估我们的方法。数据来自四个临床中心（标记为 Set-A/B/C/D）。由于扫描仪、采集协议及许多其他因素的差异，领域转移发生，这对将基于学习的方法转化为临床实践造成了严重限制 [13]。图 4 显示了示例图像和强度直方图。我们调整 MASF 以分割四个类别：背景、灰质（GM）、白质（WM）、脑脊液（CSF）。我们采用 U-Net [38]，该网络通常用于此任务。对于 Lglobal ，通过对一个类别的所有像素进行平均来计算 z―c(k)。我们的度量嵌入有两层 1×1 卷积，并使用对比损失来优化 Llocal 。我们随机将每个领域划分为 80% 用于训练和 20% 用于测试的实验设置。

5 Conclusions
5 结论

We have presented promising results for a new approach to domain generalization of predictive models by incorporating global and local constraints for learning semantic feature spaces. The better generalization capability is demonstrated by new state-of-the-art results on popular benchmarks and a dense classification task (i.e., semantic segmentation) for medical images. The proposed loss functions are generally orthogonal to other algorithms, and evaluating the benefit of their integration is an appealing future direction. Our learning procedure could also be interesting to explore in the context of generative models, which may greatly benefit from semantic guidance when learning low-dimensional data representations from multiple sources.
我们提出了一种新方法，通过结合全局和局部约束来进行预测模型的领域泛化，取得了令人鼓舞的结果。更好的泛化能力通过在流行基准和密集分类任务（即语义分割）上取得的新最先进结果得以证明，尤其是在医学图像方面。所提出的损失函数通常与其他算法正交，评估其整合的益处是一个有吸引力的未来方向。我们的学习过程在生成模型的背景下也可能值得探索，这可能在从多个来源学习低维数据表示时大大受益于语义指导。
