1.1 Problem Background

1.1 问题背景

Illegal wildlife trade has evolved into a major crisis of our time, with investigations revealing that it ranks second only to habitat destruction as a comprehensive threat to species survival. [1] This illicit trade not only poses a severe threat to global biodiversity but also causes significant damage to ecosystems and cultural environments worldwide. Concurrently, relevant studies suggest that the accelerated decline in the population of wild animals will have enduring adverse effects on local communities, exacerbating poverty and inequality, leading to instability, fostering conflict, and threatening national security. [2]

非法野生动物贸易已演变成我们时代的一场重大危机，调查显示其仅次于栖息地破坏，成为威胁物种生存的第二大综合性因素。[1] 这种非法贸易不仅对全球生物多样性构成严重威胁，还对全球生态系统和文化环境造成重大破坏。同时，相关研究表明，野生动物种群的加速减少将对当地社区产生持久的不利影响，加剧贫困和不平等，导致社会不稳定，激化冲突，并威胁国家安全。[2]

In the contemporary context, the illegal trade of wildlife products like ivory, pangolins, rhinoceros horns, tiger bones, bear bile, and redwood persists, posing a significant threat to wildlife conservation. This illicit trade not only endangers individual species but also disrupts ecosystems. From the poaching of elephants for ivory to the trafficking of pangolins and the extraction of rhinoceros horns, each element contributes to a complex set of challenges. Addressing these issues necessitates a comprehensive approach, emphasizing global collaboration, awareness, and sustainable conservation efforts. [3]

在当代背景下，象牙、穿山甲、犀牛角、虎骨、熊胆和红木等野生动物制品的非法贸易依然存在，对野生动物保护构成重大威胁。这种非法贸易不仅危及单一物种，还扰乱生态系统。从象牙盗猎到穿山甲走私，再到犀牛角的提取，每一环节都带来复杂的挑战。解决这些问题需要综合方法，强调全球合作、提高意识和可持续保护努力。[3]

To effectively reduce the adverse environmental impact of illegal wildlife trade, it's crucial to unite efforts and take decisive actions. This includes strengthening law enforcement, fostering international collaboration, engaging local communities, and promoting sustainable alternatives. By bringing together governments, conservation organizations, and the public, we can create a powerful front against illegal wildlife trade, safeguarding endangered species, preserving ecosystems, and promoting the overall well-being of our planet.

为了有效减少非法野生动物贸易对环境的负面影响，关键在于团结力量并采取果断行动。这包括加强执法、促进国际合作、动员地方社区以及推广可持续替代方案。通过汇聚政府、保护组织和公众的力量，我们能够形成打击非法野生动物贸易的强大合力，保护濒危物种，维护生态系统，并促进地球整体福祉。
