Abstract
摘要

Domain generalization (DG) methods aim to achieve generalizability to an unseen target domain by using only training data from the source domains. Although a variety of DG methods have been proposed, a recent study shows that under a fair evaluation protocol, called DomainBed, the simple empirical risk minimization (ERM) approach works comparable to or even outperforms previous methods. Unfortunately, simply solving ERM on a complex, non-convex loss function can easily lead to sub-optimal generalizability by seeking sharp minima. In this paper, we theoretically show that finding flat minima results in a smaller domain generalization gap. We also propose a simple yet effective method, named Stochastic Weight Averaging Densely (SWAD), to find flat minima. SWAD finds flatter minima and suffers less from overfitting than does the vanilla SWA by a dense and overfit-aware stochastic weight sampling strategy. SWAD shows state-of-the-art performances on five DG benchmarks, namely PACS, VLCS, OfficeHome, TerraIncognita, and DomainNet,with consistent and large margins of +1.6% averagely on out-of-domain accuracy. We also compare SWAD with conventional generalization methods, such as data augmentation and consistency regularization methods, to verify that the remarkable performance improvements are originated from by seeking flat minima, not from better in-domain generalizability. Last but not least, SWAD is readily adaptable to existing DG methods without modification; the combination of SWAD and an existing DG method further improves DG performances. Source code is available at https://github.com/khanrc/swad

1 Introduction
1 引言

Independent and identically distributed (i.i.d.) condition is the underlying assumption of machine learning experiments. However, this assumption may not hold in real-world scenarios, i.e., the training and the test data distribution may differ significantly by distribution shifts. For example, a self-driving car should adapt to adverse weather or day-to-night shifts [1, 2]. Even in a simple image recognition scenario, systems rely on wrong cues for their prediction, e.g., geographic distribution [3], demographic statistics [4], texture [5], or backgrounds [6]. Consequently, a practical system should require generalizability to distribution shift, which is yet often failed by traditional approaches.
独立同分布（i.i.d.）条件是机器学习实验的基本假设。然而，这一假设在现实世界场景中可能不成立，即训练数据和测试数据的分布可能因分布变化而显著不同。例如，自驾车应能适应恶劣天气或昼夜变化 [1, 2]。即使在简单的图像识别场景中，系统也依赖于错误的线索进行预测，例如，地理分布 [3]、人口统计统计 [4]、纹理 [5] 或背景 [6]。因此，实际系统应具备对分布变化的泛化能力，而传统方法往往未能做到这一点。

Unfortunately, although ERM showed surprising empirical success on DomainBed, simply minimizing the empirical loss on a complex and non-convex loss landscape is typically not sufficient to arrive at a good generalization [23-26]. In particular, the connection between the generalization gap and the flatness of loss landscapes has been actively discussed under the i.i.d. condition [23-28]. Izmailov et al. [25] argued that seeking flat minima will lead to robustness against the loss landscape shift between training and test datasets, while a simple ERM converges to the boundary of a wide flat minimum and achieves insufficient generalization. In the DG scenario, because training and test loss landscapes differ more drastically due to the domain shift, we conjecture that the generalization gap between flat and sharp minima is larger than expected in the i.i.d. scenario.
不幸的是，尽管 ERM 在 DomainBed 上表现出惊人的经验成功，但仅仅在复杂且非凸的损失景观上最小化经验损失通常不足以实现良好的泛化 [23-26]。特别是，泛化间隙与损失景观的平坦性之间的关系在独立同分布 (i.i.d.) 条件下得到了积极讨论 [23-28]。Izmailov 等人 [25] 认为，寻求平坦的极小值将导致对训练和测试数据集之间损失景观变化的鲁棒性，而简单的 ERM 收敛于宽平坦极小值的边界，从而实现不足的泛化。在 DG 场景中，由于领域转移导致训练和测试损失景观的差异更为显著，我们推测平坦极小值与尖锐极小值之间的泛化间隙比在 i.i.d. 场景中预期的要大。
To show that flatter minima generalize better to unseen domains, we formulate a robust risk minimization (RRM) problem defined by the worst-case empirical risks within neighborhoods in parameter space [26, 34]. We theoretically show that the generalization gap of DG, i.e., the error on the target domain, is upper bounded by RRM, i.e., a flat optimal solution. Based on our theoretical observation, we modify stochastic weight averaging (SWA) [25], one of the popular existing flatness-aware solvers, by introducing a dense and overfit-aware stochastic weight sampling strategy. First, we suggest to sample weights densely, i.e., for every iteration. Also, we search the start and end iterations for averaging by considering the validation loss to avoid overfitting. We empirically show that the proposed Stochastic Weight Averaging Densely (SWAD) finds flatter minima than the vanilla SWA does, resulting in better generalization to unseen domains.
为了表明平坦的极小值在未见领域中具有更好的泛化能力，我们提出了一个由参数空间内邻域的最坏情况经验风险定义的稳健风险最小化（RRM）问题 [26, 34]。我们从理论上证明了领域泛化（DG）的泛化差距，即目标领域的误差，被RRM上界，即一个平坦的最优解。基于我们的理论观察，我们修改了随机权重平均（SWA） [25]，这是现有流行的平坦性感知求解器之一，通过引入一种密集且关注过拟合的随机权重采样策略。首先，我们建议密集采样权重，即在每次迭代中进行采样。此外，我们通过考虑验证损失来搜索平均的起始和结束迭代，以避免过拟合。我们通过实验证明，所提出的密集随机权重平均（SWAD）找到的平坦极小值比传统的SWA更平坦，从而在未见领域中实现更好的泛化能力。
Contribution. Our main contribution is introducing flatness into DG, and showing remarkably outperforming performances against existing DG methods. As shown in Table II our SWAD improves the average DG performances by 3.6pp against the ERM baseline and 1.6pp against the existing best methods. Furthermore,by combining SWAD and previous SOTA [31], we even achieve 0.4pp improvements against the vanilla SWAD results. We also empirically show that while popular in-domain generalization methods without considering flatness, e.g., Mixup [35] or CutMix [36], are not effective to out-of-domain generalization (Table 3), flatness-aware methods, e.g., SWA [25] or SAM [26], are only effective methods to both in-domain and out-of-domain generalization.
贡献。我们的主要贡献是将平坦性引入领域泛化（DG），并展示其在性能上显著优于现有的DG方法。如表II所示，我们的SWAD在与经验风险最小化（ERM）基线相比时，平均DG性能提高了3.6个百分点，与现有最佳方法相比提高了1.6个百分点。此外，通过结合SWAD和之前的最先进技术（SOTA） [31]，我们甚至在传统SWAD结果的基础上实现了0.4个百分点的提升。我们还通过实验证明，尽管不考虑平坦性的流行领域内泛化方法，如Mixup [35]或CutMix [36]，在领域外泛化中并不有效（表3），而关注平坦性的方法，如SWA [25]或SAM [26]，是领域内和领域外泛化的唯一有效方法。

2 A Theoretical Relationship between Flatness and Domain Generalization
2 平坦性与领域泛化之间的理论关系

Let D:={Di}iI be a set of training domains,where Di is a distribution over input space X ,and I is the total number of domains. From each domain,we observe n training data points which consist of input x and target label y,(xji,yji)j=1n∼Di . We also define a set of target domain T:={Ti}iT similarly,where the number of target domains T is usually set to one. For the sake of simplicity, unlike Ben-David et al. [37],we assume that there exists a global labeling function h(x) that generates target label for multiple domains,i.e., yji=h(xji) for all i and j . Domain generalization (DG) aims to find a model parameter θ∈Θ which generalizes well over both multiple training domains D and unseen target domain T . More specifically,let us consider a bounded instance loss function ℓ:Y×Y→[0,c] ,such that ℓ(y1,y2)=0 holds if and only if y1=y2 where Y is a set of labels. For simplicity,we set c to one in our proofs,but we note that ℓ(⋅,⋅) can be generalized for any bounded loss function. Then, we can define a population loss over multiple domains by ED(θ)=1I∑i=1I⁡Exi∼Di[ℓ(f(xi;θ),yi)] ,where f(⋅;θ) is a model parameterized by θ . Formally, the goal of DG is to find a model which minimizes both ED(θ) and ET(θ) by only minimizing an empirical risk E^D(θ):=1In∑i=1I⁡∑j=1n⁡ℓ(f(xi;θ),yi) ) over training domains D .
设 D:={Di}iI 为一组训练域，其中 Di 是输入空间 X 上的分布，而 I 是域的总数。我们从每个域观察到 n 个训练数据点，这些数据点由输入 x 和目标标签 y,(xji,yji)j=1n∼Di 组成。我们还类似地定义了一组目标域 T:={Ti}iT，其中目标域的数量 T 通常设为一个。为了简化起见，与 Ben-David 等人 [37] 不同，我们假设存在一个全局标记函数 h(x)，该函数为多个域生成目标标签，即 yji=h(xji) 对于所有 i 和 j 都成立。领域泛化（DG）的目标是找到一个模型参数 θ∈Θ，该参数在多个训练域 D 和未见目标域 T 上都能很好地泛化。更具体地说，我们考虑一个有界实例损失函数 ℓ:Y×Y→[0,c]，使得 ℓ(y1,y2)=0 当且仅当 y1=y2 成立，其中 Y 是一组标签。为了简化，我们在证明中将 c 设为一，但我们注意到 ℓ(⋅,⋅) 可以推广到任何有界损失函数。然后，我们可以通过 ED(θ)=1I∑i=1I⁡Exi∼Di[ℓ(f(xi;θ),yi)] 定义多个域上的总体损失，其中 f(⋅;θ) 是一个由 θ 参数化的模型。正式地，DG 的目标是找到一个模型，通过仅最小化训练域 D 上的经验风险 E^D(θ):=1In∑i=1I⁡∑j=1n⁡ℓ(f(xi;θ),yi) 来最小化 ED(θ) 和 ET(θ)。
In practice,ERM,i.e., arg⁡minθ⁡E^D(θ) ,can have multiple solutions that provide similar values of the training losses but significantly different generalizability on ED(θ) and ET(θ) . Unfortunately,the typical optimization methods, such as SGD and Adam [38], often lead sub-optimal generalizability as finding sharp and narrow minima even under the i.i.d. assumption [23-28]. In the DG scenario, the generalization gap between empirical loss and target domain loss becomes even worse due to domain shift. Here, we provide a theoretical interpretation of the relationship between finding a flat minimum and minimizing the domain generalization gap, inspired by previous studies [23-28].
在实践中，ERM，即 arg⁡minθ⁡E^D(θ)，可能有多个解决方案，这些解决方案提供相似的训练损失值，但在 ED(θ) 和 ET(θ) 上的泛化能力却显著不同。不幸的是，典型的优化方法，如 SGD 和 Adam [38]，通常导致次优的泛化能力，因为即使在独立同分布假设下 [23-28]，也会找到尖锐而狭窄的极小值。在领域泛化（DG）场景中，由于领域转移，经验损失与目标领域损失之间的泛化差距变得更加严重。在这里，我们提供了一个理论解释，阐明了寻找平坦极小值与最小化领域泛化差距之间的关系，这一解释受到之前研究的启发 [23-28]。

Intuitively,if γ is sufficiently larger than the "radius" of a sharp optimum θs of E^D(θ),θs is no longer an optimum of E^Dγ(θ) as well as its neighborhoods within the γ -ball. 3 SWAD: Domain Generalization by Seeking Flat Minima
3 SWAD：通过寻求平坦最小值进行领域泛化

We have shown that flat minima will bring a better domain generalization. In this section, we propose Stochastic Weight Averaging Densely (SWAD) algorithm, and provide empirical quantitative and qualitative analyses on SWAD and flatness to understand why SWAD works better than ERM.
我们已经表明平坦最小值将带来更好的领域泛化。在本节中，我们提出了随机权重平均密集（SWAD）算法，并提供了关于 SWAD 和平坦性的实证定量和定性分析，以理解为什么 SWAD 的效果优于 ERM。

3.1 A baseline method: stochastic weight averaging
3.1 基线方法：随机权重平均

Since the importance of flatness in loss landscapes has emerged [23-28], several methods have been proposed to find flat minima [25, 26, 39]. We select stochastic weight averaging (SWA) [25] as a baseline, which finds flat minima by a weight ensemble approach. More specifically, SWA updates a pretrained model (namely,a model trained with sufficiently enough training epochs, K0 ) with a cyclical [40] or high constant learning rate scheduling. SWA gathers model parameters for every K epochs during the update and averages them for the model ensemble. SWA finds an ensembled solution of different local optima found by a sufficiently large learning rate to escape a local minimum. Izmailov et al. [25] empirically showed that SWA finds flatter minima than ERM. We also considered sharpness-aware minimization (SAM) [26], which is another popular flatness-aware solver, but SWA finds flatter minima than SAM (See Figure 3). We illustrate an overview of SWA in Figure 2a
自从平坦性在损失景观中的重要性被提出以来 [23-28]，已经提出了几种方法来寻找平坦的极小值 [25, 26, 39]。我们选择随机权重平均（SWA） [25] 作为基线，它通过权重集成方法找到平坦的极小值。更具体地说，SWA 使用周期性 [40] 或高恒定学习率调度来更新一个预训练模型（即，一个经过足够训练轮次的模型，K0）。SWA 在更新过程中为每个 K 轮次收集模型参数并对其进行平均以形成模型集成。SWA 通过足够大的学习率找到不同局部最优解的集成解，以逃离局部极小值。Izmailov 等人 [25] 实证表明，SWA 找到的平坦极小值比 ERM 更平坦。我们还考虑了对尖锐度敏感的最小化（SAM） [26]，这是一种流行的平坦性敏感求解器，但 S WA 找到的平坦极小值比 SAM 更平坦（见图 3）。我们在图 2a 中展示了 SWA 的概述。

3.2 Dense and overfit-aware stochastic weight sampling strategy
3.2 密集且关注过拟合的随机权重采样策略

Despite its advantages, directly applying SWA to DG task has two problems. First, SWA averages a few weights (usually less than ten) by sampling weights for every K epochs,results in an inaccurate approximation of flat minima on a high-dimensional parameter space (e.g., 23M for ResNet-50 [41]). Furthermore, a common DG benchmark protocol uses relatively small training epochs (e.g., Gulrajani and Lopez-Paz [22] trained with less than two epochs for DomainNet benchmark), resulting in insufficient stochastic weights for SWA. From this motivation, we propose a "dense" sampling strategy for gathering sufficiently enough stochastic weights.
尽管 SWA 有其优势，但直接将其应用于 DG 任务存在两个问题。首先，SWA 通过为每个 K 轮次采样权重来平均少量权重（通常少于十个），这导致在高维参数空间（例如，ResNet-50 的 23M [41]）上对平坦极小值的近似不准确。此外，常见的 DG 基准协议使用相对较少的训练轮次（例如，Gulrajani 和 Lopez-Paz [22] 在 DomainNet 基准上训练的轮次少于两个），导致 SWA 的随机权重不足。基于此动机，我们提出了一种“密集”采样策略，以收集足够的随机权重。
In addition,widely used DG datasets,such as PACS ( ≈10K images,7 classes) and VLCS ( ≈11K images, 5 classes), are relatively smaller than large-scale datasets, such as ImageNet [42] ( ≈1.2M images, 1K classes). In this case,we observe that a simple ERM approach is rapidly reached to a local optimum only within a few epochs, and easily suffers from the overfitting issue, i.e., the validation loss is increased after a few training epochs. It implies that directly applying the vanilla SWA will suffer from the overfitting issue by averaging sub-optimal solutions (i.e., overfitted parameters). Hence, we need an "overfit-aware" sampling scheduling to omit the sub-optimal solutions for SWA.
此外，广泛使用的 DG 数据集，如 PACS ( ≈10K 图像，7 类) 和 VLCS ( ≈11K 图像，5 类)，相较于大规模数据集（如 ImageNet [42] ( ≈1.2M 图像， 1K 类)）相对较小。在这种情况下，我们观察到简单的 ERM 方法在仅仅几个训练周期内迅速达到局部最优，并且容易遭受过拟合问题，即在经过几个训练周期后验证损失增加。这意味着直接应用普通的 SWA 会因平均次优解（即过拟合参数）而遭受过拟合问题。因此，我们需要一种“过拟合感知”的采样调度，以省略 SWA 的次优解。

The main idea of Stochastic Weight Averaging Densely (SWAD) is a dense and overfit-aware stochastic weight gathering strategy. First,instead of collecting weights for every K epochs,SWAD collects weights for every iteration. This dense sampling strategy easily collects sufficiently many weights than the sparse one. We also employ overfit-aware sampling scheduling by considering traces of the validation loss. Instead of sampling weights from K0 pretraining epochs to the final epoch,we search the start iteration (when the validation loss achieves a local optimum for the first time) and the end iteration (when the validation loss is no longer decreased, but keep increasing). More specifically, we introduce three parameters: an optimum patient parameter Ns ,an overfitting patient parameter Ne ,and the tolerance rate r for searching the start iteration ts and the end iteration te . First,we search ts which satisfies mini∈[0,…,Ns−1]⁡Eval (ts+i)=Eval (ts) ,where Eval (i) denotes the validation loss at iteration i . Simply, ts is the first iteration where the loss value is no longer decreased during Ns iterations. Then,we find te satisfying mini∈[0,1,…,Ne−1]⁡Eval (te+i)>rEval (ts) . In other words, te is the first iteration where the validation loss values exceed the tolerance r during Ne iterations.
随机权重平均密集（SWAD）的主要思想是一种密集且关注过拟合的随机权重收集策略。首先，SWAD不是为每个 K 纪元收集权重，而是为每次迭代收集权重。这种密集采样策略比稀疏采样更容易收集到足够多的权重。我们还通过考虑验证损失的轨迹来采用关注过拟合的采样调度。我们不是从 K0 预训练纪元采样到最终纪元，而是搜索起始迭代（验证损失首次达到局部最优时）和结束迭代（验证损失不再下降，而是持续增加）。更具体地说，我们引入三个参数：一个最优耐心参数 Ns，一个过拟合耐心参数 Ne，以及搜索起始迭代 ts 和结束迭代 te 的容忍率 r。首先，我们搜索 ts，使其满足 mini∈[0,…,Ns−1]⁡Eval (ts+i)=Eval (ts)，其中 Eval (i) 表示在迭代 i 时的验证损失。简单来说，ts 是在 Ns 次迭代中损失值不再下降的第一次迭代。然后，我们找到满足 mini∈[0,1,…,Ne−1]⁡Eval (te+i)>rEval (ts) 的 te。换句话说，te 是在 Ne 次迭代中验证损失值超过容忍度 r 的第一次迭代。

4.1 Evaluation protocols
4.1 评估协议

5 Discussion and Limitations
5 讨论与局限性

Despite many benefits from SWAD, such as the significant performance improvements, model selection-free property, working plug-and-play manner for various methods, there are some potential limitations. Here, we discuss the limitations of SWAD for further improvements.
尽管 SWAD 带来了许多好处，例如显著的性能提升、无模型选择的特性以及对各种方法的即插即用工作方式，但仍存在一些潜在的局限性。在这里，我们讨论了 SWAD 的局限性以便进一步改进。
Confidence error in Theorem 1,While the confidence error in Theorem 1 tells the effect of γ on generalization error bound, there exists a limitation in that the confidence error term shows improper behavior with respect to γ if γ is close to zero. The behavior we expect is that the confidence error of RRM converges to the confidence error of ERM as γ decreases to zero,however,the current theorem does not show such tendency since the confidence bound diverges to infinity when γ goes to zero. However, we would like to note that this limitation is not a drawback of RRM, but it is caused by the looseness of the union bound which is a mathematical technique used to derive the confidence error of RRM. Our RRM formulation has a similarity to previous works [26, 34] and we note that the counter-intuitive behavior of the confidence bound and γ also appears in Foret et al. [26].
定理 1 中的置信误差，虽然定理 1 中的置信误差表明了 γ 对泛化误差界限的影响，但存在一个局限性，即当 γ 接近零时，置信误差项在与 γ 的关系上表现出不当行为。我们期望的行为是，当 γ 减小到零时，RRM 的置信误差收敛到 ERM 的置信误差，然而，当前的定理并未显示出这种趋势，因为当 γ 趋近于零时，置信界限发散到无穷大。然而，我们想指出，这一局限性并不是 RRM 的缺陷，而是由于联合界限的松散性造成的，联合界限是一种用于推导 RRM 置信误差的数学技术。我们的 RRM 公式与之前的工作 [26, 34] 有相似之处，我们注意到置信界限与 γ 的反直觉行为也出现在 Foret 等人 [26] 的研究中。
SWAD is not a perfect flatness-aware optimization method. Note that SWAD is not a perfect and theoretically guaranteed solver for flat minima, but a heuristic approximation with empirical benefits. However, even if a better flatness-aware optimization method is proposed, our theoretical contribution still holds: showing the relationship between flat minima and DG.
SWAD 并不是一种完美的平坦性意识优化方法。请注意，SWAD 并不是一个完美且理论上保证的平坦极小值求解器，而是一种具有经验优势的启发式近似方法。然而，即使提出了一种更好的平坦性意识优化方法，我们的理论贡献仍然有效：展示平坦极小值与 DG 之间的关系。
SWAD does not strongly utilize domain-specific information. In Theorem 2, the domain generalization gap is bounded by three factors: flat minima, domain discrepancy, and confidence bound. Most of the existing approaches focus on domain discrepancy, reducing the difference between the source domains and the target domain by domain invariant learning [8-12]. SWAD focuses on the first factor, the flat minima. While the domain labels are used to construct a mini-batch, SWAD does not strongly utilize domain-specific information. It implies that if one can consider both flatness and domain discrepancy, better domain generalization can be achievable. Table 4 gives us a clue: the combination of CORAL (utilizing domain-specific information) and SWAD (seeking flat minima) shows the best performance among all comparison methods. As a future research direction, we encourage studying a method that can achieve both flat optima and small domain discrepancy.
SWAD 并不强烈利用特定领域的信息。在定理 2 中，领域泛化差距受到三个因素的限制：平坦极小值、领域差异和置信界限。现有的大多数方法集中于领域差异，通过领域不变学习 [8-12] 减少源领域与目标领域之间的差异。SWAD 专注于第一个因素，即平坦极小值。尽管领域标签用于构建小批量数据，但 SWAD 并不强烈利用特定领域的信息。这意味着如果能够同时考虑平坦性和领域差异，可能实现更好的领域泛化。表 4 给了我们一个线索：CORAL（利用特定领域信息）和 SWAD（寻求平坦极小值）的组合在所有比较方法中表现最佳。作为未来的研究方向，我们鼓励研究一种能够同时实现平坦最优解和小领域差异的方法。

6 Concluding Remarks
6 结论

In this paper, we theoretically and empirically demonstrate that domain generalization (DG) is achievable by seeking flat minima. We propose SWAD that captures flatter minima than the vanilla SWA does. The extensive experiments on five DG benchmarks show superior performances of SWAD compared with existing DG methods. In addition, combinations of SWAD and existing DG methods even show better performances than the vanilla SWAD. We theoretically and empirically observe that seeking flat minima can achieve better generalizability to both in-domain and out-of-domain, while strong in-domain generalization methods without consideration of flatness, e.g., Mixup or CutMix, cannot guarantee to achieve out-of-domain generalizability in both theory and practice. This study first brings the concept of flatness into DG tasks, and shows strong empirical performances not only in DG but also in ImageNet benchmarks. We hope that this study promotes a new research direction of seeking flat minima for domain generalization and other robustness tasks.
在本文中，我们理论和实证地证明，通过寻求平坦极小值可以实现领域泛化（DG）。我们提出了 SWAD，它捕捉到比普通 SWA 更平坦的极小值。在五个 DG 基准上的广泛实验显示，SWAD 相较于现有 DG 方法表现优越。此外，SWAD 与现有 DG 方法的组合甚至表现出比普通 SWAD 更好的性能。我们理论和实证观察到，寻求平坦极小值可以实现更好的领域内和领域外的泛化，而不考虑平坦性的强领域内泛化方法，例如 Mixup 或 CutMix，无法在理论和实践中保证实现领域外的泛化。本研究首次将平坦性概念引入 DG 任务，并在 DG 以及 ImageNet 基准中显示出强大的实证性能。我们希望本研究能够促进寻求平坦极小值在领域泛化和其他鲁棒性任务中的新研究方向。
