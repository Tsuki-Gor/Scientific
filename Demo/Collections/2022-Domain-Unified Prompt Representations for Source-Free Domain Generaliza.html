Domain-Unified Prompt Representations for Source-Free Domain Generalization
领域统一提示表示用于无需源域的领域泛化

Hongjing Niu*,Hanting Li*,Feng Zhao † ,Member,IEEE,Bin Li,Member,IEEE
邱静 Niu*, 李函廷 Li*, 赵峰 Zhao † ，IEEE会员，李斌 Bin Li，IEEE会员
Abstract—Domain generalization (DG), aiming to make models work on unseen domains, is a surefire way toward general artificial intelligence. Limited by the scale and diversity of current DG datasets, it is difficult for existing methods to scale to diverse domains in open-world scenarios (e.g., science fiction and pixelate style). Therefore, the source-free domain generalization (SFDG) task is necessary and challenging. To address this issue, we propose an approach based on large-scale vision-language pretraining models (e.g., CLIP), which exploits the extensive domain information embedded in it. The proposed scheme generates diverse prompts from a domain bank that contains many more diverse domains than existing DG datasets. Furthermore, our method yields domain-unified representations from these prompts, thus being able to cope with samples from open-world domains. Extensive experiments on mainstream DG datasets, namely PACS, VLCS, OfficeHome, and DomainNet, show that the proposed method achieves competitive performance compared to state-of-the-art (SOTA) DG methods that require source domain data for training. Besides, we collect a small datasets consists of two domains to evaluate the open-world domain generalization ability of the proposed method. The source code and the dataset will be made publicly available at https://github.com/muse1998/Source-Free-Domain-Generalization
摘要—领域泛化（DG），旨在使模型能够在未见过的领域中工作，是通往通用人工智能的可靠途径。由于当前DG数据集的规模和多样性有限，现有的方法很难在开放世界场景（例如科幻和像素风格）中扩展到多种领域。因此，无需源域的领域泛化（SFDG）任务既是必要的，也是具有挑战性的。为了解决这个问题，我们提出了一种基于大规模视觉语言预训练模型（例如CLIP）的方法，该方法利用了其中嵌入的广泛领域信息。所提出的方案从包含比现有DG数据集更多样化领域的领域库中生成多样化的提示。此外，我们的方法从这些提示中产生领域统一的表示，因此能够应对来自开放世界领域的样本。在主流DG数据集（即PACS、VLCS、OfficeHome和DomainNet）上的大量实验表明，与需要源域数据训练的最先进（SOTA）DG方法相比，所提出的方法具有竞争力。此外，我们收集了一个包含两个领域的小数据集，以评估所提出方法的开放世界领域泛化能力。源代码和数据集将公开提供在 https://github.com/muse1998/Source-Free-Domain-Generalization

1 INTRODUCTION
1 引言

Deep learning [1], [2] has achieved great success in many fields, especially computer vision. However, the generalization of deep learning methods is not yet good enough, which has become a constraint for wide applications. In recent years, deep learning generalizability has attracted increasing attentions and sprouted several subfields. Although these studies differ somewhat in their task settings, their ultimate goals are the same, i.e., to enabling models to cope with a wide range of possible scenarios in the open world.
深度学习 [1], [2] 在许多领域取得了巨大成功，特别是在计算机视觉领域。然而，深度学习方法的泛化能力尚未足够好，这已经成为广泛应用的约束。近年来，深度学习的泛化能力引起了越来越多的关注，并催生了几个子领域。尽管这些研究在任务设置上有所不同，但它们的最终目标是相同的，即使模型能够应对开放世界中可能出现的广泛场景。
Generalizability has long been an essential topic in the field of machine learning. In practical application scenarios, the ability to make reasonable predictions on unseen data is critical for machine learning models. In recent years, thanks to the advancement of algorithms and the increase in data scale, deep learning models have achieved satisfactory performance in testing scenarios that are independently and identically distributed with the training dataset. Furthermore, deep learning models could face more challenges, where the testing and training data may obey different distributions.
泛化能力一直是机器学习领域的一个重要话题。在实际应用场景中，对未见数据进行合理预测的能力对机器学习模型至关重要。近年来，得益于算法的进步和数据规模的增加，深度学习模型在独立同分布的测试场景中取得了满意的表现。此外，深度学习模型可能面临更多的挑战，其中测试和训练数据可能遵循不同的分布。
Domain generalization (DG) is one of the most representative tasks and has attracted considerable attentions. For image classification, the DG tasks provide datasets from multiple domains that may come from different acquisition methods or have different image styles. These data are divided into a source domain (for training) and a target domain (for testing), without overlapping between them. For example, models are trained on a photo dataset and tested on a sketch dataset.
领域泛化（Domain Generalization, DG）是最具代表性的任务之一，并引起了相当多的关注。对于图像分类，DG任务提供了来自多个领域的数据集，这些数据集可能来自不同的获取方法或具有不同的图像风格。这些数据被分为源领域（用于训练）和目标领域（用于测试），它们之间没有重叠。例如，模型在照片数据集上训练，在草图数据集上测试。
For DG tasks, the source domain data are often an important factor indicating the difficulty of the task. When the source domain data is rich, the model learns domain-invariant features more easily and presents better generalization capabilities. In the case of PACS [3] dataset, for example, an approach using three source domains can achieve nearly double performance of the one that utilizes a single source domain. In real-world tasks, obtaining sufficiently rich source domain data is often not guaranteed. Reducing the dependence on source domain data in domain generalization is a widely applied and exciting problem, so we propose the source-free domain generalization (SFDG) task. The model is used directly to predict the target domain samples without using the source domain data for training.
对于领域泛化任务来说，源域数据通常是表示任务难度的一个重要因素。当源域数据丰富时，模型更容易学习到领域不变特征，并展现出更好的泛化能力。例如，在PACS [3] 数据集的情况下，使用三个源域的方法可以达到仅使用单个源域的性能近两倍。在现实世界任务中，往往不能保证获得足够丰富的源域数据。在领域泛化中减少对源域数据的依赖是一个广泛应用的且令人兴奋的问题，因此我们提出了无源域领域泛化（SFDG）任务。模型直接用于预测目标域样本，而不使用源域数据来进行训练。
To solve the SFDG problem, we apply a vision-language model for feature extraction. Compared to acquiring a huge amount of images as source domain data, representing the source domain features in the form of text is almost costless. After obtaining the textual encodings of different domains by constructing various textual prompts, we propse a domain-unified prompt representation generator (DUPRG) to integrate these text encodings and aggregate a set of domain-unified text representations. For the target domain samples from the open world, the model prediction is made based on the domain-unified text representations. With the vision-language model, we only need to process the text encoding to obtain domain invariance in the training phase, thus bypassing the need for a large amount of source domain data and achieving source-free domain generalization.
为了解决SFDG问题，我们应用了一种视觉-语言模型来进行特征提取。与获取大量图像作为源域数据相比，以文本形式表示源域特征几乎是无成本的。通过构建各种文本提示，获得不同领域的文本编码后，我们提出了一个领域统一提示表示生成器（DUPRG），以整合这些文本编码并汇聚一组领域统一的文本表示。对于来自开放世界的目标域样本，模型预测是基于领域统一的文本表示进行的。使用视觉-语言模型，我们只需要在训练阶段处理文本编码以获得领域不变性，从而绕过了需要大量源域数据的需求，实现了无源域领域泛化。
Unlike most DG methods, our model does not require source domain data for training, which greatly extends the application scenarios of the task.
与大多数领域泛化方法不同，我们的模型在训练过程中不需要源域数据，这极大地扩展了任务的应用场景。

2 Related Work
2 相关工作

2.1 Domain Generalization
2.1 领域泛化

In this paper, a more challenging task, named source-free domain generalization is proposed based on the domain generalization task. In computer vision, a seminal work [4] suggests that dataset biases can lead to poor generalization performance. For example, a person classifier trained on Caltech101 [5] could obtain a very low accuracy (11.8%) on LabelMe [6]. The domain generalization issue raised attentions, and many wonderful studies have tried to address it since then. From a methodological point of view, the DG approaches can be broadly divided into domain alignment [7], [8], meta-learning [9], data augmentation [10], ensemble learning [11], self-supervised learning [12], disentangling representations [13], regularization strategies [14], etc., which has been discussed in detail in a survey [15].
本文基于领域泛化任务提出了一个更具挑战性的任务，名为无源领域泛化。在计算机视觉领域，开创性的工作[4]表明数据集偏差可能导致泛化性能差。例如，一个在Caltech101[5]上训练的人分类器在LabelMe[6]上可能获得非常低的准确率（11.8%）。领域泛化问题引起了关注，从那时起，许多优秀的研究试图解决这个问题。从方法论的角度来看，DG方法可以大致分为领域对齐[7]、[8]、元学习[9]、数据增强[10]、集成学习[11]、自监督学习[12]、表示解耦[13]、正则化策略[14]等，这些已经在[15]的综述中详细讨论过。
Here, we discuss some nuances of the existing DG tasks from a task setting perspective. In the basic DG task, the dataset consists of samples from multiple domains [3], [16], [17], [18], [19], and in addition to the category labels, domain labels are also provided. These samples are divided into source and target domains, where the source domain is used for training and validation, while the target domain is used for testing only. Domainbed [20] provides a complete process, and many methods follow its experimental settings. Single-source DG [21] is more challenging, since its source domain has only one domain. This makes it difficult to find common domain features. Therefore, most approaches opt for less affected strategies such as data augmentation. Some attempts, like unsupervised DG [22], are made to improve the generalizability from a pretraining perspective. By replacing the commonly used ImageNet pretraining with an unsupervised pretraining approach, the model achieves better performance on downstream DG tasks. Zero-shot DG [23] modifies the category space of the target domain so that it no longer coincides with the category space of the source domain.
在这里，我们从任务设置的角度讨论现有DG任务的一些细微差别。在基本的DG任务中，数据集由来自多个领域的样本组成[3]，[16]，[17]，[18]，[19]，除了类别标签外，还提供了领域标签。这些样本被分为源领域和目标领域，其中源领域用于训练和验证，而目标领域仅用于测试。Domainbed [20] 提供了一个完整的过程，许多方法都遵循其实验设置。单一源DG [21] 具有更大的挑战性，因为其源领域只有一个领域。这使得寻找共同的领域特征变得困难。因此，大多数方法选择采用受影响较小的策略，如数据增强。一些尝试，如无监督DG [22]，旨在从预训练的角度提高泛化性。通过将常用的ImageNet预训练替换为无监督预训练方法，模型在下游DG任务上取得了更好的性能。零样本DG [23] 修改了目标领域的类别空间，使其不再与源领域的类别空间重合。
It can be seen that various difficult versions of previous DG tasks, including unsupervised DG and zero-shot DG, cannot completely discard the source domain.
可以看到，包括无监督DG和零样本DG在内的先前DG任务的各个困难版本，都不能完全放弃源领域。

2.2 Vision-language pretraining
2.2 视觉-语言预训练

In contrast to natural images, language, as a symbolic representation created by humans, is inherently rich in prior knowledge and well interpretable, which inspires researchers to exploit the rich semantic information contained in natural language to help the neural networks to learn a better visual representation [24], [25], [26], [27], [28]. These methods effectively enhance the performance of many cross-modal tasks, such as visual question answering (VQA) [29] and visual commonsense reasoning (VCR) [30].
与自然图像相比，语言作为人类创造的符号表示，本质上富含先验知识且易于解释，这激发了研究人员利用自然语言中包含的丰富语义信息来帮助神经网络学习更好的视觉表示[24]，[25]，[26]，[27]，[28]。这些方法有效地提升了多种跨模态任务的表现，例如视觉问答（VQA）[29]和视觉常识推理（VCR）[30]。
Specially, contrastive language-image pretraining (CLIP) [31] utilizes 400 million (image, text) pairs collected from the internet to learn robust and superior image representations through contrastive learning. Recently, some methods find that CLIP is exceptional at encoding the semantic meaning of visual depictions, regardless of their styles [32], [33], which is in line with the original purpose of the domain generalization task, i.e., learning the uniform visual semantic representations across domains. Therefore, [34] introduced a novel domain generalization paradigm to better leverage various large-scale pretraining models, including CLIP. [35] devised domain prompt learning (DPL) to generate a domain-specific prompt for each image. Similarly, Zheng et al. trained a prompt adapter to produce a suitable prompt for each input image. [36] derived a tractable variational lower bound via approximating the oracle model by a pre-trained model, termed as mutual information regularization with oracle (MIRO).
特别是，对比语言-图像预训练（CLIP）[31] 使用从互联网收集的4亿个（图像，文本）对来通过对比学习学习健壮且优越的图像表示。最近，一些方法发现CLIP在编码视觉描述的语义意义上非常出色，无论其风格如何[32]，[33]，这与领域泛化任务的原始目的相符，即学习跨领域的统一视觉语义表示。因此，[34]引入了一种新颖的领域泛化范式，以更好地利用各种大规模预训练模型，包括CLIP。[35]设计了领域提示学习（DPL），为每张图像生成一个特定领域的提示。同样，Zheng等人训练了一个提示适配器，为每个输入图像生成合适的提示。[36]通过用预训练模型近似 oracle 模型，导出了一个可操作的变分下界，称为带有 oracle 的互信息正则化（MIRO）。
The above approaches require more or less training on the source domain to learn the uniform semantics between different domains. They suffer from two main problems: 1) Limited source domain data is hard to be extended to the rich unknown domains in open-world scenarios (e.g., science fiction and pixelate style), and 2) Well-developed visual semantic and rich domain information embedded in large-scale vision-language pretraining models are not fully utilized. Therefore, we propose a novel domain-unified prompt representation generator to deal with the source-free domain generalization task in open-world scenarios.
上述方法在源域上或多或少需要一些训练，以学习不同域之间的统一语义。它们存在两个主要问题：1）有限的源域数据难以扩展到开放世界场景中的丰富未知域（例如，科幻和像素风格），2）嵌入在大规模视觉语言预训练模型中的成熟的视觉语义和丰富的领域信息没有得到充分利用。因此，我们提出了一种新颖的领域统一提示表示生成器，以处理开放世界场景中的无源域泛化任务。

3 Method
3 方法

3.1 Problem Formulation
3.1 问题公式化

The domain generalization task aims to address the shift in data distribution between different domains by zero-shot transferring knowledge from the source domain to the unseen domain. DG methods usually train a model f:RH×W↦RC on several source domains Ds= {D1,D2,⋯DS} and then evaluate it on unseen target domains Dt={D1,D2,⋯DT}.DK={(xi,yi)}i=1N represents a dataset of the domain K ,which samples N sample points from an input image space XK∈RH×W and a domain-shared label space Y∈RC ,where H and W are the height and width of the input images,and C is the number of the classes.
领域泛化任务的目标是解决不同领域间数据分布的偏移，通过零样本迁移将从源领域获得的知识转移到未见过的领域。DG方法通常在几个源领域 f:RH×W↦RC Ds= {D1,D2,⋯DS} 上训练一个模型 Dt={D1,D2,⋯DT}.DK={(xi,yi)}i=1N，然后在不 seen 目标领域进行评估 K 表示一个领域的数据集 N，它从输入图像空间 XK∈RH×W 和领域共享的标签空间 Y∈RC 中采样 H 个样本点，其中 W 和 C 分别是输入图像的高度和宽度，C 是类别的数量。
However, the existing domain generalization datasets (e.g., PACS [37], and DomainNet [38]) contain only a very limited number of domains (e.g., painting, sketch, and cartoon), which makes it difficult for networks trained on these datasets to generalize to unseen domains (e.g., mosaic and abstract) in the open-world scenarios. Therefore we first introduce a source-free domain generalization task that aims to generalize the visual representations learned by the neural networks to any unseen domain of real-world scenarios without training through the source domain data Ds (i.e.,without sampling images from the source domain).
然而，现有的领域泛化数据集（例如，PACS [37] 和 DomainNet [38]）仅包含非常有限的领域（例如，绘画、草图和卡通），这使得在这些数据集上训练的网络难以在开放世界场景中将泛化到未见过的领域（例如，马赛克和抽象）。因此，我们首先引入了一个无源领域泛化任务，旨在将神经网络学到的视觉表示泛化到任何现实世界场景的未见领域，而无需通过源领域数据进行训练 Ds（即，不从源领域采样图像）。
Fig. 1. Overview of our method for SFDG. First,based on the rich variety of domains in domain bank (DB), we generate M text descriptions under different domains for each class,where M is the size of the domain bank. After that,our proposed DUPRG generates a domain-unified prompt representation for each class for inference. It is worth noting that only DUPRG needs to be trained in the whole paradigm while the parameters of both text and image encoders are frozen.
图 1. 我们用于SFDG的方法概述。首先，基于领域库（DB）中领域的丰富多样性，我们为每个类别生成 M 不同的领域下的文本描述，其中 M 是领域库的大小。之后，我们提出的DUPRG为每个类别生成一个领域统一的提示表示，用于推理。需要注意的是，在整个范例中，只有DUPRG需要被训练，而文本和图像编码器的参数是冻结的。

3.2 CLIP for source-free domain generalization task
3.2 CLIP 用于无源领域泛化任务

To achieve generalization in open-world scenarios, our model needs to consistently model different semantics under a rich set of domains. However, it is not easy to encompass the domain knowledge in the real world using only the current limited-scale DG datasets.
为了在开放世界场景中实现泛化，我们的模型需要在丰富的领域集合下一致性地建模不同的语义。然而，仅使用当前有限规模的DG数据集来涵盖现实世界中的领域知识并非易事。
Recently, large-scale vision-language pretraining models [31], [39] obtain uniform and robust semantic representations [33] by contrast learning over a large number of (image, text) pairs. Especially, CLIP match the accuracy of the original ResNet50 [40] on ImageNet [41] zero-shot without needing to use any images from ImageNet. We believe that the extensive domain information embedded in CLIP is the key to solving the SFDG task.
最近，大规模的视觉语言预训练模型 [31]，[39] 通过对大量的（图像，文本）对进行对比学习，获得了统一且稳健的语义表示 [33]。特别是CLIP在ImageNet [41]上无需使用ImageNet中的任何图像就能达到原始ResNet50 [40]的准确性。我们认为CLIP中嵌入的广泛领域信息是解决SFDG任务的关键。
As a vanilla approach, CLIP uses its text encoder and image encoder to embed the input prompts ("a photo of a {class}") and images into text features Ti∈Rd and image features Ij∈Rd of the same dimension d ,respectively. Then CLIP gives the inference result by calculating the cosine similarity of Ti and Ij ,which can be formulated as,
作为一种标准方法，CLIP使用其文本编码器和水印编码器将输入提示（"一张{类别}的照片"）和图像分别嵌入到相同维度的文本特征 Ti∈Rd 和图像特征 Ij∈Rd 中 d。然后CLIP通过计算 Ti 和 Ij 的余弦相似度来给出推理结果，可以表示为，
y^j=arg⁡maxi(⟨Ij,Ti⟩)
(1)i∈{1,2,⋯,C}.
Where y^j is the prediction made by CLIP on the j -th image, C is the number of the classes,and ⟨⋅,⋅⟩ represents the cosine similarity between two vectors. For SFDG tasks, a naive strategy utilizing CLIP is to use a standard prompt to generalize text prompts for each category, and then finish the prediction following the above inference process.
其中 y^j 是CLIP对第 j 张图像的预测，C 是类别的数量，⟨⋅,⋅⟩ 表示两个向量之间的余弦相似度。对于SFDG任务，一种利用CLIP的朴素策略是使用标准提示来泛化每个类别的文本提示，然后遵循上述推理过程完成预测。

3.3 DUPRG: a new paradigm for source-free domain generalization task
3.3 DUPRG：一种新的无源领域泛化任务范式

Although experiments in [35] show a good performance on several DG datasets with the standard prompt ("a photo of a {class}"). The standard prompt cannot provide an equally robust prompt for visual semantic representations of different open-world domains. For example, "a {watercolor} photo of {class}" can be a better prompt for watercolor images. To accomplish the SFDG task, we need to generate domain-unified prompt representations for each category to cope with the rich open-world domains.
尽管[35]中的实验在几个DG数据集上使用标准提示语（“一张{类别}的照片”）表现出良好的性能，但标准提示语无法为不同开放世界领域的视觉语义表示提供同样健壮的提示。例如，“一张{类别}的水彩画照片”可能更适合水彩图像的提示。为了完成SFDG任务，我们需要为每个类别生成领域统一的提示表示，以应对丰富的开放世界领域。
Based on this viewpoint, we build a domain bank, which contains various domains that exist in the open-world scenarios. As shown in Fig. 1, according to the domain type in the domain bank, M prompts are generated for each class, which means that we can get M×C prompt representations embedded by the text encoder.
基于这一观点，我们构建了一个领域库，其中包含存在于开放世界场景中的各种领域。如图1所示，根据领域库中的领域类型，为每个类别生成了M提示语，这意味着我们可以获得由文本编码器嵌入的M×C提示表示。
Fig. 2. Two implementations of the proposed domain-unified prompt representation generator.
图2. 提出的领域统一提示表示生成器的两种实现。
After getting the M prompt representations of each class, these prompt representations are aggregated by the DUPRG into a domain-unified prompt representation for final inference. We propose two specific implementations of DUPRG in Fig. 2.
获取到每个类别的M提示表示后，这些提示表示通过DUPRG聚合为一个领域统一的提示表示，用于最终的推理。我们在图2中提出了DUPRG的两种具体实现。
Mean Pooling: As shown in Fig. 2(a), the most intuitive way to aggregate these M representations is to take the mean value as the domain-unified prompt representation. The domain-unified prompt representation of the i -th class can be defined as,
平均池化：如图2(a)所示，将这些M表示聚合的最直观方法是取平均值作为领域统一的提示表示。第i类的领域统一提示表示可以定义为，
(2)Ti=∑j=1M⁡TijM,i∈{1,2,⋯,C},
where Tij denotes the prompt representation of the i -th class in the j -th domain.
其中Tij表示第i类在第j个领域中的提示表示。
Cosine Autoencoder: As shown in Fig. 2(b), we propose a cosine autoencoder (CAE) to extract the shared part of the original M prompt representations (i.e.,class semantic related part). A naive autoencoder computes the mean squared error (MSE) between the input and output as the reconstruction loss function [42]. However, the inference process of CLIP uses the cosine similarity to make the final prediction. Therefore, to preserve the semantically relevant orientation in the reconstruction process, we use cosine similarity as the reconstruction loss of the proposed CAE. The reconstruction loss can be formulated as,
(3)Lrec =−∑i=1C⁡∑j=1M⁡⟨Tij,Tij^⟩MC
where Tij^ is the reconstruction output of CAE for Tij . In addition, in order to obtain a domain-unified representation, an intra-class loss is used to narrow the gap between representations of different domains, which can be computed
as,
(4)Lintra =−∑i=1C⁡∑j=1M⁡⟨Tij,Ti―⟩MC
where Ti― is the mean value of M prompt representations of i -th class. Finally,an inter-class loss is defined to make the cosine similarity of the representations of different classes in the same domain as low as possible (i.e., obtain a larger inter-class distance in each domain). The inter-class loss can be defined by,
(5)Linter =∑i=1M⁡∑j=1C⁡∑k=1,j≠kC⁡⟨Tji,Tki⟩MC(C−1).
The overall loss function of CAE is defined as follows,
(6)Lall =Lrec +λ1Lintra +λ2Linter 
Where λ1 and λ2 are two hyper-parameters controlling loss coefficients. The domain-unified prompt representation can be directly calculated by,
(7)Ti=∑j=1M⁡Tij^M,i∈{1,2,⋯,C},
It is worth noting that no images are involved in the whole training process of CAE. Our method takes M×C prompt representations generated from the domain bank as the input to train the CAE. Since our method does not overfit the limited source domain image data and learns a unified prompt representation from rich domain type in the domain bank, it can be adapted to more unseen domains in richer open-world scenarios.

4 EXPERIMENTS

Our code is implemented in PyTorch (and will be open source). The datasets and experiment settings used are described below.
我们的代码是在 PyTorch 中实现的（并将开源）。下面描述了所使用的数据集和实验设置。
Datasets. We experiment on 5 benchmark datasets including PACS [3] (4 domains, 9,991 samples, 7 classes), VLCS [16] (4 domains, 10,729 samples, 5 classes), Office-Home [17] (4 domains, 15,588 samples, 65 classes), Ter-raIncognita [18] (4 domains, 24,778 samples, 10 classes), and DomainNet [19] (6 domains, 586,575 samples, 345 classes). These datasets are all representative and widely used in DG tasks. In a DG task, experiments are usually performed using the leave-one-out strategy. For a dataset, one of the domains is selected as the target domain at a time, and the other domains are used as the source domains. In the SFDG task proposed in this paper, one domain is also selected as the target domain each time, but the data from other domains will not be used as the source domain.
数据集。我们在 5 个基准数据集上进行实验，包括 PACS [3]（4 个领域，9,991 个样本，7 个类别）、VLCS [16]（4 个领域，10,729 个样本，5 个类别）、Office-Home [17]（4 个领域，15,588 个样本，65 个类别）、Ter-raIncognita [18]（4 个领域，24,778 个样本，10 个类别）和 DomainNet [19]（6 个领域，586,575 个样本，345 个类别）。这些数据集在域泛化任务中都具有代表性且被广泛使用。在域泛化任务中，实验通常采用留一法策略进行。对于数据集，一次选择一个领域作为目标领域，其他领域作为源领域。在本文提出的 SFDG 任务中，每次也选择一个领域作为目标领域，但是其他领域的数据将不会被用作源领域。
Details. We adopt the pretraining weights of CLIP and apply ViT-B/16 [43] as the backbone for the experiments without special instructions. More experiments using other backbones can be found in Appendix.2. The CAE is an autoencoder consisting of linear layers and activation functions, and the number of neurons in the hidden layer is (512, 256, 512). The optimizer is AdamW with a learning rate of 0.04 . The max epochs is 1000 , since the training data is scarce and CAE needs to be finely tuned to achieve a better reconstruction.
详细信息。我们采用 CLIP 的预训练权重，并且在没有特别说明的情况下，使用 ViT-B/16 [43] 作为实验的骨干网络。更多使用其他骨干网络的实验可以在附录 2 中找到。CAE 是一个由线性层和激活函数组成的自动编码器，隐藏层中的神经元数量为（512, 256, 512）。优化器是 AdamW，学习率为 0.04。最大训练周期数为 1000，因为训练数据较少，CAE 需要精细调整以达到更好的重建效果。

4.1 Domain Generalization
4.1 域泛化

We compare our approach with some strong DG baselines including SOTA. The results are shown in Table 1. As discussed in Section 2, some of the compared methods incorporate elaborate learning algorithms and some works incorporate ensemble learning. It is noteworthy that all these methods require more or less training on the source domain. Table 1 shows that our method achieves over or near SOTA performance on most datasets. We also notice that our method does not perform well on TerraIncognita. We speculate that the possible reasons are (1) different domains are simply taken from different cameras, so the domain gaps of TerraIncognita are much smaller compared to other datasets such as PACS; (2) some categories in Ter-raIncognita are rare (e.g. bobcat) and have small differences between classes (e.g., bobcat and cat), which makes source-free prediction extremely difficult.
我们将我们的方法与一些强大的DG基线进行了比较，包括SOTA。结果如表1所示。如第2节所述，一些比较的方法集成了精心设计的学习算法，而一些工作则集成了集成学习。值得注意的是，所有这些方法都需要在源域上进行或多或少的训练。表1显示，我们的方法在大多数数据集上实现了超过或接近SOTA的性能。我们还注意到，我们的方法在TerraIncognita上的表现不佳。我们推测可能的原因是：（1）不同的域仅仅是来自不同的相机，因此与PACS等其他数据集相比，TerraIncognita的域差距要小得多；（2）TerraIncognita中的一些类别很罕见（例如bobcat），并且类别之间的差异很小（例如，bobcat和cat），这使得无源预测变得极其困难。

Domain bank. The rich and diverse prompt can be considered as the raw material for constructing domain-unified prompt representations. Compared to collecting domain-specific image datasets, generating prompts in textual form can be very easy. We integrated the domains of all the used datasets into a domain bank as the defalut setting for all the experiments in this paper, which corresponds to "combined" in Table 3. In fact, the domain bank can be easily expanded or reduced. Table 3 shows the experimental results corresponding to different domain banks. On one hand, when the bank shrinks to the limit (empty set), the prompt set degenerates to the standard prompt. 为了进一步展示所提出CAE在生成领域统一提示表示上的效果，我们通过t-SNE [56] 在PACS和OfficeHome数据集上可视化了CAE的输入和输出特征分布。如图4所示，不同领域（橙色）的输入提示表示相对分散，而输出提示表示（蓝色）则紧密分布。这意味着我们的CAE过滤掉了领域相关部分，同时保留了类别相关部分。
Since both strategies calculate the domain-unified prompt representations by utilizing the mean pooling operation in Fig. 2, the center of the prompt representations serves as the domain-unified prompt representations for each class. 由于两种策略都通过利用图2中的均值池化操作来计算域统一提示表示，提示表示的中心作为每个类的域统一提示表示。如图4(b)的放大部分所示，输出特征（蓝色）的中心与输入特征（橙色）的中心相比有明显的偏移，这是由域相关部分的过滤带来的。因此，我们的CAE可以用来生成更好的域统一提示表示。

5 Conclusion
5 结论

In this work, we develop a more challenging version of the domain generalization problem that requires good performance in the target domain without using the source domain data. This paper also proposes a feasible solution to the source-free domain generalization problem. By transforming the need for image feature consistency into constructing domain-unified prompt representations through a vision-language model, we bypass the need for source domain data. Experimental results demonstrate that our method exhibits excellent performance on most of the tested datasets without using source domain data, achieving a competitive performance compared to other approaches trained with source domain data. More importantly, we substantially outperformed current SOTA DG methods on open-world domains without training with multi-source domain data.
在这项工作中，我们开发了一个更具挑战性的领域泛化问题版本，该版本要求在目标域中不使用源域数据就能获得良好的性能。本文还提出了一个可行的无源领域泛化问题的解决方案。通过将图像特征一致性的需求转化为通过视觉语言模型构建领域统一提示表示，我们绕过了对源域数据的需求。实验结果表明，我们的方法在没有使用源域数据的情况下，在大多数测试数据集上表现出卓越的性能，与其他使用源域数据进行训练的方法相比具有竞争力。更重要的是，在没有使用多源域数据进行训练的情况下，我们在开放世界域上显著超越了当前的最新领域泛化方法。

.2 Backbones
.2 主干网络

In the proposed framework, the only part that needs to be trained is the proposed CAE (domain-unified prompt representation generator), while the parameters of the text and image encoders are fixed. These encoders are replaced to verify the generalizability of the proposed approach for different model structures. We implemented ResNet [2] and ViT [43] and generate prompt representations with different strategies, "SP": using standard prompt ("a photo of a {class}") to generate the only representation for each category; "MP": the prompts generated by different domains are aggregated by the mean pooling operation; "CAE": using CAE to generate the unified representations. The results are shown in Table 5. Regardless of the model structure, using CAE to generate unified-representations is always the best for both ViT-based backbones and is comparable with the mean pooling strategy for ResNet50, which demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed CAE.
在所提出的框架中，唯一需要训练的部分是提出的 CAE（域统一提示表示生成器），而文本和图像编码器的参数是固定的。这些编码器被替换以验证所提出方法对不同模型结构的泛化性。我们实现了 ResNet [2] 和 ViT [43]，并采用不同策略生成提示表示，"SP"：使用标准提示（"一张 {类别} 的照片"）为每个类别生成唯一的表示；"MP"：通过均值池操作聚合不同域生成的提示；"CAE"：使用 CAE 生成统一的表示。结果如表 5 所示。无论模型结构如何，使用 CAE 生成统一表示始终是对于基于 ViT 的骨架来说最好的，并且对于 ResNet50 与均值池策略相当，这证明了所提出 CAE 的有效性。
